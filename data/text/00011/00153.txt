WELCOME TO OUR NEW MEMBERS
The Center is at the present time engaged in an extensive membership campaign. Listed below are the names of those
who have affiliated with the Center during the past fiscal year. We welcome these additions to our Center family and wish
for them and ourselves long years of happy association and mutual benefit.

the lifeblood of our synagogue. Every member should constitute himself a committee of one to report to the Center office the
name of any person who he thinks would be interested in affiliating with the Center.
