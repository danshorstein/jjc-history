AL H. EDWARDS
Although comparatively inactive during the past year,
the Y. M. H. A. looks forward to the coming year with
renewed vigor.
The annual “March of Dimes” dance proved to be
outstandingly successful as in previous years and the “Y”
is planning other affairs of similar nature that will be of
community wide benefit.
As part of their community service program the “Y”
presented the nurses of Hope Haven Hospital with a com¬
bination Radio-Television set.
During the coming year the “Y” will strive with other
local organizations to build a bigger and better Jewish
Community in Jacksonville.
