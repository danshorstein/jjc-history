ATHLETICS
The Center’s new Activities Building will be put to
use for recreational purposes in the coming season and the
athletic program promises to be the best the city has known.
It will include:
★ All types of indoor athletics and recreation.
★ Health rooms and individual exercises.
★ Outstanding basketball court.
★ Seating for 700 at games.
★ Finest showers and locker rooms.
★ Gym clothing storage accommodations for 300.
THE YEAR’S RECORD
SOFTBALL LEAGUE 1950-51 was the 5th season under
Ben Cohen’s direction. Games ran from April to Labor
Day in two halves. A.Z.A. won the first and second
halves, becoming the champions for the year. Games are
played Sunday mornings at 9 o’clock.
In the Open Church League, supervised by the City
Recreation Department, our team did well the first half.
Coach Syd Cohen’s absence from the city and players
joining the Armed Forces made the going hard towards
the end of the season.
BASKETBALL LEAGUE The Center also had a team
entered in the Springfield Church Basketball League. The
team placed second with a record of 18 wins and 2 losses.
Our team also made its annual visit to Savannah and
is looking forward to a return visit this fall.
