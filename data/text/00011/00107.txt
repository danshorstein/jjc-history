Shabbat Hagadol. The Sabbath which precedes the great Festival of
Passover derives its name from the Haftorah which contains a reference
to the “Great Day of the Lord.’’
Pesah (Passover) celebrates Israel’s deliverance from Egyptian bond¬
age. The festival lasts eight days during which matzot are eaten in
the place of leavened bread. The celebration of the Passover with the
beautiful ceremony of the Seder on the first and second evenings has
fostered by Jewish people the ideals of liberty and freedom.
