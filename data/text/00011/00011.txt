One of the most gratifying achievements of the ever
expanding program of the Jacksonville Jewish Center has
been the evolution of the Center Year Book into the un¬
usual publication that it is today.
Not only does the Book chronicle the progress of the
institution year by year, but it has developed into an un¬
paralleled fund-raising activity of the Center Sisterhood.
This development is underscored by the character of this
year’s publication, representing as it does the largest and
most complete book ever published, and including the
events which took place as part of the dedication rites of
the Center’s New Activities building. It is a certainty that
this year’s book will become a permanent record in the
home of every Center member.
A highly commendable feature of the Center’s year
book is the fact that no paid solicitors are engaged, and
outside of actual printing costs, all proceeds go to the
educational, religious and charitable work of the Center.
It is for this reason that the Center membership sincerely
appreciates the cooperation and good will of those ad¬
vertisers who have this year inserted their advertising to
the greatest extent ever.
Much of the success of this year’s edition is due to the
sacrificial devotion and zeal of the chairman of the Com¬
mittee, Hyman Selber and to the two chairmen of the
Sisterhood’s special solicitations committee, Mrs. Hyman
Selber and Mrs. Joseph Krestul. Thanks also go to Miss
Ethel Joel for her efficient handling of the section devoted
to New Year’s greetings.' Remittances for these greetings
should be made to Miss Joel at 2772 Post Street or at the
Center office.
