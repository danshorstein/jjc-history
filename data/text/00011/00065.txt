The Young Peoples League is the national organization
affiliated with the Conservative Synagogues throughout
America.
At the Jacksonville Jewish Center, Y. P. L. seeks to
develop a program of religious, cultural and social activities
which will provide us with a stepping stone to our future
role in the Jewish community. In line with these objec¬
tives, we have tried to bring the cultural aspects of Judaism
to our meetings through round table discussions and out¬
side speakers. On the religious level, we took an active
part in the Youth Services during the High Holy Days,
and sponsored the annual Student Homecoming Services.
HORTY THIER
Our social activities provide an important link in rounding
out our program.
Membership in Y. P. L. is open to all young adults of
the Center.
We hope that the attainment of these objectives will
help us in assuming our proper place in the congregation.
