The National Council of Jewish Women is a service
and educational organization that unites all Jewish women
—Orthodox, Conservative and Reform. Council’s program
presents an opportunity to serve effectively the interests of
our community, country, and world.

Council’s overseas services include support of the
School of Education of Hebrew University in Jerusalem,
maintenance of a home in Paris for unattached Jewish
women, shipments of work and play materials to young
people abroad and scholarships to women overseas who
are trained in America for welfare work so they may go
back to their own country and better the conditions abroad.
There is a place for every Jewish woman in Council’s
overall program.
