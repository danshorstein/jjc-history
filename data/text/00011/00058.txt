3. THE PYRAMID BRIDGE — Under the Chairmanship of Mrs. Shelby Trager, this provided many lovely parties
to raise funds.
4. THE CORONATION BALL — Under the chairmanship of Mrs. Maurice Cherry, Mrs. Mannie Goldstein, and
Mrs. Joe Krestul produced a memorable occasion for the first social affair in the new building. The ball was such
an outstanding success that the Sisterhood has asked the same chairmen to sponsor the event for the coming year
and hope to develop it into an annual affair.
5. SPECIAL CONTRIBUTIONS — These contributions have been made as follows:
Silverware for the kitchen was purchased by a contribution from a trust fund held by Miss Ethel Joel, Mrs. A1
Sager and the late Mrs. Ida Feldman.
One dozen serving trays from a group under the chairmanship of Mrs. Morris Margol.
Contribution from group composed of Mrs. Max Rose, Mrs. W. M. Schemer, Mrs. Rose Sloat and Mrs. Harry
Slott.
Special gifts from Center members in honor of happy events such as Bar Mitzvahs, Bas Mitzvahs and Con¬
firmation.
6. THE DONORS — A direct solicitation of funds by Mrs. Ralph Mizrahi and her many Captains which culminated
with a lovely tea honoring all those who contributed to this project.
