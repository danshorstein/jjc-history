The Ladies Hebrew Sheltering Aid Society was founded
in 1922 by a small group of women, staunch in purpose
and great of heart, resolved to work together to give aid
and sustenance to those less fortunate than themselves.
They dreamed a dream and with vision and foresight,
courage and fortitude, gave unstintingly of their energy
toward working this dream into a reality. The zest and
sincerity with which they worked was infectious and soon
many warm hearted people wanted to help, for they too
realized the need for a home for the aged in Jacksonville.
MRS. MAURICE PALOT
Today, our beautiful River Garden Home for the aged
stands as a living monument, a shining symbol, an example
of achievement made possible by the tireless efforts, the
driving force of compassion toward their fellowman;
which twenty-nine years ago made a small group of women
of vision gather together and dream a wonderful dream.
