The Progressive Credit Union, now in its eleventh year
of operation has grown to a membership of almost three
hundred and with assets of over $150,000.00.
The Progressive Credit Union is operated under a State
charter and under the supervision of the State Banking
Department. The affairs of the Credit Union are regularly
examined by the Banking Department of the State of
Florida and by the Supervisory Committee of the Credit
Union. The report of the State Examiners have always
been favorable to the organization in that it always com¬
plies with all the rigid banking regulations for the protec¬
tion of its members. All persons handling the Credit Union
Funds are under surety bonds in amounts required by the
Credit Union National Association. Funds in the Credit

Our organization carries life Savings Insurance and
Loan Protection Insurance with the Cuna Mutual In¬
surance Society for the benefit of its members. The
premiums for this coverage is paid for by the Credit Union
without any cost to the individual members. The Life
Savings Insurance insures the life of each insurable mem¬
ber in an amount equal to the savings accumulated previ¬
ous to age fifty-five not exceeding one thousand dollars.
Members are insured in decreasing percentage of savings
deposited at ages beyond fifty-five. Loan Protection In¬
surance covers the life of each insurable borrower, under
the age of seventy, in the amount of the unpaid balance
of his loan.
