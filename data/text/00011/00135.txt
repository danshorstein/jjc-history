Young Judea is the organization of Zionist youth clubs
welcoming members from the ages of 10 to 17. It is
sponsored locally by Hadassah and the Jacksonville Zionist
District and is under the auspices of the Southern Zionist
Youth Commission.
Last year Young Judea progressed under the capable
leadership of Mrs. Milton Wasserman, Young Judea
Chairman and Mrs. Raymond Winterfield, Co-chairman.
Assisting them were Mrs. Jacob J. Becker, Youth Com¬
mission Chairman and Mrs. L. M. Levy, Co-chairman,
Miss Ruth Leonard, Chairman of the Leader’s Council,
Mrs. William Goldman, Fair Co-chairman and the fol¬
lowing club leaders: Herbert Smullian Rae Greenfield,
Anita Friedman, Louise Chonin, Evelyn Herzberg, Dolores
Feinberg, Toby Thier, and Junior leaders, Barbara Chard-
koff, Harry Katz, Jr., Helene Zimmerman and Gary Bloom.
The Senior clubs launched the year’s activities by hold¬
ing a Conclave in Jacksonville, at which time a regional
executive meeting was also held. It was attended by 80
local and out-of-town members and proved an inspiring
beginning to a year of devoted club work. In addition to
their regular cultural and social programs, skits, dances,
a hayride and fashion shows were presented by the dif¬
ferent clubs. An outstanding affair of the season was the
Masquerade Ball which promises to be a successful annual
event. Senior and Intermediate clubs participated in the
program “Operations Tel Yehudah” given during the
Hadassah Conference.
Junior clubs presented a Parents Night program and
also sponsored a Country Fair.
All the Jacksonville Young Judean Clubs are proud to
report that they have successfully filled their quotas for
H. E. D. (Hava Emergency Drive). The Hava is a new
training center to be built near Jerusalem by the American
Judeans and the Tsofim (Scouts of Israel).
Summer camp scholarships were awarded to Rose Lynn
Levin, Jean Portnoy and Beatrice Chardkoff to Camp Tel
Yehudah, and Vivian Eff to Camp Hatsofeh.
Any girl or boy desiring to join a club may contact
Mrs. M. W. Goldstein, Young Judean Chairman for the
coming year.
