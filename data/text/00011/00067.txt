MISS RUTH SPIWAK
Organized two years ago, the Center Youth League
has steadily progressed to become one of the leading youth
groups in the city. Serving as a religious, cultural, and
social outlet for the Jewish teenagers of the community,
the League has become an important element working to¬
gether with its affiliated groups for the advancement of
the Jewish Center.
The year’s activities included meetings of cultural value
during which time a number of informative speakers were
brought before the group. The group also participated in
many planned social activities. Our Spring dance, en¬
joyed by all, served as a successful ending to an equally
successful year.
Under the able leadership of Gary Bloom, the group
progressed steadily and smoothly during the past year. A
vote of thanks is also due Miss Ruth Spiwak, advisor,
whose tireless efforts greatly enhanced the success of the
league.
With the new building and all its facilities as an ad¬
ditional asset, we of the Center Youth League cannot help
but anticipate another year of great achievement.
