Oh, yes, we can well be proud of the membership
of the Jacksonville Jewish Center. We are on the
threshold of our greatest development. This is
but one milestone, but we can now see that if we
all do our continued share in the undertaking, we
can see completion in sight.
Mr. President, on behalf of the Building and
Expansion Committee, its co-chairmen, and all
its members, on behalf of all who have contributed
towards its erection, on behalf of all the sub¬
committees that have toiled so incessantly — in
this consecrated hour, seated as we are in the com¬
pany of good and loyal people, good Jews, mem¬
bers of the Jacksonville Jewish Center, I present
to you, as the chosen representative of our syna¬
gogue, this beautiful Youth Activities Building.
May its use be of everlasting joy and benefit to
all who enter therein. May the philosophy of its
endeavors endear themselves to all of us. May
God grant us wisdom and strength to exercise the
use of the building to its fullest extent, and may
God grant that this dedication be not only for the
material building itself, but that we remain dedi¬
cated and re-dedicated to the service of our fellow
man, our fellow Jews everywhere, and to the
¡service of our Faith and to God.
