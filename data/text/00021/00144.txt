Left to right: Jason Solomon, Religious Ch.; Elaine Lin-

Our newest USY group consists of 8th grade boys and
girls who meet bi-weekly with emphasis being placed on
Pre-USY orientation. Their activities will be geared to
prepare them for membership in our older USY chapters.
Ada Hinda Block and Linda Gartner will be their advisors
for 1962-63.
