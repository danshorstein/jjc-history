The Brownie and Girl Scout Troops sponsored by the
Center Sisterhood have again shown how our youth group ac¬
tivities are growing through the Center. Looking back through
the years we find some of our leaders today were our Girl
Scouts of yesteryears.
We have two active Girl Scout Troops, one in the Arlington
area and one in the Southside area. Our Brownies have one
Troop in Arlington and two in Southside.
This year Girl Scouts celebrated their 50th anniversary. In
this period the organization started with 12 girls in Savannah,
Ga. Today three and a half million girls and adults in all
fifty states and in foreign countries belong to Girl Scouts of
the U.S.A. This shows Scouting holds something for girls
through the generations.
This coming year we look forward to continuing our pro¬
gram with the help of our mothers who give their time and
efforts to those girls who are eager to learn just what Scouting
is.
