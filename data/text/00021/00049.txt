The newest addition to our already outstanding synagogue
program is the creation of our Foundation School. We have
completed one year with our first grade and the results have
been fruitful and gratifying. Our first graders are adept both
in English and Hebrew and their progress in relation to the
general school curriculum has been excellent.
Dr. and Mrs. Jack Rosner served as the chairmen of the
Parents’ Committee; Mr. Jack Becker did a yeoman task in
raising the funds to make our school possible; Mrs. Marsha
Berdit was in charge of transportation. The cooperation of all
parents was indeed of the finest.
Mrs. Sheldon Gendzier will be the chairman of the P.T.A.
this year with Mrs. Louis Safer as Secretary and Mrs. Robert
Wolf as Treasurer. The room mothers for the first grade are
Mrs. Edward Bernard and Mrs. Irving Hacker. For second
grade the room mothers are Mrs. Raymond Cohen and Mrs.
Jack Shorstein. Mrs. Frank Rosenberg and Mrs. Morton Ben¬
jamin will serve as Chairman and Co-Chairman of the fund
raising committee. Program coordinator will be Mrs. Moshe
V. Goldblum.
Mrs. Marilyn Sandler and Mrs. Jewell Varnedo have been
engaged to teach this year and will be assisted by Mrs. Leon
Zucker.
