B’nai B’rith promotes a platform of brotherhood, fellowship
and interfaith amity. B’nai B’rith works tirelessly on behalf of
many worthy community projects.
A. B. Weil Lodge #800, B’nai B’rith will strive under its
new officers to build up its membership and achieve a goal for
the betterment of this community. Interesting luncheon meetings
are held at the Hotel George Washington the second and fourth
Thursdays of every month, with four dinner meetings during
the year. The annual Scholarship Fund Dance will be held on
November 17 to provide funds for scholarship loans to worthy
Jewish boys and girls to further their education.
The men of B’nai B’rith are united to make its program play
a vital role in the future of our community.

the future we must deeply serve others before we can serve
ourselves. The Jacksonville chapter has made tremendous prog¬

Fidelity and Devotion, by supporting various national B’nai
B’rith philanthrophies such as National Jewish Hospital, Leo
N. Levi Hospital, Bellfaire Children’s Home and support to
Israel projects.
