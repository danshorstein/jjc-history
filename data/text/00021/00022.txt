B’nai B’rith Girls, junior order of B’nai B’rith Women,
climaxed a tremendously successful year. Among our many ac¬
tivities, B.B.G. ,along with our brother Alephs sponsored a
North Llorida Council Workshop and an Inter-Laith Service.
Some of the highlights of the year in B.B.G. were a "Roaring
Twenties" dance, several bake sales, a trip to Hope Haven Hos¬
pital, a salami sale, a candy sale, a contribution to the Interna¬
tional Service Lund, and an end-of-the-year party.
To conclude this year an annual Installation Luncheon was
held. A plaque for the "Most Outstanding Girl" was presented
to Lesley Kidder by the Past President of B’nai B’rith Women,
Mrs. Jack Dorman.
