Serving the entire Jewish community of Jacksonville, the
Jewish Community Council is the central Welfare Fund and
Community Relations body of the local Jewish community. An
association of individuals, synagogues, organizations and social
service agencies, the program encompasses key activities which
give unity, cohesion and coordination to local Jewish communal
life.
A community-wide annual Welfare Fund Campaign pro¬
vides support to significant local, national and overseas organ¬
izations. The National United Jewish Appeal, has expressed
American Jewry’s responsibility for the welfare and rehabilita¬
tion needs of Jewish communities the world over for over 20
years. River Garden Hebrew Home for the Aged and the Jew¬
ish Welfare Society are the local beneficiaries.
The Council publishes a monthly newspaper known as the
Commentator. It is the official organ of the Council and its
member organizations.
The Council conducts a leisure time activities program which
includes Youth Council programs such as college preparation
workshops, a youth news page in the Commentator, Sunday
sports and a youth division in the annual Jewish Welfare Fund
Campaign. The Council also sponsors a community summer
Day Camp for children between the ages of 4 and 15.
