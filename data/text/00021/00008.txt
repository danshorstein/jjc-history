Another banner year in the annals of Scouting for Troop 14
started this past year with a swim and hot dog party at the home
of Mr. and Mrs. Joe Wilensky, with several new boys joining
the Troop and bringing our total membership to 44 active
Scouts. The next long hike took place with the three day Ross
Allen annual Camporee in October at Silver Springs, Fla. Our
entire troop came home with an excellent rating in this under¬
taking. Other excursions throughout the year were visits to
the U.S.S. Roosevelt Aircraft Carrier, a three day hike to Camp
Echokotee, another three day hike to Keystone Heights on Lake
Brooklyn and our big summer Scouting activity, the summer
Scout Camp where 21 of our Scouts enjoyed the outdoors for
an entire two week period. While at camp Jordan Harris, Joe
Saportas and Leon Talpalar were tapped and initiated into the
Order of the Arrow, an honorary society for Scouts.
