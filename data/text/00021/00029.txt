SHIV’AH ASAR B’TAMMUZ (17th Day of Tammuz), a fast day
followed by 3 weeks of mourning. It commemorates the breach¬
ing of the walls of Jerusalem by the Babylonians, which led
to the exile of the Jews. (Our symbol, the catapult.)
TISHA B’AB (9th Day of AB), anniversary of the destruction
of the 1st and 2nd Temples—a fast day climaxing a 9-day
period of mourning.
