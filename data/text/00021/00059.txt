Our New Sunday Afternoon Program
90 boys and 30 girls took part in our Sunday Afternoon Program which consisted of social ballroom dancing instruction with
Maria Douglas studios, Gymn athletics consisting of volleyball, Jai Alai, basketball and badminton, while the boys took part in a
six-team intramural baseball league.
Our six team baseball league was coached by Ed Witten, Chuck Slott, Jerry Cohn, Fred Shenkman, Ivan Tobias and Benjie Felder.
A year round program of football, basketball and baseball is planned for the coming year, under the supervision of Robert
Fischer.
