This year the Esquire Club has had a very successful year.
We wish to thank our sweetheart, Bonnie Barnert, and our
Advisor, Byron Selber, for their efforts on our behalf.
The outstanding social events were the Annual Holiday
Dance at the Robert Meyer Hotel and the Esquire Week-end
at Atlantic Beach.
The service projects included contributions to the Center
and participation in the annual Cerebral Palsy Telethon.
