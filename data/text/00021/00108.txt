OFFICERS
The Progressive Credit Union sponsored by the Jewish
Progressive Society and chartered by the State of Florida, has
continued to grow in its 23 years. The combined membership
of Credit Unions in the U.S. totals more than 14 million mem¬
bers and has over four billion dollars in assets.
From a very modest beginning of 20 members and $500.00
in assets, the Progressive Credit Union has grown to over 350
members and almost ]/4 million in assets. It is organized to
encourage thrift and provide a safe, convenient medium whereby
members can invest and accumulate savings. Being regularly
examined by the Comptroller of the State, the savings of mem¬
bers are safeguarded in several ways.
The Progressive Credit Union carries life savings insurance
and loan protection insurance with the Cuna Mutual Insurance
Society for the benefit of its members. The premiums for this
coverage are paid for by the Credit Union without any cost to
the individual members. Last year the Board declared a 3%
dividend on savings and 10% rebate of interest to borrowing
members.
The year’s activities climax with the annual dinner meeting
at the Center. A cordial invitation is extended to anyone who
wishes to join.
