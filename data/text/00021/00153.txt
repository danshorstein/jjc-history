The Mr. and Mrs. Club of Jacksonville, now in its ninth
year, provides for its membership of approximately 100 couples
a congenial and close fellowship in a year-round social atmos¬
phere. It has become one of the Jewish community’s outstand¬
ing organizations.
Each month throughout the year social functions, planned
and carried out by committees, are provided the membership.
MR. AND MRS. CLUB BOARD
These monthly affairs may vary from a family picnic to an
elaborate dinner-dance. The .annual Installation Banquet and
the New Year’s Eve Party are the highlights of each year.
Each member couple serves on at least one monthly committee.
Membership in the Club is by application, and couples
interested in affiliation are invited to contact any of the officers
or members of the Board.
