The essential function of the Zionist Organization of Amer¬
ica is to keep alive in this country an interest in the State of
Israel. This interest is many-sided, as the efforts of Zionism
always were. The political objective was the creation of a Jewish
State. To this end it was necessary to revive the Hebrew lan¬
guage, acquire land, train colonists for settlement and raise funds
to finance the project. All this the Zionist movement did.
Today, the Z.O.A. continues to foster support for the State
of Israel. Moreover, the Z.O.A. seeks to strengthen the bonds
between American Jewry and the Jewish State on the basis of
the cultural tradition that is our common heritage.
Religious differences present no obstacle as Zionism en¬
compasses all shades of religious belief.
The Z.O.A. invites all Jewish men to join its ranks.
