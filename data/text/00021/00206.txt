Youth Activities
Center Youth Organizations
Center Youth League—A co-ed USY Chapter for 11th and 12th graders, meeting bi¬
weekly with varied programs throughout the year.
CPC Boys—USY Chapter for 9th and 10th graders affiliated with national organization.
CPC Girls—Similar to above with a female accent on activities.
Pre-USY—Composed of 8th grade boys and girls preparing for USY membership, meet¬
ing bi-weekly.
United Synagogue Youth of which the above four clubs are members are served
by the Southeast Region Office, Rabbi Allen Rutchik, Dir., 1075 - 95th Street, Miami
Beach 54, Fla.; National Headquarters, Dr. Morton Siegel, Dir. ,1123 Broadway,
#803, New York 10, N. Y.
Boy Scouts Troop 14—For boys 11 to 14, meeting weekly on Tuesday evenings.
Cub Scouts, Pack 14—Boys 8 to 11 participate in this organization, meeting weekly at
Den Mothers’ homes with monthly Pack meetings on the last Thursday of the month.
Girl Scouts, Troop 197—For girls who have outgrown the Brownies and wish to con¬
tinue Scouting.
Brownie Troops 28, 193, 210 and 346—A Scouting organization for younger girls with
weekly gatherings.
Majors Pre-Teens Social club, monthly, for 7th grade boys and girls.
Tween-Agers—Boys and girls of the 5th and 6th grades participate in this social dub
meeting monthly.
