The local Brandéis University National Women’s Committee
was organized eleven years ago. No fund-raising activities are
held; all money is derived from Annual and Life Memberships
and from contributions to the Book Fund. Meetings are held
twice a year. All activities are directed to the support of the
Library, the heart of the University.
