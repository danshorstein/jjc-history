The National Council of Jewish Women is a volunteer
service organization which through a program of service, edu¬
cation and social action helps to meet human needs.
This past year Council sponsored Jacksonville’s first Mental
Health Fair in conjunction with the Mental Health Association
of Duval County. Because of the success enjoyed by the Fair,
Council has been asked to sponsor it again this ye,ar. Also in¬
cluded in this year’s new projects will be helping the hard-of-
hearing children under the guidance of a trained specialist in
the school system.
Council’s local projects also include recreational activities
at the Juvenile Shelter and at River Garden. In the past three
years Council has tested the hearing of almost 30,000 school
children, has continued to provide wheelchairs, crutches, and

Council continues its support to Israel by training leaders
for welfare and educational work through scholarships and by
sending toys and clothing to children in Israel.
Through its programs and projects Council provides a
strong, unifying cord of mutual interests and aims for all
Jewish women, regardless of a diversity of thinking and back¬
ground, so that they may work together in the spirit of Judaism
for the good of all humanity.
The Jacksonville Section has both a daytime and evening
group, meeting every third Wed. and fourth Thurs., respec¬
tively.
