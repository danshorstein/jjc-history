Ladies Hebrew Sheltering Aid Society was founded 39 years
ago as an aid society to help the needy of the Jewish faith. It
was always the dream and hope of our founders to have a
Home for the Aged in Jacksonville. This dream became a
reality when River Garden Hebrew Home for the Aged was
founded. Since that time, our role became that of the auxiliary
to the Home.
We provide extra-budgetary needs such as furnishings,
redecorating, air conditioning, kitchen and infirmary equipment.
Funds for these needs were raised through Tombola, Donor
and Social Printing projects as well as annual dues and life
memberships in Ladies Aid. Linens were provided by our an¬
nual Linen Shower.
We sponsor a Grey Ladies Program, regularly scheduled
entertainment and parties. River Garden residents participate

in projects served by the auxiliary such as the residents’ maga¬
zine, Ceramic classes and the Hobby Shop.
The Golden Sunshine Club of River Garden which emerged
from our weekly Senior Citizens meetings has grown into a
self-sufficient organization giving fellowship and companion¬
ship as well as participating in civic and philanthropic activities.
This year Ladies Aid pledges to continue its efforts and
cooperation in bringing not only the material but the spiritual
values that our elder citizens hold so dear.
