MRS. LOUIS SLOTT
To understand the importance of Sisterhood women, we
must be able to visualize the place of Sisterhood in the entire
communal pattern. The Sisterhood is organized primarily to
help the Synagogue. As an auxiliary of the Synagogue its func¬
tion is to promote Jewish educational, religious and social activ¬
ity among the wives of Synagogue members as well as their
friends and mothers of the children who attend its religious
schools.
Many thanks to the members of the Sisterhood executive
board, to the chairmen of the various committees and to the
many women who gave of their time and their talents to make
the past year so successful in every phase of Sisterhood activity.
Thanks also to the members of the entire congregation and
their friends who so loyally supported our fund-raising affairs,
for without you it would have been difficult to achieve our
many goals.
Our plans for the future are great, but we need YOU!
We can pool our efforts and together create or achieve some¬
thing richer and finer than what anyone of us can do alone.
The best wishes of the Jacksonville Jewish Center Sister¬
hood are extended to you and your dear ones for a happy and
healthy New Year.
