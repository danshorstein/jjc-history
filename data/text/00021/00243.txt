This coming year promises to be a banner year for the
Center Men’s Club. Primary emphasis will be placed on en¬
couraging regular attendance at the monthly dinner meetings
on the first Thursday of every month, through a constant effort
to have interesting and informative programs, delicious meals
and attractive door prizes. The membership drive is now under
way and all interested men are encouraged to contact one of
the officers or the Center Office. Our annual paid-up member¬
ship dinner will be held on Sunday, November 4th.
In addition to providing a fine opportunity for fellowship,
the Men’s Club provides scholarships for Camp Ramah, a
Blood Bank, adult education program, sponsors Sunday Re¬
ligious School picnic, supports the Sunday morning Minyan
breakfasts, and provides awards for the Scouting program along
with scholarships to Camp Philmont.
