Rabbis Page
One year has passed since I was deeply honored
to become the Rabbi of the Jacksonville Jewish Cen¬
ter. No year has ever passed so quickly and no year
has been so full of activities and experiences. Com¬
ing to know the program and policy of the congrega¬
tion has been a challenge and learning to know the
congregation itself has been a most gratifying experi¬
ence.
This coming year presents me with an opportu¬
nity of serving you on a much more personal and
knowledgeable basis. Our opportunities to work to¬
gether will be enhanced and mutual understanding
will become wider and deeper.
RABBI MOSHE V. GOLDBLUM
Rabbi Aronson delivering his message
at the Rabbi's Installation.
My feelings of a close personal relationship with
the Center program I hope is a contagious one. It is
so vitally necessary that we all feel that the goals of
the congregation are ours and their realization will
benefit us personally. Our personal participation and
concern will determine the success of Jewish life in
our midst.
On behalf of my wife and family, may I wish you
a very happy and healthy New Year, one of meaning¬
ful life and one of peace for all mankind.
