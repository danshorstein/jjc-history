Summer at Camp Ki Tov was an amazing experience
campers will forever remember with a smile! The
eight weeks were filled from beginning to end with
fun activities for all ages. Campers enjoyed exciting
daily camp activities such as sports, swimming, arts
& crafts, and even drama. New friendships were
formed, old ones were rekindled, and a great time
was had by all!
Each week campers participated in activities
revolving around different themes. A few of the
favorites were “Hooray for Hollywood”, “Weird Science” and “Around the World”.
Campers had a blast dressing up their counselors, competing in Color Wars, and splashing
each other at FlagPOOL. Both campers and counselors enjoyed the songs and
costumes during the Camp Zimriah and the laughter could not be contained at
our carnival.
Field trips included a day at World Golf
Village, Diamond D Ranch, Marineland,
St. Augustine Alligator Farm, The
Jacksonville Zoo, MOSH, and more!
Electives, such as cooking, computers,
and guitar were a huge hit. Middle school
campers enjoyed creating their own video
games during Computer Camp. ls,-5th
grade campers learned lots during Lego Camp and our youngest campers
created fabulous dishes during Cooking Camp!
Of course, summer camp would not be complete without sleepovers and undemights. Our Chaverim (lst-8th grade)
campers become night prowlers when they spent the night
at the Jacksonville Zoo and our Rishonim (1-5 year olds)
campers turned
