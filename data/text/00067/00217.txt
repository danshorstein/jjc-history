Margo’s Catering
Your simcha is in good hands with Margo’s. From planning to
presentation, you’ll appreciate the professional touches that the catering staff
brings to every event. The menus are varied, creative and delicious. The service
is excellent, and the ambiance is always inviting. Best of all congregants can
count on Margo, Andrew and Catrina to be helpful, friendly and reassuring.
It’s a pleasure to plan a party or congregational program at the Center. Whether
it’s a Bar or Bat Mitzvah, wedding or other occasion, you can trust Margo’s
Catering to make it memorable.
Maintenance & Housekeeping Staff
The maintenance and housekeeping staff works diligently to keep our building clean and
presentable at all times. Richard Ford, our facilities supervisor, takes pride in his staff, and
devotes much of his time to taking care of all the physical needs required by the many events
which take place throughout the year. Volunteers, event planners and Center staff appreciate
the special attention they receive from the maintenance department. Richard and his staff offer
invaluable support to the members of our congregation.
