Special Events
2008 Raffle for Education
The 33rd Annual Center Raffle/
Fundraiser for Excellence was a
beautiful celebration of Jewish
education and the Jacksonville
Jewish Center schools. This
wonderful morning was chaired
by Holly and Mark Emas,
Andrea and Steven Mail and
Honorary Chairpersons Edith
and Bruce Horovitz. Along
with an amazing committee
they created an opportunity for
550 adults to attend a beautiful brunch at the Jacksonville Jewish Center. In
addition, 170 children attended the complimentary 4Mini-Raffle’ which had
fun activities including a puppet show by Joanne Davis. Seventy Center
students participated in the main Raffle event singing HaTikvah, God Bless
America and Its a Small World in Hebrew (Zeh Olam Katan Meod). The
main prize was won by Betsy and Dennis Mizrahi who have two daughters,
Rachel and Evelyn, in the Martin J Gottlieb Day School.
The Rabbi David Gaffney Leadership in Education Award was presented to
Mrs. Zipora Zucker for her dedication and service to our community and
to the students whose lives she touched during her 40 years at the Center.
Fundraising goals were exceeded by the generous support of the community.
All funds were designated to projects which promote excellence in Jewish
and secular education at the Center Schools.
