Jewish Family & Community Services is here for you
JFCS is here to provide needed services to
Jacksonville’s Jewish community and beyond. Members of
our Jewish Community are always our priority.
Our goal is to fully meet the needs of the Jewish
community while providing significant services to the
broader community in the Jewish tradition of tikkun 01am.
JFCS provides services for every phase of the life cycle, from birth (adoption)
to end of life (Jewish Hospice) and for all the life challenges in between by offering
counseling, support groups, children’s programs, financial assistance and senior
services including, Jewish Healing Network and our new Medical Advocate Program
(MAP). The positive impact of these programs is seen and felt by members of the
Jewish community who come through our doors everyday.
The programs JFCS offer tie threads together to change a life. And that one
life will touch many others. On behalf of our board, staff and clients we are offering
sincere thanks to our Jewish community for making it possible for JFCS to provide
services to our Jewish citizens, of all ages and backgrounds.
Thank you.
