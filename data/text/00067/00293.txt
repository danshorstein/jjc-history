Shalom Baby;
Mommy & Me with a Jewish Twist
The Jacksonville Jewish Center Preschool’s Shalom Baby program has been operating for two years now. Classes meet
weekly to offer mommies and babies an environment to play and learn. Each class has a Jewish twist to it... either in story, song,
craft or circle time. Each class also has an exciting topic to bring moms together for discussion.
Shalom Baby offers its classes following the Preschool’s calendar and also the camp schedule to make it easily available
to moms or caregivers with older children in the Preschool or Day School.
If you are interested, please stop by the Preschool office for more information, or call Jennifer Katz (Class instructor) at
268-4200x143. Hope to see you soon!
