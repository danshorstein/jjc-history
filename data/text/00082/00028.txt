Troop 14, Jewish Center Boy Scout troop, is entering upon the eighteenth
year as a member of the Greater Jacksonville Council of the Scout movement.
During that time the troop has participated in many outstanding events, and
during the past year has won several athletic championships.
The troop offered its services to organizations affiliated with the Center
at their various functions, and reached the climax of their activity in the special
Friday night service which they sponsored and in which five of their number
participated.
The troop successfully defended their city-wide Scout basketball champion¬
ship, by completing their second successive undefeated season. Although the
group dropped two games in both the football and diamond ball leagues, their
showing in both sports was commendable.
It is hoped that closer cooperation by the troop committee with the troop
will result in even greater progress for the organization.
