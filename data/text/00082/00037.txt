The F. T. E. Club was organized in 1931 at the Jacksonville Jewish Center
for social and charitable purposes, its membership consisting of young Jewish
girls.
During the past year, the Club experienced the most active and profitable
season since its organization, many enjoyable affairs being given including the
annual Florida-Georgia Football Dance. The Club celebrated its Fifth Anniver-
sry last November by a brilliant banquet which was held at the George Wash¬
ington Hotel. A special attraction of the event was the presentation of a Club
pin to each member. As a closing event of the season, a “Come as You Please
Dance” was given in June of this year at the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
A large portion of the proceeds from the Club’s various affairs is donated
to charity throughout the year. The Club has been foremost in aiding the
Jewish Center by donating money, presenting the Center several years ago with
some new prayer books. For the past three years th F. T. E. Club has played
a large part in sponsoring Mother’s Day at the Center at one of the Friday
night services, at which time the Club presents each Mother with a rose.
The Club is again looking forward to a very enjoyable season. The
various affairs that are being planned include the annual Florida-Georgia
Football Dance to be held November 6 and the Anniversary celebration, the
date of which will be announced later.
