The local chapter of Junior Hadassah, the young women’s division of the
Zionist Movement, experienced another year of extensive activity and splendid
work, and looks forward to the coming year with sincere hopes of even bettering
their outstanding record of the past year.
Junior Hadassah fills a vital need in Jewish life today by mobilizing the
forces of American young Jewish womanhood in the historic movement of
effecting a renaissance in Jewish life through the rebuilding of Palestine as
the Jewish National Home.
In carrying out its program last year, Junior Hadassah sponsored a number
of events, included among which were the annual Yom Kippur dance, several
parties, raffles, a dance at the Windsor Hotel, the sponsoring of a picture with
the Senior Hadassah, several donor affairs, a membership tea, and a paid-up
membership affair in the nature of a Barn Dance.
