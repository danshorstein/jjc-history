Sprinkler Is First
At Downtown Blaze
An automatic sprinkler system at
Duval Motor Co., beat the fire de¬
partment to a fire there at 4 a. m.
today.
A spontaneous combustion blaze
in a pile of oil soaked rags set off
two units of the sprinkler system
and also set off the fire alarm.
Damage was less than $25, officials
of Moore Pipe & Sprinkler Co. said
after a survey. They installed the
Grinnell system at the Duval Mo¬
tor Co.
The fire department carries re¬
placement parts for the sprinkler
system, which was repaired im¬
mediately. The fire was out when
apparatus arrived.
