B’nai B’rith Women are dedicated to serving you,
your family, your country and humanity. This is car¬
ried through by supporting different national B’nai
B’rith philanthropies such as Bellefaire Children’s
Center; Leo N. Levi Memorial Hospital; National
Jewish Hospital and B’nai B’rith Center for Jewish
patients at Mayo Clinic. B’nai B’rith Women also
sponsors the Children’s Home in Israel and other Is¬
raeli projects. Locally, our activities include: The Anti-
Defamation League (“Dolls for Democracy” program,
distribution of memo calendar books to the public
schools and keeping members aware of the problems
in the Jewish Community); helping at River Garden
Home; sponsoring the B.B. Girls; Hillel at U. of Fla.;
and helping in all community drives. In the coming
year we hope to make the name B’NAI B’RITH syn¬
onymous with our motto: Service, Fidelity and Devo¬
tion.
THE INFINITE GOD’S BEAUTIES, CREATURES
AND THE GARDEN OF EDEN
God’s beauty in our little star
Among the numberless billions and not far.
Beauties and angel, always permanent
But the Satan destructive of no comment.
The Almighty Creator had it by saying: TO BE
To an invisible space with glorious deed.
Beauties and ugliness are the creation of he Lord
I shouted with prayer as I am in Spirit of many fold.
An adimrable view of space
That fills the infinite atmosphere in every place.
God’s miracle and beauties are found in every step of life
In act and good building that keeps us alive.
No miracle to HIM
But to HIS children that praise HIM.
Beauties and God’s love are an infinite foundation
The whole world bows to HIM, so do every nation.
Can we glorify HIM; can we love HIM
What can we do, if it wasn’t for HIM!
Beauties is the life of the Venus star
That lift the soul with joy, high above the cloud.
Beauties in all His creatures
As of good machinery well manufactured.
The wealth of our planet begins with blessing to HIS
As the smile of a mother to her child with cuties.
Beauties in all phases
As the light of God on all faces.
Will close as the beauties of flower of pretty roses.
Which made by His act by saying TO BE and Rose
Beauties and God’s love are an infinite foundation
As a winning war well fought.
By: Isaac M. Kabbany
