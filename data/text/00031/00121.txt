Girl Scouting is more than a club, it is a nation¬
wide, worldwide movement for girls age 7 to 17. Girl
Scouts make a promise and live by the Girl Scout laws.
These simple guides prepare a young girl to live in
our bewildering modem world.
GIRL SCOUT PROMISE
On my honor, I will try:
To do my duty to God and my country,
To help other people at all times,
To obey the Girl Scout laws.
Troop 162 has fulfilled all the obligations of Scout¬
ing throughout the past year by their excellent parti¬
cipation in troop and city wide events. Under the leader¬
ship of Mrs. Albert Levy, assisted by the Troop Com¬
mittee: Mesdames Harry Berman, Leonard Saraga,
Harvey Leff and Arnold Seebol, the girls have had a
busy and rewarding year.
