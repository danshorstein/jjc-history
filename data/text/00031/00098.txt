Our Synagogue Activities offer unique op¬
portunities to stimulate educational, spiritual
and social growth within a Judaic environ¬
ment.
From early teens through college age, our
program is designed to offer the individual the
resources and direction to enable him to pro¬
mote self-identity in a changing world and to
express himself in a sometimes “fun atmos¬
phere”.
Through adult activities we re-dedicate
ourselves to the past traditions of Judaism
and are concerned with today’s issues of Peace,
Morality, Civil Liberties, Soviet Jewry, the
Arts, and all contemporary issues and prob¬
lems.
