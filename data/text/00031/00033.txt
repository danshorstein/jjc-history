It is with a great deal of pride that I write this
message to the members, friends and supporters of the
Jacksonville Jewish Center.
Another year of growth, accomplishments and
challnges has been completed. Dedicated leaders,
members and staff, together have deepened and
extended the activities and programs of our congrega¬
tion.
In a generation whose primary concern is the
future of Judaism, it is gratifying and comforting to
know that we are part of a Synagogue-Center com¬
mitted to the enrichment of Jewish life, Jewish educa¬
tion and Jewish youth.
As we strive to insure the continued growth of the
Jacksonville Jewish Center, it is good to know that
our friends, many of whose names appear within these
pages, have joined us in our endeavor.
May the cooperative efforts of all concerned grow
from strength to strength and Jewish lives be made
more meaningful and secure through the work of our
congregation.
On behalf of the entire staff of the Jacksonville
Jewish Center, I feel privileged to extend warm
greetings for a happy, healthy and prosperous New
Year.
A. ROBERT GAST
Executive Director
