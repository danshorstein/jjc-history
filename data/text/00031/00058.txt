ATID is the College Age Organization of Judaism
sponsored by the United Synagogue of America. It
was formed and is led by college students and college-
agers who want to live and learn Judaistn and con¬
tinue their identification with the Jewish Movement.
ATID invites all college age students who wish to
involve themselves in Jewish life and thought. ATID
meets on a weekly basis during the summer vacation
to discuss current topics of interest, followed by a social
