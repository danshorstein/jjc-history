River Garden Auxiliary was founded 44 years ago
by a very dedicated group of women whose aim was to
create a home for Jewish aged in Jacksonville. This
dream became a reality in i946 when River Garden
Hebrew Home for the Aged was officially founded.
The Auxiliary provides the extra budgetary needs
as furnishing, redecorating, air conditioning, kitchen
and infirmary equipment, and hobby shop materials.
Funds are raised through our Tombola, Donor, social
printing projects, as well as annual dues and life mem¬
berships. Linen at the Home is replenished through our
annual Linen Shower affair.
In the realm of service Ladies Aid sponsors activ¬
ity and volunteer programs. Our Red Cross Volunteer
service to our residents is outstanding. The Red Cross
Officers of the Auxiliary
Junior Aids (of which we have 18) have functioned
superbly. Our members provide transportation for non¬
residents to attend the weekly Golden Sunshine meet¬
ings held at River Garden. The Auxiliary continues to
lend support to the Golden Sunshine Club. Our mem¬
bers provide entertainment and refreshments for the
residents of special holidays.
We renew our pledge of continued devotion and
service to River Garden and its residents in the hope
that we can help bring some measure of comfort and
meaning to their lives.
