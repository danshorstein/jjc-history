Nobody consciously looks for troubles but with all
the affluences, comforts, and luxury around us, and
heaven almost within our grasp, troubles, tragedy, and
unhappiness remain with us exaggerated and sharpened
by the contrast. Frustration and alienation frequently
dog the successes we strive for. Problems and personal
crises confront us in all groups.
The deeply ingrained concern for one’s fellow man
that led to the founding of the Jewish Family and
Children’s Services fifty years ago also remains with
us as does the constant search for new and more
refined techniques and resources to meet the perennial
as well as the newer problems.
To help meet our current needs the JGCS now
offers:
1. Family and Individual Counselling
2. Employment and Vocational Counselling
3. Services to Senior Citizens
4. Adoption Program
5. Foster Home Care
6. New American Resettlement and Restitution
Programs
7. Short-term financial assistance
8. Transient Services
9. Program to help Persons in Prison
10. Day Camp and College Scholarship Program
11. Friendly Visiting Program
If a personal crisis should confront you or if you
would like to help someone with problems, you will
be assured of a warm, confidential, and professional
response.
