The Jacksonville Jewish Center Men's Basketball Team played in the
city wide church league and wound up in second place at the end of the
season, winning 22 games and losing four. The Center Team has been play¬

same next year and win first place.

four teams under the leadership of Stanley Kantor. We also had a baseball
league in connection with the Center Day Camp and Handball every Sun¬
