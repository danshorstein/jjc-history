The Mr. and Mrs. Club is now in its fifteenth
successful year with a dual goal of promoting friend¬
ship and enabling married couples to share good times.
Each month, exciting and novel affairs are held and
guided through the leadership of committees formed
to arrange each gala event.
The themes vary from month to month, but tradi¬
tional events include an extravagant New Year’s Eve
party, a mid year Installation Banquet, and a family
picnic or outing during the summer months.
As in all other social clubs, there is a central
core of officers and board members, and the organiza¬
tion is governed by husbands and wives serving and
working together. Through each member’s sharing the
responsibility of serving on a monthly committee, an
atmosphere of warm congeniality always prevails, and
the promotion of good fellowship is the end result.
All couples between thirty-five and fifty-five years
of age, who are interestd in sharing good times and
friendly companionship, are urged to contact any club
officer or board member.
