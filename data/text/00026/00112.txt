SCENES AT SCOUT CAMP
Ten boys attended a one-week session of summer
Camp at Echocotee during the month of June. Lee
and Roy Lasris and Mr. Harry Berman, assistant
Scoutmaster, were tapped out for the Order of the
Arrow during ceremonies held at Camp. We came back
with almost 30 merit badges and advancements, the
largest single total in several years.
Bruce Darlow had an additional week aat Baden-
Powell Scout Reservation when he was selected to
attend the Jr. Leadership Training Course there.
Troop 14 meets every Tuesday night when school
is in session and goes on a hike about once every six
weeks, leaving on Friday afternoon and returning Sun¬
day noon. During the summer we spend one week in
camp and then meet once every other Tuesday to work
on a project. The project at present is the building
of a canoe. Any boy over the age of 11 is invited to
join.
