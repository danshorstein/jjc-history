The Choral Society at the Annual Concert
On Sunday, May 7 the Center Choral Society and
Glee Club presented the 19th Annual Concert of
Jewish Music and featured Hazzan Isaac Goodfriend
of Ahahath Achim Congregation, Atlanta, Georgia as
guest artist. The theme for the Concert was “Contri¬
bution of East European Jewry to Jewish Music.”
The Choral Society is the parent organization of
the Center Choir that serves on Friday nights and an
augmented Choir for the High Holidays.
The Glee Club is composed of members of our
Religious School and performs particularly at various
Holidays in the year.
The entire musical program is under the direction
of Cantor A. Marton. The music program of the Cen¬
ter was organized in order to serve our congregation
and to stimulate an appreciation for Jewish music and
culture.
CANTOR A. MARTON
