The Officers and Board of Directors of the Jack¬
sonville Jewish Center were installed at the Installa¬
tion Dinner held on Sunday, March 12th, with Mr.
Jack Becker installed as President. Rabbi Sol Landau
of Miami, Florida was the guest speaker at the Dinner
which was chaired by Mr. David R. Lewis.
Shown on this page are pictures taken at the af¬
fair: The head table which includes Mr. M. D. Evans,
Chairman of the Building Fund, Mrs. Evans, Rabbi
and Mrs. Lipnick, Mr. and Mrs. David R. Lewis, Mr.
and Mrs. Jack Becker, Mr. and Mrs. Burton I. Gross-
man, Chairman of the Board, Cantor and Mrs. A.
Marton; picture of Rabbi Lipnick, Rabbi Landau and
Mr. Becker; bottom picture of Rabbi Lipnick, Ben
Friedman, Co-Chairman of the Building Fund, Jack
Becker and Wilbur Margol, Co-Chairman of the Build¬
ing Fund.
