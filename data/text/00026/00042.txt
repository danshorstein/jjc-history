We have great things in store for the new look of Sister¬
hood for the coming year. Perhaps you’ve heard — we’re
introducing the CIRCLE SYSTEM. Just to give you a
preview of the Circles, they are:
JEWISH FAMILY LIVING IN THE HOME
JEWISH FAMILY LIVING IN SYNAGOGUE
ADULT EDUCATION
SOCIAL ACTIONS
YOUTH
ARTS AND CRAFTS
PUBLICITY
PROGRAMMING
SUNSHINE
Each member and prospective member will be contacted
so we can function with new zeal and enthusiasm. The circle
will be composed of 20 to 25 women. The individual circles
will meet preferably once a month, a day and time suitable
to the majority of the circle. The circle will contain a Chair¬
man; vice-chairman; secretary; telephone chairman; and de¬
votional chairman. The circle will meet in individal homes on
a rotating basis. In addition to the circle meetngs, for we
are but a spoke in the entire wheel of Sisterhood, the entire
Sisterhood will continue to hold regular monthly meetings.
The change will be that a particular circle will be called upon
to contribute for a specific meeting.
The purpose and aim of the circle is to:
Create a warmer atmosphere with more personal
contact.
Create a source of supply for every facet of Sister¬
hood work.
Increase attendance at ALL Sisterhood functions,
by creating greater interest of overall program.
Discover and develop potential leadership,
to achieve better understanding of aims, objectives,
purposes and policies of Sisterhood.
Now let’s all work together as a really active Sisterhood—
so that the meaning of Sisterhood is really “Sister-Friend”
and not just empty words.
