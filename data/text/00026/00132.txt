The activities of the Tofield Memorial Library this
year have been centered on making the congregation
more aware of the library facilities available.
Increased bulletin publicity, window and bulletin
board displays, continued Friday night library hours
and presentation of the panorama of library activities
at the Oneg Shabbat in conjunction with the Center
Sisterhood were committee activities to implement
this awareness.
