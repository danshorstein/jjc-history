Another successful year of Cub Scouting has
passed and all the leaders under the able direction of
Committe Chairman Melvin Jawitz are already recruit¬
ing new members for the coming year. All interested
boys between 8 and 10 years of age are urged to con¬
tact the Center Office for further information.
Den meetings are held weekly with Pack Meet¬
ings being held the last Thursday of each month.
Cub Pack 14 was commended for taking part in
the city wide Goodwill collection drive.
At the annual Blue and Gold Banquet several
awards and recognitions were given to the Troop and
its leaders. Sidney Engelhardt is the Cubmaster and
has served in this capacity for some time. Mrs. Lawr¬
ence Maslow was our very able Chief Den Mother.
