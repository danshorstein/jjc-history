FOUNDATION SCHOOL
On a comparative basis, the Solomon Schechter
Day School is representing a much larger percentage
of children population in our congregation than in
many of the larger metropolitan centers. Thanks to
the donation of a TV set, our pupils can take advant¬
age of the weekly educational programs on Channel 7.
Classes are ideally arranged with the largest having
only 16 pupils. The Center is fortunate in having an
outstanding group of teachers whose experience
through the years have added to the value of this
special program.
