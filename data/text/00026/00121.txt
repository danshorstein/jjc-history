miles south of Beauclerc Country Club. The Camp

Picnc area, Snack Bar and room available in large white
house for meetings.
The Recreation Program is under the chairman¬

site Committee solicits family memberships and rental
for private parties, organizational gatherings and gen¬
erally strives to promote the use of our beautiful 36
acre campsite.
