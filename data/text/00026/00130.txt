“The Thirty-Two Club”, an organization to pro¬
vide fellowship between young couples of the Jewish
faith through social activities, has just celebrated its
sixth birthday. The group meets on the first Saturday
evening of each month and has varied parties ranging
from the Florida-Georgia Football Game Party to the
Gala New Year’s Eve Ball.
Interested couples may contact this year’s Presi¬
dent, Mr. Norman Gross, concerning membership.
