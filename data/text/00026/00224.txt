Our maintenance staff shoulders one of the heaviest burdens in
our Center program, that of maintaining our facilities so that they
are always in a presentable state for our use. Tim Riley, who has
been with the Center for over twenty years, coordinates all mainten¬
ance at the Center under the direction of our Excutive Director.
Annie Mae Fort, who has been with the Center for almost twenty
years, maintains our kitchen and prepares meals for adult programs, meet¬
ings and the vast food program for our school children.
Also preparing tasty food at many of the Center's affairs and members’
simchas are our local caterers: Mr. and Mrs. Albert Meyerhoff and Mrs.
Mary Safer.
Our appreciation to this loyal and devoted staff who have many years
of unselfish service to the Center.
