Officers of Charles Palot Club
After a trial period of splitting our membership into a
boys5 and girls’ group, this 9th and 10th grade club voted
to reunite and continue its organization as a co-ed USY
group.
Their programs during the past year included several
outstanding topics, i.e., handwriting expert, Religious Editor
of the Times^Union, Judo exhibition, long distance tele¬
phone call, a discussion with the Rabbi, etc. The group had
a large delegation at the Regional Convention in Mont¬
gomery and are sending 12 delegates to the summer L.T.I.
held annually at Gamp Blue Star in August.
In its fund raising program the Club had several projects
and also contributed $109.00 to make Jacksonville’s total
contribution $209 to the National B.S.B. program. Many
thanks are due to our advisors, Enid Goldberg, Evelyn
Brauner and Pepi Spielberger for their efforts on behalf of
our organization.
The following are the delegates from G.P.C. to L.T.I. at
Camp Blue Star in Hendersonville, N. Car.: Peggy Cohen,
Leslie Edwards, Dolly Fleet, Barbara Gefen, Gilda Goldman,
Hilda Goldman, Beverly Hart, Wendy Klein, Jack Mizrahi,
Donald Safer, Billy Wilensky and Leon Yergin.
