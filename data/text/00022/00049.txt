Our 5 th successful summer under the direction of
Robert Fischer concluded with an ever increasing enroll¬
ment and outstanding program. The baseball program, CIT
program, Color War and other highlights instituted under
our current leadership were the mainstays of our successful
summer.
Our enrollment averaged 224 campers per week with
over 370 youngsters taking advantage of our nine week
summer camp. With an eye towards the future, the Day
Camp Committee and staff look forward to a new begin¬
ning with the 1964 season at its newly acquired site on
State Road 13 with a permanent home for our summer
operation.
