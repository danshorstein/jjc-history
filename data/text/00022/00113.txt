The Center’s three Girl Scout Troops and two Brownie
Troops completed another successful year with their an¬
nual Banquet held in June. Several awards and recogni¬
tions were presented at the Banquet with Mrs. Bond of
the Girl Scout office praising the Center’s sponsorship of
our five troops.
Sincere appreciations go to our Troop leaders and
their co-leaders for the devoted task which they perform
during the year. The Troops meet weekly at the leaders’
homes with groups located in each section of the city.
