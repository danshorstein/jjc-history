Building Fund Committee
Chairman: Louis Safer Treasurer: Louis Portnoy
Through the efforts of the Land Acquisition Committee under the co-chairmanship of Dr. Neal Roth and Sam Spevak,
the Center has recently acquired for its future needs a 36 acre site on State Road #13, 2/2 miles south of Beauclerc Coun¬
try Club. In planning the future of our dynamic Synagogue-Center, the Board feels that this large site will enable us to fully
nurture the type of program that our members desire.
This past year witnessed the refurbishing of our entire Youth Activities Building and the complete painting of the ex¬
terior of the entire Synagogue and Activities Buildings. In striving to maintain our building in top functional condition, the
air conditioning system was increased from its original capacity of 80 tons of cooling to a new capacity of over 200 tons.
We hope that our membership will make maximum use of our facilities and enjoy the comforts of our beautiful structure
with its new adequate cooling system.
Our members can feel proud of past accomplishments and we urge our members to continue their loyal support of the
Building Fund so that our physical facilities will always be a credit to our congregation.
