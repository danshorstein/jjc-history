Since its inception in January of 1963 this young single group has grown to a
membership of 70 members with monthly affairs under the chairmanship of the
current officers. The monthly functions are held at various locations as per the
theme of the affair. Some of the events this past year included a Party at the Robert Meyer Hotel Pool, a Splash at the
Julington Creek Marina, a Get-Acquainted Party at the Center and a Cocktail Party at the home of Leon Rosenberg.
The group is open to all single people between 18 and 30 years of age. Anyone interested is asked to contact any of
the above officers.
Newly elected officers for the 1963-64 season are Frank Freedman, Chairman; Barbara Silverman, Assistant Chairman
and Martin Esroff, Treasurer.
