For twenty-seven years the Esquire Club has been a leading social club for
young men in Jacksonville. Interests of the club include social, athletic, cultural and
religious activities. Outstanding social events were the annual Holiday Dance and the
Western Hayride. Esquire is a member of the Jacksonville Youth Council, an af¬
filiate of the Jacksonville Jewish Community Council.

popular sweetheart, Miss Mary Friedman, and our advisors, Esquire expects 1963-64
to be its greatest year.
