Young Judaea, sponsored by Hadassah and Z.O.A., has completed a
prosperous and successful year. Young Judaea activities included Israeli
dancing at the Civic Auditorium, three regional conventions, and various
religious and social programs.
Thirteen Jacksonville Judaeans attended the Southern Region Sum¬
mer Convention at Camp Judaea in Hendersonville, N. C. in June. The
following were elected to serve as officers: Jerry Linsey, Southern Region
President; Howard Moscovitz, Southern Region Vice-President; Michael
Pousman, National Representative; Ira Brukner, Regional Programming
Committee; Evelyn Goldstein, “Miss Southern Region.”
Camp Judaea in Hendersonville is a major project of Young Judaea.
Hadassah and Z. O. A. sponsor this 4 or 8 week camp period with a full
camping and Zionist program. Camp Judaea serves to develop in youth
the ideals of American freedom, an appreciation of the Jewish people,
a bond with the land of Israel and an understanding of the religious
and ethical concepts of Judaism.
Club leaders for the coming year are Carol Feinberg, Bas Yisrael;
Mrs. I. Brukner and Jerry Linsey, T.A.C., Sybil Horovitz, Tova.
