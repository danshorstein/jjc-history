Cantor And Choir
Center Choral Society
The 30 voice adult Choral Society composed of Center members which performs at various Center and community
functions throughout the year.
The Center Choral Society and Glee Club, pictured on this page along
with our music Director Abraham Marton, conducted the 15th Annual Con¬
cert staged at the Jacksonville Jewish Center. This annual event is held in
conjunction with National Jewish Music Month and is dedicated to the
Cantors’ Institute of the Jewish Theological Seminary of America.
This past year’s Concert held on March 3rd featured “Avodath Hako-
kesh”, the Sacred Service by Bloch, and was attended by over 500 from the
community. The entire Center membership looks forward to this annual event
at which time our Choral Society and Glee Club are highlighted along with
outstanding guest soloists. This year’s soloists included Edward Doe, Karen
Armel, Paul Vasvari, Roselyn Klein and Sam Sherter and members of the
St. Johns Opera Co., with Jonathan Bowers as pianist.

Center Glee Club
Boys and girls from our Hebrew School and Sunday School make up this 70 voice Glee Club which meets throughout
the year and performs at many community celebrations and Center festivities.
