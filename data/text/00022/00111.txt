Another successful year was ended for the Cub
Scouts with a Swim Party and Cook Out during June.
The 46 member Pack enjoyed a banner year with out¬
standing monthly Pack Meetings and an active Com¬
mittee under the capable chairmanship of Milton
Greenspan.
Several Den Mothers and Cuhmaster Sidney Engel¬
hardt have completed a training course given by the
Scout Office to enrich our leaders with the leadership
qualities needed for Cubbing.
The Pack meets on the last Thursday of each
month at the Center throughout the whole year and
each Friday during the school year at the respective Den
Mothers5 homes. Our sincere thanks to the Pack Com¬
mittee and the Den Mothers for a yeoman’s job well
done.
