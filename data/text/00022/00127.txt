Now in its third year of operation, this young social
club for married couples with either spouse being under the
age of 32, completed another successful year. The monthly
affairs under the advisorship of the Vice-President and a
different member of the Board are in charge along with a
committee composed of the membership at large. Each
member and his wife is asked to work on one affair per
year. The highlight of our year’s activities is the extravagant
New Year’s Eve Party held at the Robert Meyer Hotel with
two bands and an outstanding entertainment bill.
Our organization is open to all married couples and any¬
one interested is asked to contact our membership chairmen,
Mr. and Mrs. Sy Bellack, 725-8513, or Mr. and Mrs.
Lawrence Dubow, FL 9-9054.
