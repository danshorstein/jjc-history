B’NAI B’RITH’s mission is to unite persons of the Jewish faith in the work of
promoting their highest interests and those of humanity. This we do through
service, study and benevolence.
Our programs include A.D.L., Hillel, BBYO, Vocational Guidance, Adult
Jewish Education, Citizenship, Veterans and Community Affairs projects, Scholar¬
ship Fund and support of national B’NAI B’RITH philanthropies.

B’nai B’rith Women are dedicated to serving you, your
family, your country and humanity. This is carried through
by supporting different national B’nai B’rith philanthropies
such as Belief aire Children’s Center; Leo N. Levi Memorial
Hospital; National Jewish Hospital and B’nai B’rith Center
for Jewish patients at Mayo Clinic. B’nai B’rith Women
also sponsors the Children’s Home in Israel and other Israeli
projects. Locally, our activities include: the Anti-Defamation
League (“Dolls for Democracy” program, distribution of
Board of B’nai B’rith Women
memo calendar books to the public schools and keeping
members aware of the problems in the Jewish Community);
teaching home economics at Pinecastle School; helping at
River Garden Home; sponsoring the B.B. Girls and their
Vocational Guidance Service; Hillel at U. of Fla.; and
helping in all community drives. In the coming year we
hope to make the name B’NAI B’RITH synonymous with
our motto: Service, Fidelity and Devotion.
