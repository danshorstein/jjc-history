The National Council of Jewish Women is an organi¬
zation dedicated to furthering human welfare in the Jewish
and general communities, locally, nationally and interna¬
tionally.
NCJW Officers
The Evening Group
Some of our community projects are: Audiometer, test¬
ing the hearing of all first grade children in Duval County;
Juvenile Shelter, a planned year-round recreational pro¬
gram and furnishing books for their library; Medical Loan
Shelf, supply hospital and sickroom equipment to indigents
through VNA; Mental Health Fair, co-sponsored with the
Mental Health Association of Duval County; Sewing Com¬
mittee, makes layettes and baby clothes for the needy of the
community; Tribute Fund, purchase of hearing aids for
indigent children.
