The Foundation School curriculum was vastly enriched
this year in both secular and religious departments. Regular bi¬
weekly trips were made to the Public Library where the
children were allowed to select books for home reading and
monthly field trips were made to various places of interest
in and around the city.
As part of their regular Hebrew studies, the children en¬
joyed a Chanukah party planned by a mothers’ committee of
the PTA. To celebrate Purim a delightful program was pre¬
sented by the students for their parents and friends after
which a delicious luncheon was enjoyed by all. At Pesah
time a model Seder was held at River Garden Home to the
delight of the residents who attended.
