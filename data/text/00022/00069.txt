Religious Education
A summary of the school’s progress should contain two essentials: a high goal
showing that the school is never static, and progressive innovations to show how this
goal is being achieved.
The goal of the Jacksonville Jewish Center school is to have the highest stand¬
ard in the country. The methods pursued during the past year include the following:
Continuous education from 3 to 18 years of age.
Advanced departments.
A Foundation School of First and Second grades
Sunday, daily and Saturday classes of Hebrew.
Maximum use of available time.
Separated congregational levels on Saturday and Sunday.
Special tutoring during vacation time.
Enrichment programs as assemblies and library research.
Pupils who can take over all pulpit skills.
Introduction of experimental programs as team teaching and latest texts.
