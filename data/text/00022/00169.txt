Ladies Hebrew Sheltering Aid Society was founded 40
years ago as an aid society to help the needy of the Jewish
faith. It was always the dream and hope of our founders to
have a Home for the Aged in Jacksonville. This dream be¬
came a reality when River Garden Hebrew Home for the
Aged was founded. Since that time, our role became that of
the auxiliary to the Home.
We provide extra-budgetary needs such as furnishings,
redecorating, air conditioning, kitchen and infirmary equip¬
ment. Funds for these needs were raised through Tombola,
Donor and Social Printing projects as well as annual dues
and life memberships in Ladies Aid. Linens were provided by
our annual Linen Shower.
We sponsor a Grey Ladies Program, regularly scheduled
entertainment and parties. River Garden residents participate
in projects served by the auxiliary such as the residents’

magazine, Ceramic classes and the Hobby Shop.
The Golden Sunshine Club of River Garden which
emerged from our weekly Senior Citizens meetings has
grown into a self-sufficient organization giving fellowship
and companionship as well as participating in civic and
philanthropic activities.
This year Ladies Aid pledges to continue its efforts and
cooperation in bringing not only the material but the
spiritual values that our elder citizens hold so dear.
