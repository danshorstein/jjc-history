The essential function of the Zionist Organization of
America is to keep alive in this country an interest in the
State of Israel. This interest is many-sided, as the efforts of
Zionism always were. The political objective was the creation
of a Jewish State. To this end it was necessary to revive the
Hebrew language, acquire land, train colonists for settle¬
ment and raise funds to finance the project. All this the
Zionist movement did and our district organization received

Today, the Z.O.A. continues to foster support for the
State of Israel. Moreover, the Z.O.A. seeks to strengthen
the bonds between American Jewry and the Jewish State on
the basis of the cultural tradition that is our common
heritage.
Religious differences present no obstacle as Zionism en¬
compasses all shades of religious belief in every global area.
The Z.O.A. invites all Jewish men to join its ranks.
