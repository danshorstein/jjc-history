The Jewish Family and Children’s Service is now located
in beautiful new quarters at 1415 LaSalle St. where it will
be better able to serve the Jewish community with warmth,
dignity and convenience. This new and spacious building
is off Hendricks Ave., directly west of the Southside Library,
phone 359-2941.
JFCS has a program which can be listed under seven
major categories:
1.Family and individual casework: marital, emotional,
economic, health and aging problems.
Mr. Jack Coleman
2. Child welfare, to maintain children in their own
homes with their own family, or to license, maintain and
supervise them in foster homes or in adoptive homes.
3. Counselling and maintaining unmarried mothers and
placing their children for adoption.
4. Processing applications and providing case work for
the residents of River Garden Home for the Aged.
5. Resettlement of New Americans with funds provided
by the Jewish Community Council.
6. Financial assistance to local residents and transients.
7. A counselling service to Jewish prisoners at Raiford.
Anticipated new service will be the training and coordi¬
nation of volunteers in a friendly visiting program to aged
in their own homes.
