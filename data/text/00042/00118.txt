B'nai B'rith
Youth Organization
The B'nai B'rith Youth Organization (BBYO) is
the largest high school Jewish youth organization
in the country. It is successful because it reaches
put to every and any Jewish youth, no matter
what sect of Judaism he follows.
The core of the program, Leadership Training,
provides knowledge and skills in a congenial social
atmosphere. Inherent in the organization is a
demand for commitment and involvement; the
fiber that holds Jewish youth together no matter
how small or large the community.
If you are interested and would like to get
involved in Jacksonville's AZA/BBG chapters just
call Robin Selber 733-6485 or David Burman 268-
0757.
Jewish Men's Bowling
The Jewish Men's Bowling League is the second
oldest league in Jacksonville.
Jewish men of all ages are invited to join for the
Fall '83 season. Bowling takes place at the Plaza
Lanes in Arlington Expressway on Monday
evenings 6:45-9:00.
For more information call Elliot Starr 724-2044.
