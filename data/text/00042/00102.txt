Kadima Convention-^
Three hundred Kadimaniks from 20 different chapters of the Southeast Region assembled for the largest Kadima
Convention ever. The theme for the weekend was “The New Anti-Semitism”. The Center’s Kadima Chapter conducted the
Shabbat Services and was responsible for many aspects of the weekend’s activites.
Steve Simenowitz, a Jewish blues singer, entertained Saturday evening. A carnival “topped off” the fabulous convention.
