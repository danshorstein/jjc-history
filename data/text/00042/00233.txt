Consecration Classes
The first grade children and new students of the Solomon Schechter Day School along with the Alef Class of the
Afternoon Hebrew School were consecrated in a ceremony at the Jacksonville Jewish Center on Saturday morning, April
30, 1983. The ceremony marks the beginning of the children’s formal Hebrew and Judaic education and is a very meaningful
event. The children receive certificates, miniature Torahs and the traditional jar of honey. The teacher for both classes was
Zipora Zucker.
