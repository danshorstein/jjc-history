President's Message
As we approach another year, our thoughts review the many
activities of our Synagogue Center. The religious and Holiday services
continue to be meaningful and inspiring; the edcuational programs
have been interesting and innovative; the award winning Youth
Program continues to attract youth of all ages; and our recreation
program is unique and diversified.
Our Synagogue doors are always open.
Great emphasis has been placed on the Solomon Schechter Day
School and our Afternoon Hebrew School, for it is with our youth that
the future of Judaism rests. Our Day Care Program allows children of
working parents to spend a full day in a totally Jewish environment,
under expert supervision. With your support, we can insure that the
Jewish children of this community will always have a home away from
home.
Our school doors are always open.
As the “People of the Book”, we cannot allow education to be secondary. Our leadership has the knowledge, desire and ability
to make our educational program as outstanding as any private or public educational system in the country. To achieve this, we
must have your financial assistance - through donations to our various funds, trusts, testamentary bequests, donations of real
estate, and stocks and bonds.
The Tzedakah Box is always open.
The Baal Shem Tov said, “Mind is the foundation of Man. If the foundation is solid, the building is secure.” We must continue -
to do so, we ask you OPEN YOUR HEARTS and YOUR POCKETS.
Sol Proctor, President
