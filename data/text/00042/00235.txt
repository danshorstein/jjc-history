Sunday School
The Sunday School of the Jacksonville Jewish Center is planned expressly for children in Kindergarten, First and Second
grades. This two hour program provides each child the opportunity to experience the richness of their Jewish heritage
through song, dance, role playing, cooking, short stories, games, arts and crafts. Emphasis is placed on the holidays and
festivals.
This past year the program coordinator was Tricia Bloom. This year, Mrs. Peggy Organes will co-ordinate the program and
will be assisted by Betsy Gay, Alina Kentof and Robin Rand.
