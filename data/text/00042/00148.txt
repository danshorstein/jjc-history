The original Amity Club was organized in December, 1951, and for many years was a great opportunity for the young
Jewish couples in our community to become acquainted and to enjoy a sociable evening together once a month. It continued
for many years before disbanding.
Feeling the need for an Amity Club again, a group of couples headed by Sylvia and Izzy Weiss sent fliers out and an open
organizational meeting was held on February 17, 1983. At this meeting, it was agreed to call ourselves Amity II, to have three
events a year including New Year’s Eve, and that membership be limited to 135 couples.
