WELCOME ABOARD THE
J.J.C. PRINCESS
Saturday, May 28,1983
Every one of the lucky people who attended this year's raffle enjoyed the vicarious experience of sailing on a cruise ship.
The food, fun and entertainment and special effects all added to the excitement of the evening.
Captains of our ship, Linda DuBow and Alan Gordon did a fabulous job of co-ordinating the ticket sales. The special effects
were created by chairman Isabel Balotin and Marilyn Garber with assistance from Shirley Bielski, Sandi Burman, Barbara
Safer, Randee Steinberg and Tim Riley. As usual the delicious food was catered by the Center Catering Service under the
direction of Blanche Slott.
