The Jacksonville Jewish Community Council was founded
in 1935 and became the Jacksonville Jewish Federation in
1979. We had many “homes” over the years but on June 5,
1983 a dream came true -- we reached a milestone in our
history when we dedicated our new home at 10829 Old St.
Augustine Road. Our Dedication Plaque which adorns our
lobby reads as follows:
JACKSONVILLE JEWISH
FEDERATON
1983 5743
This building. is dedicated to those in
this community who in the past,
present and future, with their acts of
kindness, love and giving, will not let
the Jewish people perish from this
earth.
We are looking forward to increasing our services to the
community. Our meeting rooms are available to the Jewish
organizations in our community and we invited everyone to
come visit.
We will continue to conduct the annual Combined Jewish
Appeal which raises monies for the United Jewish Appeal as
well as other national agencies. Our local beneficiaries are
River Garden, Jewish Family and Children Services and the
Community Kosher Nutrition Program.
We wish to announce to our community that on
September 1, 1983 Judah Segal will assume the
responsibilities of Executive Director of the Federation. We
know that under his direction we will continue to grow and
expand with the needs.
