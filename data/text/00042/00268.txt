Tradition with Innovation
The Solomon Schechter Day School has continued to provide excellence in both General Studies and
Judaic Studies for the past 23 years. While we continue to offer traditional courses of study which are of
proven value, we also continue to grow by including new techniques and innovations which will enrich our
students experience. Computers are significant as an accepted part of our teaching and learning modes.
Junior Congregation, Torah reading and Trop are an integral part of our curriculum. Our students
enrich their lives by attending services and learning how to lead the service.
A course in sex education is taught by professionals. Enrichment programs are offered throughout the
year in the Pre-School/Kindergarten classes as well as upper grades.
The Jacksonville Jewish Center has provided an excellent Extended Day Care Program for the last two
years. Due to the success of this program, we have extended it. Day Care is available from 7:30 a.m. until
5:30 p.m. This is a Total Learning Program and an asset to the Center as well as the entire Jewish
community.
