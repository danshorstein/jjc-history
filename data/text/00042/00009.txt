Hebrew High School
The Hebrew High School of the Jacksonville Jewish Center is the creation of the synagogue to offer Jewish youth an
opportunity to explore the broad expanse of resources available through our heritage. This program seeks to provide exciting,
innovative exploration of Judaism upon which each student might build. Students 8-11 grade are involved.
COURSE OF STUDY: Hebrew Language, Famous Jewish People, Jewish Philosophy, Holocaust, Comparative Religion,
Jewish History, Confirmation.
