The Jacksonville District of the Zionist Organization of
America has had a successful year. We have for over thirty
years, held our annual Jewish National Fund function
planting a grove of 1000 trees in Israel. This year's grove was
planted in memory of Sam Galinsky and in honor of Esther
Galinsky. In addition we co-sponsored a program with Beth
Shalom Congregation, Father Paul Ischi, “Lebanon One Year
Later.”
ZOA is our national organization which has been very
effective and important in our government for mobilizing
support of Israel.
