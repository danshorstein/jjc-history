PASSOVER FOR ALL PROJECT
“Let all who are hungry come to eat” is recited during the Seder when the Matzah is uncovered. To insure that all will be
able to partake of Passover, the young people of each school at the Jewish Center undertook a most meaningful project. Each
student was requested to bring one dollar to school. These monies were used to purchase food stuffs for gift boxes to be given
to those families who, at this time, were unable to provide the foods of the holiday for themselves. Youngsters of our school
also called the congregation to contribute to this charity.
Members from each class were selected to purchase the food and the older students helped deliver.
