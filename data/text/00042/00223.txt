CALENDAR 217
Message From The Editor
As we marched on San Jose Blvd. on Yom Torah - You were there - carrying a Torah, a
banner, “a new heart and a new spirit”, to our new home. We stood in the bare Sanctuary
and we cheered, sang, danced, cried, - Together.
We were there, in our Synagogue Center, sharing simchas and sorrows - Together.
And side by side, we volunteered for fund raisers and socials, always working, and caring,
and striving to make the Jacksonville Jewish Center the “BEST”.
As you can see, the key word is “Together”, for there lies our strength and success. Our
Synagogue is too large for one person or small group to handle our varied programs. All of
us must work together, and strive for a stronger, more cohesive Synagogue Center.
I am grateful for the opportunity of working and volunteering at the Jacksonville Jewish
Center. I look forward to seeing many of you this coming year. My husband Allen, my
children and I wish you a New Year of happiness and peace.
