Neal F. Portnoy Youth Lounge
The Neal F. Portnoy Youth Lounge was made possible
through the generosity of the Louis Portnoy family. This well-
equipped and well designed facility is used to serve the needs
of the youth of our congregation and the Jewish youth of the
entire community. It is the central location for formal
meetings, programs and activities, it also serves as a
synagogue for youth services.
Equipped with all sorts of games, such as pinball machines,
foozball, bumper pool, electronic and board games, the youth
lounge has something for everyone.
