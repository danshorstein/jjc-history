Florence Robin Retires
After 27 years of dedicated service to the Jacksonville Jewish Center, Florence Robin has
retired as Executive Secretary.
In her career, Florence has worked with Rabbis, Executive Directors and Center presidents,
adapting to the mannerisms and style of each without missing a step. Her work was always 100%
perfect.
Her pleasant manner, willingness to help and her excellent secretarial skills were an asset to the
Center. As a token of the congregation's appreciation, the Board of Directors honored Florence
at a Board Meeting on Wednesday evening, May 11. We wish Florence health and happiness.
