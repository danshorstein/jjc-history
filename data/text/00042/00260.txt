Center Choral Society Concert
“Serve the Lord with gladness, come before His presence with singing.”
(Psalms 100:2)
The Annual Center Choral Society Concert was a
beautiful musical experience. Cantor Jeffrey Nadel
returned as the guest vocal artist and thrilled the
audience with his enchanting repertoire of classical &
Hebrew selections.
The soloists were delightful as well: Raymond
Cohen, Roz Klein, Paul Goldstein, Mary Haimowitz,
Debra Massave, Peggy Organes, Beth Shorstein. Judy
Geller served as assistant conductor and William
Saunders was the piano accompanist.
The Center takes great pride in its “home grown”
talents and the talents of those who recently joined the
Center family.
Our sincere thanks to Cantor Emeritus Abraham
Marton who co-ordinated this delightful event.
Cantor Jeffrey Nadel thrills the audience with his enchanting
voice.

U-Thful Seniors (U.S.)
“Enthusiasm is the electricity of life”
A new and vital outreach program for mature adults
of the Center was initiated this year. Social, recreational
as well as religious and cultural programs were
scheduled and planned to suit the needs of the mature
adult community.
“Bob and Annette”, a musical duo, “kicked off” the
year with their unusual entertainment. A crowd of over
100 attended. Another favorite was a book review by
the humorous and eloquent Mary Wolf.
Picnics, special holiday celebrations and social
gatherings have been planned for the coming year. Be
there.
