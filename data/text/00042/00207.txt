Purim Play
All the majesty of the Purim story unfolded dramatically in the richly produced, superbly presented play, “Esther and the
King of Shushan.”
The production was an artistic blend of the fabulous music of the “King and I” and an original script collaborated by Isabel
Balotin, Shirley Bielski, and Marilyn Garber. The music lighting, sound system, and direction set a new standard of
professionalism in the Center’s reputation of high quality entertainment.
We are extremely proud of our very talented and versatile members who donated their time to make this a very outstanding
production.
