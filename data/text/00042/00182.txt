Where everyday is an adventure
Camping can be one of the most meaningful experi¬
ences a child will ever have. Our Camp offers not only a
situation in which children can learn and develop by
interacting with other children, but it offers a full scope
of activities designed to make a child aware of our
Jewish heritage, to develop new friendships, enjoy the
wonders of nature that may be found on our large
wooded campsite and the importance of active sports.
This year specialty camps in Art, Sports and
Computers highlighted the summer camp program.
