Hanukkah Celebrations
The Drama Group of the Solomon Schechter Day School presented a Hanukkah play “A Marionette’s Mutiny,” directed by
Rose Lynn Plotkin and assisted by Marilyn Garber and Shirley Bielski.
Children of the Afternoon School presented a delightful Hanukkah play and enjoyed making Latkes. Special thanks to their
teacher, Alina Kentof.
