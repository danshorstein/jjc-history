Summer Programs
There are many programs available for those students
who wish a Jewish living experience for the summer
months:
Camp Ramah provides the opportunity of a total
Jewish living experience for children and teenagers in the
relaxed atmosphere of a summer camp. Values built on a
shared experience and emotion reach our sons and
daughters in a manner impossible in a formal school
program. Over the years, RAMAH has helped instill a
deeper commitment to Judaism in many of its
participants.
The Center believes that benefits accrue to the congregation, not just to the individual. We have
therefore made monies available to reduce the financial cost of qualified students regardless of need.
These monies are not part of the regular Center budget, but rather are raised by donations to the Myra
Thier Lazarus Ramah Fund, and Sisterhood, and from other sources.
Two families have helped us greatly. The Gordon Family has established a scholarship fund in memory
of the beloved Robert J. Gordon. A long list of deserving boys and girls have been able to go-to Ramah
because of this scholarship. Abe Selber established in his will a perpetual trust for specific Jewish
education purposes. With the full support of his children, Iris and Franklin, who have never lost interest
in the Center, the proceeds of the Abe Selber Trust have been used to send campers to Ramah.
