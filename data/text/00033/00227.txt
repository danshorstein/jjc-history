The comprehensive program offered by the Jacksonville Jewish Center
depends on the proper maintenance of our facilities. The efforts of Tim

for many years, enable the various activities to take place in presentable
surroundings. Annie Mae Fort also maintains the Kitchen and prepares
many meals.
The local caterers, Mr. and Mrs. Albert Meyerhoff, Mrs. Mary Safer,
Mrs. Stanley Ruby and Mrs. Pat Mandell prepare many Simchas for our
members and their families.
