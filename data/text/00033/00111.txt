Fellow C.P.C/ers:
During the past year it is very obvious that we
tried to achieve the goals of U.S.Y. in all areas, re-
ligious, social and service activities. To be successful
a club must build strength by attendance at meetings
which is most important to the program ruach and
morale of the membership and officers.
Our membership must continue to improve all
areas of United Synagogue Youth, to make a strong
commitment to their Jewish heritage and identity.
Jacksonville USY alone collected over $3,000 in
Walkathon money this past year. This large amount
could not have been reached unless the members had
not strived toward some certain goal. Deep inside you
know we can be the best and all that it takes is your
help.
