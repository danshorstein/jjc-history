June at the Center Campsite with Brownie Troop 147

Junior Girl Scout Troop 162 is made up of girls
in the 4th, 5th and 6th grades. The Scouts began the
year by participating in the Veteran’s Day Parade
and sold Girl Scout calendars. They held a meeting
in Rabbi and Mrs. Gaffney’s Sukkah and helped de-
corate it.
In December the girls went on an overnight
campout to Camp Chowenwah. They made yam dolls
for the children at the Juvenile Shelter. They also
toured the Naval Air Station. During March they
participated in Girl Scout Sabbath and sponsored the
