The Officers and Operating Committee Chairmen
of this Lodge send Greetings and Sincere Wishes for
Good Health, Peace and Contentment to the entire
Community.
B’nai B’rith, through its many programs, has been
serving Jewry and Humanity since October 13, 1843.
A.D.L. has been our watchman and our voice,
exposing the fungus of Anti-Semitism on the surface
of our beautiful land; also speaking out against in-
justice wherever it rears its head, affecting Jews and
Non-Jews alike.
Through Hillel, A.Z.A. and B’nai B’rith Girls,
this Organization provides avenues for Judaic social
and religious experiences for our youth.
One of the purposes of B’nai B’rith is to provide
a service club type of fraternal organization for Jewish
men.
On the level of service to the total community
this Lodge provides recognition trophies, distributed
to teachers, selected by their peers, for understanding
and empathy with their students. This understanding
and good relations with students makes for a more
productive teaching-learning situation. Jack Coleman
guided this project.
Another project is joining other Service Clubs on
December 24th, in manning the Salvation Army
Christmas Collection Kettles. Jack Coleman was also
the man at the helm on this project.
