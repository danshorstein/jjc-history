Mount Carmel Gardens apartments are designed,
even in the smallest detail, to provide comfortable and
pleasant surroundings. From the initial concept, Mount
Carmel Gardens has strived to create a living com-

Mount Carmel Gardens offers a 17-story building on
a beautifully landscaped site, 24-hour staffing, com-
munity facilities, and planned programs, all of which

concept responding to the desires of retirees for se-

with others.
