The local JWV post participates in Memorial Day
Services, including decorating the graves of our de-
ceased brothers, in Veterans Day observances, and is
a member of the Jewish Community Council with
active participation and support of all of its programs
for Israel and the Community.
For information concerning joining this dedicated
organization, call any of the Post officers.
The Jewish War Veterans is the oldest active
veterans organization in the United States. As such,
it has become an expressive and influential voice in
the American Veterans community. It is dedicated to
the principles of Americanism and Jewishness, and
has become a major factor in the halls of Congress
where its impact on behalf of the Veteran and Jewry
are well heeded. Its major programs include services
to active duty personnel, hospitalized veterans, and
families of servicemen.
