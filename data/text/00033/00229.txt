The Fleet of Buses and Drivers
The Transportation Committee of the Center is very active in develop-
ing a system of routes as well as providing the necessary vehicles.
We have three 66 passenger buses as well as a 12 passenger mini-bus,
Our staff of drivers including Carolyn Dreher, Willie Hay, George
Starks and Spencer Everett, each with an enviable record of safety and
responsibility, transport our children from their homes and schools to
classes, activities and services at the Center.
Our transportation system, under the capable direction of Kenneth
Bowden, is efficient and constantly adapts to our ever expanding needs.
