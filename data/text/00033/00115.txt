Troop 14 has enjoyed many overnight campouts
at such places as Gold Head State Park and Camp
Frances Johnson as well as its participation in various
civic programs in the community which were carried
out throughout the city during the year.
Troop 14 is all set for the coming 1974-75 year
with a full Scouting program for any boy 11 years old
and up who would like to join.
The Center Boy Scout Troop No. 14 underthe.
guidance of Scoutmasters David Weintraub, Scott
Zimmerman and Gene Crystal just completed a very
successful year marking its 55th birthday at the An-
nual Blue and Gold Banquet in February.
Troop 14 is one of the oldest troops in the North
Florida Council of Boy Scouts. It offers an excellent
program for all boys 11 years of age and up. The
Scouting program of today is designed to help make
the young boys of today leaders for tomorrow.
