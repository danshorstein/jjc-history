held at Wolfson High School. Featur-

School orchestra and the Glee Club
from the Solomon Schechter Day
School, the Concert was one of the
finest performances in the history of
the Choral Society. A repeat Concert
was also performed at Jacksonville
University with Cantor Marton as
solist.
