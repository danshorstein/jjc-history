During the year, Cubs participated in the Good-
will collection program, Pinewood Derby, Rocket
Derby, the annual Blue and Gold Banquet, Boy Scout
Sabbath, Expo ’74, Cub Olympia. They also visited
a battleship at Mayport. Recruitment of new members
starts in September and we look forward to a reward-
ing year.
The Cub Scouts, as an integral part of the na-
tional organization of Boy Scouts of America, continues
to be one of the outstanding opportunities offered to
boys between the ages of 8 and 10 years to become
part of a growing organization that strives to teach
them how to become better citizens, to explore the
out-of-doors and participate in new experiences.
