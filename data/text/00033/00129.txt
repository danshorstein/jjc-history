The Center Basketball Clinic and League enjoyed another
successful year. Championship games were held on Sunday,
March 24.
Certificates and trophies were presented to the participants
in the Center’s Basketball Clinic and League at ceremonies
held at the Campsite June 2nd. A surprise highlight of the
program was the appearance of Mr. Bob Gottlieb, Coach of
the J.U. Dolphins.
Most valuable player trophies: First and Second grade -
Bradley Rosenthal, Seth Movsovitz, Alan Biller, Scott Sel-
manoff; 3rd, 4th and 5th - Blair Selber, Paul Baker, Mark
Miller, Lance Cherry; 6th, 7th, 8th and 9th grades - Scott
Greenfield, Reid Baker, Hal Schemer, Mark Usdin. Cham-
pionship Trophies: 1st and 2nd division - Michael Levin,
Andrew Ruby, Robin Selber, Seth Movsovitz; 3rd, 4th and
5th - Charles Krestul, Lane Strumlauf, Michael Freedman,
Lance Cherry, Robert Gordon, Yale Balotin, Marla Rosin;
6th, 7th, 8th and 9th Grades - Roland Mizrahi, Kevin Sack,
Mark Usdin, Stephen Greenfield, Barry Newman, Gary Gold-
stein, Dale Fletcher, Cheerleaders were also given certificates
of achievement. Bennie Margol, Samuel R. Shorstein and
Ronald Pollock are Co-Chairmen of the Athletic Committee.
