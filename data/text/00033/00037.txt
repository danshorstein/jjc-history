CONGREGATION SIGNS BUILDING CONTRACT
Board Approves Construction Contract
NEW JEWISH CENTER
TO BE READY BY SEPTEMBER 1975
The Board of Directors, at a special meeting held on Tuesday,
June 25, voted their approval of a contract with Daniel Construction
Company of Florida. Three contractors Auchter, Gay and Daniel had
submitted bids with Daniel Construction presenting the lowest bid. On
site construction will begin within thirty days with completion expected
by September 1975. Site development and road work have already
been completed.
Wilbur Margol, Co-Chairman of the Building Development Fund
reviewed the challenges of the upcoming Building Fund Campaign.
“The Jewish Center after seventy years of proven progress and success
is entering a new era of service and activity for our members and all
of us share the hope and pride of a glorious future. The excitement and
enthusiasm and, best of all, the realization of the new Jewish Center on
San Jose Blvd. within a year will give us the incentive to do our best,
to spare no effort to make the campaign a major success.״

On Thursday, July 18th our President, Charles J. Mizrahi, and
Secretary, Leo Jacobson, signed our formal building contract and
construction loan agreement for the new Sanctuary-School complex on
our San Jose Blvd. property.
These milestone agreements were made with Daniel Construction
Company for the new building and with the Atlantic National Bank
of Jacksonville for the financing of the project.
Completion of the construction is planned for September, 1975.
