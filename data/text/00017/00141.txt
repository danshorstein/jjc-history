The National Womens Committee helps advance and promote the general welfare of Brandéis University in Waltham,
Massachusetts, as well as provide financial assistance.
In seven years 90 chapters have been established with a membership of 55,000. The money secured through member¬
ships and contributions to the Book Funds is used to meet all the needs of the University Library, so there is no fund- raising.
The Women’s Committee notes the development of Brandéis with pride. The women throughout the country have made
incalculable contributions by fostering good will for Brandéis in their respective communities. The university has expanded
its student body, gathered a distinguished faculty and realized its building program.
