RONNIE LEVIN
This past A.Z.A., the junior order of B’nai B nth, had a very successful year, one of the most outstanding in its history.
We published our first annual Yearbook which was distributed at our Sweetheart Dance. A great deal of credit for the success of our
social event is due our capable Sweetheart.
For community service projects we worked at the River Garden Home and are holding a raffle for the benefit of the Cancer Fund.
Our chapter had the largest delegation present at the North Florida Council Convention. Jerry Rothstein and Ronnie Levin were both
elected as officers. Jerry Rothstein was also elected reported at the Florida Regional Convention. Several members participated in the
District Convention at the University of North Carolina.
Gator Chapter 272 is looking forward to another successful year under its new administration and under the guidance of the
Advisor, Sam Silver.

EDNA STEINFELD
The B’nai B’rith Girls, the junior organization of the B’nai B’rith Women, carried out their seven-fold program this past year.
Among the community service projects was helping clothe girls at the Juvenile Shelter.
The highlight of the year’s social activities was a Beach party. We also had an enjoyable slumber party at which we wrote songs
for the forthcoming Distria Convention.
Our summer program consists of bowling, tennis, swimming, miniature golf and many other activities.
