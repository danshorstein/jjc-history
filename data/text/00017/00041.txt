The Mr. and Mrs. Club is a social club affiliated with the Jacksonville Jewish Center. Its membership is comprised of many old
Jacksonville residents and many newcomers to the city, and welcomes new members to join its ranks.
Interested parties are requested to contact any of the officers or members for details.
The Mr. and Mrs. Club has sponsored many enjoyable events since its inception five years ago. The Club has been instrumental in
forming numerous lasting friendships, and has provided sociability and entertainment for all who belong.
The outstanding events of the year were the Installation affair, the Invitational affair which is one affair where the membership
is permitted to invite local guests, and the New Year’s party. These affairs were very successful and were included in the regular fees.
Each month we have a small get-together and a large affair with a dance and dinner handled by capable committees from the member¬
ship.
Due to the cooperation on the part of the Officers and Board of Directors, as well as the various committees, the Club has been
established as one of the city’s leading social organizations.
Husbands and wives serve together on the various committees and Board to plan the social events.
