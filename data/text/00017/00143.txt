The Jacksonville Zionist District witnessed in the past year one of the most fruitful
years of public service and education to our community and the State of Israel. Several pub¬
lic meetings were dedicated to issues affecting Jewish life in America in relationship to the
people of Israel.
The annual Jewish National Fund Banquet was the most successful in recent years.
Guest speaker for the event was the former Mayor of Miami Beach, the Honorable Harold
Shapiro.
One of the best attended meetings of the year was the showing of the film "Hill 24
Doesn’t Answer” at the Prudential Auditorium. Cantor Marton was invited to appear before
several organizations to discuss Zionism, Jewish music and other Jewish topics of the day.
The American Jewish community is becoming more and more aware of the role that
organized Zionism has played in the last 50 years towards the creation of the State of Is¬
rael and are assuming more responsibility for the security and economic stability of the State.
Cantor Marton on behalf of Z.O.A. wishes to take this opportunity to extend to all a
Happy New Year. "Le Shanah Tovah Tikatevu.”
Cantor A. Marton
