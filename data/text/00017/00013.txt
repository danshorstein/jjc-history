MR. DAVID I. WEINSTEIN
As Administrator of an ever expanding Synagogue-Center which is serving more individuals and organizations than ever
before, Mr. Weinstein’s duties are manifold.
He executes policies adopted by the Officers and Board of Directors. He helps plan and implement the work and de¬
cision of many committees.
Specifically, he assists the Finance Committee in constant supervision of Center income and expenditure; the House Com¬
mittee by supervising the maintenance staff which includes Tim Riley, Morris Montford, Eugene Davis, Wallace Mazon,
Annie Mae Fort and Mary Williams, in the daily upkeep of the premises; the Membership Committee by preparing infor¬
mation for enrollment of new members.
His responsibilities include the scheduling of the many personal events in the lives of the membership and functions
of Center affiliated and non-affiliated organizations.
Together with the courteous and efficient office personnel, Mrs. Bernard Stegman, Mrs. Nathan Robin and Mrs. Esther
Fishier, our Administrator brings to the membership and the community as a whole the importance of the Jacksonville
Jewish Center.
