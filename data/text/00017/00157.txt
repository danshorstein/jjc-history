The Jewish Community Alliance is affiliated with the National Jewish Welfare Board and is akin to the YMHAs and YWHAs and
Jewish Community Centers.
In addition to serving some 600 persons who are members, it has been a community gathering place for the following organiza¬
tions which have used its facilities this year: Guys and Dolls, Temple Teens, K.T.K. Fraternity of J.U., Khem Temple Patrol, the Cen¬
ter Girl Scouts, DCT Club of Englewood High, Landon Jr. High School Chorus, Daughters of the Nile and many others.
The J. C. A. is located at 8301 Fort Caroline Road on the St. Johns River.
