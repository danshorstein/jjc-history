The Center Boy Scout Troop 14 has had a very active program this past year.
The troop has gained quite a few members, but there is still room for more. Every
boy between the ages of 11 and 14 should avail himself of the program, as Scouting
offers so much to boys.
The year was highlighted by the attainment of the rank of Eagle by Junior
Assistant Scoutmaster, George Glossman, and the Bronze Palm by Byron Kolitz.
Simon Selber was also awarded the New Tamid, the highest religious award in
Scouting.
The year was climaxed by a week at Scout Camp, at which time Joe Borkson
and Nathan Franzblau were inducted into the Order of the Arrow, an honor
society for campers.
The troop is looking forward to bigger and better things in the coming year.
PHILIP SELBER
