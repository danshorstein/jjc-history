The Jewish War Veterans of the United States is a national organization for Jews who have honorably served our coun¬
try in any of its wars. Organized in 1896, it continues to dedicate itself through comradeship and service to the highest
ideals of God and Country.
Post 199 participated in community affairs this past year, including the United Jewish Appeal and Israel Bond drives and
patriotic observances on Memorial Day and Veterans Day.
The Jewish War Veterans of the United States is "the patriotic voice of American Jewry.” A national Shrine to the Jew¬
ish War Dead is maintained at Washington, D. C.
The 63rd National Convention will be held at Los Angeles, Calif, during the week of August 3rd to August 10th, 1958.
