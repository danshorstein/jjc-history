HARRY SHORSTEIN
The Esquire Club had a very successful year under the leadership of Harry Shorstein. We wish to thank the Mascot, Linda
Edwards, and our Advisor, Stanley Kantor, for all their efforts on behalf of the Club.
Several outstanding social events were held including the annual Holiday Dance at the Beauclerc Country Club and the Esquire
Week-end at the Beach.
This past year the Esquire Club contributed to the Cerebral Palsy drive, Hope Haven Hospital and the Jacksonville Jewish
Center where weekly meetings are held.
Esquire Club has just completed its 21st year of existence.
