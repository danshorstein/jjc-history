It is with a great deal of pride that we bring you greetings from the Ladies Hebrew Sheltering Aid Society, the auxiliary to
River Garden.
The Ladies Aid accepted the responsibility of providing the furnishings and equipment which have transformed River Garden
into a beautiful and comfortable home for the aged. But, just as important as our financial aid to the Home is the direct service we
try to give each and every one of the residents.
This year we have added many new projects to our program. The Blood Bank and Gray Ladies were both '’firsts” for the River
Garden. The Hobby Shop under the direction of a trained worker, the Ceramic project, the sewing group, the English and Hebrew
classes, the Home Newspaper, have all provided many happy hours to our many residents.
The financing of our many projects has been made possible through the wonderful cooperation of our fund raising committees:
the Social Printing project, Donors, Tombola, annual dues and a "Life Membership” in Ladies Aid.
The members of Ladies Aid hope that next year’s accomplishments will be even greater. We want to express our appreciation
for your cooperation in the past year.
