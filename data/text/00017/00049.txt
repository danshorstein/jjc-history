Cub Scouts Pack 14
The Cub Pack
C. C. GHELERTER
This was an outstanding year for the Cub Scouts of the Jacksonville Jewish Center, Pack 14. In February, 60 Cub Scouts were
registered at Scout Headquarters.
Pack meetings are held monthly at the Center. Each den prepared an exhibit for their monthly project on the theme of the month.
One of the successful events of the year was a Carnival held in the Park across the street from the Center. The entire Cub Pack
were guests of the Jacksonville Baseball Club. Another activity was the annual Father and Son Banquet.
Dr. Harry Yergin was Cubmaster the first half of the year. Clarence Ghelerter took over the Pack the first of February.
We look forward to another enjoyable year for the boys as well as parents.
