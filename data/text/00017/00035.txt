DAVID R. LEWIS
The main objective of the Jacksonville Jewish Center Men’s Club is to stimulate social and cultural activities through the men
of the Center membership. These activities have been inter-related with certain religious and youth activities of the congregation.
Through the medium of monthly stag dinners we have been enjoying the benefit of timely informal talks from local political,
educational, sports and newsworthy figures.
The Men’s Club continues to support Cantor Marton with his guidance of the Minyan Club which is composed of Bar Mitzvah
age boys who meet on Sunday mornings for a special service and get-together breakfast. The Men’s Club sponsors the Bernard
Glossman Award for Boy Scout Troop 14, other awards to the Cub Scout Pack, and awards for Hebrew School and Sunday School
participation. Our annual Father-Son-Daughter Banquet is now one of our most popular events.
In conjunction with the Center’s continued building growth, the Men’s Club has pledged itself for even greater support for the
youth needs of our community, and for more social activities including the children.
We look forward to another year of advancement through the help and support of the men of our congregation who have shown
their great interest in the undertakings and activities of the Club.
