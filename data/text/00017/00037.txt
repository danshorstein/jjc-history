After six years as a successful social organization, the Couples Club has undergone a reorganization that has broadened
its scope and activities.
The group, open to all young married couples, now emphasizes not only social get-togethers and dances, but also in¬
formative programs and speakers, as well as entertaining special events.
Monthly affairs are planned for the members, and as part of its new format, special affairs and activities will also be
scheduled.
The club offers newcomers an opportunity of becoming a part of the community and provides all its members with last¬
ing friendships and varied experiences.
