The Progressive Credit Union, sponsored by the Jewish Progressive Society, has con¬
tinued to grow in its 16 years of operation. It is one of more than 10,000 Credit Unions
in the United States, having more than 5 million members and more than one billion dol¬
lars in assets.
From a very modest beginning of 20 members and $500.00 in assets, we have grown
to over 350 members and more than $200,000.00 in assets. During our existence we have
made over 1600 loans to members, amounting to over $2,000,000.00 without a single loss.
We are organized to encourage thrift and provide a safe, convenient medium whereby
members can invest and accumulate savings. We are chartered by the State of Florida and
regularly examined by the Comptroller of the State, and the savings of members are safe¬
guarded in several ways.
Life Insurance equal to a memberV savings up to $1,000.00 and Loan Protection In¬
surance is paid by the Progressive Credit Union without any cost to the individual mem¬
ber. The climax of each year’s activities comes with the annual meeting held in the Jack¬
sonville Jewish Center auditorium. Last year the Board declared a 2 % dividend on sav¬
ings and 15% rebate of interest to borrowing members.
