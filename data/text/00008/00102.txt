FRANKLIN DELANO ROOSEVELT
January 30, 1882 - April 12, 1945
HARRY S. TRUMAN
May 8, 1884 -
LIKE LINCOLN, Franklin Delano Roosevelt died
at the hour of triumph. We mourn with all the
freedom -loving people of the world the passing
of a man who, during his long term as President
of these United States, had as his guiding star the
freedom of all mankind. We rejoice in his suc¬
cessor. We feel profoundly grateful that another
great American, Harry S. Truman, is given us by
Providence to carry on the great task. He can not*
will not fail.
