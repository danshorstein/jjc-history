The Pushaloter Ladies' Aid Society was formed
over 30 years ago to a'd worthy causes in Pushalot,
Lithuania. All members of the society either were
born in Pushalot or are married to natives of that
city. Until the Nazi invasion severed contacts with
the Jewish communities abroad, money collected
from the local members was sent to Pushalot at reg¬
ular intervals, for such causes as Passover, rebuild¬
ing of the Synagogue, etc. Today the group aids
indigent Pushaloters in this country and also con¬
tributes to local charities.
