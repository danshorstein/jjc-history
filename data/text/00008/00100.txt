The Brandéis Branch was organized in February,
1944, with a membership of 12, which has now
grown to 55. Its activities for the year began with
initiation of officers on February 1, at which time
a banquet at the Seminole was attended by 200
guests. Louis Segal, national secretary of the JNWA
(Farband) was principal speaker. In May the Far-
band honored Rev. Joseph Robin, on the occasion
of his 70th birthday for his years of activity in be¬
half of the Jewish National Fund. Over $2,000 was
raised for the JNF. The main speaker at the dinner
held in the Jewish Center was Rabbi Lehman of Mi¬
ami Beach. Over 200 guests were in attendance.
