The New Cantor
Rev. Harry Bettman, formerly of Congregation
Beth Shalom, New York City, will be in charge of
all musical activities of the Center, in addition to
his regular duties of conducting services. Cantor
Bettman served for twelve years at Congregation
Emanuel in Providence, Rhode Island, after which
he served for three years with the Roumanian Syna¬
gogue in Chicago, Illinois. He also has engaged in
considerable radio and concert work in addition to
his regular duties.
A native of Roumania, he came here while still a
youth. His early study of music was under the guid-
ane of his father, also a Cantor. He also studied in
Paris, later continuing his training in New York City.
He is a student of the well known Cantor Jacob
Schwartz of the Conservative West End Synagogue,
of which Rabbi Israel Goldstein is the spiritual lead¬
er. Cantor Bettman is married and has one son, Cor¬
poral Louis Bettman, at present stationed in Seattle,
Washington.
