FOREWORD
It is with a great deal of pleasure and pride that we present our forty-
second annual Year Book, the largest and best, we believe, that we have
ever published.
We wish to thank our more than nine hundred advertisers for their
.support and wish to take this opportunity to wish each one of them a
healthy, happy and prosperous New Year. Without their support this beek
would not be possible.
There are numerous special features herein valuable for year-round in¬
formation and that will repay your constant study. The English-Hebrew
Calendar may be referred to on every occasion where a holiday or memorial
date is required. Our complete membership list, will be referred to time and
again. The Honor Roll listing our boys in the Services stands as a recorded
tribute that will be cherished by many. The outline of our complete activi¬
ties, and of our plans and aspirations for the future, deserve careful reading.
The complete statistics on births, Bar Mitzvahs, marriages, deaths, and the
resume of our year’s activities are worthy of especial mention.
May we call our memberships’ attention to the directory of advertisers.
Their addresses and telephone numbers are listed, and we feel certain you
will want to patronize them—mentioning the Year Book when you do so.
Our thanks to all of the organizations that have so graciously co-operated
with our editorial staff. And, in closing, thanks to all who have assisted in
this effort.
CENTER YEAR BOOK STAFF,
HYMAN SELBER, Chairman
