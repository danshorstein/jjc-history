All Bnai Brith Committees have been very active this year. A Bnai
Brith Youth Organization was created, headed by Harry Jaffee, Chair¬
man. Under this Organization are three Youth groups, A.Z.A., B.B.G.,' '
and B.B.Y.W. Two B.B.G. delegates, Marcia Edwards and Bernice
Margol, attended a conference held in Washington on June 24th.
Our War Service Committee is likewise very active under the lead¬
ership of Sidney Brown, past President of Bnai Brith. Like the Youth
Committee, it is composed of an equal number of men and women. Sev¬
eral day rooms have been completed at the near-by camps. Regular
visits to the camps are made in the furtherance of our work. We spon¬
sor dances and other weekly affairs for the men in service. During the
holidays a large committee was in attendance at Camp Blanding and
rendered valuable service. We have placed several organs and game
kits on boats which were launched at local ports.
Under the able direction of Morris Witten, our interest and activi¬
ties in Hillel are always kept alive.
