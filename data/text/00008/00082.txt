The Workmen’s Circle, with general offices in
New York City, is a fraternal order, consisting of
73,000 members, and 760 branches throughout the
United States and Canada. The organization has a
sanitarium in Liberty, New York, and 160 Yiddish
schools throughout the country. It has a special
fund for all members, it pays various benefits and
issues insurance policies to its members from $100
to $3,000. The assets of the organization are $8,-
500,000.
The local branch meets in the Center every first
and third Wednesday in the month. It has a loan
fund for its members. Loans are issued from $25
to $300.
Riverside Memorial Park Association is the
Workmen’s Circle own plot.
