The Business and Professional Women’s Division of Hadassah is
that portion of the Senior Chapter which is unable to attend the regu¬
lar monthly meetings because of business and professional demands.
It holds its own monthly meetings, and closely follows the program es¬
tablished by the Senior Group. During the past year, the membership
of the Business and Professional group was nine per cent of the Senior
Chapter. Figures prove that the B and P Women fulfilled an average
of over nine percent on all quotas.
Besides its Zionist program, the B and P has contributed time and
energy to important local and national activities. Outstanding is its
participation in USO activities in the form of several Saturday night
dances, and in obtaining home hospitality for the service men and
women stationed in this locality.
The membership of the Business and Professional Women are to be
complimented on their participation in the work of Hadassah. In spite
of their lack of leisure time, they realize the importance of Hadassah’s
humanitarian scope, and they devote their time and energy to the es¬
tablishment of a National Home for the Jews.
