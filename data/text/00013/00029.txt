SYLVIA BOLASKY
Junior Hadassah, The Young Women’s Zionist Or¬
ganization of America, provides fully organized social,
educational and cultural activities. From the very be¬
ginning it has been Junior Hadassah’s purpose to partici¬
pate actively and concretely in the building of the Jewish
homeland through specific projects. Meier Shfeyah, the
underprivileged children’s village is a source of pride to
our group. We also assist in the support of the Henrietta
Szold School of Nursing, Youth Aliyah and the Jewish
National Fund.
Not only are Junior Hadassah members inspired by the
tremendous task of building our homeland, but we also
remember our responsibility as American citizens, and as
members of our local community. Junior Hadassah plays
an integral part in the development of the Jewish com¬
munity.
The Jacksonville Chapter of Junior Hadassah has been
recognized by our national organization as one of the
leading chapters in the Southern region. With the help
of a strong membership, we can continue to accomplish
our tasks.
