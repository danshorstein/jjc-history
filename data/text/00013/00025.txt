CANTOR MARTON
The Jacksonville Zionist District takes pride in hav¬
ing been cited on the Honor Scroll” at the 53rd Herzel
Anniversary Convention held in New York on June 23rd,
for outstanding service to the cause of Zionism. Our
two-fold program, service to Israel and to the Commun¬
ity, has shown remarkable progress in the past year. The
District was instrumental in sponsoring Mayor Haydon
Burn’s trip to Israel.
Five public meetings were held with education and
culture as the keynote. The annual J.N.F. program with
Mayor Burns as guest speaker, was most successful, due
to the consecrated service of Mr. and Mrs. Max Rubin.
Two distinguished members, David Moss and Harry Lib¬
by, were honored for loyal service in the membership
campaign, which culminated in an enrollment of over
300 members. Cantor Marton and Jack Becker were
called upon to address various organizations on the sub¬
ject of "Judaism and Zionism.”
With the State of Israel now in its 7th year of exis¬
tence, American Jewry has come to realize that it will
take constant vigilance and loyal support to maintain its
security.
The Jacksonville District will be host this fall to the
Southeastern Zionist Region 18th Convention on Septem¬
ber 3rd through 5th when leaders of several state will
gather.
