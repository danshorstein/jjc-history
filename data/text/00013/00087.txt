ISRAEL LIPSON
The Progressive Credit Union, sponsored by the
Jewish Progressive Society, has continued to grow in its
14 years of operation. It is one of more than 10,000
Credit Unions in the United States, having almost 5 mil¬
lion members and more than one billion dollars in assets.
From a very modest beginning of 20 members and
$500.00 in assets, we have grown to over 350 members,
and more than $200,000.00 in assets. During our exist¬
ence we have made over 1400 loans to members, amount¬
ing to approximately $1,800,000.00 without a single loss.
We are organized to encourage thrift and provide a safe,
covenient medium whereby members can invest and
accumulate savings. We are chartered by the State of
Florida and regularly examined by the Comptroller of
the said State, and the savings of members are safeguard¬
ed in several ways.
The Credit Union being a cooperative, assists its
members when circumstances require and permit such
assistance. The purchasing and business power of the
members is increased by their ability to borrow and re¬
pay loans under convenient arrangements such as are
afforded them by our Credit Union. We sponsor the
Blue Cross Hospitalization Plan under group rates for
those members who desire it. Life Insurance equal to a
member’s savings up to $1,000.00 and Loan Protection
Insurance is paid by the Progressive Credit Union with¬
out any cost to the individual member.
The climax of each year’s activities comes with the
annual meeting and banquet held in the Jacksonville
Jewish Center auditorium. Last year the board declared
a 2% dividend on savings and a 20% rebate of interest
to borrowing members.
