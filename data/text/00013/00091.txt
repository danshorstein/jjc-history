Panorama Of Events In Auditorium
Depicted on this page are scenes from various events
held in the Center Auditorium. The wide range of events
show pictorially the versatility of our auditorium even
more graphically than can be described in words.
The top picture was taken at the Sabbath Service
honoring Mr. Joseph Hackel on his 75th birthday. Harry
Gendzier is shown presenting him with a resolution on
behalf of the congregation in the presence of Mrs.
Hackel, Rabbi Tofield, and Philip Selber, president of
the Center.
The center photograph shows the auditorium used as
a synagogue for the marriage ceremony of Shirley Brown-
steen and Leonard Silver, during the time the Sanctuary
was in the process of being remodeled.
The lower photograph is a bird’s eye view of the
Setzer-Cohen wedding dinner, showing the auditorium as
a lovely and spacious banquet hall.
Below is a scene from the annual Seder sponsored by
the second night of passover.
