ALBERT LEVY
Until pressure of business forced Mr. Sam Silver to
discontinue as volunteer Athletic Director late last spring,
he conducted the department most effectively. The pro¬
gram set forth in last year’s issue of the Year Book was
followed. Sports enthusiasts using the Center facilities
are grateful to A1 Levy for taking over. In the months
that have passed he has demonstrated his ability and
great loyalty.
The coming season will witness a more varied pro¬
gram. Adult and youth groups, men and women, will
be able to join regularly scheduled activities that will be
announced through the Center Bulletin. We shall also
continue the policy of coordinating recreational events
with our department of Religious Education. Youth
groups meeting in the Center will be given a regular
schedule of game time in the gym.
