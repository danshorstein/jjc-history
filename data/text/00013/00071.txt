The Cantor And Choir
On June 13 th Cantor Abraham received an Honorary Degree
of Achievement from the Jewish Theological Seminary of Ameri¬
ca. During the past year the Cantor was called on to appear as guest
soloist at concerts held in Memphis, Tennessee, and Orlando and
Lakeland, Florida.
The Choral Society presents a varied repertoire of liturgical and
Jewish folk music. The work of this group has contributed in large
measure to the meaningful and inspirational development of the
Synagogue music now a part of the Center’s religious services.
From the larger group of the Choral Society are selected the volun¬
teer choir for Friday evening services and augmented choir for the
High Holiday Services. Cantor Marton is Conductor and Sheldon
M. Torn, Assistant Conductor.
The Junior Glee Club was formed this past year by Cantor
Marton and is composed of pupils of the Center’s Religious School.
They have made several public appearances and were very well re¬
ceived.
