The Mr. and Mrs. Club was organized in November
of 1953 by a small number of couples who formed the
nucleus of a group designed to fill the social needs in
our community. Our membership is drawn from the
Jacksonville Jewish Community and has as its purpose
the increase of social fellowship and companionship.
The membership has grown steadily and has increased
from 11 to 100 couples.
