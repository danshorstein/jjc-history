MRS. HYMAN SELBER
The Center Year Book needs no introduction, either to
members of the congregation or to the host of friends that it
has won over the years in the commercial world.
We welcome this opportunity, however, to record a word of
thanks to our members and to our friends.
To Mr. Hyman Selber for rendering a public service and for
his dedicated interest in the project.
To our advertisers, our constant gratitude, not only for
making the publication possible, but also for the support that
they lend to our youth work. We urge our members to recipro¬
cate with their patronage.
To Miss Ethel Joel for taking care of the New Year Greet¬
ings. Please assist her by mailing remittances to her at 2772
Post Street.
To the Sisterhood and its committee headed by Mrs. Hyman
Selber, assisted by Mrs. Ben Setzer, Mrs. Joseph Krestul and
their co-workers listed on this page.
And an especial vote of thanks to Mrs. Joe M. Witten and
Mrs. Isadore Zaslow for a job well done.
To Mr. Raymond Cohen for his inspirational drawing of our
cover designs.
Thanks to all who helped to make this our largest and best
year book. We welcome and seek more volunteers for our book
next year.
