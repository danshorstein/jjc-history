By our participation in the Torah Fund, sponsored by
the National Women’s League, we contribute towards
the Jewish Theological Seminary and its schools for the
training of Rabbis and teachers who are badly needed
throughout the country.
Our Book and Gift Shop is unique in that it not only
provides us with funds, but more important, has acted
as a stimulus to the placement and use of ceremonial and
religious objects in our homes.
It is our belief that there is one field in which Sister
hood could concern itself more actively, and that is in
the religious life of the Center. As an example, Sister¬
hood should stress and encourage more home observance
of our beautiful Sabbath customs and traditions. There
should be a greater effort made by members to encourage
our children to practice the Jewish Way of Life which is
taught in our schools. Sisterhood should set a pattern for
Synagogue attendance, by encouraging their families to
attend services regularly in our beautifully remodeled
Sanctuary.
Sisterhood needs the help and support of each and
every member in order to render the utmost in service
and assistance to our parent organization.
