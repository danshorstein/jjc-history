ROBERT LASRIS
The Couples Club, now in its third year, was organ¬
ized to promote social fellowship and companionship
among the young married couples of Jewish faith in the
community. Because of the great interest shown, the
membership has been expanded from its original 75
couples to 125 couples.
The Club sponsors one social affair and one cultural
affair each month.
We are looking forward to the new season with hopes
of even more varied activities.
There is a place in the Couples Club for every young
Jewish couple interested in opportunities for enjoyable
Jewish association.
