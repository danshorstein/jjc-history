BOYS’ BASKETBALL
BASEBALL
Athletics
The Athletic Committee was pleased to have ob¬
tained the services of Mr. Roy Lewis as Coach and
organizer of Basketball and other active sports for our
youth. Mr. Lewis was well experienced, with a back¬
ground of services in this field from community cen¬
ters in Augusta, Georgia, Columbus, Georgia and San
Antonio, Texas.
Basketball was offered in the Center Gym each
Sunday morning during February and March for boys
in the 7th, 8th and 9th grades. The emphasis of this
program centered around the fundamental skills nec¬
essary for participating in sports as well as building
good relationships among the children themselves.
A professional Baseball Clinic was started at the
Campsite on April 14 and continued through June 9th.
The team was coached by Roy Lewis with the assist¬
ance of A1 Davis, Louis Slott, Sonny Hacker, and Marc
Backer.
Approximately 40 boys ranging in age from six
years through thirteen participated in this program
which taught them the finer points of baseball and
helped to develop players for the Center Day Camp
teams.
Participants in Baseball Clinic: Coach Roy Lewis, Michael Overstreet, Mark Kraemer,
Stephen Weisstein, Jeff Cohen, Michael Shorstein, Jeff Nykerk, Mark Shorstein,
Nathan Slaff, Stephen Hurwitz, David Zavon, Michael Nykerk, Richard Ansbacher,
and Bradley Hacker.
