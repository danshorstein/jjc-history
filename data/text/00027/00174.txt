Serving the entire Jewish community of Jackson¬
ville, the Jewish Community Council is the centra]
Welfare Fund and Community Relations body of the

synagogues, organizations and social service agences,

munal life.
A community-wide annual Welfare Fund Cam¬

The Council publishes a monthly newspaper known
as the Commentator. It is the official organ of the
Council and its member organizations.
The Council conducts a leisure time activities

annual Jewish Welfare Fund Campaign. The Council

children between the ages of 4 and 15.
