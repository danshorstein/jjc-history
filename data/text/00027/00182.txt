“Toward Unity and Creativity in the American
Jewish Community'’ was the theme of the Young
Judaeans this year. Young Judaea, the Zionist youth
Young Judea Dance Group
Singing Group
movement, is dedicated to instilling in our youth an
appreciation of our Jewish heritage, ideals, and values
in the American community and Israel.
This year the Junior members attended a rally
in Savannah, Georgia and the Senior Judaeans were
host to a Tri-City Rally for Savannah, Brunswick and
Way cross, Ga. Other activities of the year included
the annual presentation of Israeli dances and songs
at River Garden, fund-raising, and discussion lecture
groups. Seventeen Judaeans attended Camp Tel Yehu-
dah, the national camp of Young Judaea and fourteen
others to Camp Judaea in Hendersonville, N. C. At the
summer convention held at Camp Judaea, Patsy Fein¬
berg and Scott Lewis were elected to the Regional
Board.
