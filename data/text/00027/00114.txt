The purpose of the Center Day Camp is to help
children develop sound bodies and healthy minds in a
total Jewish atmosphere. During the nine week Camp
period, we strive to help the individual child discover
his own talents and interests and to develop them to
the best of his ability. The Camp is located on State
Road 13 just minutes from the city on a beautiful 36
acre tract complete with Olympic Swimming Pool,
Tennis Courts, Baseball Diamonds, Handball Court,
new and enlarged Children’s Playground and many
other features which make it an ideal environment for
a healthy, happy summer.
A wide range of activities is offered including
swimming, skiing, boating, many exciting fields trips,
ice skating, roller skating, horseback riding, bowling,
arts and crafts, Israeli singing and dancing and an en¬
richment program in reading and Hebrew skills.
