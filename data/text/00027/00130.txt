The Amity Club is a social organization for mar¬
ried couples of the Jewish faith having a limited mem¬
bership. It is a congenial group having many successful
affairs at member’s homes, restaurants and clubs. The
highlight of the season was New Year’s Eve at the
Alhambar Dinner Theatre.
Interested parties are invited to call any of the
above officers for information.

Jacksonville boasts a very active Young Adults
Club, organized this past year by an interested group
of young adults who felt the tremendous need to have
an organization where young single men and women
between the ages of 18 and 35 would have the oppor¬
tunity to meet other single adults for social events
and service to the community when needed.
Many successful affairs were held including par¬
ties, dances and a statewide Convention at the Thun-
derbird Motel.
N.E.T.W.O.R.K. welcomes all young adults in the
community to join, meet new people and renew old
friendships.
