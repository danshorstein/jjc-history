The theme of the coming National Sisterhood
Convention is “Hineni”, “Here Am I”. Many women
will join us in Sisterhood work for this coming year.
Many are new women who are not experienced in
Sisterhood work, who are busy in other phases of the
community, who have families to care for, but they
have told us, “Hineni”. Those wonderful members who
have worked before will guide the newcomers and both
will profit.
Our Gift Shop Committee is excited with plans
to enlarge their facilities and increase their stock to
include gifts for all occasions. Plan now to do your
shopping in person or by phone with our Gift Shop
Chairmen. Plans are under way for completion of an¬
other beautiful Sisterhood tablecloth and for our own
cook book.
Our calendar for the coming year includes a
variety of programming, “programming that lingers in
the mind”. Our Jewish Family Living will be updated
and geared for the modern homemaker to help us
keep our bearings in a fast changing world. Our youth
activities will continue through the year at dizzying
speed, with something going every day to keep our
young people in a positive Jewish atmosphere.
Above all we expect to have fun this year, to work
together, to enjoy our meetings and to enjoy our
relationship with each other. When our Membership
Committee calls . . . when our activity chairmen call
. . . when a fellow Sisterhood members calls on you,
answer “Hineni, Here Am I”.
