The Mr. and Mrs. Club is now in its sixteenth
successful year, with a dual goal of promoting friend¬
ship, and enabling married couples to share good
times. Each month exciting and novel affairs are held
and guided through the leadership of committees
formed to arrange each gala event.
The themes vary from month to month, but tra¬
ditional events include an extravagant New Year’s
Eve party, a mid-year Installation banquet, and a
picnic or outing during the summer months.
As in all other social clubs, there is a central core
of officers and board members, and the organization
is governed by husbands and wives serving and work¬
ing together. Through each member’s sharing the re¬
sponsibility of serving on a monthly committee, an
atmosphere of warm congeniality always prevails, and
the promotion of good fellowship is the end result.
All couples between thirty-five and fifty-five years
of age, who are interested in sharing good and friendly
companionship, are urged to contact any club officer
or board member.
