This Year Book is a bird’s-eye view of the Jewish Center program of
this last year. The book is a summary of the various activities of this
Synagogue-Center depicted in pictures and words. We are grateful to the
many organizations of this community who have cooperated in providing us
with the material.
We appreciate the efforts of our staff and other members of the Center
who have devoted many hours and effort on behalf of our 1968-69 Year
Book. Our special thanks to Hyman Selber, Business Chairman, and Irvin
Ghelerter, Ad Layouts.
TO OUR ADVERTISERS:
Our constant gratitude, not only for making the publication possible,
but also for the support that they lend to our Youth Activities. We urge
our members to reciprocate with their patronage.
• A reference and guide for the Jacksonville Jewish Center membership.
• Serves over 7,000 members and advertisers.
• All proceeds devoted to the Center’s program.
