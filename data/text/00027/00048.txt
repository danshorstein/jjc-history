The Center Men’s Club, amost important spoke
in the humming wheel of Center activities, offers the
men of our Congregation the opportunity to partici¬
pate in a program covering social, cultural, religious
and service projects to combine with the overall Syna¬
gogue program.
Dinner meetings are held on the first Thursday
night of each month and offer a variety of interesting
topics.
During the past year a very successful debate
covered the topic of Jewish institutions participation
in current events of a political nature. Other interest¬
ing programs included an informative talk by Mr.
Danny Bridges, Director of the City of Jacksonville
Sports Complex, a joint Brotherhood meeting with the
other local Jewish congregations at the Etz Chaim
Synagogue, and a program based on the History of
Jewish Music presented by Cantor Abraham Marton.
Each year the Men’s Club contributes to the Jack¬
sonville Blood Bank and urges the support of all mem¬
bers and their families for this worthy cause.
Students of the Center Religious Schools enjoy
the Holiday treats presented by the Men’s Club on
Purim and Simhat Torah. The Men’s Club has also
played an important role by offering scholarships to
deserving students who attend Camp Ramah and hope
in the future to offer scholarships for other Synagogue
sponsored youth programs.
The officers of the Men’s Club invite your support
and participation as they look forward to a banner
year of accomplishment.
