Cantor Marton
The twentieth Annual Concert of the Center
Choral Society and Glee Club was presented on Sun¬
day, April 7, 1968. The feature presentation was
Avodat Hakodesh (Sacred Service) by Ernest Bloch.
Clem Boatwright was guest Conductor and Cantor
Abraham Marton, Soloist.
This Concert climaxed twenty years of dedicated
service to the congregation and the community at
large. The Choral Society and the Glee Club are to
be congratulated and commended for their contribu¬
tions throughout the years for having created and
stimulated an appreciation for good Jewish music.
Mazal Tov to Cantor Marton for this achieve¬
ment and may you go from strength to strength.
