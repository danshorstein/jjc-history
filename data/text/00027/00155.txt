IN HONOR OF HARRY GENDZIER
Dr. Artz, Harry Gendzier and Jack Becker
The Jewish Community Council, the River Garden
Hebrew Home for the Aged and the Jacksonville
Jewish Center combined their efforts for a Testimonial
Dinner in honor of Harry Gendzier.
Over 300 people paid tribute to Mr. Gendzier for
fifty years of service to the Jewish community. He
received as gifts a trip to Israel, a Torah and a beau¬
tiful scroll.
Rabbi Max Arzt, Vice-Chancellor of the Jewish
Theological Seminary and a long time friend, delivered
the address of the evening. Mr. Jack Becker served as
Chairman.
