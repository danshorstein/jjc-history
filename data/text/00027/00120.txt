Under the direction of Fred Kupperman, Athletic
Chairman of the Center, the Men's Basketball Team
concluded a very successful season in the City Recre¬

second with 18 wins and 2 losses.

at the Center Campsite were kept busy with players
taking advantage of the fine handball, basketball, ten¬
nis and volleyball courts.
We look forward to a great athletic season for the
entire Center family. Available to members will be the
excellent facilities of the Center and Campsite. Keep
fit! Join the Center teams! Watch the Center Bulletin
for time and place.
