Many exciting and challenging projects were un¬
dertaken by Cub Pack No. 14 this year. Some of the
activities were the annual Blue and Gold Banquet,
Scout Sabbath, the Goodwill collection drive, the pine-
wood Derby, and the rocket Derby.
Many new Cubs were welcomed into the Pack,
thereby increasing the roster of four Dens.
The Webelos Den followed the new, improved
program designed to fully prepare the ten year olds for
their big step into Boy Scouting. The five boys who
graduated this year into Boy Scout Troop No. 14 are
David Evans, Donald Zimmerman, Lane Jawitz, Neal
Berinhout and Bobby Schulman.
Cub Pack No. 14 is proud of its Cubmaster Sidney

Sylvester Kilishek, Mrs. Donald Kupfer, Mrs. Leon
Robins, Assistant; Mrs. Harry Schemer; with Mrs.
Robert Berinhout serving as Den Leader Coach. Robert
Berinhout led our Webelos Den and was assisted by
Mark Zimmerman. Our special thanks go to Mrs. Louis
Slott, Center Youth Chairman, and Ted Freedman,
Institutional Representative, for their assistance.
Den meetings are held weekly and Pack meetings
ar held on the last Thursday of each month. All
interested boys between 8 and 10 years of age may
contact any Den Mother or the Center Office.
