The Center Youth League has once again gone
through another tremendous year. Under the able and
enthusiastic leadership of our President, Marty Ber¬
man, our Advisor, Miss Rita Weinstein, and the rest
of our officers, C.Y.L. prospered and grew with such
programs as "Smoking, Drinking and Drugs’’, "Com¬
parative Religions” and "Morality”. We also had two
very profitable Sabbath Weekends out at the Center
Day Camp and one Friday evening meal and Service
at the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
The highlight for C.Y.L. was, along with C.P.C.,
hosting the Seventeenth Annual Regional U.S.Y. Con¬
vention here in Jacksonville. Jeff Goldstein and Debbie
Morris, the Convention Co-Chairmen, and the Con¬
vention Committee helped make this Convention one
of the greatest in HaNegev Region history. At the
Convention as Eliot Safer, the Regional President,
once C.Y.L.’s President, gave his farewell address,
everyone wondered who would be the new officers. That
night at elections Jeff Goldstein was elected First
Vice-President of the Region.
As the year closes for us, C.Y.L. held its chapter
elections and Harry Sinoff was elected C.Y.L. Presi¬
dent. Following the elections, the annual Installation
Banquet was held on June ninth. Susan Gendzier and
Debbie Michelson served as Co-Chairmen.
Our year didn’t end there and on July fifth,
U.S.Y. on Wheels arrived in Jacksonville for a good
southern Sabbath Weekend. All went well and the
