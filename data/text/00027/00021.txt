THIS IS MY SYNAGOGUE
This is MY SYNAGOGUE. It is composed of people like me. We make
it what it is.
It will be friendly, if I am. Its pews will be filled, if I help to fill them.
It will do a great work, if I work. It will bring other people into its worship
and fellowship, if I bring them.
It will be a Synagogue of loyalty and love, of fearlessness and faith, and
a Synagogue with a noble spirit — if I, who make it what it is, am filled
with these.
Therefore, with the help of Sod, I shall dedicate myself t othe task of
being all these things which I want my Synagogue to be.
Compliments of
LOUIS PORTNOY - WALTER PORTNOY
