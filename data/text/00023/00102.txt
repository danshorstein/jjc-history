United Synagogue Youth
Center Youth League
G.Y.L. (Center Youth League) is an organization
affiliated with United Synagogue Youth, whose main
interests are closely associated with the Synagogue plus
a varied program of cultural, social, and athletic activi¬
ties.
The total aims of the USY program itself may be
summed up in the words ‘"Bring the Youth Back to the
Synagogue/'
C.Y.L. had a very successful year during the past
season under the guidance and direction of its two very
competent advisors, Buddy Shorstein and Evelyn
Brauner. The year was highlighted by the first BSB
National Send-off which received regional and national
recognition. The ruach built up at this Kinus Katon
provided a real incentive for an inspirational as well as
enjoyable National Convention.
At our National Kinus this past year in New York
at the Hotel Statler-Hilton, C.Y.L. received a chapter
award for programming, five individual membership
awards as well as a BSB Fund Raising award for one
of the largest contributions in the nation. Along with
its national recognition, C.Y.L. had a number of com¬
munity service projects which included a play for the
residents of the River Garden Hebrew Home for the
Aged.
Advisors for 1964-65 will be: S. Robert Shorstein
and Miss Rita Weinstein.

All in all a very worthwhile and enjoyable year was
had by all. C.Y.L. cordially invites all potential mem¬
bers between the ages of 15-17 to participate with them
this coming year and help them to grow even stronger,
larger, and more dedicated to the synagogue.
