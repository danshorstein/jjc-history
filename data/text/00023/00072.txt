Goals . . .
The progress of any type of school program is usually
measured by two essentials: a high goal showing that the
school is progressive and never static, and progressive innova¬
tions to show how this high goal is being achieved.
It is the goal of the Jacksonville Jewish Center educational
program to have the highest standard possible with the use of
the latest possible teaching methods.
Those who have followed the program's development
during the recent year must be impressed by the remarkable
innovations that the school has sponsored.
