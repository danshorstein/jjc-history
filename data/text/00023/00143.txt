PORTRAIT OF A LEADER
Frank E. Diamond, C.L.U.
He did it again! By providing a great life with Gulf Life for
a great number of our area citizens, Frank E. Diamond has
again become a qualifying and repeating member of the
Million Dollar Round Table. This time it was for his 1963
performance. He also qualified for the National Quality
Award.
Frank started with Gulf Life in Atlanta in 1937 and later
headed up the Estate Planning Department of the Home
Office. He is now Manager of the Gateway Ordinary Agency
at 1639 Atlantic Boulevard—telephone 359-2778.
A recognized authority in his field, Frank has taught many
C.L.U. classes in taxation, business insurance, estate planning
and related subjects.
Mr. Diamond is past president of the local C.L.U. chapter,
and member of the Jacksonville Life Underwriters Association
and the Northwest Florida Estate Planning Council.
