The Progressive Credit Union sponsored by the Jewish
Progressive Society is organized to encourage thrift and pro¬
vide a safe, convenient medium whereby members can invest
and accumulate savings. The Credit Union carries life sav¬
ings insurance recently increased to $2,000 and loan protec¬
tion insurance without any direct cost to the members. Last
year the Board declared a 3%% dividend on savings and
10% rebate of interest to borrowing members.
