Boy Scout Troop 14
Boy Scout Troop 14 under the continued and dedi¬
cated leadership of the Scoutmaster, Dr. Harry Yergin,
is currently among the top troops in the Mohawk District
and always continues to strive for higher goals.
Camping trips were made to Camp Echokotee, Ross
Allen’s at Silver Springs, the national Jamboree and other
weekend retreats whenever school vacations permitted.
Chairman of the Scout Committee, Henry Lasris, along
with 14 Scouts from our Troop spent an entire week at
the summer camp at Echokotee, living in bivouacs
throughout the week. Special thanks are due to Mr. Lasris
for his efforts.
Special mention should also be made of the two
Scouts Ira Price and Richard Darlow representing Troop
14 at the International Boy Scout Jamboree in Valley
Forge, Pa. with over 52,000 Boy Scouts in attendance.
Our annual Blue and Gold Banquet held jointly with
the Cub Scouts had over 250 parents and friends of
Scouting participate in the closing event of our Scout
Weekend.
