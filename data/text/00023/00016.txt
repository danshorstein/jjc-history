Transportation
Left to right: Our station wagon used for small pick-ups with our three 66 passenger buses
and the drivers.
To keep our transportation at its peak performance and maintain our outstanding safety record we have Morris
Montford who has been with us for the past twenty years, Eugene Davis a 14 year veteran behind the wheel, with
newcomer W. Henry Mathews. This devoted trio of bus drivers covers the city eight times daily for Kindergarten,
Hebrew School and Foundation School pick-ups during the winter and provides Day Camp transportation during
the summer months. The entire transportation system of routes and maintenance is under the supervision of our
Comptroller, Kenneth Bowden, and the chairman of the Transportation Committee, Mr. Herbert Lewitt.
Maintenance
Our loyal maintenance crew composed of Tim Riley,
Wallace Mazon and Frank Edwards are responsible for
the constant upkeep and set-up of our Synagogue-
Center. These veterans of many years have devoted
countless hours to seeing that all functions are prepared
for in the best possible manner.
Kitchen
A familiar figure in the Kitchen at all Center dinners
and banquets is Annie Mae Fort, our kitchen supervisor
for the past 18 years.
Providing the tasty food at most Center affairs and
members' Simhas are our two caterers Mrs. Mary Safer
and the Meyerhoffs whose meals speak for themselves.
On behalf of the Board of Directors, Officers and myself, we would like to take this opportunity to pay special
tribute to this loyal and devoted transportation, maintenance and kitchen staff who have given unselfishly of them¬
selves during the past twenty years.
