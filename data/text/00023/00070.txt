Our Achievements
The goal of the Jacksonville Jewish Center school is to
have the highest standard in the country. The accomplish¬
ments achieved during the past year include the following:
• Continuous education from 3 to 18 years of age.
• Advanced departments in Hebrew School and
Sunday School.
• A Foundation School of First, Second Grades and
opening of Third Grade.
