Now in its 5th year of operation, the Tofield Memorial
Library under the capable and loyal direction of Mrs. Philip
Selber and Librarians Mrs. Bernard Glossman and Mrs.
Archie Boatwright has shown great strides and appreciable
increase in its selection of reading material.
Always expanding their services and hours of operation
the Library is now open each Sunday morning and with the
cooperation and assistance of the Sisterhood Library Com¬
mittee under the chairmanship of Mrs. Byron Morris has
made it possible for the Library to be open Monday through
Thursday from 3 to 5 P.M. In addition to the above hours
the Library is also available in conjunction with the Adult
Education classes.
