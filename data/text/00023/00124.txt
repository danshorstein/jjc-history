The Mr. and Mrs. Club, a social organization now in
its 11th year, meets at the Center for its social and busi¬
ness activities. The Club which limits its membership,
fosters congeniality and good fellowship among its loyal
membership, who form monthly committees to conduct
the dinner and dance affairs each month.
The committees vie with each other in preparing food,
entertainment and decorations in order to earn an ‘Ttzik”
award in the various categories. A family picnic affair is held
in the summer months, an Installation Banquet mid-year
and a New Year’s Eve Party highlight the social whirl
conducted by the Club.
Each member is expected to serve at least once a year on
a monthly committee. Membership in the Club is by ap¬
plication and those interested may contact any of the
Officers or Board members.
