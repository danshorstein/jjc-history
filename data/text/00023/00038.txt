The year 1963-64 was started with a delightful opening
luncheon titled “Hats Off To Noemie”, honoring Mrs.
Arnold Turetsky and introducing her to our membership.
The October meeting was a huge success with a guest
speaker, Mrs. Mitzi Mann, from Miami who spoke on the
Combined Campaign. Mrs. Louis Moss gave a large contri¬
bution and was our first Cha’yay Olam Contributor.
The annual Membership Tea was held in the evening
and together with our husbands, we enjoyed the magnificent
program ‘'Jewish Home Beautiful”. The beautiful holiday
tables were set up traditionally and presented by members
of the Sisterhood in program form.
The Chanukah Fair was a huge success and was attended
by children, parents and grandparents. Food, games and
white elephants were available to all. As exciting as the year
before was the Art Exhibit chaired by Mrs. William H.
Schemer and Mrs. Harry Yoffee featuring original paintings
and crafts by Sisterhood and Center Members.
An Oneg Shabbat was held after services January 11th.
Brief book reviews were presented by Mrs. Lewis Ansbacher,
Mrs. Harold Newman, Miss Minnie Schreiber and Mrs.
Bernard Meyer.
In February, the Paid-Up Donor Affair program was
“I Remember Daughter” starring Mrs. Samuel Thier, a past
President, and a cast of many of our lovely members and
their children who modeled beautifully.
