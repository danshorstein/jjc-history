“A Short Glimpse of the Greenies”
During the short span of just five years Coach Bob Fischer has
built a teenage basketball program that the entire Center can be
proud of.
The four “Greenie” basketball teams have combined to win 53
games while losing only 9, one of the best independent team records
in the city.
In its initial season the senior-led team posted a record of 7
victories against 3 defeats. Steve Moss along with Ed and Bruce
Witten paced the team.
The next team was the center’s best, as it won 16 and lost only
1. Martin Edwards, Rick Stromberg, and Charlie Gusky were the
team leaders.
The ’61 -’62 Center Greenie squad posted a fine mark of 14-5
and featured a spirited guard combination of Jerry Cohn, Jack
Fleet, and forward Steve Strumlauf.
Last year’s undefeated team was led by Mike Roth and Ben
Michaelson. The record was 16-0.
This season the Greenies did not participate as a teenage team,
however Coach Fischer assures us they will be back on the court
again during the upcoming campaign. — Martin Edwards
