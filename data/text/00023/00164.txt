Serving the entire Jewish community of Jacksonville, the
Jewish Community Council is the central Welfare Fund and
Community Relations body of the local Jewish community.
An association of individuals, synagogues, organizations and
social service agencies, the program encompasses key ac¬
tivities which give unity, cohesion and coordination to local
Jewish communal life.
A community-wide annual Welfare Fund Campaign pro¬
vides support to significant local, national and overseas or¬
ganizations. The National United Jewish Appeal, has ex¬
pressed American Jewry’s responsibility for the welfare and
rehabilitation needs of Jewish communities the world over
for over 20 years. River Garden Hebrew Home for the Aged
and the Jewish Welfare Society are the local beneficiaries.
The Council publishes a monthly newspaper known as
the Commentator. It is the official organ of the Council and
its member organizations.
The Council conducts a leisure time activities program
which includes Youth Council programs such as college
preparation workshops, a youth news page in the Com¬
mentator, and a youth division in the annual Jewish Welfare
Fund Campaign. The Council also sponsors a community
summer Day Camp for children between the ages of 4 and 15.
