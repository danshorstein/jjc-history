Daily Kindergarten
Our Daily Kindergarten and Nursery now in its
24th year of operation is among the best in the city.
Our outstanding staff is composed of Mrs. M. J. Reiser,
who had the first graduating class in 1942, and the
veteran teachers who have been with us more than ten
years, Mrs. Carl Hackel, instructor of the oldest group,
Mrs. Harry Jaffe, instructor of four year olds and Mrs.
Edward Sussman, teacher of the youngest group. The
program and curriculum are under the direction of
Ted Gross, principal.
