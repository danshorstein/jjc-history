Cub Scouts Pack 14
The enthusiasm and devotion shown by our Den Mothers and Cub Scouts
has lifted our Pack among the leaders in the city. The 38 Cubs between the ages
of 8 through 10 participate in weekly Den meetings at the respective Den
Mothers' homes with monthly Pack meetings held on the last Thursday of the
month at the Center. Special mention should be made of our first Webelos
Den under the direction of Harry Kowitz, assistant Cubmaster, and to Cub-
master Sidney Engelhardt for his interesting programs at the monthly Pack
meetings. Our sincere thanks to the Pack Committee and the Den Mothers for
a yeoman's job in Cubbing.
Cmdr. Sidney Englhardt
Cubmaster
