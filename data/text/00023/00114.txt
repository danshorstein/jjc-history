Our 6th successful summer under the direction of
Robert Fischer concluded with increased enthusiasm dis¬
played by campers and counselors alike who engaged in
an outstanding program which featured the use of our
new and beautiful 36 acre Camp Site.
The baseball program which featured three teams in
City League competition, CIT program, paddle ball
participation, swimming in the new Olympic sized pool,
and Color War among numerous other activities were the
highlights instituted under our current leadership.
The enrollment included boys and girls 4 through
14 years of age with over 380 youngsters taking advantage
of our nine week summer camp.
Now after 18 years of using rented facilities, the
Camp is proud to have the beautiful new site located on
State Road 13 which will serve as the permanent location
for all our future seasons.
Our entire camp pictured on the basketball court at our new Camp Site.
