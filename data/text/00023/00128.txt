DAVID GOLDRING,
President
Now in its 4th year of operation, this young social
club for married couples with either spouse being under
the age of 32, completed another successful year. The
monthly affairs under the advisorship of the Vice-Presi¬
dent and a different member of the Board are in charge
along with a committee composed of the membership at
large. Each member and his wife is asked to work on one
affair per year. The highlight of our year’s activities is the
extravagant New Year's Eve Party. Another outstanding
affair this year was the trip to Daytona Beach for Jai Alai.
Our organization is open to all married couples and
anyone interested is asked to contact our membership
chairmen, Mr. and Mrs. Sy Bellack, 725-8513, or Mr. and
Mrs. Lawrence Dubow, FL 9-9054.
