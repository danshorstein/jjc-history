Girl Scouts and Brownies
The Center's two Girl Scout Troops and two Brownie Troops under the
past leadership of Mrs. Leon Schinasi has grown in stature and will be
ably continued under the newly elected Chairman for 1964-65, Mrs.
Howard Hecht. Special thanks are due to the Troop Leaders and Co-
Leaders shown with their Troops below.
The Center Girl Scout program is sponsored and coordinated by the
Sisterhood Vice-President in charge of youth.
The annual closing Banquet in June marks the highlight of the year’s
activities for these girls ages 8 through 13. The girls meet weekly at the
Leaders' homes located in each section of the city.
With this year's completion of the Center Camp Site, these groups look
forward to increased activities made possible by this new facility on the
Southside.
MRS. HOWARD HECHT,
Chairman
