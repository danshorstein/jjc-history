Major’s Pre-Teens
With the revision to include two grades in the
Major's Pre-Teens, this group of 7th and 8th grade boys
and girls now are functioning with monthly activities
throughout the school year. Programs are geared to this
pre-teen level through their monthly social, civic and
athletic programs.
The meetings are held immediately following Sunday
School with varied programs from month to month.
Special thanks are due to Mrs. M. J. Reiser who co¬
ordinates the group and the mothers' committee com¬
posed of Mesdames Marvin Ganson, Irwin Gendzier,
Manuel Katz, David Paikin and Isidore Weiss. Appre¬
ciations are also due to the CYL sponsors Melvin
Gottlieb and Anita Mesnick.

Our youngest group composed of 5th and 6th grade
boys and girls is supervised by Mrs. Bernard Glossman
with a dedicated committee of mothers composed of
Mesdames Byron Morris, Robert Kauss, Ben Zimmer¬
man, Harry Berman, Seymore Hoffenberg, Harold Hart,
Joe Goldman, Henry Bettman and Leonard Whitlon,
along with the following five CPC sponsors: Lauren
Silverberg, Gail Price, Howard Hosenblatt, Bob Wolfson
and Beverly Hart.
Among the monthly functions from September
through May are such diversified activities as Israeli
hootenanny, square dancing, bowling, ice skating and
many others held on Sundays immediately following
Sunday School. Newly elected officers for the coming
school year will strive to continue the club’s program on
the high level which last year‘s administration attained.
