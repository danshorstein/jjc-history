Center Choir and Glee Club
Pictured on this page is the Center Choir and Clee Club under the
capable direction of Cantor Abraham Marton who for the past 16 years
has presented the annual Concert of the Choral Society, a 30 voice adult
choir, augmented by the children's Glee Club of 70 voices. This past year’s
Concert held on March 8 highlighted a Friday Evening Service with
Samuel Sherter as soloist with the secular part of the program highlighting
Nelle Watkins, Martha Tanner and Edward Doe as soloists.
Proceeds of the Concert are annually donated to the Cantor's Institute
of the Jewish Theological Seminary of America.
