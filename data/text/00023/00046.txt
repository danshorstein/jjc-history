1964 Ramah Campers
(Not Pictured—Ned Price and Davida Weiss)
The great demand for attendance at Camp Ramah can be witnessed by the
24 students of our Religious Schools who attended this eight week Hebrew oriented
camp sponsored by the United Synagogue through its Commission on Camp Ramah.
Representing the Jacksonville Jewish Center at Camp Ramah from all grade levels
represents a 100% increase over our 1963 delegation and the largest contingent
from any synagogue in the Southeast. Our congregation can well be proud of this
achievement and step in the right direction.
