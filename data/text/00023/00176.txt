Young Judaea
Young Judaea, sponsored by Hadassah and Z.O.A.,
has completed another prosperous and successful year
with three regional conventions, various religious and
social programs and an Israeli Fair at the River Garden
Home for the Aged. The Hashomerim boys also helped
with the Blue Box project.
Eight Jacksonville Judaeans attended the Southern
Region Summer Convention at Camp Judaea in Hender¬
sonville, N.C. in June. Camp Judaea in Hendersonville
is a major project of Young Judaea. Hadassah and
Z.O.A. sponsor this 4 or 8 week camp period with a
full camping and Zionist program. Camp Judaea serves
to develop in youth the ideals of American freedom,
an appreciation of the Jewish people, and ethical con¬
cepts of Judaism.
