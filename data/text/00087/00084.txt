The Jewish Center has completed a very
successful Athletic Program in its first year
of activity. The purpose of the program was
to reach all age groups in all phases of acti¬

Committee included Junior Boys Basketball,
Varsity Basketball, Junior Varsity Basketball,
Adult Volley Ball, Jewish Center Softball
Team, Center Softball League, Mens Health
Club, Ladies Health Club, Junior Girls Volley
Ball, Junior Girls Basketball, and Ping-Pong
for all age groups.
