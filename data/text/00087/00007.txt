In furtherance of the expanded scope and program of
Center activities, the past year saw the formation of the
Couples Club, the purpose of which was to increase social
fellowship and companionship among its members and to
present an opportunity for and encourage its members to
indulge in entertaining self-education.
Opening its membership to all young Jewish couples, the
Club quickly attained its membership limit of 75 couples, and
because of the great interest shown by the community, the
Club has now expanded its membership to 100 couples.

To promote the purposes desired, the Club sponsors one
social affair each month and one cultural affair. Among
the social events have been dances, swimming parties and
theater parties. Included in the cultural affairs were a
child psychology program, a panel discussion on the Passover
holiday, and a marriage clinic.
In addition to the above mentioned officers, now serving
the Club, the following persons were among the first officers:
Cele Sachs, Secretary; Mr. and Mrs. Melvin Margol, Joe
Falis, Max Mendelson, Herbert Weinstein, members of the
Board of Directors.
