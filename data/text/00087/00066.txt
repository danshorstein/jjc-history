The Girl Scout and Brownie Movement of the Jack¬
sonville Jewish Center is sponsored by the Center Sisterhood.
The purpose of the Girl Scout movement is to develop
girls as individuals and as useful, active citizens in a democ¬
racy. The Girl Scout Promise and Law is the ethical code
of the group. The principles and ideals of the Organization
are promoted by: 1. Having the Troops consist of small
groups; 2. Volunteer trained leaders; 3. A wide program of
varied activities carried on in a democratic manner; 4. Open
membership to all girls of all races, religions and economic
levels; 5. Development of citizenship through community
participation.
Girls of the Center’s Brownie and Girl Scout Troops
have participated in all phases of the Girl Scouting program.
They have volunteered their services to the American Red
Cross and the Community Chest; sent clothing to Israel and
a Jewish girl’s orphanage in Hungary and cooperated with
Troops of other denominations and races in the city.
