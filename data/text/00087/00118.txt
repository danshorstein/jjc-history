The Progressive Credit Union, sponsored by the Jewish
Progressive Society, has continued to grow in its 12th year of
operation. Our Credit Union is one of more than 10,000 Credit
Unions in existence in the United States, having almost 5
million members and more than 1 billion dollars in assets.
While the Credit Union movement in the United States has
grown phenominally, the Progressive Credit Union in our own
community has also grown.
We look with pride upon our accomplishments. Prom
a very modest beginning of 20 members and $500.00 in assets,
we now have over 300 members, and more than $165,000 in
assets. We are especially proud of the fact that during our
existence, we have made over 1200 loans amounting to ap¬
proximately $1,500,000 without a single loss. This has been
accomplished by the membership striving to make evident, by
practice, the benefits of cooperation.. We are organized to
encourage thrift and provide a safe, convenient medium
whereby members can invest and accumulate savings. We
use the members’ surplus to assist another member when
circumstances require it. To eliminate usury and increase
the purchasing power of our membership by enabling them to
borrow for provident, productive and other beneficial purposes.
We sponsor the Blue Cross Hospitalization Plan under group
rates for those members who desire it. We carry Life Savings
Insurance equal to a member’s savings up to $1000.00, and
Loan Protection Insurance. This coverage is paid by the
Progressive Credit Union without any cost to the individual
member.
The climax of last year’s activities came with the annual
meeting and banquet held at the Jacksonville Jewish Center
Auditorium. At that time our Board declared a 2% dividend
on savings and a 25% rebate of interest to borrowing mem¬
bers. Our Credit Union is one of the few that make it a
practice to return to our borrower’s a percentage of the
interest they pay on loans. For the past year we show a
steady growth in all departments and look forward to even
greater growth in the coming year.
