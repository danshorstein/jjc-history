CENTER EVENTS OF THE YEAR
Sept. 4, 1946—Opening of Hebrew School.
Sept. 5, 1946—Opening of Nursery-Kindergarten.
Sept. 8, 1946—Opening of Sunday Religious School.
Oct. 14, 1946—Daughters of Israel Luncheon
meeting.
Oct. 22, 1946—Men’s Club—“Meet the Press” forum:
Harry Gendzier, Jack Becker, Philip
Selber, Joseph Hackel.
Nov. 19, 1946—Jewish Community Council Lecture;
Samuel Levenson, humorist.
Nov. 26, 1946—Men’s Club Illustrative lecture on
Jewish music—Cantor Marton.
Nov. 27, 1946—Daughters of Israel Thanksgiving
Dance at Roosevelt Hotel.
Nov. 29, 1946—Sabbath Services Mr. Herschell
Auerback, Zionist speaker.
Dec. 13, 1946—Joint Temple and Center Hanukah
Services, Rabbi Sidney M. Lefkowitz,
speaker.
Dec. 15, 1946—Jewish Community Council concert—
Marguerite Kozenn and Julius Chajes.
Dec. 31, 1946—Daughters of Israel New Year’s
Eve Dance.
Jan. 7, 1947—Ladies Hebrew Sheltering Aid Society
Card Party.
Jan. 13, 1947—Jewish Community Council concert—
Interpretive dancing: Zvi Schooler
and Benjamin Zemach.
Jan. 14, 1947—Joint meeting of all Zionist groups.
Jan. 15, 1947—Center annual election meeting, Jo¬
seph Hackel elected President.
Jan. 16, 1947—Progressive Credit Union Banquet.
Jan. 26, 1947—B’Nai B’Rith Initiation of Class,
Justice Maier Steinbrink, speaker.
Jan. 28, 1947—Men’s Club Dinner Meeting Debate
on Religious Education: Jack Becker,
Harry Kolitz, Louis Safer, Philip
Selber.
Jan. 29, 1947—First Meeting Center Board Buffet
Supper, home of Mr. and Mrs. Joseph
Hackel.
Feb. 4, 1947—Special Center Board Meeting, Rabbi
Tofield called to Center pulpit.
Feb. 7, 1947—Boy Scout Sabbath, Joseph Mizrahi,
Scoutmaster, and Troop members
participating.
Feb. 11, 1947—Z.O.A. Meeting, Dr. Joseph Goldberg,
speaker.
Feb. 21, 1947—J. W. Y. Sabbath, Joseph Wilensky,
Commander, speaks on “The Spirit
of Washington”.
Feb. 25, 1947—B & P W Card Party.
Feb. 26, 1947—Men’s Club Annual Ladies Night
Play “Purim Hi-Jinx” presented by
pupils of Hebrew School.
Mar. 2, 1947—Men’s Club “Monte Carlo”.
Mar. 6, 1947—Annual Purim Seudah and Installa¬
tion Banquet for Rabbi Tofield and
newly elected Officers and Board of
Center. Dr. Abraham E. Millgram of
of the Jewish Theological Seminary,
speaker.
Mar. 7, 1947—Joint Purim Service held at the
Temple—Rabbi Tofield, speaker.
Mar. 9, 1947—Young Judaean Carnival.
Mar. 9, 1947—Purim Assembly.
Mar. 10, 1947—Jewish War Veterans Bingo Party.
Mar. 14, 1947—B. B. Young Women Sabbath.
Mar. 21, 1947—Young Judaea Sabbath.
Mar. 31, 1947—Community Council Concert,
Molly Picon.
Apr. 1, 1947—Men’s Club Model Sedar, Masada
Club invited as guests.
Apr. 18, 1947—Hadassah Sabbath Mrs. Harry Katz,
“Underground to Palestine”.
Apr. 27, 1947—Opening of Men’s Club Soft Ball
League.
May 8, 1947—Jr. Hadassah and Masada program,
Miss Chava Schelzer, guest speaker.
May 9, 1947—Mother’s Day Service, greetings by
Mrs. Jack Becker, Daughters of Israel
and Mr. Herbert Sandler, Center
Men’s Club; Girl Scouts participating.
May 11, 1947—A. Z. A. Minstrel.
May 18, 1947—Boy Scout and Cub Scout Banquet.
May 20, 1947—Daughters of Israel closing luncheon.
May 23, 1947—Choir Sabbath “Gift of Music”
sermon topic by Rabbi Tofield;
Presentations; Cantor Marton, Joseph
Hackel, Harry Gendzier, Jacob
Becker, Leo Moscowitz, Mrs. J.
Becker.
May 25, 1947—Confirmation Services.
May 29, 1947—Closing exercises of Hebrew School
at Sabbath services.
May 30, 1947—Closing exercises of Center Sunday
Religious School.
June 4, 1947—Men’s Club Dinner Dance, Installation
of Officers, Dr. J. Roy Sloat, president.
