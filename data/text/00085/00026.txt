Summer Day Camp—Jewish institutions and agencies
all over the country have become increasingly aware of
the possibilities inherent in vacation time programming,
a trend that is evident in the general community both re¬
ligious and secular. The Center is keeping pace with this
tendency. Every year progressive measures are taken to
insure a summer replete with wholesome play, civic in¬
terest, Jewish religious and cultural attainments, and fellow¬
ship. The agency is the Summer Day Camp.
