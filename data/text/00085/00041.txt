CITY RECORDER
ADEQUATE WIRING
means
ENOUGH OUTLETS FOR CONVENIENCE
The wiring in most homes was installed years ago when the electric load was
comparatively light — serving only your lights and probably a flat iron.
Each year the load has grown heavier as you add more electric servants — a
refrigerator, washer, vacuum cleaner, radio and more lighting for better seeing.
So in your new home, insist on having a sufficient number of circuits, not only
to provide current for your present appliances, but also for future electrical innova¬
tions. The slight additional cost will repay itself many times over!
CITY ELECTRIC DEPARTMENT
GEORGE A. PIERCE, Commissioner of Utilities
