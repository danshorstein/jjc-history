Rosh Hodesh
Shabbat Hazon this Sabbath which precedes Tisha B’Ab
derives its name from the first words of the Haftorah “Ha¬
zon”—the vision of Isaiah, which fortells the destruction of
Jerusalem.
Tisha B’Ab (ninth day of Ab) is a fast day which marks
the destruction of the first Temple in Jerusalem by the Baby¬
lonians (586 B.C.E.) and also of the second Temple by the
Romans (70 B.C.E.).
Shabbat Nahamu (Sabbath of Consolidation), derives its
name from the first words of the Haftorah “Nahamu, na¬
hamu, Comfort ye, My People.”
