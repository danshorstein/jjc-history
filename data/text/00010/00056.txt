To record in detail all the activities of the Center
would take more space than is provided in this volume.
The reader who is acquainted with Synagogue life realizes
that the account of a year s activity by affiliated organiza¬
tions and major departments do not convey a complete
picture. The picture would be equally incomplete if only
events of which the Center was the sole sponsor were
spread over these pages. As a Center Synagogue we co¬
operate with and encourage communal organizations whose
programs are compatible with our own. The chronological
list of events of the Center or held at the Center, whether
at worship or on other occasions, it is hoped will aid in
the appreciation of the service rendered by the organization
to the local community and to the community of Israel.
For a complete record and closer examination of the Cen¬
ter’s functioning, one would have to go to the files of the
Center Bulletin published weekly through the active season.
