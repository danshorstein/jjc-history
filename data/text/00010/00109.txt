Jacksonville Young Judea is under the supervision of
the Jacksonville Zionist Youth Commission, an agency of
all the Zionist organizations in the community. In the past
year the Zonist Youth Commission was headed by two
outstanding leaders, Mrs. Maurice Cherry, chairman and
Mrs. Nat F. Cohen, co-chairman. Mrs. Jacob J. Becker
was Young Judea representative of Senior Hadassah and
Miss Ruth Leonard was chairman of the Leader s Council.
Nine clubs of Senior, Intermediate and Junior status were
active with cultural and social programs. Their annual
events have gained a place for Young Judea as the most
active Zionist educational agency in Jacksonville. Young
Judea was organized into the following clubs:
