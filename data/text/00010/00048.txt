SOFT BALL LEAGUES
CENTER SUNDAY LEAGUE
From the end of April to the beginning of August,
the Jacksonville Jewish Center Sunday Softball League
was directed by Ben Cohen on behalf of our Athletic
Committee. 1 he following teams participated: Center
Men’s Club, Esquires, Masada, Amigos, Young People s
League, Temple Brotherhood, Jewish War Veterans. The
latter won the first half and the Young People’s League
the second half. In a 2 out of 3 play-off for the cham¬
pionship the Young People’s League was victorious and
will receive the Harry Finkelstein award.
OPEN CHURCH LEAGUE
Our so ftball team played in the Church League spon¬
sored by the Recreation Department and won the first
half but lost the second half to the Wesley Memorial
team. In the championship play-off games our team
was defeated by Wesley Memorial 2 to 1. Our team
however, gave a good accounting of itself and made a
fine appearance in its blue and white uniforms supplied
by the Men s Club. Itz Greenberg was voted the most
talented player. Sid X. Cohen was the coach and Ben
Cohen was the manager.
