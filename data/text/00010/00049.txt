BASKETBALL SEASON
CENTER BASKETBALL LEAGUE CENTER TEAM IN CHURCH LEAGUE
Sponsored by the Athletic Committee of the Center
and the Center Men’s Club, Basketball games were played
in school gyms Wednesday and Sunday mornings with
Sid X. Cohen as coach. 1 earns were entered by Pi
Lams, Esquires, A. Z. A. and Masada and in the final
standing they were rated in the order given. The season
closed with a game between a Center team and one from
the Savannah Jewish Alliance.
The Jacksonville Jewish Center entered a team in
the Church League sponsored by the Recreation Depart¬
ment and played the following games: Opportunity House,
Epperson Memorial, First Baptist, Baptist Temple and
Lakeshore Baptist.
Opportunity House won the first half and the Cen¬
ter s team went through to victory in the second half. In
the play off series Opportunity House came out on top.
Our team showed good spirit in the contests that took
place before large attendances.
