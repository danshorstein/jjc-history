The Jewish War Veterans of America, organized in
1896, is the oldest veterans organization in the United
States. Its value to the American Jewish Community
rests on its special nature as a veterans organization.
Members of the J. W. V. participate actively, as members,
in such veterans organizations as the American Legion,
Veterans of Foreign Wars, Disabled America Veterans,
etc., on the local, state and national level. They take
active part in helping to shape and direct legislative ac¬
tivities of these organizations. Representatves of J. W.
V. are members of local, state and national Veterans
Councils and Committees. Because of its special rela¬
tionship with both past and present leadership in vet¬
erans circles, J. W. V. is able to effectively serve Jewish
interests in important areas.
