The Business and Professional Women of Hadassah
engage in the same activities and sponsor the projects of
Senior Hadassah. Since its membership is made up of
women, as its name implies, occupied during business
hours, meetings and programs are scheduled to suit their
particular convenience. The Jacksonville chapter has
been making steady progress and forms a valuable link
in the chain of Zionist organizations.
