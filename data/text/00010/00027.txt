Belonging to a Synagogue and Sisterhood is a mark
of good Jewish citizenship. This implies the acceptance
of its teachings and inspirations. Sisterhood affiliation
implies an intelligent, sympathetic appreciation of all
those other efforts which enrich the Jewish personality,
help the survival of Judaism and which provide for the
welfare of the Jewish community.
Our obligation as a Sisterhood member is to help
preserve the Synagogue, Judaism and Jews and to create
proper attitudes among Jewish women toward Judasim.
As an auxiliary of the Synagogue, the Sisterhood is or¬
ganized to promote Jewish educational, religious and

and their friends as well as the mothers of the children

work for those values which the Synagogue is constituted
to provide.
