Rosh Hodesh
Shovuot is so called because it completes exactly seven
weeks from the second day of Passover on which the Omer
(a measure) of the new barley was brought to the Temple.
The holiday celebrates the early wheat harvest in Palestine
and presentation of Kiddurim (first fruits). It also com¬
memorates the covenant which was established at Sinai be¬
tween God and Israel and giving of the Ten Commandments.
