MASADA CARRIES OM
During the past year, s year of great national crisis, the Jacksonville Chapter
of Masada has carried on its cultural work, and its Zionist activities with great
zeal. Towards the end of the year? the membership has implemented the ideals
of Zionism by active participation in the battle for the liberation of Israel and
mankind—the majority have joined the armed forces of the U. S. A.
A very great honor was bestowed on the chapter when Hal Leitman was re¬
elected a vice-president of the national organization at the National Con¬
vention in New York. He was also elected to the presidency o f the Southeastern
Region of Masada, at the first regional conclave held in Jacksonville last year.
In cooperation with Junior Hadassah, Masada has led in a campaign for
funds among the Jewish youth of our city. In this spirit of cooperation Masada
and Junior Hadassah held several joint meetings, at one of which the film, J he
City of I el Aviv, was shown. Every member of Masada also became a member
of the Y. M. H. A. when it was reorganized.
An installation banquet for the officers of the chapter was held at the Sem¬
inole Hotel on May 10th. A very entertaining musical program was enjoyed and
a number of Zionist subjects were discussed by the officers of the organization.
Due to the fact that so many members ot the organization have joined the
armed forces of our country, the local chapter of Masada has turned over its
financial assets to the Jacksonville Jewish Center? to be held in trust for the
period of the emergency, until a new recognized Masada organization can be
formed. However Masada will carry on its cultural program and social work as
long as any members are left in the city.
