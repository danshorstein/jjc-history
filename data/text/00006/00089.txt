REV. JOSEPH ROBIN
I his September Rev. Robin com¬
pletes eleven years of service with the
Jacksonville Jewish Center. During
that time, Rev. Robin has not only
become an integral part of the organ¬
ization, but has also endeared him¬
self to all of our members, young and
old, and to the Jewish community at
large. Reverend Robin supervises the
daily ‘ Minyon and is responsible for
the constant and uninterrupted fun¬
ction of these services. He also keeps
a correct record ol Hebrew dates for
the observence of Yahrzeits for the
deceased relatives ol our members.
Reverend Robin also serves in the
capacity of a teacher in our Hebrew
school, taking care o f the B ar Mitzvah
classes. More than a hundred students
were prepared by him for B ar Mitzvah,
all of them having been imbued with
a sense of reverence for our religion
because of his genial personality.
In addition there are a number
ol other odd tasks which Rev. Robin
performs, such as officiating on the
High Holy Days and reciting memor¬
ial prayers, both in the Synagogue and
on the Cemetary at appropriate times.
Rev. Robin has also been in charge of
the collection of dues, a task which
was made less difficult for the Center
because of the friendly relationship he
has established with the membership.
Rev . and Mrs. Robin have this
year observed their fiftieth wedding
anniversary. The entire congregation
extends its congratulations and best
wishes to them and wishes them many
more happy years together.
