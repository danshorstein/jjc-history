Since February 1940 Tbe Progressive Credit Union, sponsored by tbe Jewish
Progressive Society, has been rendering a great service to the progressive and
liberal minded people of this community.
In a letter from the Comptroller s office of the State of Florida dated April
15, 1942 is found the following observation, This credit union is different, it is
in conformity with both the letter and the spirit of the law governing credit unions .
Every member of the Board of Directors and other active members of the organ¬
ization have excelled in the services each has rendered. In particular three persons
are outstanding for their activity, they are: Treasurer L. Klepper, for his care ful
and diligent guardianship of the finances ; President of the Credit Union, Dave
Davis, for his conscientious leadership; and President ol the Jewish Progressive
Society, Alfred Stein, who has and is still devoting a great deal of his time and
energy to both organizations.
All work is performed without compensation other than the knowledge of
service to one’s fellow men. Expenses paid out are for the folowing services: (a)
Insurance, (b) Treasurer’s bond, (c) Bank charges, (d) Examination tax? and (e)
Credit Union League membership. All other expenses are paid by the Jewish
Progressive Society.
The Progressive Credit Union is a saving and loan organization for mutual
benefits, one of a group of about 12,000 with a total membership of over 3,000,-
000 in the United States, chartered February 1942 under the Supervision of the
Florida State Banking Department. To date 319 loans totaling $173,800.00 h ave
been made without a single loss ol any kind. Deposits total $35,173.86.
