Masquerade Winners
The merriment and all age participation of the holiday of Purim can be felt in the Synagogue while the
Megillah is being read. Always a capacity crowd, the holiday creates a joyous atmosphere the children
remember from year to year. The sound of the grogger and dangling jewelry, beards and mustaches,
three-cornered hats all add to the excitement. The following pictures were taken this past year.

Sisterhood Purim Play
“Southside of Persia”

through the music of the show “South Pacific.” So much talent was displayed that it looks as if we may have
to do an annual play.
