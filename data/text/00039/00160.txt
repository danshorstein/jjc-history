Holocaust Commemoration
and
Israel Independence Day Celebration
A very important and ambitious program was planned in com¬
memoration of the Holocaust and the celebration of Yom Haatzmaut,
Israel’s Independence Day. The program was called “From Darkness
to Light” to underscore the theme of light and hope with which Jews
faced life. As tragic as the holocaust was, it gave way nevertheless to
the greatest contemporary miracle, the restoration and building of
Israel.
The four congregations and their rabbis participated in the day’s
events, which included a poster contest, music, dance, poetry and
dramatic presentation by the 9th grade Hebrew High Class of “The
Fighters of the Warsaw Ghetto.”
