KADIMA CONVENTIONS
Approximately 250 young people converged upon
the Jacksonville Jewish Center on February 8th - 10th
for our second annual Kadima convention. There was
a wealth of activities for all, including a song and dance
festival that was held during the weekend. The over-all
theme of the convention was: “The Many Faces of
Judaism,” which dealt with the four movements in
Judaism. This material was explored at the study groups
schedule throughout the weekend. This was rounded
out with athletics, a dynamic disco-social, and fire sign.
