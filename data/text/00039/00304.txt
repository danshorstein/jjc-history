ABRAHAM MARTON, CANTOR EMERITUS
Cantor Abraham Marton has served the Jacksonville Jewish Center
for 32 years before his retirement.
Although semi-retired, the Cantor is quite busy with visitations to the
hospitals, occasional services for Bar or Bat Mitzvahs, weddings, and
sad occasions in Jacksonville and surrounding communities. Cantor
Marton also conducts services on the High Holidays with a full chorus.
Plans are being made for a concert sometime in March.
A wish to everyone for a Happy New Year form Cantor Marton and
his wife, Pearl.
