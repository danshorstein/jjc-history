WHAT IS SZECHUAN CUISINE?
Szechuan Cuisine is a brilliant freak which breaks all the rules of conventional Chinese Mandarin cooking and gets away
with it. The use of relishes and seasonings is another language. The manifold flavour is the chief characteristic of Szechuan
food. Many dishes are simultaneously sour, sweet, hot, salty, and aromatic. One is often surprised by the first taste, but
after awhile one can see that there is a different logic that makes Szechuan cuisine so unique and colorful in character that
it stands out as one of the world’s best cuisines.
Ask manager Hok Leung to explain the delightful difference of Szechuan Gardens. However you prefer your Chinese
dishes, (our special hot & spicy ... or mild ... or sweet) we will gladly prepare for you!
