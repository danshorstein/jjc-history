JUNIOR CONGREGATION
Modeled after the Senior Congregation, our Junior Congregation is an organization of all students in classes.
The main purpose is to conduct Shabbat morning classes and services in their own place of worship, led by their
own advisors and the children themselves. We have found that children who attend Shabbat morning services not
only become familiar with the service and feel comfortable attending, but have an opportunity to read Hebrew
extensively and to put into practice that which is taught during the week. Shabbat School is considered a part of
our Hebrew School program.
