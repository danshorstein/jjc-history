THE JERUSALEM FAIR
The Jacksonville Jewish Center, in celebration of
the festival of Hanukah, transformed its building and
exterior to recreate the mood of the City of Jerusalem.
Upon entering this model city through the “Jaffa Gate”,
one entered an old recreated street covered with arches
reminscent of the old City of Jerusalem which leads to
thw Western Wall. As one traversed the streets of this
model of the ancient city, sacred to Jews and Christians
during this holiday season, one passed through
courtyards and bazaars lined with shops, which convey
the sense and atmosphere of Jerusalem. An Israeli
marketplace, bustling with flowers, Jaffa oranges, quaint
shops and stores carries as well the aroma of the foods of
Israel. An Israeli cinema featured the films of Israel and a
display of the history of this ancient city, emphasizing the
historic character of a city sacred to three major
religions, is within view.
