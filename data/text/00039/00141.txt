This has been a banner year for the Israel Kaplan Lodge No. 80. We have been involved in many successful community
projects. We participated in the Bell Ringer campaign for the Salvation Army at Christmas, a C.P.R. mannequin was donated to
the Red Cross, convalescent cards were sent to Jewish patients in local hospitals. We gave an award for outstanding service to a
local policeman. Lastly, and most important, as our main project for the year, we are donating a Torah to the Hillel House at
Florida State University.
An important step in lodge growth was achieved with the merger of the Wolfson Lodge with our lodge this year. We are
anxious to affiliate all interested Jewish men in our fine organization.
Much of the success of this year was due to our President, Milton Hecht and the fine group of officers who worked closely
with him. We expect the upcoming year to be even more successful and once again urge all jewish men to join and become
active.
