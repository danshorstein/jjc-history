Boasting of the longest continuous existence of any social club in the Jewish community, the member¬
ship of the Mr. and Mrs. Club consists of just what its name implies. It was organized a quarter of a century
ago for the sole purpose of promoting fellowship, friendships and good times for married Jewish couples.
Dances are held every month to the music of live bands. Meals and snacks are served and each com¬
mittee decides on its own type of entertainment. All of this is climaxed with a gala New Year’s dance
including a cocktail party, full course dinner and professional entertainment.
As this organization is non-profit, all of the aforementioned is enjoyed at a nominal cost. There is no
age limit and membership is open to the entire married Jewish Community. Couples who are interested in
joining may contact any officer or board member.
