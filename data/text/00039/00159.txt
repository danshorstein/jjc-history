The second Annual Jacksonville Jewish Center/Alexander Brest Tennis
Tournament was held during the week of December 2-9, 1979. Eleven teams
were entered in the men’s A/B division which was won by the team of Ronnie
Pollock/Rick Safier over Charlie Blalock/Bill Cohen. In the “C” Division, 12
teams were entered with the Dr. Steve Lazoff/Dr. Michael Scharf team coming
out on top over the team of Sherwin Burman/Ira Goldfield.
In all 46 men participated in good, healthy, fun-filled competition. Our
thanks go to the participants, Beauclerc Country Club and Big Tree Racquet
Club who donated valuable court time, Pic ’N Save who donated tennis balls
and the Tennis Committee of Ronnie Pollock, Arthur Goldstein, Neil Sandler,
Shelley Kulchin and Debbie Goldstein who gave of their valuable time.
A special thanks to Mr. Alexander Brest who so graciously donated the
tennis courts at the Center and in whose honor this tournament is held.
We are looking forward to the Third Annual Tournament which should
be the best yet.
Ira Goldfield, Athletics Chairman
