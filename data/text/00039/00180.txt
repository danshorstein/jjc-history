The Sunday School of the Jacksonville Jewish Center is planned expressly
for children in Kindergarten, First and Second Grades. A two hour program provides
each child the opportunity to experience the richness of their Jewish Heritage
through song, dance, role playing, cooking, short stories, games and arts and crafts.
Emphasis is placed on the holidays and festivals. Jewish values and mitzvot are
encouraged.
This year the Sunday School program was co-ordinated by Tricia Bloom.
The staff included Nancy Minkoff, kindergarten teacher; Scott Zimmerman, first
grade; and Shirley Bielski, second grade. Judy Geller served as music specialist,
teaching Hebrew songs to the children, and Tricia Bloom served as arts and
crafts specialist.
