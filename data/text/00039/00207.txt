Sunday Funday 1980 continued
to grow by “leaps and bounds”
as evidenced by an increase of 22%
in participation — totaling 202
this season.
The popular basketball program
included fourteen teams coached
by sixteen enthusiastic and dedica¬
ted volunteer coaches. Ira
Goldfield and the Athletic Com¬
mittee are to be congratulated
for another outstanding program.
Other activities rounding out
Sunday Funday included dance,
crafts, tumbling, tennis, pre-school
Funtime, and the adult stress
management class.
An excellent staff of instructors
contributed to the overall success
of Sunday Funday 1980.
Shelly Kulchin
Funday Coordinator
