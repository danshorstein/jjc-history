The Young People’s League of the Jewish Center has
become an important society for the Jewish young people
of our Community, by helping them attain both cultural
and social objectives. Y. P. L. offers the connecting link
between youth and the Center.
The past year Y.P.L. has had many activities, including
dances, outings and Friday night services welcoming home
college students during fall, winter and spring vacations.
GENE CRYSTAL
Y. P. L. also has been the link between the Jewish
service men and women stationed at the Naval Air Station,
and the Jewish community. Our Tuesday evening meetings
are open to them and they have found here a place where
the service men and women can spend a little time with
the young Jewish people of this community.
During the coming year Y. P. L. has plans for another
series of cultural, educational and social meetings. The aim
will continue to be to inspire the young Jewish people of
this community to become more closely affiliated with
Jewish life and the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
