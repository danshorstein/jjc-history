SCENES AT JUNE 14TH INSTALLATION BANQUET
The Couples Club, now in its second year, has estab-
lished itself into an integral organization of the Jackson-
ville Jewish Center. The Club was formed to increase
social fellowship and companionship.
The membership of the Club has increased from the
original 75 couples to 125 couples.
Social affairs are held once a month and include dances,
picnics, costumes affairs, etc. A New Year’s Eve Ban-
quet and Dance proved to be an outstanding event. In
addition to the monthly socials, the Club has sponsored
various cultural and educational activities, including a
Hanukah program and party, lectures on dental care,
child guidance, and a series of lectures and discussions
on religion. The Club also sponsored bridge lessons and
a golf tournament.
With an ever increasing desire for activities, cultural
as well as social, the Club hopes to attain a well rounded
program for its entire membership for the coming year.
