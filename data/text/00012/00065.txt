EVENTS OF THE YEAR
On this page and those that follow, we record events
for which there were photographs available. Many more
occasions deserved to be thus highlighted. Each one of our
organizations enjoyed a full year of successful activity.
The events they sponsored hold fond recollections for all
of us. The sampling reproduced here and the files of
the Center Bulletin, we hope renders the historical record
significant.
C. y. L. INSTALLATION
HAVDALLAH SERVICE
Photographs on this page were taken at the C.Y.L.-
U.S.Y. first meeting of the year, Sunday, October 12th at
8 P.M. The date coincided with the outgoing of Simhat
Torah. Installation of officers for the year took place at
a Havdallah Service ushering out the Festival. Each one
of the newly elected officers contributed a candle to the
Havdallah Light and recited one of the objectives from
the National U.S.Y. constitution as a pledge of trust in
living up to the term of office.
Prayers, recitations and poems were offered by mem-
bers of the outgoing administration.
In the top panel is a photograph of the Havdallah Light
composed of all the candles held by officers. The exposure
was made with this light only. Surrounding the table is the
same group photographed in the right panel with the
addition of others who participated in the ceremony.
In the left panel is pictured the attendance at the meet-
ing. The group adjourned for a social in the Youth Lounge
of the Youth Center. Photographs of that event are printed
on page 29.
In the right panel are the installed officers and others
who participated in the program of the evening. Reading
from left to right: Dr. Tofield, Carol Kantor. Byron Selber,
Dorothy Newman, Paul Newman, Larry Siegel, Diana
Katz, David Thier, Paula Aberle, Elvina Mack, Joel Selber,
Vivian Eff, Miss Myra Thier, Advisor.
