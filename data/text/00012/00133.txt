As America’s oldest national service organization, with
a heritage second to none, we proudly greet you, our mem-
bers and friends.
B’nai B’rith with its many services, such as: Anti-
Defamation League, B’nai B’rith Hillel Foundation, our
Youth Organizations, Vocational Service, B’nai B’rith-
sponsored Hospitals and various philanthropic and cul-
tural activities looks forward to a successful and healthful
year.
We have experienced in the State of Florida a dream
come true. On the campus of the University of Florida at
Gainesville we have erected, not a building, but a home
for our young people. A new Hillel House where our
Jewish boys and girls can meet socially and for cultural
purposes also.
With such a many faceted program the local organiza-
tion feels sure that each member of this community can
find an activity of interest with which to carry on our
B’nai B’rith heritage.
