CANTOR ABRAHAM MARTON
During the past year the Jacksonville Zionist District of
the Z. O. A. has shown a remarkable increase in activity
and membership and was cited by the national organ, The
American Zionist Reporter.
The Zionist Organization of America is dedicated to
assisting the State of Israel in all of its endeavors and to
serving the local communities through education. It seeks
to establish a closer bond culturally between Israel and the
Jewish community.
The local district sponsored the Chaim Weizmann Me-
morial Service' on December 8th at which time Dr. Mau-
rice L. Perlsweig, internationally known Jewish leader,
was guest speaker, and more than $2,000.00 worth of trees
in Israel were purchased in memory of the first President
of Israel.
The district also put over a very successful panel dis-
cussion on February 15th. The subject was “Religion in
Israel.”
On April 19th the district arranged for a city-wide ob-
servance of the 5th Anniversary of the State of Israel,
with Marvin Lowenthal, noted author, delivering the main
address.
The district has enjoyed splendid co-operation with the
Hadassah units in promoting various Zionist projects this
past season. Cantor Marton takes this opportunity of ex-
pressing his sincere appreciation and gratitude to the
officers and membership at large for their fine spirit of
helpfulness in the cause of Judaism and Israel. He also
wishes to thank the local committee of the American Zion-
ist Council for an outstanding job done in their public
relations work.
