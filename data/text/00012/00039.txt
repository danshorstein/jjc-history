A year of successful program planning for the Cub
Scouts of Pack 14 was climaxed by the Fathers-Sons meet-
ing held at the U.S. Naval Base, on Fathers Day, June
21st.
Beginning the program in September, 1952, under the
capable leadership of Dr. Carl Hackel, numerous monthly
themes were developed and planned with the capable
assistance of the Den Mothers. A Pack of 30 boys divided
into 4 Dens, grew to a Pack of 42 boys with 6 Dens by
year’s end.
GROUP OF CUBS AT FATHER AND SON OUTING
Outstanding monthly activities were the Cubs “Talent
Night” in conjunction with the Boy Scouts in January;
birthday party and celebration of Charter renewal in
February and the Marde Gras held in March in the
Center Auditorium. The annual Cub picnic and meeting
was held at Scout Camp in May.
The success of the Cub program as planned and de-
veloped during the past year was the result of the com-
bined efforts of the Committeemen and Den Mothers who
served during that time. We look forward to even greater
progress in the coming year.
