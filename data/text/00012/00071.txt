QUEEN ESTHER CORONATION
On Saturday night, March 28th, following the Reading of the
Megillah, the Coronation ceremony of Queen Esther 1953, Barbara
Rothstein (seated, center), formed part of the Purim celebration.
Competition is open to girls of the 9th grade in the Sunday Religious
School. Seated on the right side of the stage is Queen Esther, 1952,
Harriet Witten surrounded by her court.
CONGREGATIONAL SEDER
For the first time in recent years the Center sponsored a Congre-
gational Seder. It was held on the second night of Passover, Tues-
day, March 31st. Reservations were over-subscribed. Our member-
ship is asked to keep this in mind for the coming Passover and
another Seder is planned, please God.
