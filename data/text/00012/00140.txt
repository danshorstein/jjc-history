ISRAEL UPSON
The Progressive Credit Union, sponsored by the Jewish
Progressive Society, has continued to grow in its 13 years
of operation. It is one of more than 10,000 Credit
Unions in the United States, having almost 5 million
members and more than 1 billion dollars in assets.
From a very modest beginning of 20 members and
$500.00 in assets, we have grown to over 350 members,
and more than $185,000.00 in assets. During our exist-
ence we have made over 1,300 loans to members, amount-
ing to approximately $1,600,000.00 without a single loss.
We are organized to encourage thrift and provide a safe,
convenient medium whereby members can invest and
accumulate savings. We are chartered by the State of
Florida and regularly examined by the Comptroller of the
said State, and the savings of the members are safeguarded
in several ways. The Credit Union being a cooperative,
assists its members when circumstances require and permit
such assistance. The purchasing and business power of
the members is increased by their ability to borrow and
repay loans under convenient arrangements such as are
afforded them by our Credit Union. We sponsor the
Blue Cross Hospitalization Plan under group rates for
those members who desire it. Life Insurance equal to a
member’s savings up to $1,000.00 and Loan Protection
Insurance is paid by the Progressive Credit Union without
any cost to the individual member.
The climax of each year’s activities comes with the
annual meeting and banquet held in the Jacksonville
Jewish Center Auditorium. Last year the board declared
a 2%% dividend on savings and a 25% rebate of interest
to borrowing members.
