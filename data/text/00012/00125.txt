MRS. IKE WITTEN
Hopes, cherished ideals, dreams, these were the things
that inspired and brought together a small group of women
who combined their energies to give this community what
is known today as River Garden Home for the Hebrew
Aged. Theirs was the foresight, knowledge of the need for
such a home, and the wisdom to instill love for the aged
as commanded by the sages of our faith.
The women of the Hebrew Sheltering Aid Society have
been endowed with this wonderful legacy. Ours is a precious
gift from the elders who founded this organization. To
them we owe a debt of gratitude which can only be repaid
with continued efforts to expand and increase the measure
of services we offer to River Garden. As the auxiliary of
this home we are ever mindful of the needs which are
constantly arising and ever ready to fulfill these needs;
whether they be for linens, a Summer pavilion for outdoor
picnics or a most urgently needed new infirmary.
To carry on the aims and purposes of our organization
we have three major functions: The Linen Shower, Tom-
bola Project and Donors Social Affair.
The Women’s Auxiliary of River Garden Home have
been inspired and have inspired others to continue this
work to which the founders dedicated themselves and for
which we in turn further dedicate ourselves and pray that
we may ever carry on this blessed legacy.
