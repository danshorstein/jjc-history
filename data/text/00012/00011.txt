ACKNOWLEDGMENT
The Center Year Book needs no introduction, either to
members of the congregation or to the host of friends
that it has won over the years in the commercial world.
We welcome this opportunity, however, to record a
word of thanks to our members and to our friends.
To Mr. Hyman Selber for rendering a public service
and for his dedicated interest in the project.
To our advertisers, our constant gratitude, not only for
making the publication possible, but also for the support
that they lend to our youth work. We urge our members
to reciprocate with their patronage.
To the Sisterhood and its Committee, headed by Mrs.
Isadore Zaslow, assisted by Mrs. Hyman Selber, Mrs.
Joseph Krestul and their co-workers listed on this page.
To Miss Ethel Joel for taking care of the New Year
Greetings. Please assist her by mailing remittances to her
at 2772 Post St.
To Mr. Albert Bressler for the cover design and for
many of the distinguished group and individual photo-
graphs appearing in this book.
