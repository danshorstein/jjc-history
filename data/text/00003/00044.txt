MEMBERS OF THE SENIOR HADASSAH
Yahrzeit Reminder
The Center maintains a record of
the Hebrew dates for the correct ob¬
servance of the Yahrzeit for deceased
relatives of members and friends of
the Center. Rev. J. Robin is in charge
of this record. A postal card is sent
out by the Center one week in advance
of the Yahrzeit date as a reminder.
Those wishing this service should
communicate with Rev. Robin and en¬
ter the name of their deceased rela¬
tive in our Yahrzeit record.
