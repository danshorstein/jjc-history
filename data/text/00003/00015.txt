THE HERZELIAH YOUNG JUDEAN CLUB
The Herzeliah Young Juclean Club is a National organization, to which the Jack¬
sonville branch is a recent addition. The local group consists of young Jewish girls
between the ages of eleven and fourteen. The club meets every two weeks in
the homes of various members, and Judean books and papers are read. The Club
was also organized for social purposes and the girls have given many parties and
outings.
The Herzeliah Young Judean Club was not very active in the past year, due to
the lack of a sponsor; but this year, Miss Sophie Spivak, who was chosen Leader
of the club just before the end of the season, has aranged an extensive program.
We are looking forward to a successful and enjoyable year under her leadership.

ROTHCHILD YOUNG JUDEAN CLUB
The Rothschild Young Judean Club, a group of boys and girls between the
ages of 15 and 18 years of age, are lead by Miss Edith Weiss.
The R. Y. J. C. has been doing fine work throughout the past year. It has
contributed to the Boy Scout movement in Palestine, the Jewish National Fund, and
has studied current Jewish problems with special emphasis on the events taking
place in Palestine and with reference to the Jewish Youth.
The R. Y. J. C. sponsored a side-show at the Purim carnival conducted by the
children of the Center Hebrew School. During April, set aside as Young Judea
month, the club was honored at a special Friday night service held at the Jackson¬
ville Jewish Center, with members of the club participating.
With various outings, cultural meetings and Floridian tours, the R. Y. J. C.
enjoyed a pleasant and interesting season.
The club plans to enter the National Judea Play Contest, by presenting a series
of short plays sometime during the month of November.
The club has secured a permanent club room at the Center, in which they will
have a book case, containing books which will circulate among the members.
The club issued a newspaper, “THE R. Y. J. C. JOURNAL’’ which contained
club news, special features, editorials, and comments on club members. The next
edition will be issued at the time of the presentation of the plays.
