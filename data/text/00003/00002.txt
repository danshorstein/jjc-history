Troop 14 Basketball Champions
During the basket ball season Troop 14 entered a team in the Boy Scout
Basket Ball League. Under the captaincy of William Katz Troop 14 went through
the season undefeated, winning the championship of the League with ease. They
scored 2 victories over Troop 41, by scores of 42 to 2, and 33 to 5; 2 victories
over Troop 19 by scores of 19 to 1 and 12 to 5; 2 victories over Troop 6 by scores
of 26 to 24 and 10 to 6. They also played one outside game with the A. Z. A.
Fraternity and won that game by the score of 17 to 16. In winning all of their
games during the season, credit must be given to every member on the tear* as
each one played his hardest and above all they played together.
The members on the team were: Irvin Surasky, Capt. William Katz, Simon
Rothstein, Melvin M. Margo], Melvin O, Margo), Meyer Leibovitz, Norman
Moscovitz and Paul Weiss.
