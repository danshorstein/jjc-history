YOUNG MEN'S HEBREW ASSOCIATION
The Young MenвАЩs Hebrew Association played an important part in developing
the Jacksonville Jewish Community. The building located at 712 W. Duval was
for many years the scene of all Jewish activities. That neighborhood being now
without Jewish residents the building has lost its usefulness. All attempts in recent
years to revive the Y. M. H. A. have failed. Plans are under way to sell the
building and to build new quarters in a more suitable location.
