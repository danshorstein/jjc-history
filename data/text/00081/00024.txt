Mr. I. M. Lieberman, president of the Jewish Progressive Society. The aim of the
Tunior Society is to bring together socially Jewish boys and girls between the ages
of 18 and 25 years, and elicit a spirit of cooperation among them.
The Society has sponsored athletic activities, and has entered a diamond ball
team in the Center Diamondball League. The tearru made a fine showing. Among
the outstanding events of last year were The Barm Dance, The Benefit Scholarship
Raffle, and The Labor Day Dawn Dance, the proceeds of which were used for the
scholarship fund.

a number of educational, charitable, athletic, and social activities, which will be of
great interest to members of the club.
