NINE ODD CLUB
The Nine Odd Club consists at present of fourteen members, a mascot, and a
sponsor. The club is open to Jewish boys of our community and its main purpose
to promote social and athletic activities*. The club entered a team in the Center
Diamond Ball League, and distinguished itself by winning the championship and
Bono Trophy.
