FOREWORD
The Jacksonville Jewish Center is proud to present this beautiful Year Bock and
Directory to the Jewish public and to our Gentile friends. The planning and editing of
this bock entailed considerable effort and much detailed work, and presents a picture of
Jewsh life in Jacksonville with special emphasis on the activities sponsored by the
Jacksonville Jewish Center.
The inclusion of a Hebrew-English calendar with a list of all the dates of the
outstanding events for the coming year has spurred on the various organisations to
plan their work in advance, and gave them an early start for the season’s activities.
This also will obviate the usual conflicts between organisations in search of dates for
their functions..
