JACKSONVILLE ZIONIST DISTRICT
The local chapter of the Zionist Organization of America was re-organized
by Mr. M. Sheinbaum two years ago to further the aims and ideals of a Jewish
homeland in Palestine, and is considered the largest unit in the Southeastern dis¬
trict. The two Hadassah units are sister organizations.
The Jacksonville chapter is a regular contributor to all the Zionist Institu¬
tions in Palestine, and raised about $1500.00 for the Jewish National Fund during
the last two years.
The Zionists meet the first Wednesday of every month for a social ana cul¬
tural gathering at which time important speakers and interesting programs are
presented to the group.
