A BUSY YEAR IN THE ‘HOOD!
Kim Glasgal
Sisterhood President
This past year went
by so quickly! As in
prior years, we strove
to enhance Jewish life
for women and all
Center members. This
comes in many forms
- social, educational,
and philanthropic. You
may or may not be
aware of some of the
things we do:
Provide scholarships to send Center children
to Camp Ramah
Buy bereavement cards for the Synagogue to
send to members who have lost loved ones
Provide Jewish educational programs for adult
women - book reviews, educational speakers,
hot topic discussions and more
Organize game days, house parties, luncheons,
dinners and other social and fun events
Provide financial assistance to capital projects
for the Synagogue
Provide financial resources for Religious
School
• Provide financial assistance to our Youth
Groups
• Raise money for scholarships for Conservative
clergy through Torah Fund
• Provide volunteers for Purim festivities, the
Judaica Shop, and High Holidays
• Collect for food pantries and other Social
Action projects
• Advocate for Conservative Masorti Judaism
in Israel through Women’s League of
Conservative Judaism and Mercaz
• AND best of all, help each of us have a
meaningful experience at the Center
When you buy gifts in the Judaica Shop,
send Rosh Hashanah flowers to a friend, buy
Hamantashen at Purim, buy Chanukah gifts at the
Holiday Bazaar, send Leagrams to children for
their consecration, or simply pay your dues, it all
goes to worthy causes you can feel good about.
We have some fun things planned for the upcoming
year. To find out more, follow us on facebook or
check out our website, jjcsisterhood.com. Be a part
of it!
