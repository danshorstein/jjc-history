RELIGIOUS LIFE COMMUNITY
During the year 5773, the Religious Life Community (RLC), under the inspirational leadership and support
of Rabbi Jonathan Lubliner, has worked to shape and guide the religious tone of the Jacksonville Jewish
Center. The RLC continues to help promote the clergy’s innovative changes, to embrace the kesher services
being provided, and to continue to implement our Keruv policies.
The Jacksonville Jewish Center is proud to be one of only a few synagogues to offer daily minyans, six
days a week. Board members and all congregants are strongly urged to make a commitment to attend at least
once a month in order to ensure the continuation of this vital service.
Many thanks to our enthusiastic clergy, Rabbi Lubliner, Rabbi Olitzsky and Hazzan Holzer, as well as
to our ritual director, Scott Zimmerman, for their many hours of devotion and service to our congregation.
Thank you, as well, to the involved members of the Religious Life Community who have given of their time,
insights and wisdom to benefit the Jacksonville Jewish Center. Finally, a special thanks to Philip Bloom, whose
tireless efforts in assigning High Holiday honors is welcomed and appreciated.

HOUSE COMMITTEE
The House Committee is responsible for the regular maintenance and upkeep of the Center’s building
and grounds, preparation and presentation of estimates for major repairs and improvements to the Finance
Committee and Board of Directors, preparation and enforcement of rules and regulations for the use of the
Congregation’s physical facilities and helps assure that the Congregation meets all local and state regulations.
Over the last year there have been several significant projects. Among them are:
• The completion of the two million dollar roof repair project
• The installation of a new welcome and directional signage on the property
• The creation of a new, enhanced, security manual
• ADA access ways on grounds for wheelchairs
