ANNUAL CAMPAIGN FOR EDUCATION
L’dor V’dor
From generation to generation, the importance and significance of a Jewish education
has been a cornerstone of the Jacksonville Jewish Center. L’Dor V’Dor annual campaign
for education at the Jacksonville Jewish Center was developed by stakeholders in the
community who recognize the value of the DuBow Preschool, the Martin J. Gottlieb
Day School, the Bernard and Alice Selevan Religious School, and Makom Hebrew High.
The goal of the campaign is to annually provide financial support that will ensure the
sustainability of these vital institutions.
All gifts made to the L’Dor V’Dor campaign are vital to the advancement of the Galinsky
Academy. Your contribution will help our schools develop and maintain a challenging
curriculum, reflective of our mission to provide the highest quality of education to our children.
Interested parties can become donors to the L’Dor V’Dor Annual Campaign for
Education through many different opportunities. Please contact Carol Wagnon at 268-4200
ext. 119 or email carol.wagnon@mjgds.org
