Jacksonville Jewish Center Express Phone Service -if
Now you can call or leave voice mail directly to the party or department you want. The main number
for the JJC switchboard is 292-1000. When all circuits are busy, a recorded message will offer step-by-step
instructions to the extension of your choice.
To bypass the switchboard and go directly to a specific department or person, simply dial: 268-4200
and follow the recorded instructions. If your party is unavailable, you will be given an opportunity to
leave a voice message or return to the switchboard.
