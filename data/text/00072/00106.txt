I- A Message from the President
GET INVOLVED AND LET’S WORK TOGETHER...
Thank you for your support during our transition to a fabulous group of
new volunteer leaders. I am committed with the rest of your officers and board
members to get more of you involved here at The Center. I learned many years
ago that to effect change is to be involved as part of the process and the solution.
We are fortunate to work hand in hand with a terrific team of professionals in
order to accomplish our goals.
I have watched our congregation change over my 30 years, to a vibrant,
caring, and financially sound organization. We were fortunate over the history
of the Center to have had some terrific leadership who has helped us reach this
day. You are our leaders into tomorrow so the ball is in your court to be part
of our future.
You can help our officers by reaching out to them and say I want to “get
involved” so sign me up. We have many active committees including Membership, Religious/Ritual,
Programming, House, Finance, Youth, and Education (Galinsky Academy). The invitation is extended so
won’t you be with us? Feel free to call or send me a note and I will make it happen.
Wendy, Jereme, Zachary, Noah and I wish our entire Center family a healthy and happy New Year.
Fred Pozin
Board President
