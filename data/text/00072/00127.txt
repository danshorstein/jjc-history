Galinsky Academy
ANNUAL CAMPAIGN FOR EDUCATION
The Jacksonville Jewish Center’s L’Dor V’Dor annual celebration event was held the weekend
of June 1-2, 2013 to celebrate the four schools that together form the Galinsky Academy of the
Jacksonville Jewish Center. This celebration brought the whole community together and raised
much-needed funds for Jewish education. The celebration was dedicated to honoring Gayle
Bailys, recipient of the Rabbi David Gaffney Award for Leadership in Education. Gayle has
been a beloved member of the community for many years and has dedicated her career to the
youth of the Center as a camp director and, for the past 13 years, as the youth director.
The festive evening included the hilarious Jewish comedy of Joel Chasnoff. A Chicago
native, Joel’s comedy was based on real Jewish experiences, such as his stint on his day school
basketball team and his tour-of-duty in a combat unit of the IDF. Chasnoff has performed his
unique brand of smart, uplifting Jewish humor in nine countries, including TV spots in the
United States and Israel and more than 1,000 venues in North America.
Kim Glasgal, Shari Shuman and their wonderful committee put on a spectacular community
building and fundraising event.
