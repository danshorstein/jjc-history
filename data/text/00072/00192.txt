Shalom Baby
SHALOM BABY, A GREAT PLACE TO BEGIN!
The DuBow Preschool’s Shalom Baby classroom is a busy, happy place where parents, grandparents, babies and
toddlers meet to socialize, discuss parenting topics and celebrate Shabbat. There may be no more special time in
the lives of young Jewish families than time spent together - singing, playing, reading stories, celebrating Shabbat
and holidays, making friends and building community. Shalom Baby offers families a social environment where
parents are looking to build lasting friendships. Each class offers songs, stories, circle time, crafts, special visitors
and hot topic discussions.
Our program is open to the Jewish community. It is our hope to bring together families during this precious time
of life. Many friendships have begun in the last eight years of our program and they continue to grow and deepen. If
you are interested in learning more, please call the DuBow Preschool at 268-4200 ext. 143.
