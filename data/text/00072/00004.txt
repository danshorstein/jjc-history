PURIM SHPIEL
Star Wars: The Purim Menace debuted to a full sanctuary crowd the morning of Purim 5773. Known as
Episode DCXIII: The Jew Hope, the story line incorporated not only the Purim timeline as we know it, but the
prequel as well. A long long time ago, in a megillah far far away, the Jewish people were exiled from their
promised land to a new realm. Amidst new people and a new civilization, the Jewish people searched high and
then low...lower...and even lower...until they appointed the first Jewdi to lead the people. His name: YODA.
In spite of their relocation, the people were optimistic. The Jewdi forces were strengthening in a young Jewdi,
Mordi-one Kenobi. This would be the premise for a quirky, laughter filled Purim experience.
