TO MOTHER, MEMA, GRANDMOTHER ...
You are Our Ayshet Chayil -Woman of Valor
Whoever is found in the company of a woman of strong character
realizes how immeasurable her worth.
You inspire confidence for your ability to manage your life in a full and protective way.
You totally justify the confidence others place in you because you are confident
in yourself and in your abilities.
You are like the merchant ships: seeking the choicest, not merely the most readily available.
Quality, not quantity is your motto, and you display it in all aspects of your life.
You are extraordinarily industrious, making the most of each day, knowing life will never hand
you another quite like the one you have.
You do not wish your life away saying ''some day.” Your “some day” is today.
You are well known for your excellence in the community and are highly respected.
You follow through on projects of all kinds.
You are full of energy and are always well occupied.
You dont just say “it should be done,” you get it done.
You merit credit for who you are and all you do.
You have worked hard to become that woman, and you are truly worthy of praise.
Self-pride, honesty, industriousness, kindness — all these can be found in a woman of strong
character and in our mother, grandmother and great-grandmother:
Blanche Slott - unique in family, home, synagogue, and community.
All Our Love from Your Children,
Grandchildren and Great-Grandchildren
