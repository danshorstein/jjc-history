2006 Center Raffle for Education — a Smash Hit!
The Jacksonville Jewish Center’s Annual Raffle for Education on
Sunday, April 2, was an event worthy of an “Oscar.” Celebrities from
all the Center schools were inducted into the “Walk of Fame” for their
starring roles in children’s education. Kudos to the producers, 2006
Raffle Chairs Sandy & Lynn Zimmerman and Brad & Robin
Zimmerman, for a wonderful three act program that included
cocktails with music by Adam Shapiro, a sumptuous brunch by
Margo’s Catering, entertainment by Belle Voce, honors to Center
faculties and staff and the drawing for great prizes.
A sensational supporting cast of Benefactors, Royal Patrons, Grand Patrons,
Patrons and ticket holders broke records at the box office with all proceeds to
benefit the Samuel & Esther Galinsky Educational Programs: Martin J. Gottlieb
Day School, Center Preschool and Bernard & Alice Selevan Religious School.
Showcasing the lifetime achievements of this year’s
recipient of the Rabbi David Gaffney Leadership
in Education Award, Sandy Zimmerman proudly
recognized Ted Gross for more than 50 years of
devotion to the religious education of children at
the Jacksonville Jewish Center, as Education
Director, Baal Koray and teacher. His wife,
Eunice, was given a standing ovation as she
accepted the award on his behalf.
Congratulations to this year’s top cash prize winners:
$10,000 Grand Prize - Scott & Melissa Miller;
$1,000 — Rod & Adrienne Zimmerman;
$500 each - Steve & Sara Gross and Jeff & Sheri Weiss.
Special appreciation goes to the generous merchants and artists who donated
wonderful prizes and silent auction packages: Comedy Zone — Fred Pozin,
Diggin’ in Dreamland — Adam Shapiro, Kim Robbins, La Tienda
Jewelers, Matthew’s, Miriam’s, Native Sun, Oliver Twist - Stan Cason,
Ponte Vedra Lodge & Spa, Salon d’Orsay - Sonya Cohen,
Shirley Bielski and Center Sisterhood Judaica Shop.
