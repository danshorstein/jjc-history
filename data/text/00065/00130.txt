Camp Neshama
Another Great Summer
What an exciting summer we have had at Camp Neshama!
Everyday, campers engaged in swimming, drama, music, Judaic activities, arts
and crafts, sports and games.
We went on several fieldtrips,
slumbered at our campouts, and
had a blast at the “under night.”
Stars were born at our camp
talent show, and we showed our
spirit at the Color Wars Games!
Campers enjoyed daily treats at
our kosher canteen. We also
brought in a train ride, bounce
house, cotton candy machine,
pirates, and many special visitors.
During the first two weeks in July, an Israeli group joined us as part of
Partnership 2000 and the Jacksonville Jewish Federation. The Israelis loved our
campers, and we learned lots of new songs and dances. The youth were so kind to
teach us about Israel and its culture. Etan, the group’s leader and chaperone, is a
very accomplished musician, and he was kind enough to share his talents with
our camp. We are very grateful for the opportunities that the Israelis brought!
As with all camps
everywhere, it is the staff who
determines the degree of
success. We had a
phenomenal summer at Camp
Neshama, and I can say with
all confidence that your
child’s counselors deserve all
the credit. It was a pleasure to
work with such a loving and
dedicated group.
Jennifer Brown
Camp Director
We can’t wait to see you next year for yet another
wonderful summer of sweet memories and fun. Camp
Neshama is truly the soul of the summer here at the
Jacksonville Jewish Center.
— Jennifer Brown
