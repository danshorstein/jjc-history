Bernard & Alice Selevna Religious School Graduation
Shabbat evening, May 19, 2006
The graduation ceremony for the 7tli grade Etgar class marked
the completion of Bernard & Alice Selevan Religious School and
promotion to Beit Midrash, the community Hebrew High School.
Students conducted the Kabbalat Shabbat service and were
presented with certificates of completion.

and Max Rogozinski.
Mazel tov to the graduates and their families.

Peggy Organes and Scott Zimmerman.
Confirmation Class of 5766
6 & 7 Sivan • June 1-3, 2006
Shavuot commemorates the giving of the Torah and Ten Commandments to the Jewish
people. It is fitting that the festival should be celebrated with the accompanying sense
of commitment to Jewish life that Confirmation represents. Confirmands demonstrated
the knowledge they have gained through completion of the Beit Midrash program by
fully participating in Shavuot services. In addition, students read essays they prepared
on the theme “From Jacksonville to Jerusalem: Personal Connections to Isreal.” Among
the presentations, Center Sisterhood gave each participant a booklet of mazel tov
greetings from members of the congregation. The Center Men’s Club presented a
Kiddush cup to each confirmand.
