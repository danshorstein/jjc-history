Youth Activities
Summer Programs for Jewish Youth
More than 50 Center Youth Attended Camp Ramah and
United Synagogue Summer Programs
The Jacksonville Jewish Center is proud to have a large representation of
youngsters attending Camp Ramah Darom in Georgia. Many others participated
in USY On Wheels and various Pilgrimage programs sponsored by United
Synagogue for Concervative Judaism. Scholarships to attend these programs
are made possible through the generosity of the Abe Selber Trust Fund,
Charles Palot Youth Fund, Gordon Family Scholarship, Center Men’s
Club Horty T. Gendzier Summer Scholarship Fund, Center Israel
Scholarship Fund, Center Sisterhood, Center Youth Department, Joan
Levin Gift of Israel Program, Myra Thier Lazarus Camp Ramah Fund,
Sylvia Shorstein Scholarship Fund and many anonymous contributors.
