The future of Israel is at stake. Act now. Israel's land resources are stretched to the
breaking point. After 10 years of dramatic population growth, the economic, demographic

to bring water to the area, to build roads, homes, schools — a vibrant community
where Israelis from all walks of life can live and prosper.
