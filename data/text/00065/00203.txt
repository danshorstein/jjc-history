Did you know . . .
This past year JFCS provided help and support
to over 2000 Jewish individuals and families?
We have heard Jewish Family & Community Services
referred to as the best kept secret in Jacksonville. Clients,
donors, potential donors, even our volunteers can be heard
remarking, “I didn’t know you did that!” Our work is
done with ultimate respect for privacy and dignity. We
may not toot our horn loudly but our ability to make a
difference in so many lives speaks volumes.
So what is it we do? Through the ongoing generosity of
the Jacksonville Jewish Federation and donations from
the community we provide a diverse range of programs
and services to our Jewish friends and neighbors.
When there is no other place to turn ... we are here.

Adoptions
One in five babies will be placed with
Jewish families
Financial Assistance
Financial assistance with rent,
mortgages and security deposits services
were provided to over 700 members of
the Jewish community.
FANN (Feed A Needy Neighbor)
Over 400 members of our local Jewish
community received groceries from the on-
site Winn-Dixie Emergency Food Pantry.
Counseling
A staff of caring, qualified clinical social
workers and therapists provides
counseling services for individuals,
couples, families and children who are
coping with difficult times or disruptive
life events. Over 1,350 counseling hours
were provided to Jewish clients.
College Loans
11 college loans for Jacksonville Jewish
students, totaling $38,422, were
provided by the Jewish Educational
Loan Fund Agency, an affiliate of JJF
Support Groups
Trained counselors provide
opportunities for group interaction and
support, which includes: Bereavement
Support Group, Caregiver Issues,
Interfaith Group, Teen Discussion,
Geriatric Care.
Jewish Healing Network
Volunteers trained by dedicated JFCS
staff are currently bringing comfort to our
most frail Jewish citizens. On average 45
clients are visited each month. This
includes over 160 hospital patients that
have been visited this year.
Jewish Hospice
Through collaboration with Community
Hospice, 41 Jewish patients received
support and the expertise of professional
staff members who are knowledgeable in
Jewish customs and rituals.
Kosher Nutrition
Over 75 Jewish individuals were served
hot nutritious kosher meals
Senior Transportation (CALL2GO)
Through the support of the Jacksonville
Jewish Federation, over 1,670 trips have
been provided to Jewish citizens this past
year. 55% of all trips have been for basic
needs such as doctors’ appointments and
groceries. 17% of all trips have been to
synagogues for Shabbat and holiday
services
We extend our thanks to all of you in the
Jewish community, our donors, our
partners and the Jacksonville Jewish
Federation for making our work possible.

Home Is Where The Heart Is
Mount Carmel Gardens apartments
was designed, even to the smallest
detail, to provide comfortable and
pleasant surroundings. From the
initial concept, Mt. Carmel Gardens
has strived to create respectable
living, providing more than housing.
Mt. Carmel Gardens offers a 17-story
building on a beautifully landscaped
site, 24-hour staffing, community
facilities, and planned programs, all
of which were developed as part of an
overall concept ...a concept of
responding to the desires of retirees
for security, comfort, safety,
independence and the enrichment of
stimulating social-recreational
activities shared with others.
Mount Carmel Gardens is
conveniently located, with
transportation, shopping, hospital and
doctor’s offices nearby.
