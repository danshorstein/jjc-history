CUB SCOUT PACK 14

Cub Scout Pack 14, had a super year under the leader-
ship of Cubmaster Jim Richman. As always, the Cubs
enjoyed funfilled weekly Den meetings and monthly Pack
meetings with the whole family.

Weblos Den, under the direction of Kathy Meomke
and Rick Wolfson earned many pins and awards. In
addition to campouts and flying, Gabe Crafton, Aaron
Finck, Eric Meomke, David Slavkin, Ben Smolley,
Asher Trager, Ben Wolfson and Michael Powell
caught the city bus to the Museum of Arts and Sciences.
