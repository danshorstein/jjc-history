Havurot

A Havurah is a small group of congregation members (families, couples, singles) who
meet regularly for activities or experiences of their mutual choosing. The groups are var-
ied but all gather together to enjoy life as Jews through socializing, education, discussion
of Jewish issues, worship or sharing of Jewish holidays and celebrations.

For information call Havurah Coordinator Etta Drashin, 737-3318.
