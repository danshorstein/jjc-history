A Message From the Principal

Experience for a lifetime is the goal of the Solomon Schechter
Day School. Our classrooms are dynamic places where careful atten-
tion is given to individual learning styles, and group sizes and teach-
ing approaches are varied so each child will be reached. A dedicated
faculty leads students through an enriched curriculum in which texts
are only one source of information.

A solid foundation for learning is laid by developing the unifying
tools which allow mastery of concepts. Students learn to read with
comprehension and skill, to write with fluency, and to think critically.
The most current materials and instructional methods, through both
group an individual instruction, continuously challenge students to
reach their potential.

The richness of Jewish tradition permeates the school day.
Through: observance of daily prayer and kashrut, in holiday obser-
vances, and through teaching of Mitzvot, Judaics becomes an inte-
gral part of everyday life. Hebrew study goes far beyond just being a
language program with the goal of fluency.

The foundations of the school are solid. It has a thirty-two year
record of academic achievement and support of the Jacksonville
Jewish Center. We have doubled our size in the last ten years with-
out sacrificing quality.

Now we turn our visions to the future. We plan on establishing
our Middle School Program during this current year with a carefully
designed program to meet the challenge of this complex stage of
mental and physical development.

We invite you to share in the excitement of this commitment to
meet the needs of a lifetime.
