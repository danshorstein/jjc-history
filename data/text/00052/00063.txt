JUNIOR CHOIR

The congregation was delighted with the first musical performance
of our Junior Choir under the direction of Mrs. Beth Shorstein.
The choir, composed of elementary age students from our After-
noon Hebrew and Day Schools, had practiced diligently after
Shabbat services for several months. Members include: Crista
Cohen, Tiffany Gartner, Rachel Gerber, Cara Goldberg,
Renee Grann, Sara Mizrahi, Aliza Morris, Lisa Rand, Lind-
sey Rosenberg, Amy Schuster, Daniel Shorstein, Rebecca
Shorstein and Esther Yegelwel. They were accompanied on
piano by Mrs. Karen Appelbaum and assisted by Mrs. Stevie
Levine. We look forward to the next concert.

 

Youth Groups

 

YOUTH BANQUET

At the conclusion of the activity year, the Youth Com-
mission sponsors a banquet to honor the officers,
advisors and volunteers from each Center Youth
Group. Newly elected officers for the coming year are
introduced. The lovely ceremony and program are
always enjoyed by the young people and their families
who attend the banquet.
