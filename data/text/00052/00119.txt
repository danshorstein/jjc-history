Sunday Sports Basketball Program

There were 192 children, 27 volunteer coaches and volunteer referees who enjoyed a 10-game season. From Hanukkah to
Purim the gym was bustling with activity almost every Sunday. The competition was fierce but fun. Basketball skills were improved,
team work experienced and friends were made. It was another successful season for the Sunday Sports Association Basketball
League. A big thank you to those who volunteered their time and knowledge, you helped make our season a triumph: Brian
Bialik, Stuart Cohen, Moti Demri, Barry Efron, Bob Fleet, Michael Friedman, Bruce Glassman, Alan Goldman, Carl Holmes,
Bruce Horovitz, Dick Kravitz, Carl Kushnir, Jeff Levenson, Joel Morris, Jim Richman, Abe Rogozionski, Sandy Rosenberg, Alan
Rosner, Andy Ruffett, Steve Safer, Michael Schultz, Russell Selevan, Tom Spriggs, Mr. Stark, Jay Toblin, Alan Trager, Steve
Warfield, Ben Williamson.

Our thanks to the coordinators, Bruce and Edith Horovitz and Gayle Bailys; and referees Scott Zimmerman and Michael Schultz.
