Mr. & Mrs. Club

For thirty-nine years, Mr. & Mrs. has
been promoting fellowship and a
common bond for the furtherance of
religious, cultural and social activities
among fellow Jews.

The club meets at the Jacksonville
Jewish Center every third Saturday of
the month for a social evening of
dancing, entertainment and dessert.
There are several seated dinners
throughout the year and a Gala New
Year’s Eve Party with the works!

If you're thinking of joining the club,
why not try it out. Call any Mr. & Mrs.
officer or board member to “Be Our
Guest!” Transportation can be ar-
ranged for those who need it.

Women’s American ORT
Women’s American ORT is a fund-
raising group that helps to train teach-
ers and provide high tech educational
programs in school in Israel, the
United States and many countries
throughout the world. ORT assists
refugees who are fleeing from
persecution.

American Red Magen David

Magen David Adom (Red Star of
David) which was founded in 1930 as
a First Aid Society for the city of
Tel Aviv, now provides life-saving
and health related services for the
entire population of the State of Israel,
as well as performing the usual tasks
carried out by Red Cross Societies
throughout the world. While the
symbol of the Magen David Adom is
recognized by the United States as a
member of the international lifesaving
societies, work is still being done to
achieve international recognition.

HADASSAH

The sophisticated woman of the 90’s
has as much need as her mother and
grandmother to be involved in a car-
ing circle of women. Since 1913, the
efforts of a handful of thoughtful,
energetic women have been trans-
formed into a movement of now more

than 300,000 dedicated members.

Hadassah is an ideal bridge between
American Jewry and Israel, and is
overflowing with ideas for the sacred
task of Jewish survival and continuity.
Translating these ideas into results
is the measure of our success.
Hadassah’s greatest source of energy
for this task is its MEMBERS!

The Jacksonville Chapter is comprised
of four vital groups, which develop
programs and activities and support
combined Chapter projects. In addi-
tion, the Thrift Shop is open in a new
location. We have openings in every
area of expertise, won’t you join us?
