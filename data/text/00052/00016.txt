Afternoon Hebrew School

The Afternoon Hebrew School of Jacksonville Jewish Center is designed for students in grades 3-7 and
provides an elementary education leading to Bar/Bat Mitzvah and continuation in the high school program.

Meeting Mondays and Wednesdays and on Saturdays, “the basics” are at the core of the curriculum, with
stress on Hebrew reading, Jewish Social Studies, and current events relating to Israel. Synagogue skills, holi-
day observances and knowledge of prayers are all part of the comprehensive Afternoon Hebrew School pro-
gram.
