55+ CLUB

The 55+ Club, now in its sixth year, meets the
second Sunday of each month from 2 to 4 p.m.
at the Center. Programs include speakers, musi-
cal performers, dancing exhibitions and interest-
ing presentations. The entertainment is always
exciting, refreshments are served and there's
always time for socializing. The annual luncheon
in June is the highlight of the year. Membership is
open to the entire Jewish Community with
annual dues only $10 per person.

CHOIR

For nearly 50 years, the Center Choir has been
enhancing services on the High Holidays and
Shavuot with moving liturgical music. Over the
years they have given numerous concerts featur-
ing members of the choir as well as nationally
acclaimed soloists. Many of the current members
have been singing with this group for twenty years
or more. New members are always welcome.
