Early Childhood Department

A Message From the Director

The Early Childhood Department, of the

Jacksonville Jewish Center, exists to provide the highest
quality pre-school general and Judaic education to stu-
dents in the city. The school serves the community,
regardless of affiliation, educating Jewish children in
Jacksonville and offering financial assistance to a signifi-
cant number of families.

We provide an educational environment in which
children can explore and practice the richness of their
American and Jewish traditions.

Combining both innovative and proven approach-
es to education, the curriculum reaches beyond the
classroom to involve home, family and community.

The Day Care program continues the same excel-
lence which is provided in the Pre-school program.
The facilities, including the outdoor and inside play
areas, provide a challenging and stimulating environ-
ment for children.

The school and day care with its dedicated and
excellent faculty is committed to excellence in educa-
tion and child care.
