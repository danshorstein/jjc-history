DAILY MINYAN
CAPTAINS

The Jacksonville Jewish Center
is unique in its ability to provide a
twice daily minyan every day of
the year. It is no small task for the
captains who spend hours calling
members to ensure that those
who are reciting Kaddish, observ-
ing a yahrzeit or any of the other
occasions when a minyan is nec-
essary, have that oportunity avail-
able to them. It is a mitzvah that
deserves the recognition of the
entire community.

CATERING
COMMITTEE

Michael Fleet, coordinator

The Center is proud of the growing
success and popularity of the Cater-
ing Department. Credit for the cre-
ative menus, efficient service,
smooth operation and excellent cus-
tomer relations is due Catering Direc-
tor, Michael Fleet. Along with
Michael, this committee discusses
ways to enhance the use of the Cen-
ters wonderful kitchen and banquet
facilities. Some of the programs they
strive to promote are “Food To Go,”
rental of the Center's Receational
Park and picnic grounds and the
institution of a Fall Food Fair, an
exhibition that would feature sample
meals, party ideas and table displays
aimed at prospective event planners.
In addition, the committee discusses
holiday menus and congregational
meals.
