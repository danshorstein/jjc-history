Fund Raiser for Education a Success!!
Annual Center Raffle Sunday, January 17, 1993

This year’s Raffle Event was again a great success due to the hard work and dedication of so many
members. It was a wonderful evening and an important fundraiser that provided funds to the many
outstanding educational programs at the Center.
