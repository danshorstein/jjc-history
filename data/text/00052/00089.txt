Junior Congregation

Junior Congregation is modeled after
the main congregation. It is an organiza-
tion of students grades 3-7 who attend our
Afternoon Hebrew School and Day
School. These services allow our youth to
participate, lead, and apply knowledge and
skills gained through Hebrew training.
Students earn points for attendance and
are rewarded with Sunday field trips.

Sunday School

The Sunday School of the
Jacksonville Jewish Center is planned
expressly for children in kindergarten,
first and second grades. A weekly,
two-hour program gives each child the
opportunity to experience the richness
of his/her Jewish heritage through
song, dance, role playing, cooking,
short stories, games, arts and crafts.
Emphasis is placed on the holidays and
festivals. Jewish values and mitzvot are
encouraged.
