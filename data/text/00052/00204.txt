Students Celebrate the Holidays

CONFIRMATION
Celebrated Shavuot, 6 & 7 Sivan - May 25, 26 & 27

The biblical festival of Shavuot commemorates the receiving of
the Ten Commandments by the Jewish people at MT. Sinai. It is
fitting that this festival should be celebrated with an accompany-
ing sense of committment to Jewish life that Confirmation repre-
sents. The Confirmants, teenagers who completed the Hebrew
High program, participated fully in services over the course of the
holiday.
