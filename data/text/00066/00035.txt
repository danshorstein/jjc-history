ALL THAT
The joint was really jumpin’ for
the 32nd annual Center Raffle for
Education. Dazzling decorations, lavish
desserts, special coffees and cocktails and
smooth jazz helped create a sensational
evening for raffle attendees.
Raffle chairs, Ellen & Alan
Rosner and Perrin & Devon Rubin and
their hardworking committee have a real
hit on their hands! The place was packed
and everyone was having a swinging time.
The silent auction presented a record number
of fabulous gifts and services to bid on. A
twist was thrown in for good measure, as each of the schools’
principals was surprised with a camcorder for their school. Of course, the
funds raised for the Samuel & Esther Galinsky Educational Programs are
music to everyone’s ears.
Congratulations to this year’s cash prize winners: $7,500 Grand Prize
- Jordyn Simpson; $1000 - Liat & Blair Walker; $500 each - George
Setzer and Yefim Brunfon. The 50/50 instant raffle winner was Paul
Sandler. Plus, Shira DuBow, Chary & Michael Greenburg, Avi and
Micah Rubin and Judith & Martin Whitman won great merchandise
prizes.
