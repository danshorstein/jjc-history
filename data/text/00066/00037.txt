Special Events
8th Annual JJC Golf Classic
Big Win “Fore” Jewish Education!
Camaraderie was high and the competition was keen as the
players teed up at the 8th Annual Golf Classic, played last December
at the Ponte Vedra Ocean Course.
The credit for this outstanding
program again goes to Rick Plotkin,
tournament chair and Sandy
Zimmerman, advisor, Lori
Schoettler, Center administrative
liaison, and the committee. Additional
thanks goes to a great crew of
tournament-day volunteers: Helen
DuBow, Cheryl Plotkin and
Jeanine Rogozinski.
The Jacksonville Jewish Center proudly recognizes those who
ensured the success of this major fundraising effort through their
continued sponsorship. For the 8th consecutive year, the Jacksonville
Bank and the Gottlieb Family were the Title Sponsors of the
tournament. Again this year, Wachovia Bank/Wachovia Securities
- The Jaffe Group were the Event Sponsors.
New this year, Nimnicht Cadillac not only sponsored a Hole-
in-One automobile, but also provided wonderful promotional gifts to
all the golfers, while the DuBow Family Foundation sponsored the
reception.
The beautiful, Waterford crystal, Horty T.
Gendzier Cup was presented to the tournament
winners, Mike Crews, Mickey Salazar, Jeff
Thorson and Christy Williams.
Horty T. Gendzier was a dedicated beacon for
Jewish education and champion of youth at the
Jacksonville Jewish Center and the community.
Once again she was remembered and appreciated when this year’s
Waterford crystal award, the “HORTY,” was presented to the
tournament champions, the team from the Jacksonville Jaguars.
Proceeds of this fundraising effort directly benefit scholarships for
Jewish education at the Center.
Since 1999, the Jacksonville
Jewish Center Golf Classic has
raised thousands of dollars in
scholarship money for Samuel &
Esther Galinsky Educational
Programs at the Center. Through
the generosity of our sponsors, the
Jacksonville Jewish Center is able
to offer financial assistance
regardless of synagogue affiliation.
