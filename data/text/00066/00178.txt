Clients Served - Lives Touched
Jewish Family &Community Services is there for you
At JFCS we are awed by our numbers. How
many clients were served, who we fed, helped with
rent, counseled. For over 90 years JFCS has been
providing care at every stage of the life cycle, from
the joy of a new baby’s first cry, to the profound
sadness of the loss of a loved one.
As incredible as the numbers are, they do not
always tell the story of caring and dedicated staff.
Dedicated not to numbers but to each single person
that walks through the doors. A senior who takes a
cab ride to religious services doesn’t tell the whole
story of time spent by JFCS staff listening and caring
as she tearfully talks about the loneliness she feels
after losing her husband of 65 years. Or the case
manager who meets a struggling Jewish family at the
Greyhound station to purchase their bus tickets, with
a bag of food, so they can safely join their relatives
in the Midwest.

The numbers do not look into the eyes of our clients. They do not tell the
stories of their success and our dedication. When we say with pride, that we provided
services to over 400 Jewish individuals in only six months, maybe we should say
we helped a mother... a grandfather ... a sister ... a child. And the positive impact
of our services supported everyone around them. The work we do is not only
measured one by one, it adds up to families, relatives and loved ones. So many lives
touched.
At JFCS we take care of the Jewish Community; that is and always will be our
priority. Your support as donors, volunteers and community advocates over the last
year has made it all possible.
