The highlight of the evening was the
presentation of the Rabbi David Gaffney
Leadership in Education Award to Rose
Lynn Plotkin, for her more than 34 years of
devotion to the education of students in our Day
School. Alan Rosner read a glowing tribute
from Rabbi Gaffney in which he praised Rose
Lynn for her pioneering efforts to help make
the Jewish day school experience a meaningful
reality for her students and their parents.
Beginning with the Foundation School, until her retirement as a resource
teacher in the Solomon Schechter Day School, Rose Lynn instilled a love of
learning in hundreds of children at the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
