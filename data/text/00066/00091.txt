Ferman, Benjamin Hecht, Maci Morse, Leah
Robbins, Alex Silverberg and Rachel Whitman.
Middle School Judaic-Infusion Trip
Edith Horovitz, Vice Principal of the Middle School, plans a trip each
year to a prominent Jewish community in the United States. Students and
chaperones have spent a week in Chicago and New York.
This past April, they visited Washington, D.C. The tour included Jewish
points of interest, the National Holocaust and Spy museums, the Baltimore
Aquarium, Lincoln, Jefferson, Korean War and Vietnam Memorials, a Kosher
Chinese restaurant, fun parks, plays and much more.
Each year the Middle School presents a fabulous
musical ALL in Hebrew! This time students gave two
performances of “Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor
Dream Coat” to packed audiences. Go-Go-Go Joseph!
Todah rabah to all who staged this great musical: Edith
Horovitz, director, Leah Peer, piano, Lisa Hunt, Judy
Reppert, Cathy Sandler, Noemi Szoychen and Scott
Zimmerman, props and scenery, and Kathy Stark,
costumes.
Todah rabah to teacher, Marc Leventhal, for his
continued efforts to gain the certificate of affiliation to
the National Jr. Honor Society of Secondary Schools.
