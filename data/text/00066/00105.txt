United Synagogue summer programs. Scholarships to attend these programs are made possible through
the generosity of the Abe Selber Trust Fund, Charles Palot Youth Fund, Gordon Family
Scholarship, Center Men’s Club’s Horty T. Gendzier Summer Scholarship Fund, Center Israel
Scholarship Fund, Center Sisterhood, Center Youth Department, Joan Levin Gift of Israel
Program, Myra Thier Lazarus Camp Ramah Fund, Sylvia Shorstein Scholarship Fund and
many anonymous contributors.

USY-ON-WHEELS
USY on Wheels, sponsored by United Synagogue of Conservative
Judaism, gives teenagers an opportunity to travel around the United States
seeing landmarks, historical sites and national parks. In addition, they meet
Jewish youth in the cities in which they stay. Lilly Anderson, Eric Levenson
and Zachary Rogozinski joined other USYers from around the country.
