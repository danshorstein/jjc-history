Summer 2007 at Camp Ki Tov was one of great
fun, adventures and memories to last a lifetime! From
awesome field trips to Color Wars to Camp Olympics,
this summer was one our campers will
never forget!
Camp Chaverim (lst-8th grades)
featured many exciting activities. The
campers took field trips to numerous
places around Jacksonville, such as
Alltel Stadium, the Zoo, and Bowl
America. They enjoyed learning
outdoor skills like tent assembly,
preparing a campfire, and much more
with a specialty camp led by Rabbi Dov
Kentof. The overnights kept everyone
smiling, as did swimming, hanging out
with friends, arts and crafts, and so much more!
The Camp Rishonim campers (ages 1-5)
experienced an amazing summer! Each week was spent
exploring a different theme, such as dinosaurs, bugs,
farm animals and space. The
campers enjoyed an Olympic Day
complete with shaving cream, an
egg toss and even a slip-n-slide!
Each week, they went on a field trip
to a wonderful place, such as
Diamond D Ranch, Metro Park and
the Museum of Science and History.
Camping for these little ones proved
to be a magnificent experience!
Everyone had a
PHENOMENAL time, thanks to our
terrific staff. We look forward to
many more amazing summers at Camp Ki Tov!
— Haley Blount and Karen Morse
