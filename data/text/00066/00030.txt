Marty Gottlieb Brit Hinukh Covenant of Education Award
The Men’s Club Board of Directors established the
Marty Gottlieb B’rit Hinukh Covenant of Education Award
to recognize individual educators within the Jacksonville
Jewish Center Schools for their lifelong commitment to
intellectual, professional and religious development and to
the passionate motivation of their students to learn about
the language, laws, traditions, history and spiritual strength
of Conservative Judaism.
Dan Wohl presented the B’rit Hinukh award to Robin
Morris at the PTA Teacher Appreciation Dinner on May
3,2007.
For thirty years, Robin has been a beloved and
respected Judaica teacher in both the Preschool and
Day School at the Jacksonville Jewish Center. In
2006, she and husband, Jeff, received the JTS Shin
Award for their commitment to Jewish life,
community and the Seminary.
Yashar Kohahat to Robin and her family on
this well deserved honor.
Men’s Club Yad Shel Chai Award
In 1998, Center Men’s Club instituted this award-winning
program to encourage youngsters to continue reading Torah after
their Bar or Bat Mitzvah. Teens who read a minimum of 18 (chai)
portions by age 18 are presented with an inscribed Torah pointer
(yad) and a certificate. Over the years, many teens have enrolled
in this program, and to date 59 have received the award.
The Yad Shel Chai recipients for 5767 were: Sam Bernard,
Ben Hecht, Max Fleisher, Leah Friedman, Matthew
Kingson, Jonathan Rogozinski, Max Rogozinski, Alexandra
Silverberg and Arnie Silverberg.
In addition, Kara Levine and Gabrielle Magid earned a
Double Chai given for reading at least 36 portions. Kara and
Gabrielle read 48 and 41, respectively, and are continuing to read
Torah on a regular basis.
For more information regarding this program, contact Randall Smith at 565-4447.
