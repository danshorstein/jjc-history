Largest Book Yet Presented
MORRIS NIMOVITZ
Editor
Each year, as our institution ex¬
panded, the Center Year booh was
also increased in size. As the Center
increased its membership, beautified
its building, incorporated more and
diversified projects into its program
and opened its doors to new organi¬
zations, more and more pages were
added to this publication in order to
reflect in them the imposing pattern
of organizational life which emanates
throughout the year from the Jackson¬
ville Jewish Center.
This year, the Center Year Book
is the largest ever printed. To our
many readers, this fact will bring
great satisfaction because it is an in¬
dication of a new height in the pro¬
gress made by the Center itself. As
shown within the pages of this An¬
nual it stands today among the fore¬
most religious and educational insti¬
tutions in the South.
The staff hopes that its readers
will find this book an accurate record
o f the work consumated during the
past year in the Center and in the
many organizations affiliated with it.
To make it more interesting in con¬
tent and more refreshing to the eye,
several changes were made this year
in the editing of the Book. The cover
represents our pupils at the end of
a school session and is symbolic of
the valuable educational part of the
Center plays in the Iacksonville Jew¬
ish life. Inside ‘the book the adver¬
tisements have been attractively boxed,
the write-ups suggestively headlined
and Talmudical maxims are inter¬
spersed throughout its pages. Several
entirely new features have been added.
These changes required a greater
expenditure in the printing of the
book than ever before and were made
possible largely through the splendid
cooperation on the part of the firms
and individuals advertising herein. To
these the staff wishes to express its
sincere thanks. We feel certain that
our members will respond to their
gesture of goodwill by offering them
their patronage and support.
The staff also takes pleasure in
introducing to the Annual readers our
new advertising manager, Mr. David
Gershon, whose long advertising ex¬
perience and relentless e fforT in con¬
tacting the business firms of Jackson¬
ville are in no small measure respon¬
sible for the success of this publi¬
cation.
