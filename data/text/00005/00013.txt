Hadassah s primary aim in Palestine.
The Jewish Youth, pioneering in the
Holy Land, is the backbone of the
Jewish National Home, therefore Ha¬
dassah puts special emphasis in ca¬
tering to the needs ol youth.
In Diaspora, Hadassah endeavors to
convey to its members an understand¬
ing of the vital relation which Zionism
has to world happenings. On the other
hand Hadassah, in consonance with
Jewish ideals, works for the protection
of democracy and ies institutions in
order that together with other Ameri¬
cans we may continue to foster and
safeguard the American way of life.
The most noteworthy achieve¬
ment of Hadassah in the Holy Land
is the establishment of a countrywide
system of hospitals and medical re¬
search centers by which it seeks to
conserve and upbuild 'the health of
the civilian population, Jew, Arab and
Christian alike. To this end, the em¬
ergency program of Hadassah has al¬
ready provided for hundreds of extra
hospital beds, the establishment of a
large hospital fund for the purpose of
giving free hospitalization to indigent
patients, and the initiaton of an ap¬
parently victorious, combat against the
swamps and malaria-bearing mosquito
in the high-incidence malarial districts
of the country.
With the world at war, Hadassah
is mobilizing all its facilities to aid
in the defense o f the Holy Land. A
large corps of volunteers is being train¬
ed for hospital service in event of
emergency, while courses in air raid
precautions and war surgery are held

project. Members of Hadassah also
contributed again to the war need of

BUSINESS AND PROFESSIONAL

An important part of Senior Ha-

ional Women s Division whose pro¬
gram is cultural in nature. The fund
raising activities of this group are
conducted in cooperation with the
Senior Chapter. The B & P have
given valuable assistance to the Senior
chapter and through their effort in

local chapter has become an impor¬

which numbers a membership of
