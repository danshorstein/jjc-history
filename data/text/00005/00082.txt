Another year of successful 'Cubbing has just been completed by Cub
Pack No. 14 of the Jewish Center. Another fine group of Cubs were graduated
into the ranks of the parent Troop No. 14 and another active and enthusiastic
group of recruits have taken their place. I hus the function of Cubbing con¬
tinues as an excellent supporting program to the Boy Scout movement.
An opportunity for adventure and achievement is onen to bovs of nine,
ten and eleven years of age through the fine program of Cubbing. It is a
colorful activity that is quite natural to boys of this age and leads to the gateway
of Scouting where they begin a new and different kind of adventure.
The Cubs were extremely active during the year in passing their advancement
tests. Several very interesting picnics were planned for the boys and thoroughly
enjoyed.
Cubbing gives to the parents the finest opportunity for joint participation
with the child as Mother, Dad and the home are all tightly bound into the
Cubbing program.
Joseph Strauss, active for several years in Center affairs took over the active
leadership of the Pack, succeeding Joe Becker who founded the Pack and served
as its first Cub Master. Mr. Strauss has issued an appeal to all boys of the age
range from nine to eleven to join the Pack and thus secure for themselves the
vast benefits of Cubbing. With the continued support of the Cub Mothers and
Dads, the Pack should have another great year.
