Jacksonville Post No. 199
A valuable addition to the Center-affiliated organizations is the Jewish
War Veterans and its Ladies’ Auxiliary.
Organized three years ago, the Post has been active in promoting patriotism
and the ideals of democracy and has been instrumental in dispersing prejudice
against Jews through a campaign of information acquainting non-Jews with
the part played by the Jewish soldiers in the first and second World War.
Members of the post meet twice a month on the second floor of the Center
rear wing. Throughout the year, members of the post pay good will visits to
local neighboring hospitals and distribute gifts and magazines to their Jewish
and non-Jewish patients. On national holidays, the Post participates in parades
and other patriotic manifestations.
A highlight of the past year was the visit of the National Commander
Harris who met with members and friends of the organization in a local hoteh
and spoke on the work of the National organization.
The Ladies’ Auxiliary of the Jewish War Veterans meets on the same
nights as the post and assists its members in all its undertakings.
