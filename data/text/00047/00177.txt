Jewish Women’s Bowling League
One of the best kept secrets in Jacksonville may be the Jewish Women’s Bowling League that meets on Tuesday
mornings, 9:15, at the Mandarin Bowling Alley.
Officers for 1988-89 are: Wendy Honigman, President; Elaine Mack, Vice-President; Phyllis Raderman, Secretary; Shirley
Schinasi, Treasurer.
At the closing “awards” luncheon, the women enjoyed this poem by Michelle Frisch, with some assistance from her
mother, Eleanor Dorman, that really explains what the league is all about.
