Center Day Camp
Boardwalk
& Baseball
Punk Day
’88
TOGETHER ... we learned, we
played, experienced and we grew.
We traveled to Orlando’s Board¬
walk and Baseball, Wild Waters
and Mayport. We bowled, putt
putted, skated and rode ponies.
We learned to swim, passed Red
Cross beginners, advanced begin¬
ners and a whole lot more. We
swam for 30 minutes raising money
for the pool, $450 for the Swim-a-
thon to be exact.
Trail mix, s’mores, banana cream
pies and blueberry ice cream are
among our specialties. Campouts,
volleyball, free swim, Judaica and
computers were among our
favorites.
We met police officers, firemen,
karate experts, magicians and a
juggler. We even met the construc¬
tion workers and saw their
machines.
Campouts
are fun!
We dressed up like punks and
doctors, cartoon characters and
bandits. Each costume made with
our own creative touch.
TOGETHER WE HAD FUN!
