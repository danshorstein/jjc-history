Cantor Emeritus
Abraham Marion
Center Choral Society
On March 6, Cantor Emeritus Abraham Marton presented his annual Choral Society Concert
featuring guest soloists: Cantor David Lefkowitz of Park Avenue Synagogue in Manhattan, Cantor
Jerome B. Kopmar of Beth Abraham in Dayton, Ohio, and operatic soprano Irene Lumpkin of
Jacksonville. The choir was conducted by Daniel Marmorstein.
Our thanks to Cantor Marton for coordinating these very beautiful programs for our congregation
and community to enjoy.
We are grateful to the following Choral Society members: Celia Bressler, Gertrude Brown,
Raymond Cohen, Pollie Copans, Cynthia Disher, Margaret Fleet, Lois Gosa, Ira Grann, Dr.
Randall Haas, Iris Hill, Ruth Jacobsen, Rosalind Klein, Henry Lasris, Clara Mann, Marilyn
Mass, Charles Mizrahi, Leo Moskovitz, Peggy Organes, Natalie Osofsky, llene Reiter, Abba
Safer, Anita and Steve Solomon, Barbara Spiwak, Cathy Wilensky and the conductor, Judy
Geller.
