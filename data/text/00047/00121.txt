Solomon Schechter P.T.A.
The Solomon Schechter PTA is the link between faculty,
school administration and parents of the Solomon Schechter
Day School and Early Childhood Department of the Jackson¬
ville Jewish Center.
The PTA plays a significant role in supplying much needed
equipment and materials for the Day School and Pre-school
through our successful fund-raising projects.
We are able to achieve our goals through the magazine and
candy sales, two book fairs, T-shirt sale and paper drive. The
group is actively involved in the Hot Lunch Program, sponsors
the live productions by Theatreworks, Book-A-Bration, the
newsletter, “Your Child”, the Shabbat Box program and
holiday observances.
PTA also provides the parents with an opportunity to take
an active part in their child’s education.
With your help and enthusiasm we shall have a very
successful year. I look forward to working with all of you.'
Best wishes for a very happy and healthy new year.
Leslie Held
