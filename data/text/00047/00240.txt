Adult Education
Expansion is taking place . . .
...Jewish mind expansion that is! Adult education has become an integral part of
the ongoing activities of the Jacksonville Jewish Center. During the year new
classes and groups for study have been meeting regularly. Whether it was
attendance at a Hebrew class, the Sisterhood Bar/Bat Mitzvah class, the
Shabbaton weekend, a book review or holiday workshops, our members came
together to grow Jewishly. They thus lent testimony to the fact that men and
women of all ages in our congregation are interested in becoming better
informed Jews.
As we look to the new year in terms of adult education, there is reason to feel
excited. Stimulating speakers for the Shabbaton weekends are going to be
visiting and sharing their expertise with us. Mini-sessions are being planned as
well as the popular Hebrew classes. Most importantly, however, one of the most
basic tenets of Judaism, the importance of learning, is not only being acknow¬
ledged but it is being given a life of its own at the Center.
This year Lillie Ray Levy will be chairing the Adult Education committee. She
would appreciate your help, comments or suggestions. If you would like to serve
on the committee, call Lillie Ray at 260-2534.
