Our constant gratitude, not only for making the publication possible, but also for the
support they lend to our programs. We urge our members to reciprocate with their
patronage.
Facts About the Center Yearbook
• A reference and guide for the Jacksonville Jewish Center membership and the
Jewish community.
• Serves over 7,000 members and advertisers.
• All proceeds devoted to the Center’s program.
The Yearbook is a summary of the various activities and programs of this Synagogue-
Center depicted in pictures and words. We are grateful to the many organizations of
this community and congregants who have cooperated in providing us with material
used.
We appreciate the efforts of our staff and other members of the Center who have
devoted time and effort on behalf of our 1988-89 Center Yearbook.
