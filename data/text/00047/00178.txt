The Sunday School
A ”Hands on” Experience
The children of our Sunday School program learn
about holidays, Bible stories, life cycle events, and
symbols of Judaism. Through song, dance music,
arts and crafts, storytelling, games and a variety of
other activities, children learn about these topics by
experiencing them in creative and innovative ways.
Children also become familiar with the synagogue as
a comfortable, secure, and caring place. In addition,
the values of tzedakah and helping others are
emphasized.
