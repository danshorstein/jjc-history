Hadassah, the largest Zionist organization in the world,
as well as the largest women’s voluntary organization in the
United States, has a membership of 350,000 members who
dedicate themselves to help Israel, a developing
democracy, to help itself.
The Jacksonville Chapter has approximately 900
members and consists of three active groups: TIKVA,
which meets during the day; HANNAH SENESCH, the
evening business and professional women’s group; and
SHIR-LI, the young women’s group which also meets in the
evening.
Hadassah is celebrating its 65th birthday this year, with
a heart-warming list of achievements: a 1,000 bed
Hadassah-Hebrew University Medical Center and the Mt.
Scopus Hospital in East Jerusalem which services people of
all races and creeds. Here teaching and research are
carried on which benefit all mankind. Hadassah’s varied
projects include the Hadassah Medical Organization,
Youth Aliyah, Hadassah Israel Educational Services,
Jewish National Fund, Zionist Youth Activities and
American Affairs. Each of our three groups in Jacksonville
develops its own program of activities to support these
projects, with the Chapter co-ordinating the three groups.
We heartily welcome one and all to join us, to get to
know us and work with us. Please call our Chapter
President, Mrs. Joel Fleet (Margaret) 396-7406 for further
information. Meanwhile, we wish you and your dear ones a
healthy and happy year.
