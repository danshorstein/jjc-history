The Progressive Credit Union was established, and is
operated to serve the financial needs of its members
consisting of men and women of the Jacksonville Jewish
Community.
PROGRESSIVE CREDIT UNION
Officers and Board of Directors
In 1976, we assisted many Jacksonville businessmen
and families with loans for automobiles, appliances,
marriages, and other worthwhile personal needs. Under a
special program designed to aid students with College
tuition, 32 Jewish students were assisted in their higher
education. The Student Loan Program is fully guaranteed
by the U.S. Government.
All members receive free Life Insurance on their
savings, up to $2,000.00. All loans are covered with free
Credit Life Insurance for the full outstanding balances. With
all of our extra services rendered, the ratio charged for
interest is lower than that of other lending institutions in the
area. All Savings are insured up to $40,000.00 by the Florida
Credit Union Guarantee Corporation.
We extend a cordial invitation for all to join with us in
the Progressive Credit Union to build a stronger Jewish
Community in Jacksonville. For further information, please
call Mr. Joe Cohen at 354-4770.
