Troop 14 has gotten off to a good start for the year
1977-78 with the addition of new Scouts who have come up
from the Webelos Den.
A group of our Scouts went to a summer session at the
regional Scout Camp Shands. The session was one of
accomplishment as many merit badges were earned.
Other activities included participation in the Scout
Goodwill Day, Scout World, a city-wide program for Scout
and Cub groups, tubing down the Itchatucknee Springs,
annual Camp Blanding Fall Encampment and
advancements. We are proud that Gary Kupfer reached the
rank of Life Scout.
During the month of September, we hope to send
several of our Scouts to the Order of the Arrow Fellowship.
