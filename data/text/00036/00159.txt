HA’RAYIM
Ha’Rayim is the young couples club of the Jacksonville
Jewish Center. It is one of the most vibrant groups of the
Center. The group consists of couples mostly in the 20 - 40
age group who come together periodically to socialize and
share Jewish experiences.
Our year began with a Selichot Social. We combined a
very social atmosphere with a stimulating Jewish Identity
Program. The evening was very thought provoking and
enjoyable.
The major event of the year for Ha’Rayim was the Poor
Man’s Raffle. It was a big undertaking but worth while. Its
success can best be shown on the pictures on the preceding
pages. Lois and Alan Chepenik were our grand prize
winners.
In the spring we had our annual Hav-a-Nosh. Since
Ha’Rayim consists of couples from all over the United
States and the world, we decided to have an evening
devoted to hometown foods. Everything was served from
New York Egg Cream to Australian Pavlova. For
entertainment we placed Backgammon, cards and Twister.
It has always been our policy to invite newcomers to the
community to all our functions. Our get-togethers are
always informal, the atmosphere is always warm and
hospitable and the people are the friendliest.
Very Special plans have been made for this coming
year; the New Year’s Eve celebration is one of them.
Be part of our marvelous group!
