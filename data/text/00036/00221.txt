SUNDAY FUNDAY
Spectacular ’77
Sunday Funday enjoyed a rousing finale on April 17 with the
Fletcher-Selber Auditorium filled to capacity with
basketball palyers, cheerleaders, majorettes, Karate
experts and parents and friends. From 1:30 p.m. until the
final buzzer at 5:00 p.m., Super Sunday Funday had it all.
Over 120 youngsters participated in the Sunday Funday
program. Special thanks to our instructors Darrell Leake,
Lemon Clay, Linda Thomas, Shelly Kulchin, Jerry Zel and
Nancy Lowery. Adults who assisted were George
Strumlauf, Bob Cantor, Alan Chepenik and Lonnie Margol.
Co-Chairmen of the Athletic Committee are Bennie Margol
and Ron Pollock. Next year’s program, including better and
even more interesting programs for our youth, will start in
the fall.
Second and Third grade champions are: Bart Chepenik,
Michael Biller, Jeffrey Marón, Ken Haft and Paul Hackel;
Fourth, Fifth and Sixth grade Champions are Lane
Strumlauf, Robert Gordon, Alan Biller and Andrew Ruby.
Congratulations to all who participated.
