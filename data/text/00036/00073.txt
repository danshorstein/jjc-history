Each of the festivals of the Jewish calendar, including the
Sabbath, play a primary role in the Center’s curriculum.
Synagogue skills are taught, enabling students to chant
from the Torah, blow Shofar, and lead services in a natural
and involved manner. The Sabbath and Festivals provide
the perfect time to explore the ethic of Jewish ritual and
Torah study in order to develop the moral sensitivities,
character and Jewish loyalties of the young people.
