“The measure of a man is how he serves his fellow man.
Hyman Selber, Chairman of the Year Book Committee for over 40 years has been responsible
for raising over a half million dollars for the Jacksonville Jewish Center.

Year Book which has received national recognition as an outstanding chronicle of our
Synagogue—Center's activities and programs.
The lives of thousands of children, teenagers and adults have been enriched because
dedicated individuals such as Hyman Selber have raised the funds to help make these programs
possible.
With grateful hearts the Board of Directors, on behalf of the entire Center Family, salutes
Hyman Selber, his wife Bertha, and his entire family, for an unmatched record of dedication and
accomplishment.
May Hyman Selber’s example of unselfish service serve as an inspiration to all of us.”
