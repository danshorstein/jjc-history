The Hannah Senesch Evening Group of Hadassah
meets every second Wednesday night, October through
May at the Mt. Carmel Auditorium. We are aware of the
ever present needs in Israel and are a light to the whole
Middle East by aiding with financial assistance, to promote
peace and healing with research, through the Hadassah
Medical Organization.
Our monthly programs are entertaining and
stimulating. Join this dynamic group of Hadassah ladies and
be a part of the Women’s Zionist Organization of America.
