The members of Jacksonville Evening Chapter of ORT
are interested in eliminating poverty and igndrance and in
helping to reestablish refugees. Our schools, providing
vocational training, have helped thousands of people find a
self-sustaining existence.
Our chapter’s activities include our annual Chinese
Auction, our sales of personalized T-shirts, our Food Around
The World dinner and participation in a raffle. These and
other activities are designed to further the goals of Women’s
American ORT and provide personal involvement for our
members.
The Evening Chapter has also sponsored three field
trips for local students to view the vocational opportunities
available in our schools here. Being community minded we
have participated in the city Ethnic Food Festival and in
supplying scholarships to students in the Southside Skills
Center Vocational School.
Our chapter’s meeting is held on the first Tuesday of the
month. Anyone interested in joining should call Sue Ann
Blumenthal 737-2610 or anyone interested in finding out
more about ORT should call for more information.
The Evening Chapter has enjoyed a great sense of
growth and success in the past several years. Stimulating,
informative programs, cohesive working by our members
and successful projects have made our chapter life viable and
rewarding.
