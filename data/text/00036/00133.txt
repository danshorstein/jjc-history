In the High School, teachers emphasize student
questions and concerns, exploring together the issues
which the young people consider relevant to their lives and
times. Classes include such divergent topics as Who is a
Zionist?, Cycle of the Jewish Year, and The Bible for Today.
In addition, Russian Jewry in Crisis, Modern Jewish
Philosophy, Comparative Religion, are scheduled.
The staff views its role as providing the teenagers with
an opportunity for encountering greatness in the Jewish
past, for finding personal meaning in Jewish practices, and
for sharing the intellectual insights of the tradition.
