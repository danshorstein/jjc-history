Jewish Family and Children’s Services is the two
person case work agency of the Jewish community. Its
goals are to help individuals and families lead happier and
healthier lives approaching their potentials and to
strengthen and/or restore family functioning and stability.
JFCS offers a comprehensive counseling program
which includes individual, marital, parent-child, and family
counseling. The agency gratefully cooperates with the
Jacksonville Jewish Center to provide a limited number of
scholarships for Day Camp and provides college loans and
scholarships based on need to young people served by the
agency. JFCS extends specialized services to the elderly,
as well as assisting in applications to River Garden. JFCS
also reaches out to patients at the state hospital at
Macclenny and Jewish inmates at Raiford through monthly
visits and holiday observances. In addition, JFCS has
assisted in the resettlement of eight Russian Jewish families
in Jacksonville.
To better provide these many services and others, we
need the active participation of volunteers and membership
support. We need volunteers to teach English and friends
to help and converse with our newly arrived Russian
families, friends to visit with isolated, aged or young
persons, volunteers to visit the prison and state hospital and
volunteers to take a client to the doctor or shopping. Let us
know if you can help.
