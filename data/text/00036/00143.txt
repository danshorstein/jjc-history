FABULOUS FOLLIES REMEMBERED
December 11, 1976
“A most extraordinary evening”... “The best time I have ever had at a social event at
the Center” . . .
If you were at the Fabulous Follies you experienced it first hand, but if not, then you
missed one of the finest social happenings of the year ... if not in many years.
It all came together ... food, drinks, entertainment, atmosphere ... thanks to Suzi
Roey and her committee — Shelly G ast, Shirley Bielski, Rene Gordon and Jack Silverman. It is
hard to imagine how Suzi manages to be so successful in planning and implementing so many
rewarding and fulfilling projects. All of us are grateful for her tireless and unselfish efforts for the
benefit of the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
We are all looking forward to the next time!
“FOLLIES CHORUS UNE”
Howard Roey, Arnold Frankel, Lew Meisel, Abe Bielski, Larry Resnick, Alan Gordon, Jerry Zel, Bob Cantor and
Charles Moskovitz.
