REMEMBER THE JEWISH COMMUNITY
IN THE NEW YEAR
Throughout the year, the organized Jewish
Community helps immigrants, senior citizens, youth,
in fact all of us, both here and in Israel. Your
support, working through the Jacksonville Jewish
Community Council provides services in education,
civil rights, culture, public information and welfare
and other fields. So as the New Year begins,
remember the ways in which we work and act
together to help all.
