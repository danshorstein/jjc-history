Boys between the ages of eight and ten find many and
challenging experiences in the Cub Scout program. As they
advance from Wolf to Bear and then on to Weblos, they
look forward to the time that they will be ready for the great
out-of-doors offered in the scouting program of Troop 14.
The Cubs in each Den become a close unit as they
work together to develop new hobbies, take field trips, work
on craft projects, skits, and explore nature.
