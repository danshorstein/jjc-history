OUR NEW CENTER
Are we continuing the chain of tradition as expressed by the students of the Solomon Schechter Day School?
Are we as high as the Tower of Babel without true worship in Beth El?
Today is the creation of the world - we must increase our understanding of His word.
Still we try to succeed over our hardship, as you saved Jonah without ship.
We bow, we pray and hope, our wish to be true, as the loving soul of our mother Ruth.
We pray to the Lord to help us and to help others,
By increasing our gifts, donations and contributions from our brothers.
We need your pledge to wipe out our debt
To walk our children on smooth floors.
How nice to see beautiful flowers without climbing the tree
That revives the heart and the sweet honey of the bee.
Let us keep on beating the music drum and harp of King Solomon
To lift our head and spirit, as a shining diamond to all.
We need your help and support
As you enter the door to the Lord’s House.
I will look for your improvement more every year
So our life will grow continually without fear.
By Isaac Kabbani
