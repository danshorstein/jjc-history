U.S.Y. INSTALLS OFFICERS FOR NEW YEAR
Over 75 U.S.Y. members and guests attended the annual
Installation Banquet and Dance at the Jewish Center. Installed as
President of the United Synagogue Youth was Jay Plotkin, son of Mr.
and Mrs. Herbert Plotkin. Jay has been an active U.S.Y.’er and now
assumes the responsibility of leading the teenage group. In his
acceptance speech he promised “to bring back the true ideals of
U.S.Y.”.
Other officers include: Vice-Presidents: Programming, Terri
Katz; Religious, Scott Greenfield; Fund Raising, Allison Levy;
Corresponding Secretary, Frances Siegel; Recording Secretary,
Melinda Zisser; Treasurer, Lawrence Ansbacher; Parliamentarian,
Greg Kupperman; Member-at-Large, Randy Horwitz; Ninth grade
representatives, Barry Ansbacher and Cathy Levy. Bruce Horvitz
was the installing officer.
Selected as the recipients of the outstanding U.S.Y. Boy and
U.S.Y. Girl for 1976-77 were Roland Mizrahi and Marie Saraga.
Robert and Barbara Cantor are advisors to the organization.
Many outstanding programs and activities are being planned for Jewish teenagers in the 9th - 12th grades.
The entire Center family congratulates our U.S.Y.’ers for their achievements.
