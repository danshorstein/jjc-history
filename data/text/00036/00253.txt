The Jewish Women’s Coffee Bowling League meets
every Tuesday at a local bowling alley, it is a time when the
ladies enjoy bowling and seeing one another. There are 40
women making up ten teams. At the end of the year there is
a final party when the money prizes are awarded to the
members. Everyone gets a prize. It is good exercise and a
lot of fun! If you are interested, please call our secretary.
