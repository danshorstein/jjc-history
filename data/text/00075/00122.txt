A MESSAGE FROM THE DIRECTOR
Our Religious School enjoyed yet another
amazing year! Classes are growing, and we
concentrated this year on improving our Hebrew
program. We made wonderful additions this past
year to our Hebraic’s staff, resulting in more Hebrew
spoken in the classroom and fresh new ideas in our
curriculum. We expanded the use of our Online
Learning Center, which allows students to practice
reading and prayer skills at home, and we added more
Hebrew class time on Sundays.
Bernard & Alice
SELEVAN
RELIGIOUS SCHOOL
Our program includes Kindergarten and 1st grade Sunday School classes,
where loving, dedicated teachers introduce our youngest students to the Torah,
Hebrew, and holidays; the extremely successful “Judaism Through the Arts” for
grades 2-5, using a variety of crafts, literature, technology and music to nourish
a love of Jewish culture; and the always popular Project Etgar for our 6th and
7th grade students, which makes Jewish learning relevant and challenging
to middle-schoolers regardless of their level of formal Jewish education and
observance. Inclusive family programs, allowing for parent involvement and
instruction, round out this exciting new concept in Jewish education.
Lois Tompkins
