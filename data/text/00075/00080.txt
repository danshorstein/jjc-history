Special Events
PURIM
Purim is always a special time at the Jacksonville Jewish
Center. There were a variety of opportunities for the congregation
and the community to celebrate the festive holiday. On March
23rd the holiday began with congregants coming together to
hear a traditional megillah reading. On March 24th, the Shushan
Idol Grand Finale Dinner and Show took place with an open bar,
a wonderful dinner and a synagogue wide talent competition.
Contestants submitted videos of their talents for an online vote. The
finalist performed in front of a live audience of about 120 people.
The finalists were Lisa Hunt, Abigail Fixel, Hannah Silverstein,
Arin Nathans and winner Elior Lubliner. The PTAPurim carnival,
on March 20th, drew hundreds of people from all over the Jewish
community to enjoy an afternoon of games, rides, food, costumes,
puppet shows and fun.
