SUMMER PROGRAMS FOR JEWISH YOUTH
The Jacksonville Jewish Center is fortunate to have a large representation of youth participating in summer
programs. These programs are sponsored through the Conservative Movement and each one offers a great summer
opportunity for informal enrichment and meaningful education.
The Jacksonville Jewish Center sent 36 children to
camp this past summer. There are many opportunities
to experience Camp Ramah Darom during the
summer. In addition to a four or eight week session
Camp Ramah Darom offers:
TASTE OF RAMAH
• An introductory program for children entering 3rd
or 4th grade to expose them to the magic of Camp
Ramah Darom.
