SHABBAT SHIRA
A weekend of Song • January 22-24
This year, the Jacksonville Jewish
Center invited singer, educator and rock-
star Naomi Less to our community.
She created tailor-made training and
educational experiences for us. A founding
company member of Storahtelling and a
Jewish feminist, Less brought her musical
and educational talents to classroom,
congregation and concert over the course
of this inspirational weekend.
On Friday she ran social justice
workshops for our Martin J. Gottlieb Day
School and ran an interactive concert that
afternoon as well as for our Bernard and
Alice Selevan Religious School on the
Sunday morning of the shabbaton.
Naomi, along with her guitarist Ziv
Shalev, gave an amazing concert on
Saturday in conjunction with our Center
Sisterhood, as we honored two special
women in our community, Lara Werwa
and Edie Jordan at our annual Torah Fund
event. Bringing Naomi in on this weekend,
in which we celebrated Miriam’s role in
leading the Jewish people, as well as these
two strong female role models, was truly a
delight for all.
