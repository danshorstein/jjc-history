Yet, with all of her considerable

her talents and skills with her

of our Sefer Torah’s mantles are

the Jacksonville Jewish Center
Yearbook.
For over 75 years, Blanche Slott
devoted herself to our congregation
as a loyal member, valued volunteer,
respected professional, mentor and
friend. There are not enough words
to thank Blanche for all she did to
help grow the Jacksonville Jewish
Center into the synagogue we are
today. Her legacy will continue
for generations to come. May her
memory be for a blessing.
