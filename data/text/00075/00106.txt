L’DOR v’dor annual giving campaign
The Galinsky Academy of the Jacksonville Jewish Center
is dedicated to high educational standards and traditional
Jewish values. The schools and programs of the Academy’s
DuBow Preschool, Martin J. Gottlieb Day School, Bernard &
Alice Selevan Religious School, and Setzer Youth Education
strive to create an educational environment where children
and teens love being Jewish.
L’dor V’dor
The Galinsky Academy’s annual giving campaign, L’Dor
V’Dor, brings exceptional opportunities to the participants of
the four schools and programs thanks to the support of our many donors. Through a campaign
process of communication throughout the academic year, the entire community is given the
opportunity to be involved in the L’Dor V’Dor campaign and support the Academy’s mission
and vision.
