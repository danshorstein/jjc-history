COME TO THE TREEHOUSE
Parent & Me Classes - Ages 2 months - 4 years
With five classes- one each day of the week- we have something for every stage of baby
and toddlerhood! In Color Splash use your 5 senses to explore the world of colors. Family
Music and Movement is a perfect first class for baby as we learn about rhythm, movement,
and the world of music. Baby Sign-A-Long is an innovative program that helps parents
understand their baby’s needs and wants using sign language. Stroller Workout combines
strength training and power walking- and exercise is always more fun with friends! At
Shalom Baby playgroup, come celebrate Jewish holidays, play, chat, meet other moms,
and socialize your baby. We look forward to meeting you and your little ones - see you at
the Treehouse! Learn more on our website: http://treehouse.dubowpreschool.org
