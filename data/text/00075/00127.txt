HEBREW HIGH SCHOOL 
Hebrew High continued with its momentum from last year and brought it to new places. In different units over the course of the year, we challenged our teens to think about Jewish identity and practice and its influence on their daily lives. Together with Rabbi Tilman and other guests over the course of the year, we learned what our tradition has to teach about Gemilut Hasidim, learned about and experienced some new lifecycle rituals, discussed how Jewish culture is expressed in TV and music, and experienced ways in which Judaism informs our political beliefs and outlooks. Over the year, students learned from a variety of events, occasions and fun activities. Together, students learned that our Jewish texts and values have a lot to teach us even today, and that learning about them can be interesting and fun!
