CUBBING
Pac k 14 has completed its first year of cubbing with a membership of
19 boys. It is supervised by the Jacksonville Jewish Center and Rabbi Morris
D. Margolis is its spiritual leader. They are chartered by the Boy Scouts of
America.
Jt is a home centered institution and is divided into three dens with a plan¬
ned outline of work and play, supervised by the den mother and the den chief.
The packmaster with the assistance of the Boy Scouts of America plan
every activity of the pack.
Great strides have been made by the boys in leadership, companionship,
and sportsmanship.
Religion is also playing an active part in their work and play.
