Harry Finkelstein, first president of the Jackson¬

business man, his position of esteem and honor among
Jacksonville citizenry, and his tenacity and persistence
in pursuing the cause of the Jewish Center, Were
weighty factors leading toward success.
We who reap the fruit of his labor, are happy
