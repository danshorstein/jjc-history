During the past year under the able leadership of Leo Michelson, Troop
14 h as shown remarkable progress and promises to be one of the outstanding
troops in the city.
I be troop distinguished itself by winning the district merit badge banner
for nine months in succession. It also placed 2nd in the district inspection
and 5th in the city-wide inspection.
The troop continued to excel in athletic competition, tying for first place
in the basket ball league but losing in the play offs; taking 3rd place in touch
football and finishing in 2nd place in diamond ball.
To mark the conclusion of this most successful year a banquet was held
in the Center auditorium, May 31st, at which time many interesting demon¬
strations were presented by the members of the troop. Chief Executive M.
G. B oswell of the local Boy Scout Counci I, Cyril Marks, District Commissioner
and others gave talks commending the troop for its fine showing. Awards
were presented to several outstanding members of the troop.
The scouts sponsored their own Friday night service and were ushers
at several more.
The troop was also particularly active fast year in having engaged in
a number of hikes.
