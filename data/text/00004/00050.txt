Mr. B en Stein has been elected president ol the group, and is assisted

The Jewish Zionist Progressive Society, which has been the local Zionist
affiliate for the past few years, reliquished its charter most graciously to the
new organization, and all paid up members of that society became charter
members of the new organization.
The local District has signified its intentions of beginning a membership
drive during the high holidays in the expectation of more than doubling its
quota during the coming year. It also expects to become a member of the
Regional organization, to be formed soon at a meeting to be held in Atlanta.
