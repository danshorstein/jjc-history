Fcr the past seven years the F. T. E.
Club, although consisting of only a limited
membership, has taken an active part both
in the social and charitable activities of
the Jewish community.
During its existence the organization has
aided the Center in various ways and each
year participates in the Center Mother’s
Day services, at which time each mother
is presented with a rose by the organization.
The club for the past seven years has
sponsored the annual Georgia-Florida Foot¬
ball dance, which affair has become one
cf the outstanding social events of the
year. During the past year, the F. T. E.’s
graciously collaborated with the local
A. Z. A. chapter in staging the dance, for
the purpose of aiding them in staging
their regional convention. The Club also col¬
laborated with the A. Z. A.’s in the
latter’s Auburn-Florida football dance. A
considerable portion of the proceeds from
these affairs is donated to charity.
