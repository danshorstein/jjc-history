The Jacksonville Jewish Center Early Childhood Department
has enjoyed a reputation as one of the best in Jacksonville for many
years. Our dedicated staff is extremely proud of the excellent
educational programs we provide our students. Our environment is
structured to provide students the opportunity to grow socially,
emotionally, physically, and creatively as well as intellectually.
and
Our Pre-School also offers group instructions that enhance our
daily schedules. Our Judaic program includes education in the
fundamentals of Jewish life and Torah with emphasis on weekly Shabbat
holiday celebrations. Our Physical Education and Music classes are favorites of every age group.
This year promises to be an exciting adventure for everyone involved. We look forward to adding
innovative new programs for our children, parents and staff.
We have many things to be proud of and we would love an opportunity to share them with you. You
are welcome to come and tour our Pre-School anytime.
