Annual Congregation Meeting and Special Awards
Newly elected president,Mark Shorstein (r)
presented a gift to Evan Yegelwel, as a token of
the congregations gratitude for his efforts as
symnagogue president for the past two years.
The annual congregation meeting gives members a glimpse of
the business conducted monthly by the Center’s officers and board of
directors. With the ceremony befitting the occasion, this meeting began
with an invocation from Rabbi Gaffney, and the singing of Hatikvah
lead by Cantor Heller. President Evan Yegelwel delivered a “state of
the synagogue” message and chairmen of standing committees gave
reports on the accomplishments made throughout the year. In election
years, like this one, the slate of new officer is presented. It was a
festive evening, complete with dessert reception. This year’s meeting
was made even more special by the honors bestowed on four very
deserving people.

facts in her head, Inez can

known for her caring and

not only our children, but

occasion, unselfishly
giving of her time, energy
