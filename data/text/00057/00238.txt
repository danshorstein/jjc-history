Sunday School
The SUNDAY SCHOOL of the Jacksonville Jewish Center is
planned expressly for children in kindergarten, first and second grades. A
weekly, two-hour program gives each child the opportunity to experience
the richness of his/her Jewish heritage through song, dance, role playing,
cooking, short stories, games, arts and crafts. Emphasis is placed on the
holidays and festivals. Jewish values and mitzvot are encouraged. Many
thanks to our wonderful Sunday School teachers Susan Kadish, Sharona
Kadish and Susan Neadle.

For ten years, FAMILY COUNCIL has
provided innovative Family Education programs
involving all facets of the congregation. Several
large scale projects like Rabbi Dov Kentof’s
Fantasy Voyage on Noah’s Ark to the Zoo, Sukkah
Decorating and Hanukkah Activity Day inspire the
entire congregation including the Center
Preschool, Solomon Schechter Day School,
Afternoon Hebrew School and Sunday School.
Synagogue professionals, volunteer experts,
parents and children provide valuable input and
assistance and are rewarded by the experience.
