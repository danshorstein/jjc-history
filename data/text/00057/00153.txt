Day School/PreSchool PTA
“The very world rests on the breath of children in the schoolhouse.” —Talmud
It is with this in mind that our parents, teachers and administrators work together to
insure an enriched education for our children. For 20 years, PTA’s successful magazine sale
has played a significant role in funding excellent programming, equipment and materials. This
past year PTA provided the new preschool playground equipment, the computerization of the
Henry Kramer Library and Resource Center, and music education materials.
Some of the many services and activities sponsored for our students include:
• Shabbat Boxes - taken home each Friday by one student in every class • Kaleidoscope - an
entire day devoted to the arts • Red Ribbon Week - drug awareness program • Vision Screening
• Library Assistants - parents volunteer to help students and librarian • Holiday Observances
- help with students’ celebration of Rosh Hashanah, Chanukah, Tu B’Shevat, Purim, and
Passover • Picture Lady - art appreciation for Pre-K, Kindergarten, and First graders • Mystery
Reader - a new storytelling program for Pre-K and Kindergarten • Much, much more.
Thank you for supporting your PTA through your numerous volunteer hours and financial
assistance. Looking forward to working with you in 98-99!
— Arlene Yegelwel
