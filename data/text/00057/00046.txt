Sunday Sports Basketball Program
The Sunday Sports Association (SSA) provides area
youth with alternative athletic programs on Sunday
afternoons. All the games and practices are held in the
Fletcher-Selber Auditorium of the Jacksonville Jewish
Center. This year over 195 children, 40 volunteer coaches
and volunteer referees played hard, developed skills and
practiced sportsmanship. Thank you to Gayle Bailys who
coordinated a very successful program and the volunteer
coaches for their valuable assistance and time. We look
forward to an exciting 1998-99 season.
