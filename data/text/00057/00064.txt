Salute to Israel Parade in New York - Starring Our Sixth Grade
(Reprinted from the Center Bulletin)
Dear liana,
The 34th Annual “Salute to Israel Parade”, which
this year celebrated the 50th Birthday of the State of Israel,
was the most exciting parade ever. With over 75,000
marchers and 3,000 spectators on each of the 25 blocks
of the parade route, the spirit of unity and pride was
overwhelming.
Our wonderful marching groups will be receiving
an official thank you and parade certificate in the weeks
to come. However, we could not let a week pass without
thanking the groups which made the success even sweeter,
namely our families who had the ultimate commute
packing up enthusiasm and joy, and most importantly, the
youth of their communities and flying into New York to
celebrate with us.
To have the students of Solomon Schechter of
Jacksonville, Florida carry their sunny message to the
people of New York, was a great honor for us, and
hopefully a great honor for your children as well.
There were many positive comments regarding the
level of dedication on the part of your school’s
leadership to encourage and contribute to this extra
special day.
We thank you from the bottom of our hearts.
TOD AH RABAH V’KOL HAKAVOD!
Best regards,
Qlwda JCaupnan
Creative Educational Coordinator and the entire Parade Staff
