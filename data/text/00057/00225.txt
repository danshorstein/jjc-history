Center Youth Groups
Chalutzim - Grades 4 and 5
Research has proven that the survival of Judaism depends on the exposure of our youth to
Jewish activities, friends and experiences that commit them to perpetuating that feeling of
Jewishness and Jewish life.
The Jacksonville Jewish Center sponsors three outstanding youth groups for youngsters in
grades 4-12. Each group meets regularly and enjoys programs of a religious, cultural and social
nature.
Through active participation in these groups, a sense of pride and an identification with
Judaism are instilled. Jewish values, attitude and behavior patterns are discussed and carried out
through Mitzvah programs and study.
Conventions, regional activities and camps give our youth the opportunity to meet other
Jewish youth from all over the United States.
The youth programs are coordinated by Rabbi Dov Kentof, and are supervised
by the Youth Commission. All groups are affiliated with the Southeast Region and
United Synagogue of America.
