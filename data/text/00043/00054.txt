Center Honors Rabbi and Mrs. Gaffney
It was an evening to remember for Rabbi and Mrs. Gaffney and the congregation. An elegant dinner/dance
and testimonial was coordinated by Ginette and Charles Mizrahi. The guest speaker was Rabbi Mordecai
Waxman, one of the most noted rabbis on the American-Jewish scene. The congregation presented the
Gaffneys with a beautiful gift of remembrance and appreciation — a Lalique bowl.
