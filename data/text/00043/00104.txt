Curriculum
JUDAIC . . A full program of Judaic-Hebraic learning
experiences are integrated into the daily schedule.
Students gain significant insights into the teachings of
Judaism and develop appreciation of the ideas and
values expressed in Jewish literature, music and art.
SECULAR . . . The child is the center of the
educational process, therefore our programs are
designed to provide maximum growth of each
individual. Emphasis is on the mastery of basic
language, mathematical skills and the development of
concepts in science and social studies.
