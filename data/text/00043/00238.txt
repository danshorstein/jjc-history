JEWISH FAMILY AND COMMUNITY SERVICES SERVES PEOPLE JUST LIKE YOU!
Over the course of the past year, we’ve undergone a number of changes.
First, by now you’ve noticed that we changed our name and logo. Second, our long time executive director
retired. Most important of all, we’ve added, expanded and reassessed our services to the community.
Special new programs were instituted such as: developmental activities by our social worker with YOUR
pre-schoolers, a college assistance program (CAPS) to help get YOUR children through choosing and paying
for college, adoption information and referral packets, home health care packets, enhanced Jewish Family
Life Education programs and more.
We still perform all our traditional services from counseling to short-term financial assistance and
everything in-between.
We’re growing every day and our main goal is to serve you.
Your agency stands ready to meet your needs.
