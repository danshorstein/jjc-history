Grandparent’s Shabbat
Warmth and love for grandparents were the main ingredients for the first Grandparent’s Shabbat, which
was held on December 3, 1983, as part of a Hanukkah celebration.
A musical program, candlelighting and gifts for grandparents highlighted this very special evening
coordinated by Rabbi Dov Kentof.
