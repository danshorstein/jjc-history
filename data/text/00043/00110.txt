SUNDAY SCHOOL
The Sunday School of the Jacksonville Jewish Center is planned expressly for children in Kindergarten, First
and Second Grades. A two hour program gives each child the opportunity to experience the richness of
his/her Jewish heritage through song, dance, role playing, cooking, short stories, games, arts and crafts.
Emphasis is placed on the holidays and festivals; Jewish values and mitzvot are encouraged.
