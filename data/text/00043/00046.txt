Sisterhood
Sisterhood has played a significant role as a “basic training
area” for preparing women for broader roles in the synagogue
as well as the community at large.
A prime example is our own Marsha Pollock, who is a past
president of Sisterhood and is now serving as president of
Florida Branch of Women’s League for Conservative
Judaism.

Sisterhood Bat Mitzvah Class
The Bar/Bat Mitzvah Class is designed for men and women
who want to engage in an intensive study of the prayerbook,
the Shabbat service, Jewish history, holidays and Jewish
philosophy and thought. The course extends over a two-year
period of time. -
