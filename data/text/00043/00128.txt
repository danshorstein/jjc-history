Center Day Camp
Day Camping can be one of the most meaningful experiences a child will ever have. Our camp offers not
only a situation in which children can learn and develop by interacting with other children, but it offers a full
scope of organized activities designed to make a child aware of our Jewish heritage; to develop new
friendships; enjoy the wonders of nature that may be found on our large wooded campsite; and the
importance of active sports.
This year the Center summer camp programs offered a full- or half-day program for campers ages 2-8; and
our specialty camps included a fine arts and computer camp for older campers.
