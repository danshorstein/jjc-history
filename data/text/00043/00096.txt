St. Vincent’s Family Medical Care Centers are staffed by a full¬
time physician. We operate seven days a week, 365 days a year,
from 9 a.m. to 10 p.m. After hours, St. Vincent’s offers on-call
physicians telephone coverage for established patients. Walk
right in or call for an appointment and see how quickly and ef¬
ficiently we take care of you.
St. Vincent’s Family Medical Care Centers are modem, profes¬
sionally staffed and fully equipped health care centers. On-site X-
rays, lab procedures and tests are available as needed.
You will be pleasantly surprised to find that our services cost
significantly less than comparable treatment at a hospital
emergency room. Our goal is to bring competent medical care to
your family conveniently and economically.

Regency Square, Ponte Vedra, Glynlea, Lakewood and Orange
Park. In 1984, centers will be open in Downtown, Baymeadows
and the Roosevelt Mall area. You’re invited to stop in anytime to
visit. We’ll be happy to show you our modem facilities and ex¬
