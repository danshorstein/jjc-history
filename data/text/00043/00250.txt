Mr. and Mrs. Club
The Mr. & Mrs. Club, a Jewish Social Club of married couples, ages 30 years and up, has been in existence for 30
years, dances are held every third Saturday night of the month to live band music at the Jacksonville Jewish Center. It
offers socializing in a friendly atmosphere over food and drinks and entertainment is by local or professional talent. This
is climaxed by a giant, gala New Years affair.
Won’t you join us by becoming a member? Call Vice President Carl and Rose Goldberg at 737-8895.
