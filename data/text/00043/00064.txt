In Memory
BYRON SELBER
When man departs from this world,
Neither silver nor gold nor precious
stones accompany him;
He is remembered for his love of Torah
and his good deeds.
He who has acquired a good name
Has enriched himself.
Even a long life ends too soon,
But a good name endures forever.
Happy is the man who has acquired a good name,
And retains it when he departs this world.
