United States of Magen David Adorn
in Israel (MDA). As the nation grows,
as the population expands, as border
problems and other hostilities
continue . . . the demands on the
resources of Magen David Adorn
increase. We are resolved to maintain
the lifesaving services of Magen
David Adorn—with your help!
Founded June 7, 1930, Magen David
Adorn (MDA) was designated Israel’s
official Red Cross service in 1950 by
an act of the Knesset. As the only
emergency medical, blood,
ambulance and disaster service in
Israel, MDA is totally involved in
every aspect of health maintenance
and the preservation of human life.

increasing needs with greater

MDA BLOOD SERVICES
Magen David Adom’s chief concern
is blood—collecting it, processing it,
storing it and dispensing it to all of
Israel’s medical institutions. MDA
provides 100% of the armed forces
needs and in excess of 85% of the
civilian requirements of this precious
commodity. Headquarters for these
blood services is the Colonel David
Marcus Memorial Institute in Jaffa—
Israel’s largest blood bank and only
blood fractionation plant, which
processes through modern
technology whole blood as well as its
life-giving components: plasma,
gamma globulin, red and white cells,
albumin and fibrinogen. The new
MDA National Blood Service Center
will soon be built in Ramat Gan . . .
AND YOU CAN HELP!
MDA AMBULANCES
Magen David Adorn provides 24-hour
emergency ambulance and coronary
rescue service for all hospitals in
Israel. The fleet of fully-equipped
ambulances is manned by highly
trained doctors and paramedics, who
administer instant emergency
treatment and thus save precious
time needed when life or death
hinges on a few short minutes . . .
