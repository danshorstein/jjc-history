The Zionist Organization of America has had another
successful year; and as has been our custom for over thirty
years, we have held an annual Jewish National Fund function to
plant a grove of one thousand trees in Israel. However, in three
out of the last four years we have succeeded in planting two
groves. This years’ honorees were Max and Rose Hochman. In
addition, we co-sponsored the Maccabiah program with Beth
Shalom. This year, our most important function, however, is
our support of our national organization which has been very
effective through the years and important in mobilizing support
for Israel in our government.

On Sunday, March 18th, the Zionist
Organization of America, Jacksonville
District, sponsored a J.N.F. Banquet in
memory of Max and Rose Hochman.
Their children, Frieda Saraga and Ralph
Hochman, planted a woodland in Israel
in memory of their beloved parents. The
guest speaker as Arie Shacham, Colonel
in the Israeli Army. Participating in the
Program were Jack Coleman, Elliott
Palevski of River Garden, Dr. Joseph
Honigman and Jack Becker.
