FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
JACKSONVILLE LINKED TO PALM BEACH
For the first time in Jacksonville there will now be a direct line to the Israel Bond Office in Palm
Beach. This new line will link the people of Jacksonville with the office in Palm Beach to
facilitate greater service to the dedicated leaders in the Jacksonville Israel Bond Organization.
The great advantage will be to answer all questions directly concerning Bond transfers,
redemption and discrepancies in accounts. For campaign problems Bond buyers will be able
to speak with Bert Sales, Florida State Director or Allan Kreitman, his associate.
The new phone number is 356-3012 and the caller will be connected directly with the Palm
Beach Office.
Another step in furthering of service to Jacksonville will be via the Jewish Federation and
local synagogues. They will now have a supply of Israel Bond applications and return
envelopes so that anyone in Jacksonville may purchase an Israel Bond when there is no active
Israel Bond campaign going on at the time.
National Israel Bonds has recognized the leadership role Jacksonville has had in the sale of
Israel Bonds. They feel this new link between the cities of Jacksonville and Palm Beach will
lead to further growth of the Jacksonville Israel Bond Campaign.
