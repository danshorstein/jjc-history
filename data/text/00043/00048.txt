Mrs. Shirley Bielski, Past President of the Solomon Schechter Day
School PTA, is shown presenting an eight pound chocolate bar to the
Second Grade Class. The candy bar was first prize for selling $485.00
Barbara Safer worth of chocolate during the PTA sale.
Overall, the entire sale total for the school surpassed all goals. The
student body sold $2,240.00 worth of candy during a two week period
of sales. Of this amount, the PTA retains 50% for funding projects for
the Solomon Schechter students, PTA, and school during this event.
The Solomon Schechter PTA provides a connection between parents and the school administration and faculty. This
year our monthly meetings will be held on the third Thursday of each month.
In addition, profits realized from our fund-raising projects - such as: magazine sale, Book Fair, candy sale, will be used
to provide equipment and materials to enhance the program of the Solomon Schechter Day School. PTA also actively
serves the school: hot lunches, eye screening, art appreciation, holiday observances and library are a few of the ways.
As president of the P.T.A., I hope that all parents will join and assist in our efforts to improve the quality of the
education and communication among all involved.
Barbara Safer
