SHALOM:
Before we begin a New Year of “generating
positives” let us stop to thank those wonderful
women upon whose accomplishments we will
build. Sisterhood donors enabled us to
dedicate the synagogue kitchen in their honor.
Card Party participants purchased new tables
for all of us to use. Torah Fund contributors
help the Jewish Theological Seminary Library
and Mathilde Schechter Residence Hall. Art
Auction proceeds help support our many
Youth activities and holiday celebrations.
Besides filling our bodies with delicious food; our minds with
music; stimulating programs; and book reviews; we enrich our
souls with D’vrai Torah, Sisterhood Shabbat and a Rosh Chodesh
service.
New members, old members, Soviet Jewry, Social Action,
hospital visits. . .our Chai Lites keeps us informed about what’s
happening as well as what the busy fund raising ladies have
available. Not only does our lovely Gift Shop bring in needed
revenue, it enriches our homes and lives with Judaica.
We have a 59 year history of service to the Center and the
community. Wht about the coming year? Are you going to help
“generate positives?” Are you going to sit on the sidelines, feel like
an outsider, or will you join in making Sisterhood a warm place
where all are welcome?
As we reach out and work together we will be a true
SISTERHOOD - a network of support - Jewish women doing
Jewish things and we will be positively blessed.
Joseph, my husband, the president, joins me in wishing that each
of you and your loved ones be inscribed for a good year.
