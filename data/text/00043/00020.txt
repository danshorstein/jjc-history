Message from the Director
As we enter a New Year we have the opportunity to re-evaiuate and determine what
direction we would like to follow in the future. One question to ask — What can my
membership in the Jacksonville Jewish Center do for me? The answer — Nothing, if
you do not avail yourself of the opportunity to participate in the many diversified
activities that take place at the Center during the course of a year.
Many members are just included in the membership list in this Yearbook. Yes, you
pay your dues and you are counted, but we would like to see you face-to-face. We
would like to see you at the daily minyan, where congenial groups of men meet twice
daily. We would like to see you at Shabbat Services — a perfect place to relax in prayer,
to learn, to share and enjoy the Kiddush with old friends and meet new ones.
We would like for you to make a commitment that this year will be different. You will
serve on a committee; you will attend the Art Auction, Mazel Mania and the raffle;
participate in a Shabbaton Weekend and attend some of the many other social and
educational events that are planned; and certainly you will come to Shabbat services.
Blanche Slott
Being involved is a way of communicating, a way of sharing your talents and
receiving some benefits in exchange.
Our staff is always available to assist you at any time and all join me with
warm greetings and good wishes for a year of peace, well-being and prosperity.
Blanche Slott
