White Water Rafting
Wonderful! Terrific! Fantastic!
There are no other words to
describe the five day Kadima
Camping Trip to North
Carolina. A group consisting of
ten - thirteen year olds, and
four inexperienced adults were
led by Rabbi Kentof into the
wilds of North Carolina. This
ambitious dream turned into a
reality due to the love and
devotion that Rabbi Kentof has
for the youth of our
congregation.
While in North Carolina
many of us experienced times
that we never had before —
from services under the stars,
cooking over an open fire,
sleeping in tents, and rafting
down white water rapids.
The real challenge of this trip
was how such a close group
could remain close without
bathing for five days. However,
everyone worked together and
depended on one another to
make this one of the most
memorable trips of our lives.
As a member of the Board,
and as Youth Commission
Chairman, but most of all as a
parent, I would like to express
my thanks to Rabbi Kentof for
making such a trip possible.
Our congregation is very
fortunate to have a man of this
calibre leading our youth
programs. The benefits of
these experiences will be
everlasting.
I would like to personally
thank the entire group for
giving me the opportunity to
attend; and for bringing me
back alive!
Sincerely,
Robert Cantor
