Hadassah
Hadassah, the Women’s Zionist Organization of America, consists of over 370,000 dedicated members who work
patiently and vigorously towards bold achievements rooted in idealism and ideology.
The impact of Hadassah on contemporary Israel is felt in every corner of the land. Hadassah’s varied and broad
programs affects the lives and hopes of Israel’s citizens. Hadassah is the second largest employer in Israel.
In America, the women of Hadassah are dedicated to the furtherance of the democratic ideals of the United States. Our
American-scene projects include American Affairs, Jewish Education, Zionist Affairs and Zionist Youth Activities.
Funds are raised to support the Haddassah University Medical Center at Kiryat Hadassah and Mt. Scopus in Jerusalem,
Youth Aliyah, Hadassah Israel Education Services, Jewish National Fund and Youth Activities.
The Jacksonville Chapter is a coordinating board which includes three groups: TIKVA, which meets during the daytime,
on the second Tuesday, September through May; HANNAH SENESCH, which meets on the second Wednesday evening;
and SHIR-LI which also meets on the second Wednesday evening.
A new Business and Professional Group is currently being organized.
We invite new residents and unaffiliated women to join and learn more about Hadassah. There is a group for everyone
and we welcome your call. Please call one of the presidents or a membership chairperson and introduce yourself.
We wish everyone a Shana Tova-a good year, a year of good health and much happiness.
