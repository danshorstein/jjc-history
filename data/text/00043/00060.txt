Cemetery Committee of the
Jacksonville Jewish Center
Michael Schneider Kenneth Bowden
The Cemetery Committee provides a very necessary function of the Jacksonville Jewish Center. It is very
important that the Cemetery grounds are well kept and provide a peaceful resting place for our loved ones.
Our Cemetery Committee is chaired by Michael Schneider. Serving on the committee are Hymie Selber, Alan
Taffet, Dr. Joe Witten, Dr. Sam Witten, Jack Bettman, Joel Bressler, Nathan Sandler, Jack Becker and Joe
Becker.
Mr. Kenneth A. Bowden, Controller of the Jacksonville Jewish Center, also serves as general manager of our
Cemetery. It is his responsibility to oversee the upkeep and maintenance of the grounds. He has an excellent
working relationship with Mr. Jennewine who is general manager of Evergreen Cemetery. Mr. Jennewine has been
very generous with his time and his staff, Mr. Campbell, Mr. Miller and Mrs. Phillips. They have all taken excellent
care of the Center’s property, above and beyond what is expected. We appreciate everything they do.
Johnnie B. Mercer, who has been custodian of the cemeteries for 25 years, is in virtual retirement. Maintenance
is now handled by Russell and Winifred Baker, a very dependable couple. We are very appreciative of the help
given us by John Bergeron of the Southern Monument Company.
