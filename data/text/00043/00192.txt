CHALUTZIM
Grades 5 and 6
General Aim: Jewish families today look to the synagogue as the center for Jewish activity: religious,
education, social and recreational. A major goal of the synagogue and its school is the education of youth with
a firm base of knowledge, Jewish identification of youth, a sense of pride in their heritage, and a commitment
to Judaism. The goal of Chalutzim is to bring about the internalization of Jewish values, attitudes and
behavior patterns by supplementing the child’s formal religious education with informal Jewish learning
experience.
Call the Youth Office for more information, 268-6736, ext. 435.
Members of Chalutzim were actively involved in the Kadima Convention and had a year full of exciting activities.
