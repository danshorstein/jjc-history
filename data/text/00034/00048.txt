Question: What is a Patient Profile: Record?
Answer: A record maintained by the Pharmacy, similar to the patient
chart in a doctor's office, in order to serve patients better by
recording on an individual card what their prescription and
drug purchases are and when they have1 purchased them, the
price of the medicine, which physician prescribed them, the
patients' drug allergies, and any other pertinent drug infor¬
mation.
Question: What purposes are! served by maintaining these records?
Answer: A complete record can be supplied suitable for
1. Income tax purposes or insurance company major medical
drug records,
2. Retrieving lost prescription numbers,
3. Supplying the pharmacist with information to enable him to
help protect the patient from the possibility of a physician
or other medical practitioner, prescribing a drug to which
the patient may be allergic, providing that the patient has
fore-knowledge of his’ allergy to the drug and has conveyed
that information to the pharmacist for inclusion on the
record.
