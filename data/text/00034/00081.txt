CENTER MEN'S TEAM
CHAMPIONS IN EVERY WAY
A winning tradition has been the
legacy of the Jewish Center and
the Men's Basketball Team has
superceded our highest expecta¬
tions. In the first half of play our
Men’s Team won 11 games without
any losses and became the cham¬
pions of the Southside Church
League.
Coach Sam Leibowitz has done
an outstanding job in synthesizing
individual talents into a strong
cohesive team effort. Robert Can¬
tor is the captain and is a strong
rebounder on the boards along with
Paul Bloom; Ronald Pollock is
team manager and is responsible
for recruiting the team players;
key outside shooters are Steve
Strumlauf, Bobby Shenkman and
Steve Kadish. Additional strength
and depth come from Alan Chep-
enik and Lonnie Margol. The bal¬
ance of the team includes Ed Wit¬
ten, Harvey Goldstein, Scott Eber,
George Green and Dr. Harold
Baumgarten.
