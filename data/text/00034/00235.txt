THE FLEET OF BUSES AND DRIVERS
For over 15 years the Transportation Committee has been involved in
operating a complex system of routes for the thousands of children who
come to the Jewish Center. With the completion of our new facilities in
the heart of the Jewish community most families will be providing their
own transportation.
The Jewish Center Family extends its deepest appreciation and grati¬
tude to Kenneth Bowden who directs the Center’s transportation system
and to Lewis Ansbacher and Herb Lewitt, Co-Chairmen of the Transpor¬
tation Committee.
