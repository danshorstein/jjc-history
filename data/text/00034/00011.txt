President’s Message
We, the members of the Jewish Center are the
recipients of a “legacy of excellence”;
our spiritual beginings as B’nai Israel Congregation
in the early 1900’s . . .
the development of a comprehensive Synagogue-Center
at Third apd Silver Streets for the entire community
in the late 1920’s .’. .
the continuing expansion of services for youth and
family in the 1950’s . . .
the commitment to quality Jewish education through
the Solomon Schechter Day School in the 1960’s . . .
and now our new home at 10101 San Jose Blvd!
On one site a magnificent 2% million dollar
Synagogue-Center will serve the religious educational,
cultural, social and recreational needs of over 750
Center families as well as the entire Jewish com¬
munity of Jacksonville.
My prayer for the New Year —
“May the words of our mouths, the meditation of
our hearts and the work of our hands preserve and
prosper our “legacy of excellence” for ourselves, our
children and for generations to come.
Be strong, be strong and let us strengthen one
another.”
Charles J. Mizrahi
