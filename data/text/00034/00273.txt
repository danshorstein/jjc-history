ATID is the College Age Organization of Judaism sponsored by the
United Synagogue of America. It was formed and is led by college students
and collegians who want to live and learn Judaism and continue their
identification with the Jewish Movement.
ATID invites all college age students who wish to involve themselves
in Jewish life and thought.
Our Synagogue is very anxious to keep in touch with all of our
youth throughout the year so parents are urged to contact the Center
office and give us their address at school. Activities are planned during
vacation periods to enable our collegians to get together with their friends.
Jordan and Shirley Ansbacher serve as chairmen of the ATID Committee
with Shaul Baruch as advisor.
