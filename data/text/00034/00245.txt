The Tofield Memorial Library is a growing collec¬
tion of varied and interesting books with something to
offer every member of the congregation.
Library hours are every Friday evening and some
Saturday mornings after Services and on Sunday morn¬
ings. Books may be returned or checked out through
the school office on weekdays.
Left to right: Leo Meizlik, Minnie Schreiber, Mrs.
Nathan Shmunes, Mrs. Irvin Rothstein, Mrs. Philip
Selber, Mrs. Bernard Glossman, Rita Gendzier shown
in the Tofield Memorial Library.
A very entertaining Oneg Shabbat was held jointly
with the Center Sisterhood at which time a book re¬
view was given and reports made. The new Library
and resource center will give impetus to intellectual
activity and learning for the congregation.
