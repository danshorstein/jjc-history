A Poem Written
For Deborah Bressler, Daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Joel Bressler
on the occasion of her
BAT MITZVAH — FEBRUARY 21 and 22, 1975
On this Sabbath day and Holy worship was great in the tabernacle and fellowship
The joy of the Congregation and parents was infinite inspiration as
the glass transparent.
The Rabbi tradition was a thunder on the mountain
As you drink a healing water from a deep fountain.
The Rabbi's blessing to the parents and Bat Mitzvah family
Is an infinite gift continually.
The Cantor with heavenly melody
As the revival increase to all the community.
How nice we all gathered at tables for the reception
For ever memory as carved on the rock for Deborah, congratulations.
We are Jewish honoring in our occasion as lighting the Menorah
Lord teach us to worship you and to adore the Sefer Torah.
In conclusion, Lord help the family for ever
Bat and Bar Mitzvah
As we sing joyfully the Jerusalem song
Hatikvah.
By Isaac Kabbani
