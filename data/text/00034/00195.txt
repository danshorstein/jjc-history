Hospital on Mt. Scopus
October 1975 — The Re-Opening of
Hadassah Medical Center on Mt. Scopus
In October 1975, Hadassah returns to a new
magnificent Medical Center at Mt. Scopus. This re¬
turn is an historic event for every Hadassah member
and for the People of Israel! Most of all, it is a fitting
tribute to Dr. Haim Yassky and those who died with
him in the Scopus convoy in 1949. Hadassah returns to
begin another new chapter in its medical services to
Israel. This event indicates the lasting faith and de¬
votion of Hadassah. Mt. Scopus is a completely rebuilt
and expanded 260-bed hospital and Rehabilitation
Center. This will include a huge therapeutic Pool,
