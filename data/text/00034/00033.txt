Religious Services — Traditional prayer services are held daily, morn¬
ing and evening in the Chapel. Friday Family Services, Sabbath and
Holiday Services are observed in the Sanctuary throughout the entire
year.
Personal Religious Observances — Traditional Jewish customs are
observed in connection with Circumcision, Naming of Girls, Pidyon
Ha-ben, Bar Mitzvah, Bat Mitzvah, Confirmation, Marriages, Conver¬
sion to Judaism, Bereavement.
Education — Daily Kindergarten and Nursery with outstanding pro¬
gram of secular and religious content. Hebrew School, carefully graded
with intensive Hebrew content; Saturday! School, with a curriculum
including Jewish history and customs; Sunday School; Solomon
Schechter Day School, a graded secular and Hebrew School, 1st through
6th grade; Havurat Hanoar with Pre-Jr., Junior, Teen and Confir¬
mation Congregations and Minyan Clubs.
Extension Services — Library, outstanding collection of juvenile and
adult Jewish books; Adult Education* religious and cultural Jewish

provided by Mr. and Mrs. Club, Single Adults; Family Recreation
Program (Olympic Pool), summer weekdays and Sundays for all ages.

Athletics, programs for youth and adults; Center Day Camp & nine
