JUNIOR HADASSAH 1933
Shir Li Hadassah is one of the three groups com¬
prising the Jacksonville Chapter of Hadassah. We work
to raise money toward the support of Hadassah’s many
faceted projects and programs.
Throughout the year we have varied and interest¬
ing meetings, including speakers, demonstrations,
panels, films, group participation, cooking lessons,
study groups and on and on!
We are interested in the contemporary areas and
in working toward the fulfillment of Hadassah’s ideals
and goals.
