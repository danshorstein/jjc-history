(YOUNG FAMILY FELLOWSHIP)
Ha’Rayim, which is the young family fellowship
of the Jacksonville Jewish Center, has been in exist¬
ence for less than a year. Yet each function it sponsors
is more successful than the last.
Ha’Rayim (the Friends) is based on the principle
that religious and intellectual expression may best take
place in a social context. We combined a sensual
Havdalah service with a stimulating discussion with a
marriage counsellor. We topped off the evening by
making our own sundaes.
Our Sangria Smash and Luau were great successes
as was our Jewish Identity Program and our Picnics.
Ha’Rayim continues to attract newcomers to the
community. If you know of any young family that
have recently moved to Jacksonville, let us know.
We’re the perfect group for them.
a very special way, we learn together, pray together,
cook together, sing together and holiday together. An
exciting year is being planned for young marrieds,
single parents, and young families of the Jacksonville
Jewish Center. Join us!
When you hear the name Ha’Rayim, think of
friendship. We are all friends sharing good times to¬
gether. Sharing our common tie, Judaism, together in
Shalom,
Isabel Balotin, President of Ha’Rayim
