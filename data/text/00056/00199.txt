Special Events
Purim — Haman Hoedown
Purim is not just for children! With Haman hanging from the rafters, and “Chag Sameach “replaced by “howdy”,
the adults of our congregation had a rollicking celebration, following the Megillah readings. Denims, bandanas, cowboy
hats and boots were derigueur. The chuck-wagon cuisine
included hot dogs and hamantaschen! Some of the most
unlikely “pardners” joined in the square dancing, line
dancing, and other western-style activities. A mini-carnival
kept younger children happy all evening. A foot-stompin’,
toe-tappin’ good time was had by all!
Special thanks to Ava Axelrod, Justin Axelrod, Gayle
Bailys, Shirley Bielski, Beverly & Daniel Eagerton, Marilyn
Garber, Rollaway Square Dance Club, Dana Zaifert of Party
City, and the Center catering and maintenance departments.
Yom Ha’Atzmaut — “Flight to Israel”
The Jacksonville Jewish community celebrated Israeli Independence Day with a special
“trip to Israel” courtesy of Rabbi Dov Kentof, Center volunteers and the JCA. The Center
was transformed for this fantastic fantasy which featured a mock El A1 flight to Ben
Gurion Airport. An exciting tour of the country included
simulated stops at a Bedouin tent, the Western Wall, a biblical
petting zoo, an archeological dig at Masada, a busy Tel Aviv
market place and even a real camel ride!. Authentic Israeli
food, a lively Klezmer band and dancing with a Hora group
from Miami were enjoyed by young and old. Over 800 parents
and children experienced this exceptional fun-filled,
community celebration.
