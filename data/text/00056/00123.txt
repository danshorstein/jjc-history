Center Catering Staff
Creative menus and handsome presentation are the hallmark of the
Center Catering Department. Under the direction of Michael Fleet, a

Maintenance & Housekeeping Staff
The maintenance and housekeeping staff work diligently to keep
our building clean and presentable at all times. Richard Ford, our
maintenance supervisor, takes pride in his staff, and devotes much of his
time to taking care of all the physical needs required by the many events
Richard Ford which take place throughout the year. Volunteers appreciate the special
Maintenance Supervisor attention they receive from the maintenance department.
