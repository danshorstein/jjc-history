Religious Schools
Sunday School
The SUNDAY SCHOOL of the Jacksonville
Jewish Center is planned expressly for children in
Kindergarten, first and second grades. A weekly, two-
hour program gives each child the opportunity to
experience the richness of his/her Jewish heritage
through song, dance, role playing, cooking, short
stories, games, arts and crafts. Emphasis is placed on
the holidays and festivals. Jewish values and mitzvot
are encouraged. Many thanks to our wonderful Sunday
School teachers Gary Gendzier, Susan Kadish and
Chavi Frankl.
Family Council
For nine years, FAMILY COUNCIL has provided innovative
Family Education programs involving all facets of the congregation.
Several large scale projects like Rabbi Dov Kentof’s Fantasy Voyage
on Noah’s Ark to the Zoo, Sukkah Decorating and Hanukkah Activity
Day inspire the entire congregation including the Center Preschool,
Solomon Schechter Day School, Afternoon Hebrew School and Sunday
School. Synagogue professionals, volunteer experts, parents and children
provide valuable input and assistance and are rewarded by the experience.
