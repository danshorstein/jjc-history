Want to support the Center without spending any more money ?
join the club!
Through the Jacksonville Jewish Center’s merchandise scrip
program, the V. I.P. Club, Vested Interest Purchasing,
a portion of what you spend is given to the Center by the popular
merchants listed below. You simply order merchandise certificates
directly from the Center for each business you plan to patronize and
spend them as you would any other certificate. So every time you
shop, you contribute to the Center without spending any more money!
Great for all your regular shopping and gift giving, too. Imagine
buying cash certificates to so many different stores all in one place!
What a time saver.
The V.I.P. Club is one fundraising program in which everyone in
the congregation can participate. Synagogues all across the country
have instituted similar programs with great success. The program
taps into an existing vehicle for revenue — your purchasing power.
It allows the synagogue to benefit without any change in your
spending habits or in the places you shop. It simply requires you to
purchase certificates in advance, to spend when you like. Each
certificate is issued by the merchant, and is just like those you would
buy in their establishment. So you can give them as gifts or spend
them yourself, as usual. There are even mail-order and hotel
certificates available. Best of all, each time you order, the Center
gets a donation from the merchant, not from your pocket.
Monday is Y.I.P. Day! You may place an order any, or every,
Monday by 2 p.m. at the Jacksonville Jewish Center (no phone
orders).Your order will be ready for you to pick up at the Center on
the immediate Friday.
