Brownie Troop 882
With 27 eager and enthusiastic Brownies, Troop 882 managed to
accomplish a great deal this year. We earned eight “Try-Its”: Creative
Composing, Movers, Listening to the Past, Earth and Sky, Manners, It’s a
Small World, Water Everywhere and Caring and Sharing. Our newest
Brownies also earned their “Girl Scout Ways Try-It”.
Although we had a great deal of fun during our meetings, we had an
even better time going on the field trips! In the Fall, we went to a songfest;
made troop T-shirts at a factory; visited Westside Regional Park and Pelotes Island. In the Spring, we went to
Hanna Park and went camping at Chowenwaw. That was incredible! We also participated in several community
service projects including visiting River Garden, deliveringshalach manot packages to Mt. Carmel, and planting
flowers at the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
