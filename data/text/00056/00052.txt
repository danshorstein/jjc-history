Message from the Director
Early Childhood Department
Located in the Selevan Pre-School
The Jacksonville Jewish Center’s Early Childhood Department is
proud of our affiliation and accreditation with The Florida Kindergarten
Council. This organization is comprised of independent schools dedicated
to maintaining high standards in early childhood programs throughout
Florida. The Florida Kindergarten Council is recognized as an official
evaluative and accrediting organization for non-public, pre-primary schools
of the State of Florida.
An accredited early childhood school encompasses goals which
provide for the total development of young children as its paramount
consideration. There is hardly any subject that parents feel more passionate
about than their children’s education. A healthy, happy and challenging
environment where children are encouraged to grow and develop to the
best of their abilities is created and maintained. Our curriculum is planned to nurture the social, emotional,
intellectual and physical development of each child.
The child’s early learning experiences form the foundation for educational success. An effective curriculum
provides many opportunities for creative and cognitive learning situations which are suited to the maturation
levels of young children.
The staff of the Early Childhood Department and the Florida Kindergarten Council
members recognize the great importance of the early years of a child’s life. A good beginning
enhances a child’s joy of learning, which in turn results in positive and enthusiastic attitudes
toward academic study in the future.
Shirley Thacker
