1977: New Jacksonville Jewish Center consecrated. Egyptian President Anwar Sadat went to Jerusalem. Andye Honigman
attended summer quin of Muss High School in Israel. Louise Berman attended Ramah in Israel program. Alan Taffet went on ZOA
tour and visited brother Clifford on Kibbutz Haogen. Marsha Pollock was a delegate to National ORT Convention. Stacy and David
Epstein won a new Cadillac in the Center Raffle. October: Retirement Testimonial for Cantor Marton for 31 years of service to
congregation.
1978: February: Dr Stephen Broadman, Sherwin Burman, and Dr Joe Honigman went on a Federation Mission. Summer:
Safer Family Reunion met Jack and Sue Becker for dinner; Harry and Flo Berman and Family went to see new granddaughter, Esther
Gila; Janet Goldstein was a ’’madricha” for Young Judaea Summer Course. 7-26 September: Wendy Honigman and the late Sue
Rosenberg toured and attended the National Hadassah Convention; Dr Joel and Margaret Fleet were also delegates. ZOA/JNF
Banquet in memory of Frances Wilensky planted a Grove at Lahav.
1979: January: Janice and Steve Meisel honeymooned after wedding in Liverpool, England. 26 March: Prime Minister
Menachim Begin and Egyptian President Anwar Sadat signed Camp David Accords. August: Janet Goldstein made aliyah and
attended Hebrew University. November: Cindy Proctor moved to Arad.
1980: Jack and Sue Becker bought an apartment in Rehavia, Jerusalem. 7 October: State of Israel Bonds honored Jack Becker.
Center Yearbook was dedicated to Harry Gendzier.
1981: Seymour and Phyllis Garbose’s visited the grave of Ben-Gurion at Sede Boker. Michelle Honigman went on a Holocaust
Mission to Eastern Europe and Israel; Lenny Maiman was a “madriach” on USY Pilgrimage. 6 September: Nili Zimlich married Zvi
Grinberg in Ramat Gan. 4 November: Neil F. Portnoy Youth Lounge was dedicated. 28 November: Cindy and Moti Demri are
married in Safed.
1982: 6 June: “Operation Peace for Galilee.” Alan Taffet went into Lebanon on ZOA fact finding mission then to ZOA
Convention in Jerusalem with the Honigmans and Jack Becker. Dr Michael and Chary Greenburg went on Rabbi and Inge Gaffney’s
tour, along with Mrs Nachamovitz, Inge’s mother. Dina Saroff volunteered with Sherut L’Am and met future husband Arie Mirsky.
Janet Goldstein graduated from Hebrew University with a Bachelor’s degree. Rabbi Kentof brought the shtetl to the Center in “The
World of Our Grandparents.” The Aron Kodesh designed by Israel artist, Ami Shamir, was istalled in main sanctuary in time for
High Holidays.
1983: 2 April: ZOA/JNF Banquet in memory of Sam Galinsky also honored of Esther Galinsky. Rabbi & Inge Gaffney’s trip
includes: Bob Bossen, his daughter Karen and mother Sarah, Jack & Mollie Silverman, Sid & Grace Berkowitz, Sandy & Phil
Shapiro and Marlene Bossen. Bob recalls having the last chocolate ice cream cone in Yamit before the town was given back to the
Egyptians. 13 February: Sisterhood held 1st Women’s Minyan. October: Setzer Social Hall dedicated. December: Witten Chapel consecrated.
Center won four Regional United Synagague awards.
