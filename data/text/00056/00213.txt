To Our Advertisers:

publication possible, but also for the support you lend
to our many activities. We urge our members to
reciprocate with their patronage.
About the Cover:
A special illustration was created and
rendered in colored art pencils by
Raymond W. Cohen, in honor of the 50th
Anniversary of the founding of the State
of Israel.
Notes from the artist:
“Nearly nineteen centuries passed
between the loss of Israel’s independence and
its declaration on May 14, 1948. The rebirth
of Israel is a golden moment in our people’s
history that will shine forever in our Jewish
memory. It was made possible by men and
women who dared to dream of, and
relentlessly fight for, the creation of the State
of Israel.
The cover illustration was inspired by
those people, from many lands and many
walks of life, whose skills, devotion,
experiences and talents made a dream into a
reality.”
