2 Nisan 5758
Kadima
1991: January: Alan Taffet went on ZOA Mission of Support during Gulf War. Madrid Peace talks. May: Operation Solomon
saved 14,000 Ethiopians in 24 hours; Lenny Maiman co-chaired Israel Independence Day Celebration in Jacksonville. Summer:
Daniel Goldfield was part of USY Pilgrimage staff. 22-28 July: Wendy Honigman was a delegate to the 77th National Hadassah
Convention in Jerusalem. TNT couples club was formed.
1992: 7-20 May: Sylvia Wexler went on a Hadassah tour. Dr Joe & Sophie Witten became Hadassah Founders. Sheila
Jacobson and 30 family members travel to her niece’s wedding.
1993: January: Joel Fox welcomed as new Cantor. April: Harry L Shorstein honored with “Tree of Life Award” by JNF on the
3rd. Elaine Furman and her sister go on Rabbi Gaffney’s trip. Seth Finck, his family, along with the Sagers, Allens, Singáis,
Millstones, Helds and Adlers toured for 10 days under the guidance of Rabbi & Alina Kentof . USY Poland/Israel Pilgrimage
included Adam Frisch, Rachel Mizrahi, Morrie Safer, Lisa Schnieder, Jonathan Zisser. August: Anita & Steve Solomon celebrated
their 25th Anniversary in Israel. Center Catering Department became popular under direction of Michael Fleet.
1994: Jack Becker donated parcel of land purchased in ‘49 to the Masorati Movement in Israel (Conservative Movement
headed by his friend Rabbi Pesach Schindler); June: Lisa Solomon and Jerry Solomon were part of Rabbi Gaffney’s tour as a college
graduation gift from their grandmother, Gert Brown. July: Jeff and Robin Morris, Shira, Aliza, and Zach, along with Robin’s family,
toured Israel. August: Nancy and Jeff Mazo, Sarah, Frumi, Avi and Malki made aliyah. 30 November: Israel Bond Tribute Dinner
honored Jack Becker for his 90th Birthday; Ted Gross visited the Wall and recognized Andy Kauss, son of Marilyn and Bob Kauss,
reading Torah at one of the 12 minyanim.
1995: January: Daniel Goldfield made aliyah. Evelyn and Bernie Heneson visited their oldest granddaughter, Miriam Rivka,
who went to school in Har Nof, Jerusalem. Rabbi Gaffney’s trip included: Hannah Rosenberg, Martha Savage & her daughter Doris,
Charles and Rexine Trager and their son, Judy & Stephen Davis, Joanne & Leo Davis and their son Richie. Hilda Warfield with son
Dr Steven and grandson Sammy, go with Rabbi Kentof. David Goldfield visited brother Daniel on a Federation Single Adult tour.
Center Sisterhood presented new Huppah for chapel in memory of llene Selevan. Men’s Club brought national speaker Carol Pollard
to community.
1996: New American B’nai Mitzvah was highlight of religious calendar; members of class, Yakov and Svetlana Goldiner, and
children, became US citizens. USY Poland/Israel Pilgrimage: Josh Rubens, Simon Schuster, Elizabeth Sekine and liana Yergin.
Ramah in Israel: Josh Marks. July: Todd Berman received “smicha.” August: Hilda and Dave Mazo attended Sarah’s Bat Mitzvah.
1 November: Jack Becker honored with “Tree of Life Award” by JNF. Mary and Martin Wolf became Hadassah Founders. Dr Chaim
Rogozinski lectured in Tel Aviv.
1997: Shimon Peres, former Prime Minister of Israel spoke from our pulpit. Elie Weisel spoke at UNF thanks to Jay and
Cindy Stein. Rabbi and Alina Kentof’s 2nd tour included: Ron, Marsha and Lee Pollock, Dr Ken, Sheryl and Sarah Sekine, Dr Alan
and Jo Marks and twins Josh & Jenny, Abe and Shirley Bielski, Dr Jerry and Diane Weitzen, Marty and Randy Steinberg, and Dr Gil
Kentof. July: Mirsky family visited family. September: Todd Berman taught at Midreshet Lindenbaum. August: Cantor Aron Heller
joined Center professional staff. Rabbi David Gaffney began 25th year at Center.
