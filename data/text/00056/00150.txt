Jacksonville Jewish Federation
The Jacksonville Jewish
Federation serves as a central
resource for the Jewish community.
In summary, Federation is
dedicated to providing for the welfare
of every Jewish person living in
Jacksonville. Through a partnership
with the United Jewish Appeal, we
are equally committed to the survival
of and quality of life for every Jew
living in Israel and throughout the
Diaspora.
The four major local recipient
agencies of funds raised by
Federation are the Jewish Community
Alliance, Jewish Family and
Community Services, the River

Jacksonville Jewish Foundation. We
are proud of the invaluable services
provided by these four outstanding

Jacksonville Jewish Foundation
The Jacksonville Jewish Foundation is dedicated to accumulating
permanent assets which can provide a source of income to help support the
stability and continuity of the Agencies and Synagogues in Jacksonville. The
Foundation professionally administers lifetime and testamentary giving plans
designed to:
• Assist individuals to achieve their personal charitable goals in the most
tax-advantaged manner.
• Accumulate enduring endowed resources for use in supporting special,
emergency, and future needs; particularly those not provided for by the
Jacksonville Jewish Federation’s annual campaign.
The Jacksonville Jewish Foundation operates under its own Articles of
Incorporation and Code of Regulations as a partially supported Agency of the
Jacksonville Jewish Federation, with its own officers, Board of Trustees,
committee structure, 501(c)(3) and budget.
