1985: March: 1st adult Shushan Purim Dinner featured great skits. Hannah Rosenberg, Phyllis and Symour Garbose and
Marty and Sue Rosenberg celebrated Purim on the 75th Anniversary of Hadassah Trip in Jerusalem. March: ZOA/JNF Banquet
honored Safer-Witten-Faillace Family by planting a Woodland at Lahav. Summer: Dr Edwin and Harriet Safer went on a follow-up
Archaeology trip. May: “Hollywood Fantasy” was big hit at the Center Raffle. Center received 8 United Synagogue Awards.
1986: 22-26 January: Simcha 25 - Our Season of Torah celebrated the Silver Anniversary of our Solomon Schechter Day
School, dedication of a Sefer Torah, and establishment of the Educational Trust Fund. 14 May: Cantor Martin awarded by Cantor’s
Assembly. Summer: Todd Berman was part of USY Pilgrimage staff and attended Yeshivat HaMivtar “Brovender”. Jonathan Gray
and Tali Kentof won ZOA/MASADA Summer Program scholarships. Elaine Furman took daughter on Federation tour. 27 November:
Seth Margol married Veeda Brodetsky in B’nai B’rak. Shabbaton featured Dennis Prager.
1987: 22 March: ZOA/JNF Banquet in memory of the centennial of David Ben-Gurion. June: Simchot on Rabbi and Alina
Kentof’s tour: Dr Stephen, Ruchel, Melissa and Jennifer Broadman celebrate Suzanne’s Bat Mitzvah at Kehilat Moreshet Avraham;
Barbara, Jerry and Noah Portnoy celebrate Nicole’s Bat Mitzvah on Mt Scopus; Dr Loren, Elana and Scott dayman celebrate Mark,
Bobby and Teddy’s B’nai Mitzvot at the Wall. Barney Freedman had his Bar Mitzvah in Jerusalem. Cheryl Bossen and Beth
Greenburg went on Ramah in Israel Seminar. Doris and Dr Jeff Goldstein went on Federation tour. Center U.S.Y. awarded Chapter
of the Year by Hanegev Region.
1988: 16 January: Lois Chepenik honored with “Tree of Life Award” by JNF. 27 March: ZOA/JNF Banquet in memory of Sue
Becker; guest speaker was Charlotte Jacobson, past national president of Hadassah and the Jewish National Fund. Andye
Honigman-Zell “digs” at Tel Dan. Hilda Warfield visited cousins in Haifa. Center’s Family Council was formed. Rabbi David
Gaffney received Doctor of Divinity degree from Jewish Theological Seminary.
1989: January: Ricki Gross married Ed Manne. 26 February: ZOA/JNF Banquet in memory of Tim Riley. Annie Mae Fort,
Center cook for over 40 years, passed away. 5 April: Lawrence Dubow honored with “Tree of Life Award” by JNF. Daniel Goldfield
spent his first year in college in Nativ program. Janet Goldstein completed field work and received MSW. Cantor Abraham Marton
passed away; Memorial Tribute to his 43 years of service to Center featured concert by the Center Choral Society that he founded.
1990: May: Wendy Lipton, daughter of Sy and Phyllis Garbose made aliyah. 20 May: ZOA/JNF Banquet in memory of
Cantor A1 Marton established a Woodland in the American Independence Park; Yana Shetyn awarded a ZOA scholarship for a
MASADA SUMMER in ISRAEL program. July: Dr Edwin Safer represented FCCJ at a Biblical Archaeology Conference and made
several videos for the college. Center Yearbook dedicated to Hymie Selber, “Mr. Yearbook” since 1935. Center Men’s Club revitalized
by Ira Grann. Family Council won United Synagogue’s Solomon Schechter Award for family education programming.
