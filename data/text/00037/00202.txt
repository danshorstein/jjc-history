The Afternoon Hebrew School

The Afternoon Hebrew School of the Jacksonville Jewish Center is designed for students in grades 3 - 7 and provides an
elementary education leading to Bar/Bat Mitzvah and continuation in the high school program.
Meeting twice during the week and on Saturdays, “the basics” are at the core of the curriculum, with stress on Hebrew Reading,
Jewish Social Studies and current events relating to Israel are discussed throughly. Synagogue skills, holiday observances and
knowledge of prayers are all part of the comprehensive afternoon Hebrew School program.
