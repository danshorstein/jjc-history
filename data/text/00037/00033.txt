The Henry Kramer Library and Resource Center is being newly furnished and equipped to promote library
services for the entire congregation. Through the generosity of Mrs. Henry Kramer, this vital and important
unit of the Center will be beautifully furnished. The facilities include the Tofield Library, the Solomon Schechter
Day School Room and a reading and browsing area.

Many generations to come will derive a great deal of pleasure and knowledge from the books that fill the
library shelves. College students, professors and high school students all utilize this facility. We are proud of the
most comprehensive collection of Judaic literature in Northeast Florida.

The Library has been manned in the interim period by Mrs. Bernard Glossman, Mrs. Philip Selber, Mrs. Sidney
Stillman and Mrs. Milton Mann.

Members of the former Tofield Memorial Library Committee have been instrumental in the change over
process.
