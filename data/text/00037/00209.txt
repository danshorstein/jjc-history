YOUTH DIRECTOR

The Jewish Center has endeavored to provide the most comprehensive active and
qualitative youth program. To attain this goal, two individual youth groups now
meet all the needs of children ranging from 7th grades, Kadima - 7th and 8th
grades, U.S.Y. - 9th-12th grades. All programs are supervised under the direction
of Dov Kentof, Education and Youth Director.

The philosophy of The Jewish Center Youth is to provide an informal setting for
reinforcement of formal classroom training and to attract its members by offer-
ing a well-balanced program of social activities, learning experiences and religious
and cultural exposure. |

Experience has shown us that one of the most effective methods of imparting
Jewish values is to live them. Consequently, we have oriented ourselves toward
providing a substantial number of overnight and week-end retreats whereby
the beauty of Shabbat and the ethical message of Judaism can be more effective,
generating many times the enthusiasm that the confining walls and artificial
setting of the classroom have to offer.

In addition to a year round program of retreats, cultural activities, socials and
athletics, The Jewish Center affiliation with United Synagogue of America makes
it possible for our members to attend a variety of nationally sponsored summer
programs with scholarship funds available. Many of our youth can testify to the
benefits they received while touring AmericanJewish communities (USY on
W heels), attending Camp Ramah, or spending the summer in Israel (USY Israel
Pilgrimage) or Russia (USY Eastern Europe Pilgrimage).

To sum it all up, alot is happening. The opportunities to meet new people in Jewish
surroundings and experience Jewish living in unique and exciting situations
are endless. No matter what your interest, we have something for you.

My wife Alina, Tali, Gil and myself, wish all of you a very Happy New Year.
