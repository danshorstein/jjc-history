In the past year, BBG has done everything. Surprise breakfasts, creative services, Sweetheart Heart
Throb Weekend,a Sukkah building party are just a few of the events. We are very proud of our annual
NO-TALKATHON which raised $250.00 for Hope Haven Children’s Hospital. Visiting the Children’s
Ward with our own Raggedy Anne was a very pleasant surprise for the children. There also was tubing
down the Itchetucknee. Great times are always had at conventions and training sessions.

Plans have already been made for the coming year. Many of our successful functions will be re-
peated, by popular demand of course. We have begun attending Friday Evening Services on a rotating
basis between all the synagogues of Jacksonville. A trip to Disneyworld and tubing are in the offing.
