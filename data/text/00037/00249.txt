CENTER DAY CAMP

There are many camps and activities available for children during the
summer months, but only one Jewish Day Camp in the Jacksonville area.
The Center has felt the importance of continuingto make our excellent Camp
facilities available to all Jewish youth, giving them the opportunity to exper-
ience a wide variety of activities that promote physical, emotional and
spiritual growth encompassing the out-of-doors and nature.

An outstanding staff of specialists provide highly skilled and experienced
leadership ina wide variety of activities including: Judaica, Red CrossSwim
Instruction (as well as free swim), organized sports, arts and crafts, nature,
campcraft, canoeing, archery, tennis, overnight camping, drama, singing,
dancing and field trips.

Each summer includes four two-week sessions for campers between the
ages of five through twelve years, plus eight weekly half-day sessions for
campers three and four years of age. The camp fee is competitive with other
day camps, the program is more inclusive than most, with Jewish values
being an integral part of each day’s activities.

We look forward to many new concepts for the Day Camp 1979 and hope
that you will include our camp in the summer plans for your children.
