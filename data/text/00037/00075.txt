A 25th Anniversary is a “once in a life-time” milestone in anyone’s life. The Mr. and Mrs. club, an affiliate of the Jacksonville Jewish Center,
will reach that pinnacle in November 1978 and will celebrate at a special Silver Jubilee.

Boasting of the longest continuous existence of any social club in the Jewish community, the membership of the Mr. and Mrs. Club consists
of just what its name implies. It was organized a quarter of a century ago for the sole purpose of promoting fellowship, friendships and good-
times for married Jewish couples.

Dances are held every month to the music of live bands. Meals and snacks are served and each committee decides on its own type of enter- »
tainment. All of this is climaxed with a gala New year’s dance including a cocktail party, full-course dinner and professional entertainment.

As this organization is non-profit, all of the aforementioned is enjoyed at a nominal cost. There is no age limit and membership is open to the
entire married Jewish community.
