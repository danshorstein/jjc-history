Women’s League for Conservative Judaism has chosen for this year’s
theme, “Ve-he’ yay Berakhah - - And Be Thou a Blessing.” This theme
provides us with direction for the coming year. It contains the ideal which
Abraham was to set for himself which, according to Dr. Joseph Hertz, was
“to become a blessing to humanity by the beneficient influence of God.”
It is this ideal which motivates us to function as role models in our com-
munity, in our homes, in our synagogues and our Sisterhoods, so each of
us may become a blessing.

As we embark on a new administration of our Sisterhood, it is our hope
that we, like Abraham, will succeed in turning the Jewish People to a know-
ledge of God through the strength and commitment of Jewish womanhood.

Through our many programs and projects we plan to cultivate our
religious, educational and cultural life. With your support we can achieve
our goal.

Annetta
