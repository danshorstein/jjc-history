SOLOMON SCHECHTER AWARD

The Jacksonville Jewish Center was the proud recipient of this
Award for Worship and Ritual Program. The award, the most
distinguished award presented to an individual congregation,
was presented to the Center for excellence and high standards
achieved in the area of worship and ritual.

In Gatlingburg, Tennessee at the Biennial Convention of United
Synagogues, Rabbi Gaffney presented an outline describing the
creative and significant ways in which the beliefs, values and the
traditions of the Jewish people were highlighted through innova-
tive worship and ritual programs at the Jacksonville Jewish
Center.

These included High Holiday Prayerbook, coordination of
Sabbath services and school activities; and the involvement of
lay leaders. The programs of the Sisterhood, Minyanaire, and
Ha’Rayim and all other affiliates of the Center have also con-
tributed to the Jewish education of the congregation.
