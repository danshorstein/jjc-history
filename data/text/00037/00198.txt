MEN’S BASKETBALL

 

Many. men of the Jewish Community look forward to Tuesday evenings when they can play
a competitive game of basketball in the Fletcher-Selber Auditorium of the Jacksonville
Jewish Center.

 

With great precision and ruach the Savion Dance
Group performs for the Jewish community at the
celebration of Israel’s 30th birthday. The dancers
are left to right Ilana Manasse, Brenda Jacobs, Robin
Morris, Sheila Jacobson, Joan Levin, Deborah
Bressler, Carol Perras and Judy Silverman.

 

THE SAVION DANCE GROUP was formed two years ago by Ilana Manasse and Joan Levin after they returned from a Fred Berk Israeli Dance Work-
shop at Camp BlueStar. The troupe has performed for many groups in the community. Recently they represented Israel at the Multi-Ethnic Festival
at the Civic Auditorium for the 4th of July Celebration. The group meets once a week at the Center. For information call Ilana 733-7765.
