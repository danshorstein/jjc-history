On May 3, 1978 the Jacksonville Jewish Center along with all the Synagogues of the
Jacksonville Jewish Community held a memorial service and interfaith dialogue and program
observing Yom Hashoah (Holocaust Day).

 
 
 
 

The event was sponsored by the synagogues, Community Council and the National Council
of Christians and Jews. A very touching and inspiring program was experienced by all
those present.

 

Mrs. Phillip Freiden, Mrs. Chaim Bielski, Mrs: Jack Greenbaum, Rabbi David Gaffney, G.
Sigmund Raskin, Martin Tabak - - survivors of the Holocaust light candles in memory of the
six million Jews who perished.

 
 

 

A large crowd attends the memorial service at the Jewish Center to pay respect to the victims
of the Holocaust. Rabbi Gaffney leads the community in prayer.
