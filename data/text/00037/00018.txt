Sisterhood Bat Mitzuah Class

The class was designed by Rabbi Gaffney
to provide a basic Jewish Education for
adults who want to continue studying
Jewish life, regardless of whether or not
they had celebrated a Bat Mitzvah cere-
mony during their adolescent years.

In progress since January, the program
focuses on Jewish History, Jewish Life,
the Prayerbook. Jewish Liturgy as well as
Jewish thoughts and personalities are dis-
cussed and analyzed. In the spring of 1979
the class will conclude with a fitting cere-
mony. The enthusiasm of the ladies and
Rabbi is prevalent at each Tuesday morn-
ing session.

“Friendship”

Friendship is a priceless gift

that cannot be bought or sold,
But its value is far greater

than a mountain made of gold —
For gold is cold and lifeless,

it can neither see nor hear,
And in time of trouble

it is powerless to cheer —
It has no ears to listen,

no heart to understand.
It cannot bring you comfort

or reach out a helping hand —
So when you ask God for a gift,

be thankful if He sends

Not diamonds, pearls or riches,
