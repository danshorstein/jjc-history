IN SUPPORT OF SCOUTING

For many years the Center Sister-
hood has actively sponsored the Brownie
and Girl Scout troops. Scouting is a
very important phase of the youth
program in the Center. The Scouting
program helps girls to develop charac-
ter, to be useful at home, to love nature
and the out-of-doors, to create objects
of beauty and usefulness from simple
things, and to find their place in Synago-
que and community service.

The Brownie Troop No. 147 was led by ClaraMannandshewasassisted byIdlene

During the year they did many arts and crafts and went on nature walks. Some

swimming also.

A ceremony was held for the Brownies who were moving up to Girl Scouts.
