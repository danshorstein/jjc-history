Third Grade Thespians

The 3rd Grade Class of the Solomon Sch-
echter Day School honored their mothers,
grandmothers, aunts and friends with a delight-
ful original play called “The Photo Album”
which was written by Mrs. Judy Reppert, the
3rd grade teacher. All who attended were de-
lighted with the entire program which consisted
of songs and poems along with adelicious lunch
which the children helped prepare. Mrs. Ilana
Manasse, the children’s Hebrew teacher, and
Mrs. Reppert added to the theme of children
growing up by having a contest to see if the
mothers could pick out the entire class from
their baby pictures. The mothers were present-
ed with photo albums of the class. These con-
tained pictures of the class from their baby stage
through their grown-up years. The children
dressed according to the time period of their
lives and the album was truly a work of art.
