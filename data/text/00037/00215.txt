Chapter of Hadassah

Hadassah, the largest Zionist organization in the world, as well
as the largest women’s voluntary organization in the United
States, has a membership of 360,000 members who dedicate
themselves to help Israel, a developing democracy, to help itself.

The Jacksonville Chapter has approximately 900 members
and consists of two groups. Tikva, which meets during the day
and Hannah Senesch which meets in the evening.

(The Shir-Li group has combined with the Tikvah Group)

Hadassah celebrated its 65th birthday this past year, with a
heart-warming list of achievements; a 1,000 bed Hadassah-
Hebrew University Medical Center and the Mt. Scopus Hospital
in East Jerusalem which services people of all races and creeds.
Here teaching and research are carried on which benefit all man-
kind. Hadassah’s varied projects
Medical Organization, Youth Aliyah, Hadassah Israel Educa-
tional Services, Jewish National Fund, Zionist Youth Activities,
and American Affairs. Each of our three groups in Jacksonville
develops its own program of activities to support these projects,
with the Chapter co-ordinating the three groups.

We heartily welcome you, one and all, to join us, to get to know
us, and work with us. Please call our Chapter President, Mrs.
Joel Fleet, (Margaret) 396-7406 for further information. Mean-
while, we wish you and your dear ones a healthy and happy
year. Shalom!
