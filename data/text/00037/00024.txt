A’RAYIM

Ha’Rayim is the young family fellowship of the
Jacksonville Jewish Center. It has been in exis-
tence now for over three years. Ha’Rayim (the
friends) is based on the principle that religious and
intellectual expression can best take place in a
social context.

Last August, Ha’Rayim hosted a Wine and Cheese
Party for newcomers. It was a great success and
many new friendships were made. In September
we sponsored a Congregational Get-together on
Selihot evening. It featured a creative Havdalah
ceremony, followed by a social hour, dancing,
discussion and Selihot Service. The entire evening
was beautiful and inspiring and was an excellent
prelude to the High Holiday season.

Other successful functions were the Bonfire and
Cookout, and Disco at 10101. The purpose of the
group is not to raise funds, but merely a way for
Jewish couples to get together and share Jewish
experiences in a friendly, social and relaxed atmos-
phere.

Many exciting plans are being made for this year.
Watch your bulletin, and join us.
