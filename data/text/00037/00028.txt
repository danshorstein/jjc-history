Through its programs the Jewish Center seeks to foster individual growth and deve-
lopment, encourage participation in community life, provide opportunity for friend-
ship, fun and the acquisition and expression of Jewish knowledge and identification.
To achieve these objectives, the Jewish Center employs the services of professionally-
trained staff as well as experts ina variety of related fields. The Annual Sunday Fun-
day Program brings all Jewish adults and children of the community together in the
friendly atmosphere of the Jewish Center. The Center’s 36 acre complex contains full
and comprehensive facilities for cultural arts, community forums, social, athletic
and club activities.
