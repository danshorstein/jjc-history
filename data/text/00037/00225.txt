Our President

When the name of the Jacksonville Jewish Center is mentioned, what
immediately comes to your mind .. . what is your first thought? Exactly
what does the center mean to you?

Do you think of the professional staff, the Solomon Schechter School,
your wedding, a Bar Mitzvah, or perhaps the funeral of a loved one? Or,
do you call to mind your annual dues, building fund pledge, committee

work or other solicitations for help from the Center? Just what do you Lawrence “Laurie” DuBow
think?

 

Are you convinced that you have given more than you have gained? No one . . . not one member of this
Congregation has given or even offered to give more than they could or should have of their time, their
money or their energy, in proportion to what the Jacksonville Jewish Center has given to each of us.

It has upgraded our past, improved and enriched the present and endowed our future. The Center has
touched and affected the lives of us all. What have you done to affect the Life of the Center . . . and is it
enough? Could you or should you do more? Certainly each of us express our commitment in a different
way, but surely everyone can become involved as an expression of their dedication. Being involved is a
way of communicating what the Center really means to you.

Remember . . . if you have confidence in what we are doing and you care enough to send us the very best
.... come yourself. 3

Linda, Susan, Michael and I send our best wishes to you all for a Happy and Healthy New Year.

Laurie

 

The DuBows, Susan, Laurie, Linda and Michael.
