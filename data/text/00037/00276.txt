Blanche Slott

Call the Center and ask for extension 457 and you will reach Blanche Slott. If she
answers she is probably busy in her office calculating costs for a Kiddush, a wedding,
reception or a Bar or Bat Mitzvah. Whatever your simcha, if it will take place at the
Center, Blanche will be involved one way or another. Food, waiters, tablecloths
and the like. You name it . . . Blanche is there, always ready to help you.

If you happen to call during the summer months, you will find Blanche at the Center
Day Camp where she has served as Director for 13 years. The campers affectionately
call her “Aunt Blanche”, and rightfully so. She has a special warmth that makes
each campter feel very much at home. The past few years she has worked very hard
to increase enrollment by hiring a highly-skilled staff. And it’s worked!

Blanche served as a volunteer at the Center for over 25 years before she joined the
staff of the Center as Activities Director. She was the first woman member on the
Board of Directors of the Jacksonville Jewish Center, where she served as‘Youth
Chairman. She is a past-president of Sisterhood and a former Girl Scout leader and
Den Mother. More recently she has been recognized for her artistic ability. Her‘works
have been on display at the Hayden Burns Library, local banks, and art shows all over
the southeast. She continues to share her expertise with youngsters after school on
Tuesdays and at the Sunday Funday Program.

We are very lucky to have Blanche Slott on the Center Staff. Her involvement with

youth, her sensitivity to the needs of the members and her devotion to the Center
cannot go unnoticed.
