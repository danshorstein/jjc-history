Sunday School of
Jacksonville Jewish Center

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 

The Sunday School of the Jacksonville Jewish Center is planned
expressly for children in Kindergarten, first and second grades. A two
hour program gives each child the opportunity to experience the
richness of their Jewish Heritage through song, dance, role playing,
cooking, stories, games and arts and crafts. Emphasis is placed on
the holidays and festivals. Jewish values and mitzvot are encouraged.
