MINYANAIRE PROGRAM

The Jacksonville Jewish Center Minyanaires are dedicated to a program of prayer services, comraderie
as well as social and intellectual stimulation. A year of forums on contemporary social and religious issues
which affect the Jewish community and the community at large are being planned.

As part of its service to the health needs of the congregation, the Minyanaires in co-ordination with the
American Heart Association, sponsors C.P.R. classes (Cardiac-Pulmonary Resuscitation), and Blood Bank
Drives. 7

This Sunday morning program begins with minyan at 9:00 a.m. in the Witten Chapel, followed by break-
fast and the speaker of the-day. For information call Chairman, Gene Kornblum — 737-6127.
