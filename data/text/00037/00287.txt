Children’s Tree of Life... Anew project of Sisterhood was very successful due to the efforts

The Sisterhood Closing Luncheon

The Sisterhood members that attended
this Luncheon had a very special treat.
Sylvia Weiner, assistant Curator of the
Jewish Museum in New York, gave a vivid
slide presentation of some of the treasurers
from the Museum’s collection. Just as
Judaism is more than areligion, The Jewish
Museum is more than a museum. Its role as
custodian of the collection of treasurers and
artifacts of the Jewish Peoples history is a
proud one, and its efforts to make the re-
sources of that heritage available to today’s
generations embrace a constantly expand-
ing variety of special programs. Pictured
with Sylvia Weiner are Horty Gendzier and
Sylvia Shorstein, Chairwomen of the day.
