Troop 14

 

The year of 1977-1978 has seen Troop 14 make a transition from a group of boys just
attending a meeting, toa group of young men getting together for a purpose. Good fellow-
ship, learning to turn energy into purposeful action and earning merit badges for
advancement, had their place at all meetings and outings.

Camping trips were also a integral part of troop activities and all scouts have become
good campers and familiar with the out-of-doors.

We are proud that Gary Kupfer attained the rank of Eagle Scout which will blaze
the trail for many more Eagles for Troop No. 14, as wellas Life rank which was attained
by Josh Weiss.

Ian Mann and Irving and Roy Sucholeiki obtained much scout know-how at the
summer session of the North Florida Scout Camp. Josh Weiss and Gary Kupfer shared
a unique experience by having the opportunity to attend the scout encampment at
Philmont, New Mexico. The trip was filled with adventures and new activities from
which the entire troop will benefit.

During the coming year we will strive to maintain our momentum and continue
to grow. We welcome the new scouts who have joined our ranks from the Webelos
Cub Scouts and look forward to sharing all scout activities with them. There will be new
experiences and continuing adventures for the not so new scouts.

With the help of my two assistant scoutmasters, Harry Berman and Nathan Weiss,
we will continue to do our part to keep Troop No. 14 both enjoyable and rewarding for
all scouts.

Yours for Better Scouting,
Donald Kupfer, Scoutmaster
