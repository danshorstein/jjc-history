PARENT - TEACHER ASSOCIATION
Havurah in Hebrew: is a small group of like-minded
Jews who assemble to share communal experiences.
The 2015-16 PTA Board would like to WELCOME our new and returning families to
the Galinsky Academy PTA “Havurah.” The PTA is comprised of every parent/guardian,
teacher, and staff member of our schools. Together we help build the Galinsky Academy
community while supporting our children. Planning has already begun to provide a great
variety of new and exciting programs for the coming year. There will be opportunities for
every PTA member to participate in creating these wonderful memories. Whether you are a
parent in the Bernard & Alice Selevan Religious School, the DuBow Preschool, the Martin J.
Gottlieb Day School, Setzer Youth Education (or all four) your participation is welcome and
needed.
Our one hour meetings (PTA Happy Hour) will be held on alternate Sunday and
Wednesday mornings to accommodate all families. This is a great opportunity to hear about
what’s going on, get involved or simply be social. Stay connected via our website galinskyacademy.org/pta
This year we invite you to participate in our new, community building programming. From family campouts to
community service projects to Shabbat meals, there is something for everyone. Volunteer needs throughout the school
year include: weekly Shabbat boxes & Challah sale; holiday celebrations, Scholastic book fair, Box Tops for Education,
Vision Screening and more. We encourage you to reach out to one of our board members to get involved. Just match
your talents and interests to an event or program and let us know.
