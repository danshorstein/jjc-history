Wishing you a sweet and safe New Year from
Firehouse Subs Public Safety Foundation
AVOID exposure to Carbon Monoxide (CO),
never leave your car running in a garage and never use
portable generators inside the home.
CHANGE the batteries in your smoke detectors
every six months.
Keep grilling outside and NEVER unattended.
Store propane tanks outside and away from a heat source.
IN YOUR CAR, be sure to have jumper cables,
a flashlight, a blanket and a first aid kit.
DO NOT leave a child or pet in a locked car, the
temperature can reach 100 degrees in minutes.
