PURIM
Purim is always a wonderful celebration full of creative costumes,
food, silly shpiel’s and lively megillan reading. On March 4, the
evening began with a congregational dinner before the festivities.
The Purim Shpielers performed an exciting and entertaining Purim
Shpiel entitled “The Grogger Awards.” Clips of hilarious award
categories were dispersed throughout the evening’s program.
Our shpielers worked tirelessly to create unique and hilarious
parody moments through live performances and pre-recorded
video. Following the shpiel was the traditional congregation
megillah reading. In celebration of Purim, the Galinsky Academy
PTA planned a Sunday afternoon Purim carnival and art auction.
Families of all ages enjoyed the games, inflatables, food and a
musical performance by Koltrain.
