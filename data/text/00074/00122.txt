I HEBREW HIGH SCHOOL
Rabbi Howard Tilman
This year Hebrew High had a new format and new energy! In eight units
over the course of the year we challenged our teens to think about Jewish
identity and its influence on their daily lives. Together with Rabbi Tilman,
Steven Resnick, and many guest speakers, we examined what our tradition
has to teach us about going through teshuvah, being grateful, speaking out
against hate, perspectives on the military, being connected to Israel, as well
as other topics. Over the year, students learned from representatives of other
organizations including OneJax, JASMYN, AA, AIPAC, and Jerusalem U,
and even had the chance to see two special movies. Together, students learned
that our Jewish texts and values have a lot to teach us even today, and that
learning about them can be interesting and fun!
