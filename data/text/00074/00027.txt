Alyse Nathans
Board President
FIND MEANING AND FULFILLMENT
The Jacksonville Jewish Center has been my second home for as long
as I can remember, and I was honored to serve as the VP of Education and
Chairperson of the Galinsky Academy Education/Youth Cabinet for the past
three years. So, when the executive leadership and nominating committee
asked me to serve as the President for the 2015-2017 term, I was humbled,
honored and thrilled. I have experienced so many lifecycle events with the
love and support of the JJC, and now I get the opportunity to serve you, our
congregants, along with talented and diverse executive and general board
members. The Center is in a wonderful place, a confluence of the right people
and the right time, with dedicated clergy and staff, including everyone on the
synagogue as well as the Academy side of our community.
As we move into this New Year, I hope every congregant can find meaning and fulfillment somewhere,
somehow at the Center. From daily minyan, schools for children ages one through high school, camping,
■ scouts, committees of various interests and purposes, Men’s Club, SisterhoQik services for Shabbat and
holidays, lifecycle ceremonies, adult education, and much more, there is always room for you to participate.
I I look forward to seeing you throughout the year.
Wishing you a healthy, happy and sweet New Year.
Shanah Tovah,
Alyse Nathans along with Alan, Rebecca, Arin and Morgan
