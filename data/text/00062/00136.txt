The goal of the Youth Basketball
League (YBL) is to provide area boys
and girls with the opportunity to have
fun, improve physical skills, propmote
good sportsmanship and team spirit
and experience a strong and positive
athletic environment.
All the games and practices are
held in the Fletcher-Selber Auditorium
of the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
Thank you to Scott Zimmerman,
who coordinated a very successful
program, and to the volunteer coaches
for their valuable assistance and time.
We look forward to an exciting 2003-
2004 season.
