Statistics show that 48.6% of people age 65 and over may spend some time
in a nursing home.1 But the truth is that most people, even when faced with
long-term care needs, would rather stay at home.
A long-term care insurance policy from John Hancock can help cover the
care you receive at home. It also covers care in an assisted living facility
or a nursing home. It’s nice to know that if you or your spouse ever had a
long-term illness or injury, you both would have the option to get the
care you need in a place where you’re more comfortable, familiar with
your surroundings, and close to your family and friends.
Find out more about an insurance policy that will help you stay at home.
1. Health Insurance Association of America, Long-Term Care: Knowing the Risk, Paying
the Price, 1997.
