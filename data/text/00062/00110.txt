The Parent - Teacher Association of the Solomon Schechter Day School and the Center
Preschool is committed to providing the very best for our students. PTA enhances the
educational experience with quality programs and services.
The following are some of the great programs, services and fundraisers that PTA
provides throughout the year for the students: Holiday Observances, Red Ribbon Week (drug
awareness), Library and Art Assistants, Vision Screening, Purim Carnival, Magazine Sale,
Gift Wrapping Sale and much more.
As you can see, PTA has many great plans for this school year. An integral part_of our
success lies in the hands of our volunteers. PTA is successful because of the wonderful
parents and teachers who join and support it. PTA volunteers are appreciated for their time
and efforts.
This year, I have the honor of being PTA president. It has personal significance, as my
mother, Alix Levy, was PTA president of Solomon Schechter Day School in 1968. I have
many fond memories of my mothers’ involvement in PTA . I remember the excitement of
seeing her in the hallways at school or working PTA programs. What a wonderful legacy we
can leave our children; fond memories of our involvement in their lives.
I look forward to another exciting and busy year.
Warmest regards,
— Allison Bailys
