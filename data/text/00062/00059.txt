Annual Lag B’Omer Picnic
Nearly 300 people crowded the synagogue for the annual congregational
party which included activity booths run by the Religious School, dinner by
Margo’s Catering and over two dozen fabulous craft exhibits.
Over two dozen “Reach-to-the-Beach” members had a grand time making
latkes and playing dreidel at a dinner for those who live at the beach.
Families poured into the Center
Recreational Park & Campgrounds for
the official opening of Camp Neshama,
at the Lag B’Omer picnic in May. It was a
wonderful afternoon filled with fun, food
friends and “Mitzvah Clowns.”
Purim-a-Plenty
Queen Esther, Haman and a variety of other
characters had a blast at the Preschool and Day
School programs.
No one partied better than the adults. “My
Big, Fat Shushan Wedding” was the zaniest Purim
dinner and program on record!
