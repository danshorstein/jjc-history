About the Cover:
Dedicating this Yearbook to Raymond
Cohen is especially fitting, for he has been
involved with the Center Yearbook in a variety
of roles for some 50 years. During that time,
he created or consulted on almost half of the
covers, served on the advisory committee,
produced ads and layouts, and was editor of
the award-winning Centennial issue.
When informed by the Board of this
honor, Raymond was overwhelmed, but true
to his volunteer spirit, offered to do the cover
of this issue.
In a play on Norman Rockwell, Raymond
has created a drawing that crystallizes past and
present in a dejavu experience. In his youth and
in his prime, Raymond has not wavered from
his love of Judaism as expressed through art,
music and devotion to the synagogue. With
humor and candor, Raymond reveals that he
has matured but is still vibrant and vital, ever
ready to apply his talents in service to the
Jacksonville Jewish Center.
