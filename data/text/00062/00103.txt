Message from the Director
Dale Schemer
Director
A recent study of Jewish identity development found that “Jewishness in adulthood
appears related to a strong orientation towards Jewish life in childhood. When conveyed in
the early years, the message about the importance of being Jewish persists into adulthood.”
The staff of the Jacksonville Jewish Center Preschool feels a strong professional and
personal responsibility to provide that message by offering a Jewish education in which our
children are not just Jewish on the holidays, but feel their Jewishness in everything they do.
Our goal is to give our young children the best possible start in life. We strive to
provide them with not only the important cognitive and social skills, but also a solid Jewish
educational foundation.
Robin Morris
and Michelle Margol
