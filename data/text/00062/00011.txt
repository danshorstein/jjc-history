Honoring Raymond Cohen
for his lifelong contributions of time and talent in service to our synagogue
Congratulations Papa
For generations we have heard Ema say, “Be quiet, your father/
your grandfather/your great-grandfather is singing!”
It is hard to remember a time at the Center when you weren’t
the featured soloist, performing with the family in a play, singing at
a wedding, or leading services. You have become so much a part of
what the Center is, that it could never be the same without you.
To paraphrase Kohelet, “Rabbi’s have come and gone, and the
Cantor also changes, but it appears that the indelible imprint of
your spirit will abide in our hearts and souls forever.”
This is your legacy. Yasher koach.
Jerry & Sherry Marsha
Michelle, Mike & Matthew Tavi, Grant, Shannon & Evan
