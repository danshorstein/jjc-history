Recreation
Camp Neshama
Camp Neshama was an amazing eight week
experience for all who participated. Campers and
staff came from all over - Jacksonville, Atlanta,
New York, South Florida, even Israel! We were pleased
to welcome many new campers along
with the “old-timers.”
From beginning to end, the
summer was filled with activities.
Swimming, Judaica, gardening,
music, arts & crafts, drama, sports,
computers and cooking were part of
the regular daily line-up. Tasty,
kosher lunches from Margo’s
Catering and snacks were a
highlight.
Specialty camps featured
horseback riding, wall climbing,
kayaking, cooking, ceramics, Tae
Kwan Do, tumbling and basketball.
Camp Neshama staged several big events -
a play, talent show, carnival, color wars and an
Israel day. Field trips included bowling and skating
as well as visits to the kitchen of 3
Sisters Chocolate, Jax NAS, Aqua
Zoo, Sprinkle’s Children’s Museum,
MetroPark, Marineland, Adventure
Landing and ice skating with the
Barracudas hockey team.
Of course, summer camp would
not be complete without sleepovers.
Dancing, swimming, pajama parties
and campfires added to the fun.
Thank you to a wonderful staff.
Rest up and get ready for another
exciting and funfilled summer at
Camp Neshama 2004.
— Pamela Maman, Camp Director
