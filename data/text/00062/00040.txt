Men’s Club Man of the Year Banquet
On Sunday morning, October 27, Moti Demri was teased, taunted and lovingly
roasted by some of his closest friends. It was “No Problem” to honor him as the
Center Men’s Club’s 4th “Man of the Year,” for his longstanding service to the Men’s
Club, the Jacksonville Jewish Center and the Jewish community.
Evan Yegelwel, chairman of this event and last year’s honoree, planned a delightful
program which included a brunch buffet by Margo’s Catering. Moti was a good sport
and a great time was had by all.
The Man of the Year Banquet is the club’s biggest annual fundraiser. All proceeds
benefit the Men’s Club’s “Horty T. Gendzier Summer Scholarship Fund” that helps
needy Jewish children attend Camp Ramah and USY Pilgrimage programs.
In recognition of their achievement, Yad Shel
Chai recipients receive a certificate and
personalized “yad, ” Torah pointer.
Men’s Club Yad Shel Chai Award
In 1998, Center Men’s Club instituted this award-winning program
to encourage youngsters to continue reading Torah after their Bar or Bat
Mitzvah. Teens who read a minimum of 18 (chai) portions by age 18 are
presented with an inscribed Torah pointer (yad) and a certificate. Over
the years, many teens have enrolled in this program, and the following
have received the award: Jordan Allerding, Sarah Blankstein, Daniel
Heller, Jonathan Heller, Lauren Bloom, Crista Cohen, Jared Fleet, Aaron
Finck, Ben Finck, Seth Finck, Mark Frisch, Rachel Gerber, Max
Horovitz, Sam Horovitz, Debra Mizrahi, Dena Mizrahi, Jonathan
Mizrahi, Aliza Morris, Lindsay Morris, David Organes, Daniel Passen,
Lisa Rand, Avi Richman, Jessica Rogozinski and Rachel Rogozinski.
