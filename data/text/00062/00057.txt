Sunday, February 23, 2003
We laughed until we cried at
over 50 hilarious musical parodies
like The Phantom of the Synagogue
and Everything's Coming Up Moses.
Glickman is a master story teller and
wonderful musician whose show was appreciated by young and old. Meeting
him was a treat. This was a lighthearted opening to the season.
Sam GIaser
SiNQER - SoNQWRÍTER
Sunday, March 23, 2003
Sam Glaser was “so cool” and “so haimishe.”
The audience adored him. His contemporary rhythms
appealed to all ages. Sam’s own compositions embrace
the joy and hope of the Jewish people with power and
passion. We especially enjoyed learning and performing
songs with him.
RobyN HeIzner Trío
Sunday, May 11, 2003
The captivating performers
of the Robyn HeIzner Trio truely
connected with the audience.
From the exuberant melodies of
modem Israel to the folk songs
of Eastern Europe, love songs of
Spain, and contemporary tunes of
America, the Trio’s broad
repertoire reflected the vibrance
of Jewish music. It was a rousing
finale to a memorable Performing
Artist Series.
