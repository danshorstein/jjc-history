The Gate Petroleum Company
of Jacksonville, is proud to own
and operate three of the most
prestigious private membership
clubs in Florida— the Ponte
Vedra Inn & Club, Epping
Forest Yacht Club, and The
Lodge & Club. From this premier portfolio
of clubs, an exciting membership has
evolved...The Gate Governors Club.
A membership in the Governors Club
makes it possible to enjoy privileges at
any combination of two or more clubs
within the Gate family. Club
combinations are conveniently
packaged and are attractively priced
creating a tremendous value.
Governors Club members enjoy such
diverse features as a world class marina,
Atlantic Ocean beaches, championship gc
tennis, fitness and a luxury spa. A variety
of outstanding restaurants and an exciting
social calendar makes this membership
a desirable choice.

opportunities and benefits available with
