Religious School Simchas
Confirmation Class of 2003
6 & 7 Sivan • June 5-7, 2003
The Biblical festival of Shavuot commemorates the giving of the Torah and Ten Commandments to the
Jewish people. It is fitting that the festival should be celebrated with the accompanying sense of commitment to
Jewish life that Confirmation represents. Our Confirmands demonstrated the knowledge they have gained through
completion of the Beit Midrash - Community Hebrew High by fully participating in Shavuot services. In addition,
students read essays they had prepared on the theme “What Judaism Means to Me.”

The Beit Midrash, Hebrew High School, program involves 8th - 12th
grade students throughout the community . Classes taught cover curriculum
from the following areas: Literature, Hebrew, Israel, Jewish Community,
Jewish Education, Jewish Ethics and Values, Jewish History, Judaic
Studies and Texts. Classes meet Wednesday evenings from 7-9 p.m. For
more information visit our website at www.beitmidrashjax org, or
call 448-5000.
