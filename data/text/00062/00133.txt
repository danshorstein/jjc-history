Scouting
Cub Scout Pack 14
Cub Scout Pack 14 enjoyed a great year! We had
two camp outs and great participation from our Pack
Family. Pack 14 was seen camping at Echockotee and
Little Talbot. This years activities included hiking, learning
about whittling, playing mad libs and going on an alligator
scavenger hunt. They were also treated to a Pack 14-
style bonfire at Echockotee.
Our Pinewood Derby drew our pack’s families
together for a morning of racing competition and family
fun at the Center. We had some great award winning
designs.
Our dens learned about nature, safety, community
and Judaism. We are proud of our new den, the Tiger
Cubs. A big Scout salute to the first-grade Tiger Cub
Program. Tiger Cubs is geared towards boys in the first
grade as opposed to the traditional beginning of Cub
Scouting in the second grade. We congratulate our
WeBeLos who moved up to the Boy Scout Troop 14.
We are grateful to our den leaders: Mark Berlin,
Marty Pett, Garry Kitay, Robert Richter and Lisa
Wolfson and all our volunteer parents, who have
committed their valuable time to our children, the Cub
Scouts of Mighty Mighty Pack 14!
