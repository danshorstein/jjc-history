ANNUAL CAMPAIGN FOR EDUCATION
The Jacksonville Jewish Center is a special community
dedicated to high educational standards and traditional
Jewish values. The support of donors to the annual L’Dor
V’Dor Campaign has brought exceptional opportunities to the
children of the Academy - the DuBow Preschool, the Bernard
& Alice Selevan Religious School, the Martin J. Gottlieb Day
School and the Setzer Youth Education programs. This year
the focus of L’Dor V’Dor shifted from a fundraising event
near the end of the academic year to a campaign process of
communication throughout the academic year, giving the
entire community the opportunity for involvement and unanimous support of the Academy’s
mission and vision.
L’DOR V’DOR
Interested parties can become donors to the L’Dor V’Dor Annual Campaign for Education
through many different opportunities. Please contact Rabbi Jim Rogozen, Head of the
