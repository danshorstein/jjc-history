A BUSY YEAR IN THE ‘HOOD’!
I am honored to
serve as president
of Sisterhood and I
am looking forward
to working with the
newly slated officers.
My board is a group
of dynamic women
who will be helping
vMi enhance Jewish life
with the members
of Sisterhood at the
Marjie Rogozinski Jacksonville Jewish
Sisterhood President Center and community
My main goal is to
motivate women to learn, educate, and socialize
with each other in a Jewish setting. Sisterhood is
the backbone of the Center and philanthropically
supports so many diverse programs. Listed below
are some of the wonderful mitzvahs that Sisterhood
contributes to our synagogue:
When you buy gifts in the Judaica Shop, send
Rosh Hashanah flowers to a friend, buy hamantashen
at Purim, buy Chanukah gifts at the Holiday Bazaar,
send Leagrams to children for their consecration, or
simply pay your dues, it all goes to worthy causes
you can feel good about.
We have some fun things planned for the
upcoming year. To find out more, follow us on
facebook or check us out at www.jjcsisterhood.com.
Provide scholarships to send Center children
to Camp Ramah
Buy bereavement cards for the Synagogue to
send to members who have lost loved ones
Provide Jewish educational programs for adult
women - book reviews, educational speakers,
hot topic discussions and more
Organize game days, house parties, luncheons,
dinners and other social and fun events
Provide financial assistance to capital projects
for the Synagogue
Provide financial resources for Religious School
Provide financial assistance to our Youth Groups
Raise money for scholarships through
Torah Fund
Provide volunteers for Purim festivities, the
Judaica Shop, and High Holidays
Collect for food pantries and other Social
Action projects
Advocate for Conservative Masorti Judaism
in Israel through Women’s League of
Conservative Judaism and Mercaz
AND best of all, help each of us have a
meaningful experience at the Center
