ISRAEL RALLY
On August 3, almost 800 members of the Jacksonville community came together at the Jacksonville Jewish
Center in solidarity with the State and people of Israel. In light of events surrounding Operation Protective Edge,
I clergy, elected officials and community leaders came together in organizing an unprecedented moment for our
community. The event included prayers by our clergy, passionate speeches by our Federation leaders and our
Congressman Ander Crenshaw, as well as video presentations from our students spending the summer in Israel.
To close, the community joined in song led by Hazzan Holzer and Rabbi Cohen of the Temple proclaiming, “Am
Yisrael Chai!”
