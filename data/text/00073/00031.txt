SOSHUL NETWORK
“The SoShul Network” was established in early 2013 as a group for 20 and 30-somethings of the Jacksonville
Jewish Center to network and enjoy the company of new and old friends. The committee plans Jewish-themed
social events to engage its members as well as promotes activities and programs. Whether a recent college
graduate, young professional, married couple, new parent, or single, there is something for everyone!
During its inaugural year, many socials were planned that brought the next generation of Center leaders
together. The first event was a pool party and cookout that took place at the Center in August 2013. In October,
The SoShul Network committee held a Shabbat dinner at the Center that included songs, blessings, and Jewish
trivia. In November, the women of The SoShul Network attended “ladies night out” at Painting with a Twist
where they painted a Shabbat tabletop scene on canvas. The committee threw a Hanukkah celebration at Intuition
Ale Works in December with food and drink. Most recently in May, the group hosted a potluck brunch at a
home in Mandarin to kick-off the spring season.
The SoShul Network was successful in attracting over 30 people to each event, including many young
Jewish members who have recently moved to Jacksonville. As the committee begins planning events for the
upcoming year, it hopes to reach out to additional 20 and 30-somethings in the community who have not yet
become involved. It also intends to maintain the interest and participation of those who are already active. The
SoShul Network committee has created a working list of group members and is continuously adding names
and contact information.
If you would like to attend any upcoming socials, please email SoShulNetwork2030@gmail.com.
