HEBREW HIGH SCHOOL
This past year’s MAKOM curriculum focused on a variety of contemporary
issues that challenged our teens to think about Jewish identity and its influence
on their their daily lives. Modules facilitated by Rabbi Olitzky and Hazzan
Holzer included “Learning to be an Israel Advocate”, “Jews and the Civil
Rights Movement”, and “Love thy Neighbor”. Students continued our annual
Mitzvah projects and hosted a number of special speakers including our very
own Lauren Prager, former AIPAC staffer, and Lee Kaplan, a Jacksonville
native who shared the childhood experiences that were the backdrop for his
one man show Bully. Our 12th grade Siyyum students continued a partnership
between Rabbi Lubliner and World Relief, making a difference as friendship
partners and English-as-a-Second-Language (ESL) tutors to members of the
Khedir family, refugees from the Darfur region of the Sudan. We look forward
to a great year ahead filled with inspiring learning and fun activities thanks to
the new leadership of Steven Resnick, our Youth Director, and Rabbi Howard
Tilman, our Second Rabbi.
