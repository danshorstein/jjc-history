BOY SCOUT TROOP 14
Every year is a great year for Troop #14 of the North Florida
Council, the only Jewish Troop in Northeast Florida. As it enters its
ninety-second year, it continues to be a vibrant part of youth activities
of the Jacksonville Jewish Center. All campouts begin with Friday
night services, lighting of the Shabbat candles, and continues with
services on Saturday morning. Scouts learn not only outdoor skills,
like camping and hiking, but also leadership skills, and the importance
of service to our community.
We are open to all young males between the age of 11 and 18 years
who are interested in adventure. Please contact Allan Andrew at 904-
563-5261 or Steven Resnick at 904-292-1000 for more information.
