BLESSING FROM THE TRADITIONAL YOM KIPPUR LITURGY:
A PRAYER FOR LEADERS
Holy One of Blessing, be with us and may Your words sparkle in our mouths, as
we become channels for our congregation. May You be our source of inspiration.
May we walk in the light of Your presence and be humble in our holy responsibilities.
We approach the holy ark absorbed in our awareness of You. May our congregation
surround us with love and may You show us compassion. We raise our eyes to You
and open our hearts to the teachings of Your Torah. We pour out our hearts like
water. May the congregation be uplifted because of our intent.
I would like to thank the many members of our congregation who have added
their voices as a source of inspiration by participating in reading Torah at the
Jacksonville Jewish Center. Through their dedication and devotion, we have
maintained a high level of participation. The commitment and encouragement
of our synagogue organizations and their leaders will ensure that this quality
standard will be continued for many generations to come. I would like to thank
all of our teachers for their continued support and cooperation and on behalf of Suzanne, Casie, Aron and
myself wish you a happy and healthy new year.
Shana Tova,
Scott Zimmerman
Scott Zimmerman
Ritual Director
