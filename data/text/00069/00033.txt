CENTER CHOIR
For over 59 years, the Center Choir has been enhancing services on the High Holidays and Shavuot with
moving liturgical music. Over the years, they have given numerous concerts featuring members of the choir, and
nationally acclaimed soloists. Many of the current members have been singing with this group for twenty years or
more. New members are always welcome. Call Raymond Cohen 733-6979.

JJC GREEN TEAM
The Jacksonville Jewish Center is on our way to going GREEN and we’re excited to announce that we’ve
become part of the GreenFaith initiative.
GreenFaith is a green movement that inspires, educates and mobilizes people of diverse religious backgrounds
for environmental leadership. They believe that protecting the earth is a religious value, and that environmental
stewardship is a moral responsibility.
Three core values defining the organization are:
Spirit: People grow spiritually through a strong relationship with the earth.
Stewardship: Consumption habits can help heal and restore the earth.
Justice: All people deserve a healthy environment, regardless of their race or income.
In addition to GreenFaith’s mission, the JJC Green Team prepared their own. Our mission’s core value is Tikkun
Olam: repairing or perfecting the world. We hope to impact the Jacksonville Jewish Center community through the
education and exploration of responsible environmental ethics and practices.
If you are interested in getting involved with the JJC Green Team please contact Michele Cohen at
jjcgreenteam@gmail.com.
