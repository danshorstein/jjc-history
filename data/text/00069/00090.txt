Shalom Baby
MOMMY AND ME WITH A JEWISH TWIST
Jami Shapiro
Shalom Baby Coordinator
The prenatal group meets bimonthly and covers numerous topics
including: Head to Toe Baby with a pediatrician; Ask a Rabbi - Baby
Naming and Bris; Everything You Need To Have To Bring Home Baby;
Nursing vs. Bottle Feeding; and more. The group is designed to bring
expectant parents together to form a new community and attract new
members to the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
The other new addition, Mother’s Tea, is for newly delivered babies up to
crawling and their mothers to come, relax, mingle and put their feet up while
being served tea and cookies. This group meets at 9 am on Monday mornings in the Youth Lounge.
Jami Shapiro, the group’s leader, is the mother of three girls ages 2-9 and comes to the position with lots of
experience working with children - both her own and as a paid Jewish professional. Jami’s been a Sunday School
teacher, camp counselor and youth group advisor. She also participated in numerous programs with her own children
and will be incorporating many of these aspects into the curriculum. If you are interested in any of our Shalom Baby
programs, please stop by the Preschool office
for more information, or call 268-4200 xl43.
Hope you can join us!!
The Jacksonville Jewish Center Preschool’s Shalom Baby program is in
its fifth year now. Classes meet weekly to offer caregivers and babies an
environment to play, learn and meet new friends. Each class offers songs,
stories, circle time, crafts and hot topic discussion. Shalom Baby will
continue on Monday mornings at 10 am for crawlers and 11 am for walkers
in the JJC Preschool. Shalom Baby has added a few new programs to its
offerings including a Prenatal Group for Expectant Parents and a Mother’s
Tea for brand new babies.
