I- A Message from the President
A COMMUNITY OF CONNECTION
Thank you for your continued support of the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
Each and every congregant and family listed in our yearbook is a valuable
member of our Center Community.
This is an exciting time in our history as we work to create a clear vision that
embodies a “Community of ConnectionWe are well positioned to build on the
strengths of the Center and create meaningful opportunities for participation and
engagement. Philosophically, this will require an adjustment in our thinking as
we strive to reach people “where they are” focusing on what resonates with them.
One of my goals is to provide avenues for sustainable connections that exist
outside of our building in the greater Jacksonville Community such as: partnering
with non-profit agencies; immersing ourselves in a singular cause such as food/hunger; coordinating with and
expanding the extremely successful Middle School Mitzvah program led by Edith Horovitz.
1 invite you to become involved and engaged in the process.. ..we want you to feel vitalized, connected
and “like I am making a difference”. We are fortunate to have dedicated Lay Leaders, Board Members and
Officers, Clergy, School Administrators, Synagogue Staff and countless Volunteers committed to the continued
success and growth of the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
Helen, Sophie, Charlie and 1 wish our entire Center Community a healthy and happy New Year.
Shana Tova,
Michael DuBow
21
