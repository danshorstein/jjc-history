Shana Tova!
These are exciting times at the Jacksonville Jewish Center. The
Center is comprised of many special people. Within these walls, many
small communities come together to create one large community that is the
Jacksonville Jewish Center. Our valued members engender a feeling of
Kehillat Kesher (Community of Connection), sharing their strengths and
skills, allowing us to benefit from their experiences.
We are blessed to have highly qualified, dedicated professionals and
devoted staff working hard to ensure that your experiences here are positive,
fulfilling and exceed your expectations.
Beginning this year, the goal of our lay leaders and professional staff is to
develop these relationships, broadening our community to encompass the greater Jewish and Jacksonville
communities; sharing our strengths and skills with other organizations, and individuals, that can benefit
from them.
We invite each of you to share your skills, your knowledge and your experiences. Join us as we build
upon our Kehillat Kesher. With the dedication of our volunteers and professionals, we are limitless in
what we can accomplish.
On behalf of Jim, Kyle, Katie and Joshua, I wish you a healthy, happy and prosperous new year!
Lori Schoettler
