The Samuel & Esther Galinsky Education Programs
PARENT - TEACHER ASSOCIATION
The JJC PTA works tirelessly to put together a full and enriching year for
students of all three Center schools: the JJC Preschool; the Martin J. Gottlieb
Day School; and the Bernard and Alice Selevan Religious School. The JJC
PTA contributes to the enhancement of our children’s educational experience
by providing programs/events, equipment, technology and so much more. The
monthly technology “Coffee Talks” and speakers at PTA meetings provide parents
with educational opportunities.
Some fun events to look forward to include: “Parents Only” Game and
Social Night, Father/Daughter Dance and Mother/Son Bowling. The PTA will
continue to provide services such as Vision Screening, Red Ribbon Week, Shabbat
Boxes, Holiday Observance and the publication of our yearbook. Our annual
Kaleidoscope Day, which is a day of art and cultural education, is always highly
anticipated by our students.
All of these services are possible because of your support through membership,
fundraising and donations. Don’t miss the amazing and spectacular Chai Family
Fun Run, our largest fundraising event for all three schools. Other fundraisers for
the 2010 - 2011 school year are the annual Magazine/Gift Wrap Sale and Discount
Cards. Successful fundraising benefits every classroom and every child.
Your PTA welcomes you and encourages your involvement. Become active
in developing and nurturing the future leaders of our Jewish community. Join us
and help make this the most fantastic year ever.
