EXCELLENCE IN EDUCATION
Over one hundred and forty people gathered on May 2nd for the Excellence in Education End of the Year
Celebration to enjoy a complimentary brunch and honor one of the Martin J. Gottlieb Day School’s most loved
teachers with the Rabbi David Gaffney Leadership in Education award. Family, friends, students and colleagues
came together to celebrate the success and impact of Judy Reppert and her outstanding 35 year career as a teacher
at our school. Former students Dennis Mizrahi and Sam Horovitz shared stories from their favorite teacher’s
classroom and described how her teaching tools not only helped them learn in her class, but in future classrooms
and in life as well.
The event was an opportunity to welcome the new Head of School of the Martin J. Gottlieb Day School,
Jon Mitzmacher, and highlight the wonderful achievements of the Jacksonville Jewish Center Preschool and the
Bernard and Alice Selevan Religious School. Jon Mitzmacher discussed his vision for the school and shared his
excitement for this upcoming year. Religious School principal Lois Tompkins and Preschool principal Shereen
Canady also spoke about the exceptional programming and educational activities that have occurred during the
past school year.
The Excellence in Education End of the Year Celebration was an opportunity to introduce the L’Dor V’Dor
Society to the community. The event marked the beginning of an annual fundraising campaign that will raise the
funds necessary to perpetuate the good works the Jacksonville Jewish Center schools are known for and to ensure
that the resources needed for the schools to continue to offer the highest quality of secular and Judaic currículums
will be provided.
