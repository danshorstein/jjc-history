summer programs. Scholarships to attend these programs are made possible through the generosity of the Abe
Selber Trust Fund, Charles Palot Youth Fund, Gordon Family Scholarship, Center Men’s Club’s Horty T.
Gendzier Summer Scholarship Fund, Center Israel Scholarship Fund, Center Sisterhood, Center Youth

Scholarship Fund and many anonymous contributors.

USY-ON-WHEELS
USY on Wheels, sponsored by United Synagogue of
Conservative Judaism, gives teenagers an opportunity to travel
around the United States seeing landmarks, historical sites and
national parks. In addition, they meet Jewish youth in the cities
in which they stay. Sarah Hunter, Hallie Morris, Jereme
Pozin, Sara Richman, Ben Whitman, Jon Mizrahi, Amy
Perlman, Zach Pozin, Laura Rogozinski and Melissa Wyzan
