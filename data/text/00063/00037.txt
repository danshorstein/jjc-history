5th Annual JJC Golf Classic
Big Win “Fore ” Jewish Education!
Since 1999, the Jacksonville Jewish Center Golf Classic has raised more
than $130,000 to provide financial assistance to families, regardless of synagogue
affiliation, who desire a Jewish education for their children at the Jacksonville
Jewish Center.
Considered one of the most prestigious
charity golf events, the JJC Golf Classic was again
played at the beautiful Ocean Course at the Ponte
Vedra Inn & Club on Monday, December 15.
Mel & Debbie Gottlieb, Honorary Co-chairs
and Sandy Zimmerman, Tournament Chair, were
proud to dedicate the 2003 JJC Golf Classic to
the memory of Dr. Roy J. Sloat, a lifelong
member and active supporter of the Jacksonville
Jewish Center and an avid golfer.
Each year, the community’s response to the Golf Classic is overwhelming.
For the 5th year in a row, the Jacksonville Bank and the Gottlieb Family
were the Title Sponsors of the tournament. Over 85% of our sponsors have
supported this event every year. Merrill Lynch was again the Event Sponsor.
Wachovia Bank was again the Front 9 Sponsor, while Wachovia Securities -
The Jaffe Group was the Back 9 Sponsor. Tournament organizers express
their appreciation to all the
contributors who made this event a
financial success.
Horty T. Gendzier was a
dedicated beacon for Jewish
education and champion of youth at
the Jacksonville Jewish Center and
the community. Once again she was
remembered and appreciated when
this year’s Waterford crystal award,
the “HORTY” was presented to the
tournament champions, the team
from the Jacksonville Jaguars.
Proceeds of this fundraising effort directly benefit scholarships for Jewish
education at the Center.
Credit for this outstanding event goes
to tournament chair, Sandy Zimmerman,
and committee members, Michael
DuBow, Rick Plotkin, Alan Rosner and
Seeman Zimmerman. Game day
volunteers, Linda Bloom, Marcia Jawitz
and Jeanine Rogozinski, helped make
it a great experience for all the golfers.
