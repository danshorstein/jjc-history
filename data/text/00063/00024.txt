Men’s Club Man of the Year Banquet
Sheldon Gendzier
The Center Men’s Club 5th annual Man of the Year Event was a huge success
and whole lot of fun as we roasted and toasted our honoree, Sheldon Gendzier.
The Men’s Club thanks the many Patrons, Friends and Special Contributors who
helped to make this such a successful event. The proceeds support the “Horty T.
Gendzier Summer Scholarship Fund”. A special thanks goes to this year’s program
chairman, Dave Schmerer, and the rest of the planning committee, and of course
to Sheldon for being such a good sport.

Men’s Club Yad Shel Chai Award
In 1998, Center Men’s Club instituted this award-winning program to
encourage youngsters to continue reading Torah after their Bar or Bat
Mitzvah. Teens who read a minimum of 18 {chai) portions by age 18 are
presented with an inscribed Torah pointer (yad) and a certificate. Over the
years, many teens have enrolled in this program, and the following have
received the award: Jordan Allerding, Jeremy Bailys, Sarah Blankstein,
Lauren Bloom, Crista Cohen, Jared Fleet, Aaron Finck, Ben Finck,
Seth Finck, Jared Fleet, Josh Fleet, Mark Frisch, Rachel Gerber,
Mathew Classman, Max Horovitz, Sam Horovitz, Dana Kresel, Debra
Mizrahi, Dena Mizrahi, Jonathan Mizrahi, Aliza Morris, Lindsay Morris,
David Organes, Daniel Passen, Lisa Rand, Avi Richman, Louis Richman,
Sara Richman, Ben Rogozinski, Erin Rogozinski, Jessica Rogozinski,
Josh Rogozinski, Rachel Rogozinski, Debbie Shacter, Jonathan Taimen
and Kyle Yining. For more information regarding this program, contact
Randall Smith at 565-4447.
