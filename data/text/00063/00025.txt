The Jacksonville Jewish Center
Men’s Club is dedicated to the enrichment
of Center life and the Jacksonville Jewish
Community. The Men’s Club provides
opportunities for the men of our synagogue
to work together supporting, sponsoring,
and participating in programs that bring
meaning to our slogan, “Involving Jewish
Men in Jewish Life”.
Our Men’s Club is an active member
of the Federation of
Jewish Men’s Clubs, an
international organization
dedicated to the ideals,
philosophy and traditions
of Conservative Judaism.
Through the support of
the FJMC, we distribute
hundreds of Yellow
Holocaust Memorial
candles every Yom
HaShoah to our
congregation. This year
we will be continuing the
successful “Hearing Men’s Voices”
program; a series of workshops dedicated
to creating a forum for discussion and
sharing issues relating to being a Jewish
man in today’s world. These workshops will
cover family, religion, society and health in
thought provoking and fulfilling agendas
led by leading experts and professionals.
We support our youth with
programs like the Yad Shel Chai (Hand of
Life) award that recognizes post-Bar and
Bat Mitzvahs who continue to read Torah.
We present gifts to the Confirmation
students, and a Kiddush cup to every Bar
and Bat Mitzvah. In addition, we donate our
time and financial resources.

We are visible friends of the
Center, with programs like selling and
building Sukkot for our Center families and
helping to decorate and maintain the
Center’s Goldman Sukkah. You can find
Men’s Club members
grilling and serving
refreshments at the annual
Lag B’Omer. ushering at
morning services on
Shabbat and Holidays, as
well as conducting
services on Men’s Club
Shabbat.
These events
have earned our Men’s
Club the First Place and
Honorable Mention Torch
Awards from the Federation of Jewish
Men’s Clubs in years past and we are
actively pursuing new Torch Awards for
community service and other activities.
The Men’s Club is also a great way
for friends to have fun. We have gone on
deep sea fishing trips and had family outings
to sporting events, such as the Jacksonville
Suns baseball and Barracudas ice hockey
games. In the fall, we “kick off’ our new
year with our annual Membership Party,
featuring lunch and raffles, while we watch
the Jacksonville Jaguars play an away game
on the BIG screen TV. Also, our recent pick¬
up softball games have been a huge
success, and we will keep on playing.
