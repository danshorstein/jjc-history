The Rabbi David Gaffney Excellence
in Education Award was presented to
Marilyn Sandler, for her 34 years of
involvement with the children of the
Jacksonville Jewish Center. Marilyn began
as a teacher in the Foundation School, became
the principal of the Solomon Schechter Day
School and then became the director of our
Preschool and Day Care. She richly deserves
this honor and the appreciation of hundreds
of Center children and parents.
