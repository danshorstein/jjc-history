The Gate Petroleum
Company of Jacksonville is proud to
own and operate four of the most
prestigious private membership clubs in
Florida- the Ponte Vedra Inn & Club, Epping
Forest Yacht Club, The Lodge & Club and The
River Club. From his premier portfolio of clubs,
an exciting membership has evolved . . . The
Gate Governors Club.
A membership in the Governors Club makes
it possible to enjoy privileges at any
combination of two or more clubs within the
Gate family. Club combinations are packaged
and attractively priced creating a tremendous
value.
Governors Club members enjoy such diverse
features as a world class marina, Atlantic Ocean
beaches, championship golf, tennis, fitness and a
luxury spa. A variety of outstanding restaurants
and an exciting social calendar makes this
membership a desirable choice.
For more information on membership
opportunities and benefits available with the
Gate Governors Club, please call
(904) 739-7200. We welcome your call.
