As the direct service arm of the Jewish
community, we are seeing the strain that the economy,
downsizing and anxiety has put on families. Our
emergency food pantry has seen more than a 200%
increase in demand, including an increase in the
number of families seeking food for Passover.
Our programs have grown in response to
demand for services. Staff has responded to requests
for ways to talk to children about war and how to
care for an aging parent. Services to senior adults
continue to be a priority for the agency as the
population (and some of us!) ages.
Expanding the Jewish Healing Network by an
additional 10 trained volunteers who serve an
increased number of clients has been gratifying to
all involved. The Jewish Hospice Connection was
projected to serve 5 clients in the first year, but we’ve
actually served more than 40!
The Board of JFCS is looking forward to
another year of service to our local community, and
wishing everyone a healthy, happy and safe new year.
Warmly,
Iris Young, Executive Director
JFCS Does What?
Adoption
Through our adoption process, adoptive parents
and birthparents are matched to create a loving
placement that fits the best interest of the child.
Children’s Programs
The children programs at JFCS, include school-
based programs, counseling, suicide prevention
and play therapy and personal counseling.
Counseling
JFCS is best known for its quality mental health
services. A staff of qualified professionals works
with individuals, couples, families and children.
Child Dependency Programs
JFCS is a provider of safe and nurturing homes
for children in the State of Florida’s custody.
Emergency Financial Assistance
JFCS distributes funds to individuals who are
having difficulty paying rent, utility bills or
deposits.

Home Is Where The Heart Is
Mount Carmel Gardens
apartments was designed, even to the
smallest detail, to provide
comfortable and pleasant
surroundings. From the initial
concept, Mt. Carmel Gardens has
strived to create respectable living,
providing more than housing. Mt.
Carmel Gardens offers a 17-story
building on a beautifully landscaped
site, 24-hour staffing, community
facilities, and planned programs, all
of which were developed as part of
an overall concept ...a concept of
responding to the desires of retirees
for security, comfort, safety,
independence and the enrichment of
stimulating social-recreational
activities shared with others.
Mount Carmel Gardens is
conveniently located, with
transportation, shopping, hospital and
doctor’s offices nearby.
