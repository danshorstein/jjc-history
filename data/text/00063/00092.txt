The Parent Teacher Association, first and foremost, promotes
the welfare of the children in the school, the home, the synagogue and
the community. The PTA is designed to create a relationship between
the parents and the teachers and to enhance the educational process
for all of the students. PTA provides the high quality programs,
equipment and other facilities through fundraising and service
programs.
Each year the PTA supervises the student magazine sale, giftwrap
sale, book fair, Red Ribbon Week for Drug Awareness, Kaleidoscope Day and vision
screening. In addition, PTA helps to enrich our children’s Jewish lives by providing
Shabbat and holiday boxes for use in the home by students and their families.
This year is particularly exciting with the newly renovated library. When our new
video lab is complete, it will be possible to view live broadcasts and video transmissions
in each classroom on televisions provided with PTA funds.
We look forward to expanding the horizons of our schools with help from Dr. Judy
Stoner, Director of Education/Head of Center Schools.
We will also make the most of our final year with our wonderful principal, Mr.
Willard Kennedy.
PTA support is essential for parents, teachers and students. Without our amazing
volunteers, none of this is possible. I am honored to be a part of this PTA.
L’Shanah Tovah,
— Amy Goodman
