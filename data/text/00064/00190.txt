Hagigot HaSiddur - Consecration
Shabbat morning, January 29, 2005
For weeks, students of the Aleph class of the Religious
School and the 1st grade of the Martin J. Gottlieb Day
School prepared a very special program for this milestone
in their Jewish education. Each student received a
certificate and a sweet treat from PTA, traditionally given
to mark a sweet beginning to their formal Hebrew
education. Beautifully covered siddurim , prayerbooks,
were presented to the students by their parents. In
addition, Center Sisterhood gave each student a bound
collection of Leagrams containing well wishes from
family and friends. Todah Rabah to all the teachers:

Religious School Graduation
Shabbat evening, May 20, 2005
The graduation ceremony for the 7th grade Etgar class
marked the completion of Center Religious School and
promotion to Beit Midrash. Students took part in the
Kabbalat Shabbat service and were presented with
certificates of completion.
