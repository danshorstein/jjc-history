Installation of Rabbi Jonathan Lubliner
The sanctuary was filled on the evening of Sunday, May 15, 2005
as we installed Rabbi Jonathan Lubliner as the spiritual leader of the
Jacksonville Jewish Center.
Rabbi Lubliner began his tenure at the Jacksonville Jewish Center last
August. His warmth, enthusiasm and broad spectrum of knowledge
immediately endeared him to the congregation. Through his manner and
considerable good humor, he has fostered a welcoming atmosphere in our
synagogue, where people can regularly learn lessons of Torah and Jewish
tradition, and find direction and faith for their lives. “To feel at home in one’s
synagogue is great,” Rabbi Lubliner observed, “To make Jewish teaching
come alive in one’s home and daily life is even better.” Rabbi Lubliner, his
wife, Susan, and their sons Avichai, Elior and Itamar, are welcome
additions to the Jacksonville Jewish Center and the community.
