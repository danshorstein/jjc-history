The primary obligation of JFCS is to serve
the Jewish community. We provide hope,
opportunities and choices leading to better
tomorrows.
Our mission is rooted in Jewish values and
the commitment to support and assist our
neighbors. Maintaining clients’ dignity is a
top priority. Each individual is treated with
respect.
JFCS offers professional, sensitive
assessment and treatment to help people
address various issues and feel better about
themselves. JFCS staff understand the nature
and intimacies of Jewish life and are sensitive
to Jewish values and ethics.
The Board of JFCS is looking forward to
another year of service to our local
community and wishes everyone a healthy,
happy and safe new year.
Warmly, Iris Young, Executive Director
