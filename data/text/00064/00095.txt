Scouting
Senior Girl Scout Troop 969
The World of Senior Girl Scouting is exciting and challenging. Senior Scouts
begin to function as individuals, with a more substantial leadership role. At this
level, the adult leader takes an advisory role, ensuring the girls continue to
develop into promising young women. The girls learn to make decisions based on
how they can contribute to their communities and set long-term goals. This serves
as the inspiration for hundreds of community service projects.
The Senior Troop 969 participated in a wide variety of opportunities designed to
increase skills and responsibility, while earning interest patches. The “Cookies &
Dough” patch helped the girls reach their goals for the Annual Cookie Sale. It was
fun to simulate business problems and see how creative the girls could be in
finding solutions. The “Stress to Success” patch led to interesting discussions
about what causes the girls to feel anxiety. We brainstormed ways to handle
pressure and learned yoga techniques to help feel more relaxed.
