Maintenance & Housekeeping Staff
The maintenance and housekeeping staff works diligently to keep our
building clean and presentable at all times. Richard Ford, our facilities
supervisor, takes pride in his staff, and devotes much of his time to taking care of
all the physical needs required by the many events which take place throughout the
year. Volunteers, event planners and Center staff appreciate the special attention
they receive from the maintenance department. Richard and his staff offer
invaluable support to the members of our congregation.
