Willard & Deedie Kennedy
Dear Members of the
Jacksonville Jewish Center:
The word “thank you” seems so trite
in light of the wonderful evening
afforded both my family and me. I
find it hard to fully express my deep
appreciation for both the evening and
the personal time that was spent by
so many on behalf of the raffle.
The entire evening defined the term
“kvelT for us and makes a lasting
memory of my years at the Center.
The true winners of the evening were
the students of the Galinsky
Educational Programs. The support
of the patrons and others provide the
resources to continue to meet the
educational needs of the students.
For that, I also thank all of you.
I wish the Center continued success
as it continues to provide leadership
and resources to enhance and
provide quality Jewish Education.
With respect and admiration,
Willard M. Kennedy
