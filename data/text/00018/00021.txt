Chairman of the Board

to me as Chairman of the Board. I appreciate
the honor and the opportunity. In this particular
capacity, however, I have little to add to what
the President of the Congregation placed before
you in his well chosen words on the previous page.
To all our members we extend wishes for a
Happy and a Good New Year on behalf of all
the Officers and the members of the Board of
Directors and the Board of Trustees.
“Le Shanah Tovah Tikatevu’.
ROBERT J. GORDON
