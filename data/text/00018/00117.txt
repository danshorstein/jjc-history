Sisterhood Activities
(Continued from Page 29)
This year we held Hanukah and Pesah In¬
stitutes which attracted a very large at¬
tendance. We are happy to report that as a
result of these Institutes more Hanukah
Menorahs were lit this year and more Seders
held in our homes.
Sisterhood is affiliated with the National
Women’s League from which we get guidance,
information and suggestions enabling us to ful¬
fill our program. Our group was one of the
largest represented in the Regional Convention.
