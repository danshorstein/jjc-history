Our aims and objects: Allegiance to the U.S. of America, to encourage honor and
purity in public affairs, to combat the powers of bigotry whatever their target and fight
whenever the Jew is unjustly assailed.
To honor the memory of our heroic dead and decorate their graves annually, to
participate in patriotric function and parades, proudly displaying our Color, crowned with
the Star of David alongside the American Flag. We just concluded our 64th Annual
convention in New York and we quote the words of our own member, U. S. Senator Jacob
H. Javits: Our organization is the Nation’s oldest active veteran group and responsibility
carried is a dual one, both as a veteran organization and as the patriotic arm of U. S. Jewry.
VICTOR G. HUBSCH
