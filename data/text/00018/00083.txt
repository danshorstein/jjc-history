The Center Day Camp of 1959, Camp Yom Tov, attracted youngsters between the ages of 6 to 13 with 169 campers
taking part in the various activities offered. Instructional swimming under the guidance of the Red Cross was a major
activity.
A baseball team composed of the Senior Boys played in the City Recreation Baseball League and finished in second
place for an excellent first year performance.
Highlight of the summer was the “Presentation of Activities Night’, where each camper showed his talents to the
300 or 400 parents present. (See Other Pictures on Page 123)
