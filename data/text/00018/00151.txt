The Jacksonville Section is one of 240 sections of the National Council of Jewish Women
in the United States.
The Council continues many projects for a healthy relationship with our community
such as weekly visits to the Children’s Home Society and the Juvenile Shelter, the Medical
Loan Shelf which provided wheelchairs, crutches and hospital beds to indigent patients,
Sewing Committee, services to the foreign born, volunteers for civic drives and social legis¬
lation program. Jacksonville Section announces a new project, the testing of hearing of
children by means of Audiometers.
Overseas Council also trains leaders for welfare and educational work in Israel and
sends clothing and toys to children in Jewish institutions abroad.
Jacksonville N.C.J.W. meets every third Wed. of the month, with the evening group
meeting every fourth Wed. of the month. On October 21 the opening luncheon will be held
to celebrate the 20th anniversary of Jacksonville Section, N.C.J.W. Every Jewish woman in
Jacksonville is invited to join and share in the experience and satisfaction of helping others
who are in need of assistance.
