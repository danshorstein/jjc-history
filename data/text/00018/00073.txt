I his past year (1958-1959) has been very successful. We sent 16 delegates to the annual U.S.Y. Leadership training Institute at
Camp Blue Star, Hendersonville, N.G.; 3 delegates were sent to National Convention held in Buffalo, N.Y.; 20 members went to the
regional convention in Hollywood, Fla. At this convention Stephen Entman was elected President of the Southeast region and Barry Sinoff
was elected Treasurer.
A few of our many programs were talks given on the “K.K.K.” and '‘Judaism's contribution to Medicine/’ Our C.Y.L. Sabbath even¬
ing services were enjoyed by all. We also had discussion groups, parties, and money raising projects.
This past April third, our chapter was host to the regional board meeting at which time Marcia Leibovitz, Judy Safer, and Stewart
Goldberg were appointed committee chairmanships.
Our year was closed with an Installation Banquet. At this time the annual awards were presented for outstanding girl and boy in
C.Y.L. The awards were presented to Miss Marsha Kramer and Stewart Goldberg. The banquet was followed by a semi-formal dance.
Our deepest appreciation for their help and understanding during the past year goes to Mrs. Louis Slott, Stanley Kantor and Joel
Selber. We would also like to thank the Center Sisterhood for their assistance during the past year.
