SIGMUND S. HAUBEN
The Mr. & Mrs. Club of Jacksonville is composed of approximately 100 couples who meet socially twice a month. These affairs
include a small get-together and a large dinner-dance affair handled by committees composed from the membership. Husbands and Wives
serve together on these various social events and govern the affairs of the club.
The outstanding events of the year were the Installation affair which included the awarding of the “Itzik (Oscar) Awards,” the sum¬
mer picnic for the members and their families, and the New Year’s Eve Party.
Since its inception six years ago the club has established itself as one of Jacksonville’s leading social organizations and has attempted
to bring about a closer fellowship among the Jewish couples in our community.
Interested couples are requested to contact any of the officers or members for details concerning membership.
