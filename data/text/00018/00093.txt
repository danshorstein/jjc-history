The Couples Club is a social club affiliated with the Jacksonville Jewish Center, and membership is open to all young married couples.
During the past year, The Couples Club has sponsored many enjoyable and varied events, with capable committees from the member¬
ship planning each monthly affair. The outstanding social event of the year was the New Year’s Eve dinner-dance, complete with all
the trimmings.
We of the Couples Club feel that we have had a most successful year and have provided all our members with sociability, enter¬
tainment, and lasting friendships. The Club takes this opportunity of welcoming all newcomers and prospective members to join its ranks.
