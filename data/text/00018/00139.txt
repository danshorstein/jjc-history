The importance of the Zionist Organization of America to the State of Israel, as well
as to the American Jewish Community, is a matter of serious consideration. The entire
concept of Zionism is somewhat misunderstood by the general public. The Zionist Movement
was never a purely political one in its broadest aspects.
Zionism represents a deeply rooted will for survival, not only physically but culturally
as well, and it is that phase of spiritual distinctiveness that Zionism represents more than
any other organized Jewish group.
It is a concept of prophetic peoplehood transcending religious or social inclinations and
philosophies, and uniting us into one indefinable cohesive group, no matter where we may be.
Zionism stands for a safe and strong Israel and for a creative Jewry the world over.
