MRS. ABE HOROVITZ
The forty-fifth anniversary of Ladies Hebrew Sheltering Aid Society this year recalls our revered founders, their vision and their
untiring efforts to establish, in this community, a resident home for the aged of our people. With the establishment of the River Garden
Hebrew Home for the Aged, thirteen years ago, their dream became a reality. As the auxiliary of the Home, we are proud to nurture
its growth and to help to give expression to its goals, which include total personal adjustment, activity, self-expression and individual dignity
as vital adjuncts to physical care.
Ladies Aid has completed another successful year, both financially and servicewise. Funds are raised through Tombola, Donor and
Social Printing projects as well as annual dues and Life Memberships in Ladies Aid. Proceeds from these projects are used to furnish
and decorate the Home, provide kitchen, infirmary and Hobby Shop equipment, and to finance improvements in service facilities as needed.
Our annual Linen Shower provides for the Home’s linen supply.
We continue, as in the past, to give personal service to our residents through our Gray Ladies program, and regularly scheduled
entertainments and parties. Resident participation projects, such as the residents’ newspaper, Rhythm Band and Ceramic Classes are served
by volunteers of the Auxiliary, and Ladies Aid provides a professional instructor for the Hobby Shop in addition to the volunteer assistants.
As sponsors of an annual Blood Bank Night, we fill a vital need for the residents in time of emergency.
Synonomous with our name has grown our motto, “Service with a Heart.” Honoring our founders, we rededicate ourselves and
pledge our continued devotion and service to River Garden and its residents, wherever and whenever needed.
