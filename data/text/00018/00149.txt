DENNY WOLFSON
The Esquire Club has had a very successful year under the leadership of Denny Wolfson, first semester, and Jerry Wolfe, second
semester. We wish to thank the mascot, Nancy Brooks and our advisor Stanley Kantor, for all their efforts on behalf of the club.
Several outstanding social events were held this year including the annual Holiday Dance at the Beauclerc Country Club and the
Esquire Weekend at the Beach.
Esquire’s service projects were headlined this year by a show put on for the children at Hope Haven Hospital, as well as a party and
gifts given for their pleasure. When Esquire was called upon to help with Cerebral Palsy Telethon, the boys worked untiringly through¬
out the entire program, giving of their time and money.
