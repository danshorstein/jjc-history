The Progressive Credit Union, sponsored by the Jewish Progressive Society, has continued
to grow in its 20 years of operation. It is one of more than 10,000 Credit Unions in the United
States, having more than 10 million members and more than two billion dollars in assets.
From a very modest beginning of 20 members and $500.00 in assets, we have grown to
over 350 members and more than $200,000.00 in assets. During our existence we have made
over 2000 loans to members, amounting to over $1,500,000.00 without a single loss. We are
organized to encourage thrift and provide a safe, convenient medium whereby members can
invest and accumulate savings. We are chartered by the State of Florida and regularly exam¬
ined by the Comptroller of the State, and the savings of members are safe-guarded in
several ways.
MOE B. SAFER
Life Insurance equal to a member’s savings up to $1,000.00 and Loan Protection Insurance is paid by the Progressive Credit Union
without any cost to the individual member. The climax of each year’s activities comes with the annual meeting held in the Jacksonville
Jewish Center auditorium. Last year the Board declared a 2% dividend on savings and 15% rebate of interest to borrowing members.
