MRS. IVAN ISAACS
Brandéis University was opened in 1948 as the first Jewish founded non-sectarian institution of higher learning in the Western
Hemisphere.
In seven years 90 chapters have been established with a membership of 55,000. The money secured through memberships and contri¬
butions to the Book Funds is used to meet all the needs of the University Library, so there is no fund-raising.
The Women’s Committee notes the development of Brandéis with pride. The women throughout the country have made incalculable
contributions by fostering good will for Brandéis in their respective communities. The university has expanded its student body, gathered
a distinguished faculty and realized its building program. With the establishment of Brandéis, Jews in America for the first time have been
enabled to serve as the host to students of other religious affiliations, as in the past colleges and universities sponsored by other faiths had
Jewish students as their guests.
