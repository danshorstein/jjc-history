This was Installation year in Sisterhood and
a new slate of officers was elected to guide
Sisterhood through the coming two years.
To date we have a membership of 549.
Our projects this year included financial assis¬
tance for our Center Youth League and our
Girl Scouts, provided funds for the salaries of
the teachers in our Sunday Religious School,
furnished prizes and awards for pupils of the
Hebrew and Sunday Schools and provided re¬
freshments for special celebrations. In order
to carry out our projects and programs we
have raised monies through dues, Bazaar,
luncheons, sale of World Book Encyclopedias,
the Sisterhood Book and Gift Shop, and sale
of Barton's candies and delicacies.
This year’s “Donors for Beautification" was
most successful and the funds raised will be
used to purchase equipment for the new school
rooms and to decorate the chapels.
Our Gift Shop had a very outstanding year
and has been instrumental in fulfilling Sister¬
hood’s objective of strengthening Judaism and
making it more meaningful through the Syna¬
gogue and the Home.
Through the Torah Fund we contributed
financial aid to the Jewish Theological
Seminary.
