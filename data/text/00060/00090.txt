The Restoration 2000 campaign was initiated last year with the goal of restoring our facility to a
first class standard for the 21st century. This exciting and innovative project is on schedule to be
completed, in large measure, in time for the Center’s Centennial Celebration in December 2001.
A newly restored lobby, social hall and auditorium are major components of the Restoration
2000 project, which also includes new
skylights, wall coverings and much Du Bow Family
needed structural repairs. |
