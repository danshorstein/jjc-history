Day School/PreSchool PTA

The PTA of the Solomon Schechter Day School and the Center Pre-school is proud to
support our children and their education. We work closely with our teachers and our
administrative staff to enhance the programming of our school. By joining the PTA and
volunteering to help in any capacity, we not only enrich our children, but we also make
lasting contributions to our schools and make many wonderful friendships.

Our major fundraising efforts include the magazine sale, book fair, Passover candy
sale, and Library birthday party. Last year’s fundraising was so successful that we purchased
amyriad of new equipment and instructional enhancements for our schools, including Science
Lab supplies and equipment, a new Ellison Lettering machine, supplies for the music program,
the Accellerated Reading program, and Reading manipulatives for kindergarten through third
grades, a Library Laser scanner, a cot for the sick room, a costume closet for school plays,
and a brochure for the pre-school. We also contributed funds to each classroom and purchased
books for the library.

Some of the educational and enrichment programs we provide are: Holiday observances,
such as Shabbat boxes, weekly Challah orders, helping with celebrations of Rosh Hashanah,
Chanukah, Purim and Passover, as well as Kaleidoscope Cultural Arts Day, Red Ribbon
Week for Drug Awareness, Vision Screening, Library Assistants, and Mystery Reader.

We appreciate your commitment to the PTA. Whether you volunteer your time or give
your financial support, the result is always a successful educational experience for our children.

L’ Shana Tovah,
—Sue Levine
