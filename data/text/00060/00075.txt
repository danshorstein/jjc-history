Sunday Sports - Basketball Program

 

The Sunday Sports Association (SSA) provides area youth with alternative athletic programs on
Sunday afternoons. All the games and practices are held in the Fletcher-Selber Auditorium of the Jacksonville
Jewish Center. Thank you to Scott Zimmerman who coordinated a very successful program and to the
volunteer coaches for their valuable assistance and time. We look forward to an exciting 2001-2002 season.
