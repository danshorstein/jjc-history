About the Cover:

A 100 YEAR JOURNEY TO CROWN POINT ROAD:
An interpretive painting by Raymond Cohen

The painting is a panorama describing in graphic detail

the growth of our congregation from a modest beginning
to a nationally recognized Synagogue Center. The
illustration is interspersed with vignettes of important |
people, structures and events that marked the development
of the congregation over these 100 years.
