Saae Confirmation
Celebrated 6 & 7 Sivan 5760 ¢ May 27-29, 2001

The Biblical festival of Shavuot commemorates the giving of the Torah and Ten Commandments to the
Jewish people. It is natural that this festival should be celebrated with an accompanying sense of commitment to
Jewish life that Confirmation represents. Confirmands demonstrate the knowledge gained through the completion
of our Hebrew High program by full participation in Shavuot services. In addition, this class read essays they
had prepared on major events and people in the 100 Year History of the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
