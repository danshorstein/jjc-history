2nd Annual JJC Golf Classic

Fundraiser is a Major Success

 

Proceeds to Provide Scholarships

The JJC Golf Classic played December 18th, at the Ponte Vedra Ocean
Course, was not only a great financial success, but a GREAT TIME was had by
all who participated. A sold-out field teed off in glorious weather, on a beautiful
course. Camaraderie was high and the competition was keen. Congratulations to

all.

The credit for this outstanding program goes to the hardworking chairmen
and committee. Additional thanks goes to a great crew of tournament-day
volunteers: Robin Allerding, Allison Bailys, Gayle Bailys, Linda Bloom, Helen
DuBow, Debby Fialkow, Edith Horovitz, Lynn Maiman, Jeanine Rogozinski,

Suzi Weiss and Lynn Zimmerman.

 

The Horty T: Gendzier Cup was presented by Scott
Allerding, Tournament Chairman (ctr) to the tournament
winners (1 to r) Terry Jaymes, Brian Kelly, Lex Staley
and Jim Kennedy with Bruce Horovitz (r).
