The Early Childhood Department of the Jacksonville Jewish Center is
designed to nurture the inquisitive, outgoing spirit in our children. We help
them to be happy with themselves and others, to discover and to grow.
Meeting the emotional, educational and physical needs of each child is
central to the goals of the Early Childhood Department. Our aim is to
develop a positive attitude toward school that will continue into the
elementary years.

Dale Schemer This year our Preschool went through the re-accreditation process with the
ees Florida Kindergarten Council. We have once again received a passing report
filled with outstanding praise from the accreditation committee. We are
proud of the fact that we have continuously maintained our accreditation since 1986.

The FKC committee members reported that our staff was “qualified, friendly, cohesive and dedicated to the
well being of the students. Children as well as parents were very happy with the Preschool as evidenced at the
drop-off and pick-up time.” In addition, they reported that the Preschool should be “commended for an
outstanding Judaic and Computer Science Program.”

Our school and extended day program are committed to excellence in education and childcare. We are proud
of our dedicated and loving staff that provide a challenging and stimulating educational environment in which

children have the opportunity to grow and reach their full potential.

We look forward to the 2001-2002 school year and the opportunity to once again provide our students with the
very best preschool and extended day experience.

— Dale Schemer
