It was an evening of nostalgia, an evening of fond remembrances, an evening of high humor, an unforgettable
evening for Rabbi Gaffney, his family and all of us who were privileged to be there. Linda and Laurie DuBow were
consumate chairpersons of the event. Margo’s dinner was superb. Each speaker, from the youngest to the oldest, gave
tribute with their individual account of their personal association with our Rabbi, lauding his accomplishments, his
spiritual leadership and their friendship. The slide retrospective, narrated by Laurie DuBow, was a tour de force of epic
proportions. It was an apt tribute to 29 years of devoted service. It was an evening to express our wishes of a future with
much happiness and “God Speed” from all of us, and from all of those who made this evening possible.
