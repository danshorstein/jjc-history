Committees & Organizations
The Jacksonville Jewish Center Programming Committee has concluded 5778 with an amazing concert by
the Maccabeats, a Jewish a cappella group, that was fun for all who attended. Encouraging and supporting
our congregants to make personal connections with one another while having a good time is our mission
when programs are being created. This year we were able to plan some great events to get people excited
about being in the building and a part of this wonderful community. The Hanukkah program, Lag B’Omer
picnic and several trivia nights were just a few of many events this year that were successful community
FUN-raising events. The Programming Committee looks forward to working alongside the Membership and
Social Action Committees this year to ensure terrific programming and turnout for all the special programs
and events that take place here at the Jacksonville Jewish Center. Get ready for new events as well, both big
and small
Please contact the committee chair, Faye Hedrick at fayemhedrick@gmail.com with any ideas, questions,
or to join in on the planning for a specific program or event.
We look forward to seeing you and your family taking part in future events offered at the Jacksonville
Jewish Center.
