MEMBERSHIP COMMITTEE
People join the Jacksonville Jewish Center for
many reasons. With many life cycle events
changing our need for Jewish connection, the
reason people join is often not the reason they
stay. In an environment where there are more
and more ways to engage Jewishly, which is
wonderful, there is a reason our members call the
Jacksonville Jewish Center home year after year.
We have a special community and it is because
of our members. Without you, our synagogue
would not exist.
Become one of our member ambassadors! Our
ambassadors are dedicated and focused on
supporting all members, new and long lasting,
build meaningful relationships, feeling valued
and welcomed.
Let us know how we are doing and please join
us - EVERYONE IS WELCOME!
