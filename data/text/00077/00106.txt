Galinsky Academy
l’dor v’dor annual giving campaign
L’Dor V’Dor - from generation to generation - reminds the
Jacksonville Jewish Center and greater community annually
of the importance of supporting programs for Jewish youth
including Center youth groups, religious school, Camp Ki
Tov, DuBow Preschool and Martin J. Gottlieb Day School.
This year was an incredible celebration of the 75th anniversary
of our beloved DuBow Preschool, which led to outstanding
community support.
The Galinsky Academy is especially thankful for the
dedication and hard work of the event co-chairs, Faye
Hedrick and Haley Trager, in making the 2018 L’Dor V’Dor Celebration and Awards Dinner
so meaningful and special! Thank you to all our staff, volunteers, teachers, and distinguished
guests for creating a truly unforgettable evening and celebrating 75 years of quality education.
It is truly a testament to the commitment of this community to see the broad support for
both Jewish Education and the DuBow Family. It is wonderful that the children can be a part
of such a loving and inclusive community. In addition to receiving the support of our Jewish
Community, it was thrilling to have so many non-Jewish community leaders sharing this
milestone, which emphasizes the importance of early childhood education in our city. Our
DuBow Preschool provides a loving and nurturing environment for children and families,
regardless of religious affiliation.
As we strengthen the bonds across and within Jacksonville’s philanthropic community,
we help the people who call this place home. Let us all push our philanthropic work forward
together and continue to ignite the spirit of helping each other from generation to generation.
