►— Martin J. Gottlieb Day School
STEAMING AHEAD!
STEAM—hands-on, multidisciplinary learning agendas across classrooms and grade levels, have been popular
at the Martin J. Gottlieb Day School. We began our journey two years ago, actively engaging our students
around subjects in Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Math—all subjects where the creative process
is paramount and growing students who are innovators is the goal. Exposure to a range of life skills is the
byproduct of a STEAM-infused classroom, including the ability to problem solve, persist, and collaborate. By
emphasizing student collaboration on challenging and co-curricular projects, both students and teachers are
able to go outside the traditional classroom to enhance student learning.
Since its inception, the STEAM program has grown tremendously in both scope and resources—not least
due to grant awards and the support of generous donors. This year, we are STEAMing ahead as our STEAM
Lab moves to a more prominent location in the day school and, more importantly, is staffed by a director tasked
with overseeing the integration and implementation of STEAM-related pedagogies and practices at both the
Dubow Preschool and the Martin J. Gottlieb Day School. We are looking forward to an even more exciting
year filled with lots of STEAM-infused learning for all our students. Please look for our MakerCon Faire later
in the school year for a taste of STEAM. We hope to see you there!
