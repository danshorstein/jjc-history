Kids Club
CARRY ON THE LEARNING AND FUN INTO OUR EXTENDED DAY PROGRAM!
The DuBow Preschool and the Martin J. Gottlieb Day School are pleased to provide, in the 2018-2019 school year,
a revamped after school program for children ages one through fourteen. We offer morning care from 7:00 am until
school begins and after care until 6:00 pm. We also offer top notch winter, spring and summer camps. We will provide
engaging, enriching days full of exploring and fun when school is not in session.
During our morning care session children are able to eat breakfast, play and spend time with friends. For our
older students along with socializing and eating breakfast they are also able to study before school begins.
The learning through play and fun continues into our afternoons each school day. We are open until 6pm each
night, except for Fridays, when we close at 5:30pm to give our faculty, students and families time to prepare for
Shabbat. This year we are excited to offer several new daily clubs for our students from VPK-8th grade. Aside from
clubs they will also be provided a snack, homework help and time for outdoor sports and activities. For our DuBow
Preschool students we will be offering soccer, dance and tumbling courses as part of our after care program along
with our weekly themes. Students enjoy the cooking activities, science experiments, art projects and playground fun
coinciding with each theme. We are pleased to provide care that will aid in developing children’s cognitive, social,
language, and motor skills through play based learning and hands on experiences in a loving, nurturing environment.
