BARK MITZVAH
On October 22, almost 50 dogs, accompanied
by their owners, joined at the JJC Ansbacher
Pavilion as we performed a myriad of
meaningful and moving Bark Mitzvah
ceremonies to mark the special coming of age
moment for dogs who are roughly 13 years old
(2 in dog years). Dogs wagged their tails as they
were called up to receive their Hebrew names
and received a naming certificate to mark the
occasion. Ancestors Toto, Snoopy, Odie, Pluto,
Scooby Doo, Lady, Tramp, Rin tin tin, Hooch,
and Lassie, were recognized as these dogs were
granted blessing in all the worthy endeavors of
their paws, with all owners everywhere.
