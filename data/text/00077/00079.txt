SHABBAT AT SEA III
This past January, fifty five members of the Jacksonville Jewish Center
community went all aboard Royal Caribbean’s Majesty of the Seas for
third installment of Shabbat at Sea, or as Hazzan JJolzer would dub it,
Shabbat at Sea Part III. Many of our cruisers were traveling via cruise
ship for the very first time, but we had a core group of repeat cruisers
from our past cruise experiences. Our group ranged in age from infant
to those in their 90s, with everyone keeping themselves busy while on
board this 4 day adventure. Participants enjoyed the sights and sounds
of Nassau, Bahamas and while our relaxing day on the beaches of
Cococay was rained out, we experienced the Jacksonville Jaguar’s
miraculous pounding of the Pittsburgh Steelers from the pool deck.
There were variety shows and entertainers on board, but it was the
group experience that people enjoyed the most. Our Friday night acoustic service literally rocked the boat.
Our Saturday morning Torah study kept our minds working as we delved into what it truly means to have a
day of rest. Shabbat at Sea is a getaway we hope everyone gets a chance to take!
