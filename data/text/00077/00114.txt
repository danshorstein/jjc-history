SWINGING AROUND IN THE TREEHOUSE
Bring your baby to class! The DuBow Preschool Treehouse offers Parent & Me Classes
for ages 2 months- 2 years old. These classes (anywhere from 3-6 weeks per session) give
participants the opportunity to “dip their toes” into the preschool and learn and grow in
all areas of development. Treehouse is always aiming to add new and interesting classes
for parents & babies. Two fixtures of the Treehouse include Family Music & Movement
(since 2013), a wonderful first class for parent and baby as music is one of the earliest
ways to bond with baby and build social & language development and Shalom Baby, a
monthly free playgroup centered around Shabbat traditions and includes story time, music,
and crafts.
Treehouse has also offered: Theatre for Babies (experiencial theatre for babies 3-12
months old), Color Splash Class (sensory class), Baby-Sign-A-Long (sign language),
Stroller Workout, and Parent & Me Yoga. Treehouse classes run on different mornings of the week all 12 months of
the year with many classes at full enrollment and waiting fists expected. Visit treehouse.dubowpreschool.org or www.
facebook.com/dubowpreschooltreehouse or call 904.292.1241 to obtain schedule information or to view photos and
details on all of the classes. See you in The Treehouse!
