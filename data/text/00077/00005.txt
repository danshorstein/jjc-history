This year’s cover is a piece of art created
by Ricki Dayan. Ricki is a native Israeli that
transforms her love of Israel and Judaism into
creative masterpieces from the heart. Ricki
donated the image of this painting in honor of
Natalie and Joey Dayan and their children Eli,
Simon, Ben, and Nicole in gratitude for the
kindness and sense of community shown to
them by the Jacksonville Jewish Center during
their time in Jacksonville.
