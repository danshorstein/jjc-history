In Memory ~ David Wolinsky
David Bernard Wolinsky, z"l
1959-2018
On February 17th, our Ritual Director and B’nai Mitzvah Tutor, David Wolinsky, passed away
after a serious illness. David spent his lifetime pursuing his passion and commitment to Judaism.
Professionally, David served as a Cantor to several congregations, most recently at Agudath B’nai
Israel Synagogue in Lorain, Ohio, and Temple Am Shalom in Mentor, Ohio. David also served the
Jewish Community in Park Synagogue in Cleveland Heights, Ohio, in his role as Ritual Director
prior to his arrival in Jacksonville and the Center in 2015.
David loved nothing more than being a Jewish educator, providing pastoral care and support to
his congregants, his community and celebrating his faith through his music.
David is survived by two beautiful daughters, Shaina Wolinsky and Emma Wolinsky, and his
sister, Maya Herscowitz, of Akron, Ohio. They will remember David most fondly for his love of
music, animals and the Jewish Community to which he served.
As we remember David, we recall the fun way he taught Torah at the Martin J. Gottlieb Day
School and the Bernard & Alice Selevan Religious School. Through his work as our Ritual Director
and the skillful way he imparted Torah reading skills to our b’nai mitzvah students, David Wolinsky
touched the lives of children, parents and congregants throughout the entirety of the Jacksonville
Jewish Center community.
His presence will be missed.
