The Joni Shmunes “Mensch of the Year” Award was set up
as recognition for a graduating 7th grader from the Bernard
and Alice Religious School who displays a commitment
to Jewish studies. In memory of our beloved teacher, Joni
Shmunes z”l, we will continue each year to honor students
with consistent attendance and a sincere willingness to learn
in Religious School - especially one who puts his or her
heart and soul into learning about their Judaism despite any
challenges or roadblocks along the way! We are very proud
to recognize Elizabeth Rutansky as the honoree for the
2017-2018 school year.
