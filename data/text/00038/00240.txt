The Jacksonville Jewish Center takes great pride in its Daily Minyan. Through
the efforts of the captains and co-captains we have minyans every morning
and evening.
The minyan is of the utmost importance for many of our members who wish to
pray daily in the synagogue and for those who have yahrzeit or are bereaved.
Members of the Jewish community come to depend on the Center’s minyan.
The Center would like to thank all of the men who give of their time and to the
Captains and Co-captains who devote endless hours calling men to “make a
minyan”.
Captains, Ben Edwards and Norman Moss
look over the Sidra of the week.
