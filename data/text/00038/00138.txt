This past year B.B.G. has done very well, we’ve had plenty of activities. Everything from Ice and Roller-
skating to a trip to Disney World. This past December we had our annual No-Talk-A-Thon which raised
$300.00 for the March of Dimes. We’ve had creative services and a surprise breakfast that all turned out
very well. Our biggest event of the year was our Sweetheart Heart Throb Banquet and Dance.
Many plans have been made for the coming year. It is very hard to get in touch with the girls in Jackson-
ville. So, if you are a girl who is in the 8th through 12th grade and you’re interested in having a great time,
please contact Mona Schaffer, 731-0204, or Juli Shapiro, 733-1219. We would love to have you.

AZA Officers
This year AZA has proved to be Jacksonville’s finest young men’s or-
ganization. After a slow start, AZA managed, through the dedication of its
members, to accomplish many fine projects and programs.
Realizing that the treasury was low the group set out to fund-raise. This
years raffle winners were, Rose Lodinger, Rabbi Dov Kentof and Margie
Treece.
Jacksonville’s AZA and BBG hosted a leadership convention, where
young men and women from all over Florida came to learn the skills of
leadership, and to make important plans for the year. “Sweetheart
Hearthrob” was the best ever. The group turned the Beauclerc Country
Club into a tropical paradise. While dinner was being served, live enter-
tainment was provided by Rocky and Renee Morris. The highlight of the
evening was when Mona Schaffer was chosen as “Sweetheart”.
