Religious Services — Traditional prayer services are held
daily, morning and evening in the Witten Chapel. Friday
Family Services, Sabbath and Holiday Services are observed
throughout the entire year.
Personal Religious Observances — Traditional Jewish
customs are observed in connection with Circumcision,
Naming of Girls, Pidyon Ha-ben, Bar Mitzvah, Bat Mitzvah,
Confirmation, Marriages, Conversion to Judaism,
Bereavement.
Education — Daily Kindergarten and Nursery with
outstanding program of secular and religious content.
Hebrew School, carefully graded with intensive Hebrew
content, including Jewish history and customs, Sunday
School for kindergarten, first and second grades: Solomon
Schechter Day School, a graded secular and Hebrew
School, 1st through 6th grades; Hebrew High School with
Pre-Jr. and Junior Congregations and Minyanaire.
Extension Services — Library, outstanding collection of
juvenile and adult Jewish books; Adult Education, religious
and cultural Jewish courses; music programs of religious and
cultural Jewish music; P.T.A.; Sisterhood and Ha’Rayim, a
fellowship of young families; social activities provided by Mr.
and Mrs. Club, Single Adults; Family Recreation Program
(Olympic pool), summer weekdays and Sundays for all ages.
Youth Activities — ATID for college age youth; United
Synagogue Youth, various age levels are served by Center
Youth League, Charles Palot Club, Kadima, Boy Scouts,
Cub Scouts, Girl Scouts, Brownies; Sunday Funday;
athletics, programs for youth and adults; Center Day Camp
and summer program at our 36 acre site on San Jose Blvd.
Family Recreation Program — Center Campsite . . . swim-
ming, athletics, snack bar; available for members during the
summer months.
