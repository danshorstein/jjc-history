Thousands of years ago, Moses or Moshe Rabbeny
(Moses our teacher) found his way to Mount Sinai. The
Commandments he brought down still provide the same
impact upon Hues today.
On Sunday, March 4, 1979 at 10:00 a.m., Moses once
again brought the message of the Ten Commandments to
the descendents of these same Israelites. He appeared on
the roof of the Hecht-Gordon-Bossen School Wing of the
Jewish Center to hand down the same message he
brought from Sinai ages ago.
How joyous it was to hear and see Moses, and a great
celebration took place at that time. Children of our Sunday
School participated in this momentous occasion as did
their parents. Moses also made an appearance for the
Day School and Afternoon School students.
Our forefather Abraham visited the
Jacksonville Jewish Center. In his tent
he told the children how he destroyed
the idols his father sent him to feed, and
how he entertained the angels in his tent.
Abraham (Rabbi Dov Kentof) said he is
planning a return visit, as nomads move
around and do return to good places. Our
students are looking forward to rejoining
him to learn more about his life and times.
