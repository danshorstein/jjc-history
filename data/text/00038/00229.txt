Jewish Women’s
Bowling League
The Jewish Women’s Bowling League has been
in existence for 20 years. Bowling began at
Plaza Lanes and is presently held at Bowl-A-
Rama. The league is open to all Jewish women
of the community and all are invited to join.
Bowling is every Tuesday morning beginning
at 9:30 a.m. The only requirement is that you
establish a bowling average. For information
call Bea Caplan at 744-2607. Spend a delight-
ful morning with some very charming and
vivacious ladies.
