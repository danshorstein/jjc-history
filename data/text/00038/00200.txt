The First Annual Alexander Brest Tennis Tournament
was played this year.
Twenty-six mens teams and 12 ladies teams participated.
Each player received a T-shirt designed by George Strum-
lauf. The winners were Bud Shorstein and Leo Jacobson,
Wilbur and Bennie Margol and Ann Shorstein and Florence
Haft. Each winner was presented with a trophy. Sincere
thanks to the Tournament Committee which consisted of
Ronnie Pollock, Chairman, Sherwin Burman, Arthur Gold-
stein, Stephen Jerard, Shelley Kulchin and Marsha Pollock.
We thank these contributors for their support.
