CEMETERY COMMITTEE
OF THE
Jacksonville Jewish Center
The Cemetery Committee provides a very necessary function of the Jacksonville Jewish Center. It is very important that
the Cemetery grounds are well kept and provide a peaceful resting place for our loved ones.
Our Cemetery Committee is co-chaired by Joe Becker and Jack Becker. Serving on their committee are Max Becker,
Herman Bloom, Eugene Henry, Hymie Selber, Alan Taffet, Sumner Waitz and Dr. Joe Witten.
Mr. Kenneth A. Bowden, Controller of the Jacksonville Jewish Center, also serves as general manager of our Cemetery. It
is his responsibility to oversee the upkeep and maintenance of the grounds. He has an excellent working relationship with Mr.
Jennewine who is general manager of Evergreen Cemetery. Mr. Jennewine has been very generous with his time and his
staff, Mr. Campbell, Mr. Miller and Mrs. Phillips. They have all taken excellent care of the Center’s property, aboe and beyond
what is expected. We appreciate everything they do.
Johnny B. Mercer has been custodian of our Cemetery for over 19 years. Mr. Bergeron of Southern Monument Co., has
always been extremely helpful to us too. We are very grateful to all these people who have given so generously of their time.
