The Jacksonville
Jewish Center Kadima
hosted a convention of
approximately 200 teen-
agers (6th, 7th, and 8th
graders) on February 2,
1979. Youth from Miami,
Clearwater, Sarasota,
Tampa, St. Petersburg,
Gainesville, Orlando,
Lakeland, Atlanta, Geor-
gia, Charlotte, North
Carolina participated.
The theme of the con-
vention was “All Begin-
nings Are Difficult”, The
Bar/Bat Mitzvah Years.
Through various me-
diums such as socio-
drama, parent/teen
panel discussions, inti-
mate study groups, the
many aspects of this
period in a young per-
sons life were explored.
The convention made
excellent use of all our
facilities. Many of the
Kadimaniks described
the convention as “the
best yet!”
