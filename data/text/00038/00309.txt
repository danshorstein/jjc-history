TRIBUTE
The new logo around Jacksonville is
“Capture The Spirit”. Not only does it apply
to our city, but it certainly describes Don
Morris’ photography.
As the official photographer of the Jackson-
ville Jewish Center, Don Morris has given
generously of his time and equipment to
“Capture The Spirit of the Jacksonville Jewish
Center” and all its activities.
It has truly been an educational and delight-
ful experience working with Don. No matter
when I have asked him to come to the Center
he has been there. His cheerful disposition
and good humor have made the job of editor a
very pleasant one. Cheers to 100 more years!
—Isabel Balotin,
Editor
