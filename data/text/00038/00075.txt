TORCH
RUN
The First Annual Torch Run took place at the Jacksonville Jewish Center
on December 24, 1978. It was carried by the members of our various religious schools
and youth groups, from St. Augustine Road to San Jose Boulevard. The torch was then used
to light the Menorah, made by Rabbi Dov Kentof. This huge menorah was erected in front of
the Center to symbolize the light of freedom and hope for our people. A very moving
ceremony and candlighting program ensued. Members of the Board of Directors,
Sisterhood, youth groups, each lit a candle. Judge Louis Safer spoke about
“Freedom For All People”. The blessings were chanted by Raymond Cohen.
Each day of Hanukah, before Minyan, one flame was lit until eight candles burned.
