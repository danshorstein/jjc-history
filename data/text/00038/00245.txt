EDUCATION AND
YOUTH BANQUET
Approximately 250 youngsters and their
parents participated in our first annual
Education and Youth Banquet. Awards and
certificates were presented to our young
people in Junior Congregation and in the
Youth Department. Many appreciations
and thanks were given to our teachers in
the various departments of our educational
system.
The Day School dance group presented
some Israeli dances, and a special inter-
pretive dance was presented by our USY
and Kadima members.
