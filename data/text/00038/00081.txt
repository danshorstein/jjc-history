ADULT STUDIES
Rabbi Gaffney along with the Adult Education Committee began a most ambitious Hebrew Literacy
Campaign and adult study at the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
The Hebrew Literacy was committed to make basic reading of Hebrew a skill for every adult. Classes
were available every day, morning and evening. A staff of professional and skilled lay people instructed
these classes in a pre-determined format.
The Adult Education classes included Jewish Ethics and Values, Hebrew Folk Dancing, Basic Jewish
Skills, Major Forces in Jewish History, and study groups. The Sisterhood Bat Mitzvah Class continued
through its second year and is now preparing for a Bat Mitzvah in the fall.
The Sisterhood and Center Library Committee co-sponsored Library Shabbatonim. The purpose
was to bring to the Jewish Community, current books, and periodicals through the medium of book
reviews, debates and panel discussions.
The film series included such films as “The Fixer” and “The Apprenticeship of Duddy Kravitz” and
more. Every aspect of the Adult Study was a success. We thank Rabbi Gaffney for all the work he did to
expand our Jewish knowledge.
