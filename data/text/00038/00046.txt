In an attempt to serve the entire congregation, a Sukkah Mobile was erected on a
flat bed truck. Parents and youth of the congregation spent many hours working on it,
under the supervision of Rabbi Dov Kentof. The Sukkah Mobile traveled all over the
city bringing the spirit of Sukkot to River Garden, Mount Carmel and to our members’
homes and sister congregations.
