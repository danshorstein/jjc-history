The Solomon Schechter Day School has a bicultural
curriculum of Hebrew learning and General Studies. The
highly structured yet individualized program in General Studies
has proven to be an outstanding education. The students have
continued to score high in achievement tests and are con-
tinuing to experience success in their classwork after leaving
our school. The classes are particularly impressive in terms of
Hebraic and Judaic accomplishment. The upper classes are at
the highest Judaic achievement level in the history of the Day
School.
The Solomon Schechter Day School of the Jacksonville
Jewish Center provides all Jewish children in the community
an outstanding General Studies education and a rich and
rewarding Hebrew-Judaic education. We are extremely proud
of our Nursery, Kindergarten and six grades. Our teachers and
the Education Committee of the Center have worked hard and
should be credited with the excellent progress of the school.
Marilyn Sandler, Principal
