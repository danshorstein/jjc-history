Dr. Irving Greenberg
One of the major highlights of the Center’s calendar
year was the extraordinary Shabbaton weekend
held April 20-22. Sponsored jointly with the Com-
munity Council (Federation), we had as our guest
“Scholar-in-Residence” Dr. Irving Greenberg, who
is the Director of the National Jewish Conference
Center. Dr. Greenberg spent a full weekend with us
lecturing on contemporary Jewish issues. On
Sunday Rabbi Greenberg spoke at the opening of
the Holocaust Museum. The entire community
participated in the day’s events.
Presented by The Jacksonville Jewish Center
and the Jacksonville Jewish Community Council
