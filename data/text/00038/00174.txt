Camp Ramah
The summer is the best time for learning
and growing. The Jacksonville Jewish
Center and the generosity of two'
families have made it possible for many
youngsters to attend Camp Ramah for a
totally Jewish experience in a very
relaxed and enjoyable environment
of a summer camp. Donations made
through Sisterhood and the Center
to the Myra Thier Lazarus Fund during
the year are also used for this purpose.
