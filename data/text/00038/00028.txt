The Sunday Funday program for
1979 showed overall growth and
confirmed improvement. There
were 157 participants in 10 classes
and 10 basketball teams. A new
program that was added this year
was the Fun-Time program for pre-
schoolers. Also successful was
the Arts and Crafts Program and
Teen Disco. All the positive results
from this year’s outstanding pro-
gram demonstrates the benefits
derived from the truly co-operative
efforts of staff volunteers and
participants.
We thank Shelley Kulchin who
served as co-ordinator and Ira
Goldfield who co-ordinated the
basketball program. Well done!
