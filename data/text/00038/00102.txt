ORT of Jacksonville
ORT, the Organization for Rehabilitation through Training, is an international organization which for
a century has brought freedom to world Jewry. ORT operates in 30 countries with 76,500 students learning
90 trades in 800 training units. They represent people gaining an education and a future career in archi-
tectural drawing, telecommunications, avionics, hotel management, machine design, computer techno-
logy and more. Special programs are available for the physically handicapped, the aged, and language train-
ing programs for immigrants bound for English speaking countries. Different courses for different people
with different needs and qualifications are offered.
Jacksonville Area Council helps support the ORT program and consists of four Chapters each meeting
at different times to serve the needs of all the women in the community. The Area Council is the body which
services the four ORT chapters.
