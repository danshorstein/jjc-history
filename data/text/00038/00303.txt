TO OUR ADVERTISERS:
Our constant gratitude, not only for making the publication
possible, but also for the support they lend to our Youth
Activities. We urge our members to reciprocate with their
patronage.
• A reference and guide for the Jacksonville Jewish Center
membership and the Jewish community.
• Serves over 7,000 members and advertisers.
• All proceeds devoted to the Center’s program.
• The only Jewish publication in the community listing all
groups.
The Year Book is a summary of the various activities and
program of this Synagogue-Center depicted in pictures and
words. We are grateful to the many organizations of this
community who have cooperated in providing us with
material used.
We appreciate the efforts of our staff and other members of
the Center who have devoted time and effort on behalf of our
1979-80 Center Yearbook.
