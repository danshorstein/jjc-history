Our children are very important to the future of Judaism.
Once our children go to college we want them to know that
their synagogue still cares about them.
This year Rabbi Gaffney and Rabbi Kentof directed some of
their energies to establishing new lines of communication with
our college students. News was sent to them about Israel
and the American Jewish Community. Readings in Jewish
thought, holiday packages, Sabbath candles and wine were
also sent.
Through this effort the Center tries to convey to our young
adults who are away from home that the Jewish experience
can take place anywhere.
The reaction to the “care” packages was fantastic. Corres-
pondence is received daily from the recipients. This will con-
tinue for many years to come.
During winter, spring and semester breaks the group has
been getting together for Shalosh Seudot, discussions and
socials. Below are pictures of a recent canoe trip taken by
