Dale Schemer
Director
The Early Childhood Department of the Jacksonville Jewish Center is
entering its 49th year of providing quality programs to Jewish children in the
community, regardless of affiliation. Our ever expanding program offers not
only a Florida Kindergarten Council accredited preschool, but also includes an
extended day program available to all children enrolled in the Preschool and
Solomon Schechter Day School.
Our Preschool continues to offer the best secular and Judaic early childhood
program for children 12 months to 4 years of age.
This past year was a rewarding and exciting one. Our enrollment grew
and new programs were added, including computer classes for Nursery and
Pre-K students.
The Extended Day and Giant Steps programs saw a number of changes. New staff
was added, beginning with Cheril Clevette, our new program coordinator. Under her able
direction, the Extended Day Program provides outstanding before and after school care
with many new activities.
On behalf of the Preschool and Extended Day staff, I wish everyone a healthy and
happy New Year.
— Dale Schemer

Retirement Announcement
Ann Young retired this June after teaching for 28 years at the Center Preschool.
Ann has shaped the lives of hundreds of children with devotion, creative teaching and
affection. She has been a thoughtful co-worker, mentor to younger teachers, and a
good friend and advisor to parents of her Pre-Kindergarten students. Her students love
her for her winning smile, gentle guidance and genuine understanding. She will be
greatly missed and we wish her well in her retirement.
