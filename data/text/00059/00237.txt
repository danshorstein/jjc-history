About the Cover:
ESSENCE
by Raymond Cohen
Painted in oil, “my design for the yearbook cover
was intended to create an impression that would
capture the ‘essence’ that is my good friend Ted
Gross, and to illustrate his impact on our
congregation over the years.”
The 2000-2001 Center Yearbook
is Dedicated to
Theodore Gross
for over 50 years of devotion to the religious
education of our youth.
