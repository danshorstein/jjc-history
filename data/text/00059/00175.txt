Sunday, April 9, 2000
An enthusiastic crowd welcomed the return of Joel Fox, to the Center
stage. He sang his way from the 50’s through the 90’s — “some Jewish stuff,
some not-so Jewish stuff, all fun stuff!” — from his repertoire of traditional
and contemporary music in English, Hebrew, Yiddish, Ladino, French and
Italian. With his infectious enthusiasm, Cantor Fox involved the audience in
singing and spontaneous dancing. It was a thrilling performance and a
wonderful ending to a great entertainment series.

Sunday, October 10,1999
The Center was filled to
capacity for the American debut of
this talented ensemble, direct from
Moscow. Turn-Balalaika presented
a blend of musical and dramatic
entertainment taken from Yiddish
literature, Russian folklore and
popular music. The troupe of
versatile, gifted actors charmed the
audience, singing in English, Hebrew, Yiddish and Russian. Special thanks
to Dr. Lazar (Lonya) Pinker for making the Turn Balalaika performance possible.
