— The Samuel & Esther Galinsky Educational Programs —
Solomon Schechter Day School
We all look for inspiration from time to time. It might be
from a family member, a rabbi, hazzan or respected teacher; it
might be from a book or a piece of text. We can also find
inspiration by walking through the halls of our school. Who
cannot be proud when the first siddur is presented to an eager
student? Who doesn’t marvel at our Science exhibits, Jewish
History Fair, Seders and art projects? Who can’t be inspired by
a thriving Jewish community of students, motivated teachers
and committed parents?
We are proud that we are doing our part, not only to ensure
quantity, but quality as well. While there is room for
improvement, I hope you agree that we do some remarkable
things at Schechter.
Such accomplishments do not take place in a vacuum. They are due to the combined efforts of
our dedicated faculty, as well as the commitment and work of our volunteers and supporters. The on¬
going work of our PTA, our committee members and our board cannot be minimized. The passion of
these individuals for the sake of our students provides us with tremendous inspiration.
As we look to the future, it is up to each and every one of us to maintain the excellence and the
standards that we have set at Schechter. Then, too, each one of us can be an inspiration to others.
— Willard Kennedy
