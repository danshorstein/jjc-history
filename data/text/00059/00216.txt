Youth Activities
White Water Rafting Experience
Led by Rabbi Dov Kentof
As always, this year’s White Water Rafting Trip was FANTASTIC! As in the past,
two rivers were explored: the Nolichucky and the French Broad. Everyone had a blast,
and made it home with all their body parts intact!
In addition, participants camped, held discussion groups, went tubing and prayed
in beautiful country settings. The memories made on this trip will last a lifetime, and
strengthen Judaism for these participants, in a unique way.
Jewish Community Programs for Youth
This year Rabbi Dov Kentof applied his talents to two community activity days sponsored by the Jacksonville Jewish
Educators Council and the Jacksonville Jewish Federation. Building on programs presented by our Family Council, Rabbi
Kentof was able to bring the same high quality activities to the entire Jewish community.
Families boarded “Noah's Ark” for a re-creation of his journey. Then visited Noah's tent, fed his
animals, learned about ancient life and even rode a camel.
The Community Field Day brought kids
together for athletic activities.
