Daisy Troop 176
Daisy Girl Scout Troop 176 had a fun-filled year! We renewed friendships that we hope will last forever We had
a great time with the many projects, activities and sing-a-longs. The girls especially enjoyed learning to sew, paint and
plant. The goals set by the Girl Scouts of America program were easily achieved by these caring, sharing and
ambitious young girls. We had a great group of girls that will make wonderful Brownies.

Brownie Troops 258 and 882
Brownie Troop 882 had another wonderful and busy year.
During this year, the girls worked on many different “tryits”: animal,
creative composing, dancerize, good food, puppet, doll and plays,
and safety. They learned about each; then they either wrote or
discussed the different topics, explaining how, as Girl Scouts, it
related to them and their families.
At our annual Hanukkah party at Mt. Carmel we entertained
and gave gift baskets to the residents. Baking hamantaschen for
Purim was a delicious lesson.
We sold lots of boxes of cookies for the annual Girl Scout
Cookie Sale. Our troop also donated boxes of cookies to the Mandarin Food Bank, and received a patch for Community
Service.
The Brownies had great fun at the Girl Scout camp, Camp Chowenwaw. The girls enjoyed a roaring campfire,
and plenty of “sommores”. We walked along the shoreline of Black Creek, sang songs and had a great weekend!
It has been a great year and we are all looking forward to our next year of great activities.
