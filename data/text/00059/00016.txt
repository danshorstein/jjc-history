Special Events
JJC Golf Classic
A Hole-In-One for Education
The JJC Golf Classic, played last November at the Ponte Vedra Ocean
Course, was not only a great financial success, but a GREAT TIME was
had by all who participated. A sold-out field teed off in glorious weather,
on a beautiful course. Camaraderie was high and the competition was keen.
Congratulations to all.
The tournament included a captain’s choice format, fabulous food
and great prizes. The day concluded with an awards ceremony and silent
auction. Proceeds of the event will be used to fund scholarships to the
Solomon Schechter Day School.
