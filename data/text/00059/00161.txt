Sunday Sports - Basketball Program
The Sunday Sports Association (SSA) provides area youth with alternative
athletic programs on Sunday afternoons. All the games and practices are held in the
Fletcher-Selber Auditorium of the Jacksonville Jewish Center. Thank you to Gayle
Bailys who coordinated a very successful program and to the volunteer coaches for
their valuable assistance and time. We look forward to an exciting 200-2001season.
