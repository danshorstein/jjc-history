Abraham Joshua Heschel Leadership Group
This year the Jacksonville Jewish Center formed the Abraham Joshua Heschel Leadership
Group guided by Rabbi David Gaffney and Rabbi Dov Kentof. This unique group is made up of 43
Jewish teenagers who are serious about their religion and intent upon taking an active leadership role in
the synagogue and the community.
Through discussion groups, guest speakers, services, and other programs centered on leadership
themes, this group of young people takes positive steps towards their goals. The group involves itself in
serious issues and has become the core of leadership for the Center’s youth.
Among its activities this year, the
Heschel Group organized food
drives, hosted an outing for the
Russian/American community
and observed Havdalah and
holidays together.
The Heschel Group held a leadership seminar with representatives of the major Jewish agencies
College Outreach
Through special mailings, the
Education and Youth Department keeps our
college students up-to-date on what’s going
on back home, as well as informed of Jewish
issues around the world. Seasonal packages
also contain holiday treats. In addition, Rabbi
David Gaffney meets with students of the
University of Florida at the campus Hillel,
every year, to help keep them connected. If
you would like your college student included
in this program, contact Stella Finck, who
maintains the mailing list and facilitates the
mailings.
