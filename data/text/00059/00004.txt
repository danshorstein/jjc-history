Margo’s Catering
Following a very successful year as preferred caterer at the Jacksonville Jewish
Center, Margo Ruby’s Catering has entered a lease arrangement with the Center to
be the exclusive caterer for all in-house, congregational and private functions.
The same high quality menus and beautiful presentations that have been enjoyed
this year at life-cycle receptions and parties, will now be available to Center organizations,
clubs and schools, as well as for congregational celebrations and holidays. In addition
Margo’s Catering will service the Day School lunch program, board dinners and other
occasions.
While Margo’s Catering will provide complete and personalized food planning
services, Lori Schoettler will coordinate and schedule all aspects of event planning.
Maintenance & Housekeeping Staff
The maintenance and housekeeping staff work diligently to keep our building
clean and presentable at all times. Richard Ford, our Facilities Supervisor, takes
pride in his staff, and devotes much of his time to taking care of all the physical needs
required by the many events which take place throughout the year. Volunteers, event
planners and Center staff appreciate the special attention they receive from the
maintenance department. Richard and his staff offer invaluable support to building
restoration crews and outside vendors.
