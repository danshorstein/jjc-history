Annual Center Raffle for Education
“Money Mania” reached a feverish
pitch, as hundreds of people participated in
the festivities at the Jacksonville Jewish
Center’s Annual Raffle for Education.
Event Co-chairs Stuart Herman and
Allison Bailys along with their entire raffle
committee delivered a fabulous evening. The
highlights included a spectacular buffet by
Margo’s Catering, a wind-blown money tunnel, various
game show vignettes, entertainment by the UNF Jazz
Combo and of course the drawings for valuable prizes .
Honorary Chairs Kim and David
Robbins were presented a special gift from
the Center, by President Mark Shorstein,
for their long-time support and dedication
to the raffle.
Congratulations to the law firm of Shorstein &
Kelly P.A. which won the $10,000 Grand Prize. Early
bird drawing winners Deena and Jim Richman received
2 badges to the TPC courtesy of Coca Cola. Many others
won fabulous prizes contributed by these generous
business:
The big winner was by far the Samuel and Esther
Galinsky Educational Programs which will reap the
benefits of this major fund-raiser.
By all accounts, this year’s raffle was a huge success.
Everyone enjoyed the food and the format and, most
importantly, the financial goal of raising $50,000 for
education was reached thanks to the generosity of our
patrons and ticket holders. An exciting live auction netted
an additional $6,000 for the evening’s proceeds. Everyone
associated with this year’s raffle is to be congratulated
for a job well done.
