MRS. LEON SCHINASI
The Brownie Troops and Girl Scout Troops sponsored by the
Center Sisterhood show a great deal of growth in the number of
girls and leaders who now make up the troops. We now have 5
Brownie Troops and 2 Girl Scout Troops.
Scouting is a foundation for our youth which enables them to
grow into womanhood with the knowledge of getting along with
their neighbors and the customs and way of life of other children
around the world.
Our mothers give their time and knowledge to these groups of
children and help them have a full rewarding childhood.
