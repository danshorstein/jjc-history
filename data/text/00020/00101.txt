If success is determined by numbers, then the Center’s youngest
social club, the Tween-Agers, can boast of an outstanding year with
92 active participants. The members of this co-ed group are 5th and
6th graders meeting monthly.
The success of their varied activities is due to our Activities
Director Bob Fischer, and to the capable leadership of the mothers’
committee composed of Mrs. Fred Linsey, Mrs. Charles Kluglose,
Mrs. Abe Newman and Mrs. Lillian Speizer. Special thanks go to
Mrs. Bernard Glossman and our CYL sponsor, Karen Speizer, for
their untiring efforts.
