The Zionist Organization of America is the only overall Jewish
movement throughout the world which encompasses the totality of
Jewish life with Israel as the central point of gravity.
Before the establishment of the State, our main goal was a politi¬
cal one. Now the cultural phase of our program as well as public
relations, is the foremost task. We continue the struggle for the
revival of the Hebrew language wherever a Jew may live. The
Zionist idea permeates the overwhelming majority of Jews every¬
where . . . Regardless whether one has Orthodox, Conservative or
Reform religious affiliation, or perhaps none at all, he may never¬
theless be an ardent associate and supporter of the Zionist Move¬
ment.
The American Zionists have accomplished a fine and remarkable
record of good public relations between the American people and
the State of Israel.
The Z.O.A. welcomes all Jewish men to participate in its ac¬
tivities.
