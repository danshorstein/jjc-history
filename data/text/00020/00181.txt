1961 was a banner year for the A. B. Weil Lodge of B’nai
B’rith. Membership and activities increased and the lodge received
an award for outstanding service to the Duval County Sheriff’s
Office. A successful bowling league was formed, tours made of the
Naval Air Station and the County Jail as well as tremendous philan¬
thropies such as free medical care in our hospitals, Hillel foun¬
dations in colleges, the ADL, and scholarship loans.
The men of B nai B’rith are working together in the constant
struggle to make our community a better place to live.
