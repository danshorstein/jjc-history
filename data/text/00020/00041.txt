The Center Men’s Club is looking forward to its 17th year of
existence with renewed dedication.
Looking over the achievements of last year we find the Men’s
Club accomplished the following: three scholarships for Camp
Ramah, flags for Simhat Torah Hakofot services; paid-up member¬
ship dinner, Blood Bank drive, adult Jewish education, Sunday Re¬
ligious School picnic, Minyan breakfasts, monthly meetings and
dinners.
The Center Men’s Club has shown itself to be a very vital part
of our Jewish Community and we are striving to widen and improve
our services to you and your children.
The new administration which includes officers, board of direc¬
tors and executive committee composed of men who have been out¬
standing in their zeal for the organization is ready to initiate many
new activities while continuing with traditional programs. At our
monthly dinner meetings we will again serve delicious meals and
present programs on religion, sports and politics. Dinner meetings
are held the first Thursday of the month.
All men interested in affiliation may contact one of the officers
or come to visit us at regularly scheduled dinner meetings.
