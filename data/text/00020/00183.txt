DAVID WEINTRAUB
A.Z.A. climaxed a successful year under the faithful guidance of
Advisor Sandy Whitman.
We were hosts to a social week-end sponsored jointly by A.Z.A.
and B.B.G. and attended by members of both clubs from Daytona
Beach and Orlando.
We ended the school year with a Sweetheart Dance and the
crowning of our new sweetheart, Miss Dianne Arnold.

LESLEY KIDDER
B’nai B’rith Girls, junior order of Bnai B’rith Women, had
many activities this past year. They included visits to Hope Haven
Hospital, candy sale, rummage sale and several bake sales. We had
our first annual Installation Luncheon with the best member award
presented to Debby Hoffman by the President of B’nai B’rith
Women.
At the Florida Regional Convention Judy Weiss and Lesley
Kidder received offices. Judy Weiss and Louise Gutman also received
offices at the North Florida Council Convention.
