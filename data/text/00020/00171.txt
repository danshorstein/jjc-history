LEWIS MEISEL
Financed by Community Chest-United Fund, United Jewish Wel¬
fare Fund and the Levy Memorial Fund, the Jewish Welfare So¬
ciety is the Family and Children’s Case Work Agency of the Jewish
community. Last year it served 181 local families and individuals,
and 159 non-residents.
Its main purpose is to counsel families and individuals and to
provide resources to alleviate problems that threaten their well be¬
ing and to help prevent such problems. The scope of problems in¬
cludes parent-child, marital, emotional, economic, health, aging,
employment and conflict with law.
It also counsels and help maintain unmarried mothers through
their pregnancy and places children for adoption. It licenses, main¬
tains and supervises foster homes. It processes the applications and
provides the case work for the residents of the River Garden Hebrew
Home for the Aged. It does the resettlement work for the Jewish
Community and assists with immigration problems. It provides fi¬
nancial assistance to local families and assists transients, and pro¬
vides a limited program for prisoners at Raiford.
We are grateful for your response to our need for foster homes
and jobs and to Mrs. Sanders Tofield, who joined our staff in Octo¬
ber I960 and who so ably helped secure them for our clients. These
and the need for "Big Brothers” are pressing and continuous. If
you can help, please call us.
