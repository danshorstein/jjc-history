With ever increasing popularity our summer program continues to grow as can be witnessed by our record breaking enrollment of
162 campers average per week and outstanding program. Among the new program additions were the following:
* In addition to our Midget Baseball Team—we now have a
team in the Tadpole City League.
* A C.I.T. program for boys and girls 14 and 15 years of age.
* Ice skating and archery for a new addition to our activities
list.
* Our Tadpole Team winning the city championship in their
first year of competition.
* Color War and the Annual Awards Night were the closing
highlights of the third successful summer under the direc¬
tion of our Activities Director Bob Fischer.
