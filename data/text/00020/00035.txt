MRS. WM. ROSE
With the advent of the New Year, our Sisterhood will begin
more than 40 years of service to local Conservative Judaism. We
have just completed a successful year in which we raised funds
with our Donor to finish furnishing our school building. We
were also able to fulfill our obligation to the Center toward pay¬
ment of Sunday School teachers and assisting with the Youth
program.
The coming year brings new obligations. Our funds are de¬
rived largely from dues and any fund raising activities that we
might hold. This year we hope to have a fall dance and a gala
Purim affair. We are also continuing the sale of Barton’s candy.
It is your support with the purchase of tickets and voluntary help
in planning that makes for our success.
Speaking for the officers of the Sisterhood, may we wish you
a very happy and prosperous New Year. We hope we will see
you at all Sisterhood functions, so that together we may continue
to help the growth and progress of our synagogue.
