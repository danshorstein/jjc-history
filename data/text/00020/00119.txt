The Mr. and Mrs. Club of Jacksonville is now in its eighth year.
It is one of the community’s outstanding social organizations. With
a membership of 100 or so couples, the Club attempts to create an
atmosphere of congenial fellowship among the Jewish couples of the
community.
Two social functions are held monthly, one is an informal
small affair, while the other is an elaborate dinner dance. All social
affairs are arranged by committees selected from the membership.
There are two outstanding affairs held during the year, one the New
Year's Eve Party and the other the Installation Banquet. Couples in¬
terested in affiliation are requested to contact any of the officers of
the organization.
