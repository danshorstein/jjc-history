President's Message
An outstanding highlight of Center history this
past year has been the traditional concept of partici¬
pation by Klal Yisroel (laymen) in synagogue activi¬
ties. The zeal and diligence of our officers, commit¬
tee chairmen and laymen have continued to improve
the religious life, educational program and youth ac¬
tivities of our Center. To all of these people and to
our loyal staff I wish to extend my sincere thanks for
their invaluable aid in the administration of our insti¬
tution.
It was with great pleasure that we announced that
the position of Rabbi in our congregation was assumed
by Rabbi Moshe V. Goldblum. I take this means to wel¬
come officially Rabbi and Mrs. Goldblum and their
children, Simeon, Charles, David, Liba and Ora to our
midst. Later in the year, we plan to have an outstand¬
ing installation affair for Rabbi Goldblum. In the short
time he has been here, his warm sincerity and genuine
devotion have imbued those with whom he has come
