Youth Activities
Center Youth Organizations
Center Youth League—A co-ed USY Chapter for high school age, meeting bi-weekly with
varied programs, affiliated with national movement.
Charles Palot Club—USY Chapter similar to the above, with programs geared to Jr. High
school students, also part of national movement.
Boy Scouts Troop 14—For boys 11 to 14, meeting weekly.
Cub Scouts, Pack 14—Boys 8 to 11 participate in this organization, meeting weekly at
Den Mothers’ homes with monthly Pack meeings.
Girl Scouts, Troop 197—For girls who have outgrown the Brownies and wish to continue
Scouting.
Brownie Troops 28, 193, 210 & 346—A Scouting organization for younger girls with
weekly gatherings.
Major’s Pre-Teens—Social club, meeting monthly for 7th grade boys and girls.
Tween-Agers—Boys and girls of the 5th and 6th grades participate in this social club
meeting monthly.
