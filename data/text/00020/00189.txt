The Progressive Credit Union sponsored by the Jewish Progres¬
sive Society and chartered by the State of Florida, has continued
to grow in its 22 years. The combined membership of Credit Unions
in the U.S. totals more than 10 million members and has over
four billion dollars in assets.
From a very modest beginning of 20 members and $500.00 in
assets, the Progressive Credit Union has grown to over 350 mem¬
bers and more than $200,000.00 in assets. It is organized to en¬
courage thrift and provide a safe, convenient medium whereby mem¬
bers can invest and accumulate savings. Being regularly examined
by the Comptroller of the State, the savings of members are safe¬
guarded in several ways.
Left to right: Israel Lipson, Moe B. Safer, Joseph Cohen and Eugene
L. Henry.
Life insurance equal to a member’s savings up to $1,000.00
and Loan Protection Insurance is paid by the Progressive Credit
Union without any cost to the individual member. The year’s ac¬
tivities climax with the annual meeting at the Center.
Last year the Board declared a 3% dividend on savings and
10% rebate of interest to borrowing members. A cordial invitation
is extended to anyone who wishes to join.
