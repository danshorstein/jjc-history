Serving the entire Jewish community of Jacksonville, the Jewish
Community Council is the central Welfare Fund and Community
Relations body of the local Jewish community. An association of
individuals, synagogues, organizations and social service agencies, the
program encompases key activities which give unity, cohesion and
coordination to local Jewish communal life.
A community-wide annual Welfare Fund Campaign provides
support to significant local, national and overseas organizations. The
National United Jewish Appeal, has expressed American Jewry’s
responsibility for the welfare and rehabilitation needs of Jewish
communities the world over for over 20 years. River Garden He¬
brew Home for the Aged and the Jewish Welfare Society are the
local beneficiaries.
