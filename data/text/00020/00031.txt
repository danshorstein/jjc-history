Building Fund-1962
The generous Building Fund contributions made by the Center Family over
the past years have provided us with one of the finest physical plants in the South¬
east Region, whereby we can carry on our progressive educational, religious, social
and youth activities programs. The continued support of our Center Family is
needed in order to meet the Building Fund needs for 1962 which are as follows:
1. Closing deficit for 1962 approximately $23,800.00
2. Mortgage payments 24,900.00
3. Building renovations 5,000.00
Robert J. Gordon, Chairman
In order to maintain properly the building, a minimum
of $5,000.00 is set aside each year to provide for major
building renovations and redecorations which the ordinary
maintenance budget does not permit. For example, in 1961
thisamount was used to reinsulate all of the air conditioning
handling equipment and to place the roof and all parapet
walls in good condition. In 1962 this amount will be used
to paint both the exterior and interior of the Activities Build¬
ing except for the Auditorium.
4.Purchase of Air Conditioning
Chilling Equipment 22,500.00
The air handling units throughout the build¬
ings are now in good condition, but mechanical
chilling equipment being 11 years old, is in poor
condition and requires excessive maintenance.
More important, however, is the fact that we
have a capacity of only 80 tons instead of the
minimum of 200 tons actually needed. There
was included in last years Building Fund budget
one-half of the total cost of putting in the needed
chilling equipment. We must raise the other
half.
