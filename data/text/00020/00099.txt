Center Youth League and Charles Palot Club
Southeast Region USY outstanding chapter awards were pre¬
sented to both of our USY chapters at the Tenth Annual Regional
Convention held in Jacksonville March 3-5, 1961. Hosted by the
local chapters, this Convention was praised as being the most success¬
ful to date.
Another highlight of the year was sending five delegates to the
National Convention in Chicago, thus giving Jacksonville the largest
delegation from our region. Mr. and Mrs. Bob Fischer accompanied
the group.
Outstanding CYL boy and girl members were announced at the
closing banquet with Evelyn Brauner and Marvin Slott taking the
honors.
Individual honors were capped by CYL’s Marvin Slott—S.E. Region
First Vice President, Eddie Mizrahi—Sub Regional Corresponding Sec¬
retary, CPC’s Bruce Yergin—Sub Regional Treasurer, and more than
half of the regional and sub regional chairmanships.
Our membership, programming, services, discussions and socials
have all made 1960-61 another banner. Both chapters had a member¬
ship of over 92 members with CPC reaching 104.
Our sincerest appreciation go to our advisors, Stanley Kantor, Ina
Selber and Joel Selber. Special thanks to our Youth Chairman Mrs.
Louis Slott and our Activities Director Bob Fischer.
