The Grey Oaks Lodge located at Switzerland in St. Johns Coun¬
ty was organized in 1955 by members of the Jacksonville Jewish
community as a social and recreational club with a limited member¬
ship. The club grounds are located on a picturesque wooded bluff
overlooking the widest expanse of the St. Johns River. In addition
to the rustic clubhouse and auxiliary buildings, the club has a
1,000 foot dock, an Olympic-sized swimming pool, bath house and
other facilities.
Although membership is limited, there remains an opportunity
for members of the community to join and share in the delightful
activities of this unusual recreational organization. Many new ac¬
tivities have been added this past year and the membership increased.
