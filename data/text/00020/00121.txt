Now in its fifth year, the Amity Club’s purpose is to bring
Jewish couples in the community together in a friendly and
sociable atmosphere.
Each month the club has an elaborate social affair «arranged by
committees selected from the membership. The highlights of the
year’s activities are the New Year’s Eve dinner-dance and the
June installation banquet. Husbands and wives, serving together,
govern the affairs of the organization. They also serve together
when working with a committee arranging a monthly affair.
The organization is open to married couples between the
ages of 32 and 47 with a maximum membership of 85 couples.
Couples interested in membership are requested to contact any of
the officers or members for details.
