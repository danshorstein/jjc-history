A Time to Honor Those Who Serve
These three pearls were honored Friday, January 24,
1986 with a lovely Shabbat Dinner. Their service over
the years has contributed immeasurably to the success
of the school and other educational project with the
Center.

reception Saturday evening.

Bottom: Peggy Organes designed the Torah which stands in our Lobby.
