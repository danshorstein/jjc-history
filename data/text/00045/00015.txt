Cantor Abraham Marton
and the Center Choral Society
Award to be Given in Honor
of Cantor Marton
The Jacksonville Jewish Center is
privileged to have had the services of
Cantor Abraham Marton for more than
forty years. For many Centers members,
he has acted as a link from generation to
generation sharing our many life cycle
events. He has also been responsible for
continually bringing to the Center and
the community the beautiful music of the
Jewish people.
In appreciation of decades of devoted
service, the Jacksonville Jewish Center
has endowed the Hazzan Abraham
Marton Award which will be presented
each year to a deserving graduating
senior of the Cantors Institute of the
Jewish Theological Seminary. It is the
hope of the Center that in some small
way the endowment will allow some
cantor to fulfill the very same functions
that Cantor Marton so ably filled at the
Center for so many years.
The award will be funded by the
proceeds derived from the Cantor’s
Annual Concert which is held in the
spring.
CANTORS ASSEMBLY
acknowledges with pride the establishment of the
Hazzan Abraham
Marton Award
by the members of the Jacksonville Jewish Center
in honor of their Hazzan Emeritus and in apprecia¬
tion of decades of devoted service to endow an
award to be presented to a deserving graduating
senior of the Cantors Institute.
