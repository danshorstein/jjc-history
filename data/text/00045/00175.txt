About the Cover
This yearbook is dedicated to the Jacksonville Jewish Center Sisterhood which
has been an integral and vital part of our synagogue for over sixty years.
Whatever the season, whenever the time, the Sisterhood has always been at
the Center’s side with its financial support and volunteer participation.
We praise the good works of the Sisterhood and we thank the past presidents
for their guidance and leadership, and we extend our appreciation to its members
for their support and dedication.
