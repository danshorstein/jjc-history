“Simcha 25—A Season of Torah” afforded all members of the Jacksonville Jewish Center—both young and old—
a joyous opportunity to reaffirm their loyalty to Torah and to Judaic education.
Scheduled from January 22—January 26, 1986, “Simcha 25” embraced three important and closely related occasions:
• The Silver Anniversary of the Solomon Schechter Day School.
• The Dedication of a Sefer Torah
• The Establishment of the Jacksonville Jewish Center Educational Trust Fund.
