Solomon Schechter Day School
Founded in 1961
The Solomon Schechter Day School is designed as the first step in preparing students for their religious role
in Jewish life as well as their role as successful and contributing members of the our society. The school offers
an integrated curriculum of Judaic experiences, Hebrew language and general studies. The goal is to create
informed, intelligent American Jews, cognizant of their history and heritage and proud of their tradition and
values.
Located in the school wing of the Jacksonville Jewish Center, the Day School has the unlimited use of a
library, computer room, gym, sportsfield, stage, swimming pool, tennis courts and chapel.
