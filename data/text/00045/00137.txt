The Jacksonville , Jewish Center’s Recreation Program provides
good wholesome fun for both it’s members and for the entire
Jewish community. From the annual Alexander Brest Tennis Tournament
in November through Day Camp which begins in June, the Center provides
activities for all ages. Cub, Boy and Girl Scout Troops are sponsored by
the Center, Sunday Sports Association Basketball is the only sports program
in Jacksonville which caters to Jewish youth, and our facilities are unmatched
anywhere. An Olympic sized pool, surrounded by woods, tennis courts, ball fields,
playground, all provide unlimited recreation for your family.
