Message from the Director
Once again it is my pleasure to greet our Center members and friends through this
annual yearbook, which contains a review of the past years activities and events.
As we enter the New Year, may we take the opportunity to re-evaluate and determine
what direction we should take in the future.
We have one of the finest facilities, a warm and friendly place for worship and many
activities, but it cannot reach its full potential without the involvement of all members.
Being involved is a way of communicating, a way of sharing your talents and receiving
many benefits in exchange.
Don’t be shy, just call the Center office, let us know that you are available and we will
find the proper niche for you.
The entire Center Staff joins me with a wish for a rewarding and peaceful year to ALL.
Blanche Slott
