Afternoon Hebrew School
Principal: Rabbi Dov Kentof
The Afternoon Hebrew School of the Jacksonville Jewish Center is designed for students in grades 3-7 and provides
an elementary education leading to Bar/Bat Mitzvah and continuation in the high school program.
Meeting twice during the week and on Saturdays, “the basics” are at the core of the curriculum, with stress on
Hebrew reading, Jewish Social Studies, and current events relating to Israel. Synagogue skills, holiday observances and
knowledge of prayers are all part of the comprehensive Afternoon Hebrew School program.
