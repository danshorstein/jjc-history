The Cemetery Committee provides a very necessary function of the Jacksonville Jewish Center. It is </ery
important that the Cemetery grounds are well kept and provide a peaceful resting place for our loved ones.
Our Cemetery Committee is chaired by Michael Schneider. Serving on the committee are Hymie Selber, Alan
Taffet, Dr. Joe Witten, Dr. Sam Witten, Jack Bettman, Joel Bressler, Nathan Sandler, Jack Becker and Joe
Becker.
Mr. Kenneth A. Bowden serves as general manager of our Cemetery. It is his responsibility to oversee the
upkeep and maintenance of the grounds. He has an excellent working relationship with Mr. Dick Mueller who is
general manager of Evergreen Cemetery. Mr. Mueller has been very generous with his time and his staff. They
have all taken excellent care of the Center’s property, above and beyond what is expected. We appreciate
everything they do.
Johnnie B. Mercer, who has been custodian of the cemeteries for 25 years, is in virtual retirement. Maintenance
is now handled by Russell and Winifred Baker, a very dependable couple. We are very appreciative of the help give
us by John Bergeron of the Southern Monument Company.
