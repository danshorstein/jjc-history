1986 SWIM—A—THON, Sunday, July 13
JACKSONVILLE JEWISH CENTER CAMPSITE
The 1986 Wacky Water Races and Swim-a-thon were a great success. Children and adults enjoyed the swimming, boat
races, and other relays. The trophies and ribbons awarded helped make the day especially fun. All participants received a
commemortive T-shirt. The Swim-a-thon which followed the races raised additional money for the Day Camp. We thank all
the swimmers. Special thanks to those who made the day possible by donating goods and/or time: French Novelty, Pic 'N
Save, All American Specialty Corporation, Nancy Eber, Naida Leon, Harry Shmunes, Bruce Horovitz, Isabel
Balotin, and our judges: Gary Gendzier, Sheldon Gendzier, Jill Goldstein, Dan Thro, Wendy Verstandig, and
Karen and Scott Zimmerman. Our thanks to committee members: Janis Fleet, Wendy Verstandig, Beth Shorstein
and Rena Schoenberg, and to all who participated.
