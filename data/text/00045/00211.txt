SHALOM,
“For Everything There Is a Season and a
Time..Kohelet 3.1. This is our National theme
for the year and truly reflects our own feelings.
As our Season begins, our expectations are
extremely high that this will be our Time for a
most successful year for our Center
Sisterhood. We will be working very hard to
raise our membership to where it should be. I
urge each and every female member of the
Jacksonville Jewish Center to either join or re¬
enroll your membership in our Sisterhood. We need your
involvement in all of our activities that will benefit the Center,
Sisterhood, and yourself. In return, your board hopes to educate,
enlighten, and of course, entertain you during the year.
As our sisterhood enters its 63rd year, we can look back with
much “nachas” on all that we have done for our Synagogue. The
future is unlimited for us. We will always be there for our Adults and
Youth; as, we are a vital part of the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
Ours is a highly thought of Sisterhood throughout the Florida
Branch. We always come home from Conferences with awards in
many areas. We are blessed with having so many creative and
talented members. Pm very proud to be a member of the Jacksonville
Jewish Center Sisterhood. I ask you to share your pride with me at
this Time and throughout the Season; and again, become
INVOLVED.
Best wishes for a happy and healthy year.
