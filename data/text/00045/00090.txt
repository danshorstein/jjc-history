The Judaica High School is the creation of the synagogue to offer Jewish youth an opportunity to explore the broad expanse of
resources available through our heritage. This program seeks to provide exciting, innovative exploration of Judaism upon which
each student might build. It involves young adults in 8th through 11th grades and is required for Confirmation.
