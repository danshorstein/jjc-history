Louis & Goldie Portnoy
Scholarship Trust Fund
The Portnoy family has a long history of support for all Center activities, a support they
continue to maintain. Their main concern, however, has always been to serve the Jewish
youth of the Jacksonville community. In 1980, the Neal F. Portnoy Youth Lounge was
dedicated to the menory of Louis and Goldie Portnoy’s son, Neal. This well-designed
facility continues to be a favorite meeting place for teens and scouts.
Goldie Portnoy is a woman of vision. As a devoted mother and grandmother, she has
an investment in the future—an investment which she feels goes beyond her family to
include all Jewish children of our community. This endowment will memorialize her
husband, Louis Portnoy (z’l), may his memory be for a blessing, and will ensure the future
education of countless Jewish children of Jacksonville.
The Jacksonville Jewish Center is grateful to Goldie Portnoy for her generosity, her
commitment to youth and her loyalty to the synagogue and the purposes it serves.
This endowment will provide tuition scholarships for students who are attending the
Center schools and will help us maintain the standards of excellence which our students
have come to expect and deserve.
