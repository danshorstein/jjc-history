The Solomon Schechter P.T.A. serves as a vital segment of the Solomon Schechter Day School and Pre-School. Parents,
teachers, and students cooperate to support the activities and programs of our school.
Some of P.T.A.’s services include: the Hot Lunch Program, which provides our children with delicious kosher hot meals; the
Holiday Observance Program, which enhances the celebration of our many Jewish holidays; our Birthday Book Club, Book-A-
Bration, which provides the library with many books purchased in honor of a child’s birthday.
Through our fundraising projects, such as our annual magazine and candy sales and book fairs, the P.T.A. has purchased four
computers and computer equiment, an overhead projector, and record players and tape recorders for the Day School. As an active
participant in the Early Childhood Department, the P.T.A. purchased new playground equipment for the Pre-School, table and
chair sets, and record players for the new additional classrooms.
We are proud of the excellent P.T.A. Board that promises to have another exciting and successful year. We invite all parents to
participate in the projects planned to help maintain the high quality of education that the Solomon Schechter Day School offers.
