WOMEN’S AMERICAN ORT, Organization for Rehabilitation Through Training, is
an international organization which has provided over 100 years of vocational and
technical education for the Jewish people. By providing the tools of hand and mind, ORT
has enabled two million Jews to enter the mainstream in the societies in which they live.
Here in Jacksonville, 400 plus women give of their time, their funds, and their commitment to insure that our schools and our
children will continue to grow. Our activities are as varied as our members: Theater parties, gift wrapping at the malls, visits to
museums, garage and bake sales. A woman can join one of 4 chapters, depending on her time restrictions and interests.
RIVERFRONT: The Business and Professional group for women who prefer to meet in the evenings. Meetings are scheduled
for the second Tuesday of the month. Contact Linda Hartning 268-8866 or Suzanne Lichter 262-2942.
BOLD CITY: Career and non-career women with children who prefer to meet in the day. Meetings are scheduled for the second
Thursday morning of the month. Contact Susie Singal 262-4639, Rebecca Pendergraft 262-4338 or Sylvia Marin 268-8319.
AFTERNOON: Original chapter of Jacksonville comprised of our mature and active women who continue to give because they
care. Call Gertrude Kramer at 268-2554.
SEASIDE: For women who live at the beaches, yet want the Jewish Connection. Ort is there. Call Michelle Frisch at 737-4642.
AREA COUNCIL: The supportive and umbrella body for all chapters locally. Call if you want general information, or if you are
not quite sure which chapter suits you best. Michelle Frisch 737-4642 or Marcy Silverton 737-5188.
