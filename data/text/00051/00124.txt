Experts agree that the survival of Judaism
depends on the commitment of our youth today.
The Jacksonville Jewish Center sponsors three
outstanding youth groups for youngsters in grades
5-12. Each group meets regularly and enjoys a pro¬
gram of a religious, cultural and social nature.
Through active participation in these groups, a
sense of pride and an identification with Judaism
are instilled. Jewish values, attitude and behavior
patterns are discussed and carried out through
various Mitzvah programs and study.
Conventions, regional activities and encamp¬
ments give our youth the opportunity to meet other
Jewish youth from the Southeast Region.
The youth programs are co-ordinated by Rabbi
Dov Kentof and supervised by the Youth commis¬
sion. All groups are affiliated with the Southeast
Region and United Synagogue of America.
