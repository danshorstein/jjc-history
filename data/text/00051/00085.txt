Director’s Message
As I begin my 30th year at the Jacksonville Jewish Center, it
is with much pride and happiness. I feel fortunate to have had the
opportunity to touch so many lives.
With the beginning of a new school year comes the hope that
it will be a healthy and productive one for our staff and children.
The rich history of our school proves that we will provide for many
Jewish children in our community a time of excitement and many
challenges.
Wc Believe in Children! We believe in children’s abilities,
interests, hopes, concern and fears. Also we believe in the magic
of children.
The Early Childhood Department of the Jacksonville Jewish
Center is committed to the demands of education. Our school strives
to provide the very best early childhood education available.
Our Extended Day Care Program is to be commended. The staff
is concerned, trained, competent and loving. They provide quality
care any parent would appreciate. The Jewish Center’s plan has
always been to provide Day Care that is accessible and affordable.
The school is affiliated with SACUS, Southern Association of
Children Under Six, and FACUS, Florida Association of Children
Under Six. We are also accredited by the Florida Kindergarten Coun¬
cil and this past year was accredited by the Florida Council on In¬
dependent Schools.
The school has always been dedicated to all Jewish children and
their families.
