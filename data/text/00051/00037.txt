• Sunday Sports Basketball Program
There were 120 children, 21 volunteer coaches and volunteer referees who enjoyed a 10-game season.
From Hanukkah to Purim the gym was bustling with activity almost every Sunday. The competition was
fierce but fun. Basketball skills were improved, team work experienced and friends were made. It was another
successful season for the Sunday Sports Association Basketball League. A big thank you to those who
volunteered their time and knowledge, you helped make our season a triumph.
