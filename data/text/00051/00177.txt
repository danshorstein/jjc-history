• Adults United To Organize
AUTO is the parent-teacher-student organization for
the Supplemental Schools. Our main objectives are to
support the efforts of the school staff and bridge the gap
between school and home.
After starting the year by selling honey cakes for the
holidays, we hope to follow with other worthwhile
fundraisers—thus enabling our school to have even more
enriching curriculum tools and experiences for the
students.
We want to communicate what the children are learn¬
ing and feeling in the form of newsletters to the parents.
We strive to bring the Jewish experiences of home,
school, and synagogue together by sharing holidays,
Shabbat, and stories with one another.
Nancy Cohen and I welcome and need your input and
involvement.
Have a Happy and Healthy New Year.
Carole Goldberg
