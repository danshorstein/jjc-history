NOTE: The list of membership below does not include
those former members of the armed forces who were granted
free memberships in the Jacksonville Jewish Center for the
year 1945-1946.
It was indeed a privilege for the Center to have been able
to include these men on its roll of membership, but this action
was only a small token of the feeling of gratefulness and ap¬
preciation felt by the congregation for these men for their
services in behalf of their country.
Center Membership
Although the extension of Center membership does not carry
with it numerous direct benefits, it does convey the gesture of
letting the returned serviceman know that he “belongs” and
that he is wanted by the community. As members of the
Center, they were placed on the Center mailing list, they were
extended tickets for the High Holy Day services, their children
were enrolled in our schools and in general they were accepted
in every way as full members.
We welcome these men into the family of the Jacksonville
Jewish Center and hope that they may find happiness and
satisfaction in this association.
