ELECTRICITY
FOR YOUR POST WAR HOME
It is important in planning your Post-War home that you
arrange for enough outlets for the appliances you will want
when they are available. Your wiring should be heavy enough
to take care of all your electric needs without overloading.
Refrigerator, electric iron, toaster, mixer, vacuum cleaner,
radio, waffle iron and washing machine - these are some of
the electric conveniences that you will no doubt want for your
home. Adequate wiring is one of the most important steps in
your planning.
