During the past year the B’nai B’rith Girls, boasting of a
membership of thirty girls, have centered their activities
around community and war service.
Among their activities were trips to the NavaL Air Station
Hospital, sponsorship of U. S. 0. dances at the Center and
Temple and the collection of food and clothing for the S. 0. S.
drive.
The chief activities of the athletic program were the ping
pong and bowling tournaments. The B’nai B’rith Girls con¬
ducted Friday evening services at the Center this past year.
The cultural activities were in the form of discussions at
group meetings.
In addition to taking part in the B’nai B’rith Youth Com¬
mission Bazaar the members of the B’nai B’rith Girls also
conducted a raffle. The social events included a membership
uarty, a hayride, and a final banquet and dance which climax¬
ed the season’s activities.
