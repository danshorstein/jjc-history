The Junior Y. M. H. A. Club was organized in August ot
1945, by a group of Jacksonville boys and girls, with the
thought of helping the youth of Jacksonville to better acquaint
themselves each with the other. The meetings are held semi¬
monthly. A short business meeting is held, followed by a
well rounded social program.
During the past year, the Junior “Y” has held several suc¬
cessful affails, among them being a hayride, weiner roast,
and a spaghetti supper which was held a t the Y. M. H. A.,
with Mr. and Mrs. Jack Goldberg as the honored guests. A
bunco party, followed by a dancing program was also held.
The year’s program was brought to a close with a veiy
successful dance which was sponsored jointly with the
Esquire Club.
