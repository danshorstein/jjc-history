Although not yet fully organized the B’nai B’rith Young
Women nevertheless achieved partial success in its program
for the year. They made trips to the Naval Air Station
Hospital and helped in the S. O. S. food and clothing drive.
The members also participated in the B’nai B’rith Youth
Organization Bazaar.
Since the past year was devoted chiefly to the organization
of an effective group, it is hoped that real progress will be
realized during the coming year. Under the presidency of
Miss Miriam Cohen, recently elected to the office, much activ¬
ity is being planned for the coming months.
