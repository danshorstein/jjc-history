In Honor Of
Rabbi Dov Kentof
Congratulations, Rabbi Kentof.
You never believed that learning should be confined
to the classroom — or even to the synagogue.
Your teaching and leadership went beyond
walls and books to inspire and motivate us
to want more out of our education, our Judaism,
our life experiences, and ourselves.
It has been a privilege and an honor to have your
contributions to our community and our family
for 20 years. Good luck and thanks!
Cary & Laura Zel and Arlene Friedman
On Behalf of my family and myself,
Rabbi Kentof deserves
more than than just Mazel Tov Wishes.
He deserves all of our thanks and gratitude!
Without him, I would never have joined
Chalutzim, Kadima, or USY, which were
some of the best times of my life.
His leadership and guidance are
above and beyond the call of duty.
So, thank you Rabbi Kentof for being
such a great influence and teacher.
Liza Furman
for 20 years of devotion to the youth of our congregation
