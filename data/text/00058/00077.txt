“Touched By Dov”
Youth Banquet Honored Rabbi Dov Kentof
Sunday, May 16, 1999
Rabbi Dov Kentof has a magical way with kids.
Like the Pied Piper, he draws them to himself and to
Judaism. Like the angel on the well-known television
show, he touches their lives in so many wonderful ways.
Dov has a sixth sense for knowing what each child needs
to feel special and included.
In acknowledgment of the unique, 20-year
relationship he has had with our congregation, Rabbi
Kentof was honored at the Jacksonville Jewish Center
Youth Banquet on Sunday, May 16. Over 450 attended
the lovely dinner which featured a heartfelt tribute of
speeches, songs and presentations, made by
representatives of the Center’s Youth groups, adult and
family groups and his colleagues. Once again, all who
were there were “Touched by Dov.”
