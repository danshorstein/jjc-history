Center Catering Staff
As the Jacksonville Jewish Center makes preparations to celebrate its Centennial, the Catering Department is
busy making preparations of its own, to ensure that your catered events are wonderful experiences.
We are excited to welcome Margo’s Catering, as the preferred caterer of the Center. Margo and her talented
staff bring over 20 years of experience to us. The Center Catering Staff will continue to provide school lunches,
congregational meals and receptions. We look forward to a new century filled with new ideas, new faces and many
new simchas.
L’Shan a Tova from the entire staff of the Center’s kitchen.
— Lori “in the kitchen” Schoettler
Maintenance & Housekeeping Staff
The maintenance and housekeeping staff work diligently to keep our building
clean and presentable at all times. Richard Ford, our Maintenance Supervisor, takes
pride in his staff, and devotes much of his time to taking care of all the physical
needs required by the many events which take place throughout the year. Volunteers
appreciate the special attention they receive from the maintenance department.
