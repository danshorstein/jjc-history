Annual Center Raffle for Education
ShAltE, RaFFLe & Roll!
The Center’s Annual Raffle to Benefit Education was again a huge success this year. With thousands of
dollars raised, our children will reap the benefits of our contributions for years to come!
Perhaps the best part of the evening came when the Grand Prize of $10,000 was awarded to our own Solomon
Schechter Day School by an Anonymous Patron! We wish to sincerely thank those responsible for entering the
school in the raffle.
In addition, David Robbins won the Multiple Ticket prize of a same-day trip for two to a 1999 Jaguars Away-
Game, as guests of Linda and Laurie DuBow. Mary and Martin Wolf won the Early Bird drawing for a luxurious
beach weekend at Sawgrass, courtesy of Far East Brokers and Consultants, Inc. Congratulations to all the other
winners of the fabulous prizes.
As with all major events such as this, there are many people involved in all of the different aspects of planning,
and I wish to thank them so much for their tireless work and unparalleled devotion to the education of our children,
and to the growth of our synagogue’s schools. The raffle committee, ticket sellers, Center staff, and prize donors all
deserve our sincerest gratitude and appreciation for their hard work. Thanks, also, to all of you that purchased
tickets for the evening, and especially to our Grand Patrons and Patrons, and to our major sponsor First Union.
—Lawrence Ansbacher
