Bernie Dean
Sunday, November 1, 1998
Direct from Broadway, the Tony Award
winning Jewish Musical, “The Rothschilds,”
was presented in a unique one-man
performance by singer/actor exciting
Bernie Dean. Working from a script
written by the creators of “Fiddler on the
Roof,” Dean tailored this rags-to-riches
saga to the small stage, performing the
musical score and portraying all the main
characters. He was humorous, tender and
thoroughly entertaining.
Catskill Frolics
Sunday, February 7, 1999
The hilarious Vic Arnell and Dick Lord, and talented singer, Holly
Lipton, brought a true slice of Jewish Americana to the Center with a
genuine Catskills-resort stage show. The audience laughed uncontrollably
at the comic antics and savvy political humor of the two zany comedians
and were moved to sentimental tears by the lovely songstress.
Ira Heller
Sunday, April 18, 1999
Singer Ira Heller performed an original
blend of traditional and contemporary
Jewish music A charismatic presence on
stage, Ira electrified the audience with his
lyrics and melodies that combine eternal
Jewish themes with issues of importance
in the modem world. His vocal ability and
boundless energy made this a thrilling
evening.
