White Water Rafting Experience
Led by Rabbi Dov Kentof
LTI
Leadership Training Institute
Summer Encampment 1999
As always, this year’s White Water Rafting Trip was FANTASTIC! As in the
past, two rivers were explored: the Nolichucky and the French Broad. Everyone
had a blast, and made it home with all their body parts intact!
In addition, participants camped, held discussion groups, went tubing and
prayed in beautiful country settings. The memories made on this trip will last a
lifetime, and strengthen Judaism for these participants, in a unique way.
The trip was successful because of the help of Ava Axelrod, Rachel
Abramson, Dr. Michael Bernhardt, Philip Bloom, Joy & Mel Gottlieb, Alina
Kentof, Russell Selevan, Morris Shriftman, Lauren Snyder, Wesley Wallace,
Elliot Zisser and of course the participants. Special thanks to Laurie Schoettler
for putting together some great food!
Summers are topped off with a
unique one-week experience held each
August. This year, Ramah Darom was
the host camp. Teenagers participate in
programs that include informal classes
and discussion groups, cultural activities
and special evening activities in addition
to a full range of camp sports and social
activities. The camp provides two
unique programs: Kadima, grade 6, 7
& 8, and USY, grades 9 through 12.
Those attending were: Alexandra
Abramson, Dorie Bloom, Megan
Cohen and Erin Frisch.
Center Youth Banquet
May 16,1999
At the conclusion of the activity year, the Youth Commission holds a banquet
to honor the officers, advisors and volunteers from each Center Youth Group. Newly
elected officers for the coming year are introduced. This year’s banquet was made
even lovelier by the honoring of Rabbi Dov Kentof in a special program called
“Touched by Dov.” (See page 43 for details.)
