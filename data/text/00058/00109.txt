Message from the Director
The Early Childhood Department is located in the Selevan Pre-School
Dale Schemer
Director
The Early Childhood Department of the Jacksonville Jewish Center
is entering its 48th year of providing quality programs to Jewish children
in the community, regardless of affiliation. Our ever expanding program
offers not only a Florida Kindergarten Council accredited preschool, but
also includes an extended day program available to all children enrolled in
the Preschool, Solomon Schechter Day School and Religious Schools.
The early morning and afternoon daycare, coordinated by veteran
preschool teacher, Cheryl Gartner, provides a healthy happy environment
in which parents feel secure in the knowledge that their children are well
cared for. Ballet, computer skills and soccer are just a few of the “Giant
Steps” programs offered during extended daycare. These outstanding
programs, plus a low staff-to-student ratio, are just a few reasons that the
extended day program continues to grow.
The staff of the Early Childhood Department recognizes the great importance of
the role we play in your child’s life. Each of our programs is planned to nurture the
social, emotional, and intellectual development of every child.
This past year has been rewarding, thrilling, and a growing experience for all
involved. We anticipate an even larger enrollment of enthusiastic and loving children
for the 1999-2000 school year.
— Dale Schemer
