The Jacksonville Jewish Federation
serves as a central resource for our Jewish
community. In summary, the Federation
is dedicated to providing for the welfare
of every Jewish person living in
Jacksonville. Through a partnership with
the United Jewish Communities, formerly
known as UJA, we are equally committed
to the survival of, and quality of life for,
every Jew living in Israel and throughout
the Diaspora.
The four major local recipient
agencies of funds raised by the Federation
are the Jewish Community Alliance,
Jewish Family & Community Services,
River Garden Hebrew Home and the
Jacksonville Jewish Foundation. We are
proud of the invaluable services provided
by these four outstanding Jewish agencies.
It is a privilege to assist them in trying to
meet their goals.
Jewish education is extremely
important, and the Federation is proud to
support the Solomon Schechter Day
School, Beit Midrash and Etz Chaim
Hebrew Academy. Through the Joan Levin
Gift of Israel program, Federation is helping
to make the dream of a trip to Israel a reality
for Jewish teens in our community.
The Federation is involved with
community relations, leadership development
and Missions to Israel. We will continue to
work in cooperation with our local agencies,
UJC and the synagogues to address the vital
issues and challenges in the entire arena of
Jewish continuity.
Our Board wishes the members of the
Jacksonville Jewish Center a Happy &
Healthy New Year.
As this yearbook is dedicated to
Rabbi Dov Kentof, we wish to join with the
many members of our Jewish community,
who understand Dov’s commitment to the
Jewish education of our young people, in
saying Mazel Tov to him and his family.

The Jacksonville Jewish Foundation is dedicated to accumulating permanent
assets which can provide a source of income to help support the stability and
continuity of the Agencies and Synagogues in Jacksonville. The Foundation
professionally administers lifetime and testamentary giving plans designed to:
• Assist individuals to achieve their personal charitable goals in the most tax-
advantaged manner.
• Accumulate enduring endowed resources for use in supporting special,
emergency, and future needs, including those not provided for by the Jacksonville
Jewish Federation’s annual campaign.
The Foundation exists as a partnership of Jacksonville’s Synagogues and
Agencies to assure a better future for all generations. Through the Foundation, donors
are provided with many ways to perpetuate their charitable interests through a wide
array of funding opportunities.
