About the Cover:
The beautiful portrait, painted in oils by artist
Blanche Slott, lovingly captures Rabbi Dov Kentof
enjoying two of his passions—Judaism and the great
outdoors. Blanche created this new work especially
foi this occasion, as a gift to Rabbi Kentof.
With Deep Appreciation and Affection
The 1999-2000 Center Yearbook
is Dedicated to
Rabbi Dov Kentof
Director of Education and Youth
who, for 20 years, has worked tirelessly on behalf
of the youth and families of our congregation, our
community and throughout the Southeast.

tributes to Rabbi Kentof, begins on page 3.
