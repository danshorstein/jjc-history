Consecration 5759
January 16, 1999
The Aleph classes of Solomon Schechter Day School
and Afternoon Hebrew School celebrated the official
beginning of their religious education with a beautiful
Consecration ceremony during Shabbat services. The students
demonstrated their knowledge and love of Judaism through a
charming musical program on the Celebration of Shabbat.

Kindergarten Shabbat Dinner
January 15, 1999
The Kindergarten classes of the Solomon Schechter Day
School shared a special Shabbat Dinner with their families,
that has become an enjoyable annual tradition.
Teachers, parents and children enjoyed making challah
together in preparation for Shabbat.
Following the meal, the students presented a special
program, “Shabbat - A Day for Rest - A Day of Joy,” and
lead their families in singing Shabbat zmirot.
