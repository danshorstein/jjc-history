Sunday Sports - Basketball Program
The Sunday Sports Association (SS A) provides area youth with alternative athletic
programs on Sunday afternoons. All the games and practices are held in the Fletcher-
Selber Auditorium of the Jacksonville Jewish Center. Thank you to Gayle Bailys who
coordinated a very successful program and to the volunteer coaches for their valuable
assistance and time. We look forward to an exciting 1999-2000 season.
