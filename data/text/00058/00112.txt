The Solomon Schechter Day School PTA is a group of parents, teachers, administrators,
and students working together for a single purpose, the enrichment of our schools.
Both the Preschool and Day School benefit from the PTA on a weekly basis. Our
volunteers are truly dedicated to the children in providing quality programs throughout the
school year.
Through fundraising projects like the annual magazine sale, book fair, candy sales, and
the PTA Cookbook, we provide much needed equipment. Science program kits, playground
equipment, stencil machine equipment, computer software, and much more have been
purchased because of your participation in these projects.
Educational and fun programs enhance our children’s school days:
• Book-A-Bration honors children, teachers, and friends by directly adding to our library.
• The Cultural Arts Committee provides art enrichment programs. Volunteers serving
as the “Picture Lady” visit classrooms on a regular basis.
• Holiday observances such as model seders, Consecration, and Shabbat and Havdalah
boxes require the helping hands of the PTA.
• Red Ribbon Week for drug awareness, as well as vision screening, are needed health
programs that the PTA provides.
Your commitment to the PTA makes these programs and events successful. PTA provides a fun, often social, opportunity
for involvement in the education of our children. Try it out—pick a project —you may benefit as much as the schools!!
