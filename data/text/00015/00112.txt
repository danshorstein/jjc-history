The sole purpose of the National Women’s Committee of Brandéis University is the support of the library of
Brandéis University. This is accomplished by two means only, membership and book fund collections. “Books are the
key to knowledge.”

WALTER GREEN
The Esquire Club is happy to record that 1956 was a most successful year under the capable leadership of Walter
Green. Much credit is due Miss Iris Dee Selber for devoting so much of her time and effort to the affairs of the group.
A great deal of credit should be given Stanley Kantor and Paul Becker who superbly assisted in all of the club s
functions.
This year the Esquire Club contributed to the Charles H. Palot Memorial Fund and to the Center Building Fund.
One of the highlights of the season was the Esquire Sabbath held at the Jewish Center.
In athletics again we reigned supreme. We conquered our arch-rivals A.Z.A. in football, basketball and we captured
the softball championship.
