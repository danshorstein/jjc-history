Girl Scouts and Brownies
Head Table at Girl Scout Banquet
This year has marked another very successful one for Girl Scout and Brownie Troops, under the sponsorship of
the Center Sisterhood.
The troops under the leadership of their able leaders participated in all phases of the Girl Scout program; Inter-
National Thinking Day, Cookie Sale, Court of Awards and Girl Scout Sabbath.
In addition, this year the Girl Scouts sponsored Mother’s Day Sabbath, presented each mother with a corsage, also
acted as hostesses for the evening.
The highlight of the year was the Menorah Award, the first to be presented
in the history of the Duval County Girl Scout Council. The award is made by
the congregation in recognition of achievement in the field of religious endeavor.
This award was won by the following girls: Ada Hinda Block, Renee Slott,
Barbara Ann Stillman.
On May 23 the Girl Scouts and Brownies held a closing banquet under
the supervision of mothers from each troop. At this time, year pins, badges and
awards were given.
