Cover
Our Cover this year portrays the long promised view of the central feature
of our newly installed pulpit, the Holy Ark. It was designed and manufactured
by the Rambusch Company of New York. Hebrew lettering was executed by the
well known artist in the field, Reuben Lief. Details of the Dedication Ceremony
are found on pages 30-31 of this book, and a description of the wood carvings on
page 32.
