Center of Youth Activities
The Activities Building which was completed over four years ago
accommodates many functions. Principal among them are youth meet¬
ings and social functions. The groups are directly affiliated with the con¬
gregation or the Jewish community at large. Also meeting here are the
Boy Scouts of America, Mohawk District, for their monthly Court of
Honor. Many additional groups hold their meetings and functions in this
building.
The Activities Building is located to the left of the Synagogue build¬
ing on Third Street, facing Springfield Park
