Our Ties With the Seminary
Our congregation is participating in a Memorial Scholarship Fund
established by the Jewish Theological Seminary in remembrance of two
of its world renowned professors who passed away two years ago. It is
known as the Ginsburg-Marx Scholarship Fund. We pledged to make
annual contributions from the general treasury for a period of five years.
In addition we sponsor the Jacksonville Friends of the Seminary of which
Abe Selber is Chairman of membership. Through annual participation
our congregation aids further in the support of the Conservative Move¬
ment.
