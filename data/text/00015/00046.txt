The Young Peoples League, better known as Y.P.L.,
functions under the auspices of the Jacksonville Jewish
Center. Y.P.L. brings to the young adults of our community
cultural, religious and social activities.
As a group, we wish to extend our thanks to the Center
for the use of its most adequate facilities and to Rabbi
Sanders A. Tofield for his help and guidance.
IRVING PROCTOR
This year Y.P.L. revived its close affiliation with the National Young Peoples League of the United Synagogue of
America. Two delegates attended the National Convention held in Atlantic City, N. J. Dr. Shore was appointed to the
National Committee and Irving Proctor was elected a National Vice-President.
Y.P.L. continually opens its doors to the Jewish Service men stationed nearby. Alternate Tuesday evening meetings
are often frequented by large groups of service personnel.
For the coming year, Y.P.L. plans to continue with an active program for its members and promises to provide wider
fields in which its members can express themselves. The aim of Y.P.L. is to bring inspiration to the youth of our com¬
munity and encourage them to participate in the activities of the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
