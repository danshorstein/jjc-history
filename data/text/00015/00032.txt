Center, kindled the Ner Tamid. Ben Setzer and Ben B.
Yoffee, Co-chairmen of the Building Fund, opened the
doors of the Ark and the Torah procession began. Partici¬

4th issue of the Center Bulletin. Harry Gendzier, Chairman
of the Building Fund committee, gave the welcoming address
and introduced a series of presentations. First came Philip
Selber, Chairman of the Architect’s Committee, with the
key to the new Ark. Mrs. Rudy Moss then presented a key
to the elevator installed in memory of the late Wolfe and
Sarah Sobel, a gift of Mr. and Mrs. Rudy Moss and Mr.
and Mrs. Si Sobel of Los Angeles, California. Mr. Ralph S.
Mizrahi, Center President, accepted these presentations in
an acceptance address.
Mrs. Nat F. Cohen, Center Sisterhood President, then
presented a Torah pointer inscribed as a token of the large
sums of money that the Sisterhood’s Beautification Fund had
contributed towards the new Ark and Torah furnishings and
carpeting, as well as numerous decorative articles used in
the synagogue.
In the memorial prayer the Rabbi included names of
donors to the Building Fund who passed away in the course
of the past ten years. Among them were Moses and Ida
Feldman who left a large portion of their estate to the Center
Building Fund.
