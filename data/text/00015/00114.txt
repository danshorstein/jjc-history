VICTOR HUBSCH
This is a national organization dedicated to Community
and National service. The Post meets every Thursday night
at 8:00 P.M. Its activities are varied in the pursuance of
ideals of this great Fraternal Order, which enjoys a world
wide membership. Its founding dates back to the Year 79
A.D.
This year our Post celebrated in proper manner the
solemn and festive holidays of our country and our faith.
We participated in all patriotic affairs, visited the hospital¬
ized veterans and donated blood to the stricken. We partook
with great success in both the United Appeal and the Israeli
Bond drives. We also took part in the life of the local
Jewish Community and cooperated with the Jewish Com¬
munity Council. Through our delegations, we took part
in the National and State Conventions. Our 61st National
Convention will be held in August the week of 7-12 in
Milwaukee, Wisconsin.
