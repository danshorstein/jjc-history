Center Choral Society
Directed by
Cantor Emeritus Abraham Marton
Our Cantor Emeritus, Abraham Marton, held his Choral Society Concert, and delighted the entire audience. The selection of
music, the professional manner in which it was presented, and the entire program was a delight for all who were there. We were
enthralled with the wide range of voices and everyone rose and ebbed with each strain. It is indeed our good fortune to have
Cantor Marton, who is still our leading force in Jewish music at the Jacksonville Jewish Center, and we wish him many more
years and many more concerts. Outstanding Center talents, Raymond Cohen, tenor and sopranoes, Rosalind Klein, Peggy
Organes; also featured were Paul Goldstein and Assistant Conductor Judy Geller.
Cantor Marton and his wife, Pearl
On December 2,1981, Cantor Marton was presented with the Lion of Judah
Award in recognition of a lifetime of dedicated and devoted service to the
Jewish people and all good causes to benefit the people of Israel.
Cantor Marton was given this tribute at an Israel Bonds Dinner on behalf of
the State of Israel Bonds.
