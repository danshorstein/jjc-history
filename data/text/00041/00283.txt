The Women’s League for Conservative
Judaism states that its goal is to put Judaism
in the life of its members and to put life into
their Judaism. As the Sisterhood begins a
new year, we will stress that this goal can best
be accomplished through Volunteerism --
personal involvement of each member. Barbara Safer
Through the active participation in our
projects and programs we believe that our members will have an
opportunity for personal understanding and fulfillment which
cannot be obtained by the mere attendence at these functions.
This concept of Volunteerism will not carry out the Women’s
League goal for the individual but also enhance the richness of
Jewish Life in our Synagogue.
Our activities for the year will be many and varied. There will be
opportunities for your participation no matter where your
interests may lie. A new Bat Mitzvah class will be starting for those
who wish to deepen their involvement with Jewish heritage and
broaden their, understanding of Jewish values. Our condolence
committee, providing the traditional first meal in the house of
mourning, and the Mitzvah committee for hospital visitation
provide service to members of the congregation at times of
personal need as well as providing opportunity for our members
to participate in “good deeds” in the Jewish tradition.
Sisterhood provides general support and scholarships to the
Youth and Education program of the Center. Many of our
members are also involved with these programs and provide
assistance for special events.
Our monthly meetings will educate and entertain on a wide
scope of subjects including Israeli affairs, Jewish Family Living,
Creative Handicrafts and Social Action. Each year our Torah
Fund Luncheon helps meet our obligation to the Jewish
Theological Seminary Residence Halls, a national project of
Women’s League. The Annual Donor Event fulfills our
committment to the Building Fund of the Center.
I hope that each of you will accept the opportunity to get
involved with our activities during the coming year. Your
participation will benefit you as well as Sisterhood.
