The merriment and all participation of the holiday of Purim can be felt in the Synagogue while the Megillah is being read. Always a
capacity crowd, the holiday creates a joyous atmosphere the children remember from year to year. The sound of the grogger and
dangling jewlery, beards and mustaches, three-cornered hats all add to the excitement. The following pictures were taken this
