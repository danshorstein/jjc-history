During the coming year the Federation will be moving
into its new office building on Old St. Augustine Road. Our
Community Services will expand considerably, particularly
in the areas of family life education. These programs will be
provided in a variety of formats working with and through
the four congregations, River Garden and Jewish Family &
Children’s Services. We will also expand our Leadership
Development efforts so that future leadership will be
cultivated and available when needed.
The Federation will continue to conduct the Annual
Combined Jewish Appeal which raises monies for our local
agencies as well as the United Jewish Appeal and national
organizations. The Federation has made major increases in
its commitment to both our local Jewish Family &
Children’s Services and the River Garden Hebrew Home
for the Aged. Our Russian Resettlement program continues
to be a model program throughout the southeast part of the
United States. Federation programs will continue to
provide services in areas such as Senior Citizen
programming, Single Parent Family programming, College
Age programs, Jewish Singles in their 20’s, 30’s and 40’s,
etc.
The Federation provides a vehicle for service to our
community and it is the hope of the Officers and Board that
we unify our Jewish community for the betterment of all
individuals* and organizations.
