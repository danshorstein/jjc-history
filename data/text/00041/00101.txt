A Scribe Visits
“The special calligraphy of the Torah has a spiritual quality uniquely Jewish, since it cannot be influenced by
changing secular esthetic influences.”
Alain Kirilli, artist
Rabbi Turkeltaub, an authentic Scribe, or sofer, came to the Jacksonville Jewish Center for the express purpose of examining
and repairing our Torah scrolls that are used for our Torah reading services. Members brought mezeuzot, that needed to
be examined and repaired; tefillin with frayed threads or knots too large or too small were also repaired by Rabbi Turkeltaub.
Children of the Day School were fascinated as they watched the scribe repair our Torah scrolls.
