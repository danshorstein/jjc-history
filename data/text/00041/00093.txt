Youth Banquet
The Jacksonville Jewish Center Annual Youth Banquet took place May 23,1982. U.S.Y., Kadima, Chalutzim, Boy Scouts,
Girl Scouts, Brownies and Cub Scouts and Leaders all participated in the evening’s program. Awards were given and the new
officers were installed. A delightful talent show concluded the evening which was co-ordinated by Carol Freedman.
