The Jacksonville District of the Zionist Organization of
America has many activities, including education about, the
promotion of, and public relations for the State of Israel. Our
national organization is one of the most effective advocates for
Israel because of our constituency or membership and
traditionally has always had clout where it counts in the
government and the mass media.
