SURVIVAL WEEKEND
On May 8 and 9, Rabbi Dov Kentof and twelve teen-agers and adults traveled to Fernandina Beach to spend a challenging
weekend in the wilderness. With the co-operation of Project S.S.E.P. -Outward Bound Program, they spent the weekend
striving to reinforce! the concept of success and personal achievement while participating in a grueling weekend of obstacle
courses. The pictures shown clearly show the strain and concentration involved in the maneuvers.
