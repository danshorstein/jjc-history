The Jewish Center’s Recreation Department’s goals are to provide the Jewish community
with programs and activities that promote the improvement of physical skills, health, the
development of special interest and/or hobbies, and to provide the opportunity for relaxation
and social camaraderie.
Many of the recreation activities take place on the Center Campsite which consists of
approximately 10 acres of beautifully wooded campgrounds, complete with an olympic-size
swimming pool, bath houses, tennis courts, softball fields, and outdoor basketball court,
badminton area, children’s playground, gazebo, snack bar and shaded picnic area.
Within the main building is a regulation basketball court, the fully-equipped Neal Portnoy
Youth Lounge and an arts and crafts room, a large room for dances and a versatile stage for
musical and dramatic productions and a meat and dairy kitchen with the most modern
equipment. We take pride in recreation program and the marvelous facilities of our Center.
