Sunday Sports Basketball
The S.S.A. Basketball program finished its 8-week season on Sunday, February 28th, with final game and the presentation of
awards. Trophies were given to all players in League One, Two and Three. League Four received gold and silver medallions on
red, white and blue ribbons.
One hundred and ten boys and girls participated in this popular athletic program, with emphasis on fun, participation,
sportsmanship and skill improvement.
A special thank you goes to all of the wonderful coaches who contributed so much to the program!
