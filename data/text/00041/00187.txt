Hebrew High School
The Judaica High School is the creation of the synagogue to offer Jewish youth an opportunity to explore the broad expanse
of resources available through our heritage. This program seeks to provide exciting, innnovative exploration of Judaism upon
which each student might build. It involved children in 8th through 12th grades and is required for Confirmation.
