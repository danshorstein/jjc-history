Sesame Street
On May 24, 1982 the Masada group
of the Solomon Schechter Day School
presented a Hebrew production of
“Sesame Street” to the entire school.
The success of the program was the
result of months of work on the part of
the children in the class and their
teacher, Edith Horovitz.
The cast included Larissa Zaulyanov
as Grover, Amy Funk and Laurie
Greenburg as Snuffulufagas, Jennifer
Verstandig as Big Bird, Jana Chepenik
as Bert, Allison Ginsburg as Ernie, Val
Teper as Mr. Hooper, Sean Gibson as
Kermit, Matthew Kulchin as Cookie
Monster, Steven Cantor as the Race
Car Driver, Cheryl Stone as the Little
girl, Jonathan Goldberg as the Train
Conductor and Coleman Brodsky as
Oscar.
Judy Reppert designed the stage and
props and Shirley Bielski was the
musical director and pianist.
