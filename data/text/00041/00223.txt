Message from the Administrative Director
Dear Friends,
As I look at the beauty of our newly renovated Sanctuary, complete with the
Ark created by the vision and talent of Israeli artist, Ami Shamir, I am filled with a
sense of holiness and awe in the knowledge that such beauty will inspire our
congregation for many years to come.
I am proud that I had the privilege to be a part of the design committee,
chaired by Philip Selber, whose commitment and dedication to this project
never ceased until its completion.
We look forward to a challenging New Year as we continue with the many diversified activities that are offered in our
Synagogue Center. The finest facilities are available for all occasions -- weddings, Bar and Bat Mitzvahs, social events, sports,
youth, adult education and activities, complete with catering to suit your needs whether large or small.
In closing may I remind you that our Center is not just a building - it is a living, thriving, warm and friendly place of worship and
activity. However, it needs input from all to make it grow. Share your ideas and talents by volunteering to do your part. A call to
the office and our capable staff will find the right spot for you.
My husband, Louis, joins me in a wish for peace and a good year to the entire congregation.
Blanche Slott,
Administrative Director
