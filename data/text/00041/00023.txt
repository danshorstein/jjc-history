Cemetery Committee of the
Jacksonville Jewish Center
The Cemetery Committee provides a very necessary function of the Jacksonville Jewish Center. It is very important that the
Cemetery grounds are well kept and provide a peaceful resting place for our loved ones.
Our Cemetery Committee is chaired by Michael Schneider. Serving on the committee are Max Becker, Hymie Selber, Alan
Taffet, Dr. Joe Witten, Dr. Sam Witten, Jack Bettman, Joel Bressler, Nathan Sandler, Jack Becker and Joe Becker.
Mr. Kenneth Á. Bowden, Controller of the Jacksonville Jewish Center, also serves as general manager of our Cemetery. It is
his responsibility to oversee the upkeep and maintenance of the grounds. He has an excellent working relationship with Mr.
Jennewine who is general manager of Evergreen Cemetery. Mr. Jennewine has been very generous with his time and his staff,
Mr. Campbell, Mr. Miller and Mrs. Phillips. They have all taken excellent care of the Centers property, above and beyond what
is expected. We appreciate everything they do.
Johnny B. Mercer has been custodian of our Cemetery for over 20 years. Mr. Bergerson of Southern Monument Co., has
always been extremely helpful to us, too. We are very grateful to all these people who have given so generously of their time.
