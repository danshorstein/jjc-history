Consecration Classes
The first grade children and new students of the Solomon Schechter Day School along with the Alef Class of the Afternoon
Hebrew School were consecrated in a ceremony at the Jacksonville Jewish Center on Saturday morning, February 26,1982.
The ceremony marked the beginning of the children’s formal Hebrew and Judaic education and is a very meaningful event. Each
child received a certificate, miniature Torah and a traditional jar of honey. The children who were consecrated:
DAY SCHOOL: Leslie Adler, Jason Blume, Melissa Brodsky, Mark dayman, Brian Davis, Dana Davis, Lorien Friedlin, Aron Greenburg, Emma Greenwood,
Lee Kaplan, Paige Kornblum, Joni Press, Eduardo Romero, Havi Safer, Jennifer Sager, Ari Silverman, Andy Ussach, Irene Vavulitsky and Jonathan Witten.
AFTERNOON HEBREW SCHOOL: Valerie Baruch, Jill Ghelerter, Carol Ann Hall, Robert Hyken, Judith Jacobson, Melissa Kirshenbaum, Laurie Paul,
Jennie Schwab, Rachel Thaler and Julie Snitzer.
