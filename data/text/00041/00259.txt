PASSOVER FOR ALL PROJECT
“Let all who are hungry come to eat” is recited during the Seder when the Matzah is
uncovered. To insure that all will be able to partake of Passover, the young people of each
school at the Jewish Center undertook a most meaningful project. Each student was
requested to bring one dollar ($1.00) to school. These monies were used to purchase food
stuffs for gift boxes to be given to those families who, at this time, were unable to provide the
foods of the holiday for themselves. Please help our young people with this project when
they call you next Passover.
Members from each class were selected to purchase the food and the older students
helped deliver them.
