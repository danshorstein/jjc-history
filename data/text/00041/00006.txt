The Solomon Schechter P.T.A. is embarking on a new and exciting year. Parents of children in
the Pre-School and Day School are invited to join the P.T. A. which serves as a link between parents,
teachers and the school.
Monies derived from our major fund-raising efforts are used for educational and cultural purposes
only; encyclopedia, projectors, playground equipment, bookstand, other valuable school
equipment have been donated to the schools on behalf of the P.T.A.
I ask you for your support and look forward to a most successful year.
Fondly,
Shirley
