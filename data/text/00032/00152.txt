Troop 14, one of the oldest Troops in Jackson¬
ville, continues to offer an excellent all around Scout¬
ing program to boys. 11 years and up. Under the
leadership of Scoutmasters David Weintraub and
Nathan Franzblau, the Troop meets every Wednesday
night at the Center Southside Site.
Overnight camping holds the top spot with the
Scouts who had most successful weekends at Anastasia,
Gold Head and Olena State Parks. Plans are being
made for an enlarged program in 1973-74 including a
weekend at Silver Springs and many other exciting
and adventurous activities.
If you like the out-of-doors and good fellowship,
we would like to welcome you into our Troop.
