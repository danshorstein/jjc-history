Gators B.B.G. has had one of its best years ever. From progressive
dinners, slumber parties, Halloween collecting for the underprivileged,
selling New Year’s cards, Operation Chanukah, Surprise Breakfasts, Beach
and Swim parties, car washes, to attending conventions together, with so
much enthusiasm, and having twice as much fun.
Membership doubled as new friendships bloomed. Our love, brother¬
hood, spirit and Jewish identity constantly expands. We are very proud
to have been awarded as the number one chapter of the year in our
council, the International No. 1 programming of the year award for
making the Jewish Servicemen in Viet Nam Chanukah cards, the Council
Program of the Year award, and a Membership Achievement award. We
have also been nominated for the District Program of the year award.
The B.B.Y.O. Spring Convention was hosted by and held in Jacksonville
this year. Along with our brother chapter, A.Z.A., we put loads of planning
and work into this convention. It was one of our council’s best, and was
enjoyed by all who attended it.
There are still many goals which Gators B.B.G. is striving to reach
and many dreams that we wish to accomplish. We welcome interested new
members to step with us into a rewarding new year. Our anxious new
officers are working hard with high hopes for Gators B.B.G.
Bonnie Baumgarten
