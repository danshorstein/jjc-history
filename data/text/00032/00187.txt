The girls of Brownie Troop #147 enjoyed a
stimulating and exciting year of activities and events.
The year started with a unique decorating pro¬
ject. A room at the Center Campsite was transformed
into a decorator’s dream due to the hard work of the
girls. In addition trips were taken to a Head Start
Center, the Children’s Home, the Animal Shelter and
in every case our girls made things and worked with
other children. Our goal was to make the Brownies
more aware of sharing and giving of themselves to
others.
Other projects included participation in the Scout
Sabbath, Purim Carnival and Scout Round-up. Each
week at our meetings we were involved with arts and
crafts projects, singing, dancing and games. The year
ended with a trip to Disney World.
