In its 21st year, the Mr. and Mrs. Club continues
to enjoy success, achieved through the enthusiasm,
cooperation and imagination of its members, who
conduct the club’s entertainment and general business.
Various monthly affairs are planned by commit¬
tees consisting of married couples, who are dedicated
to presenting an original and outstanding evening of
entertainment for the club members.
An elaborate New Year’s Eve affair is traditional.
Close friendships, sustained for years by many
members’ participation in the club, speak well for the
congeniality of the group and new members are always
welcome. Couples who are interested in joining are
urged to contact the Membership Chairman, Mrs.
Harry Kolchin, 737-8773 or the Treasurer, Mr. Howard
Brown, 724-9200.
