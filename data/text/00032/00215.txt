Religious Services — Traditional prayer services are held daily, morn¬
ing and evening in the Chapel. Friday Family Services, Sabbath and
Holiday Services are observed in the Sanctuary throughout the entire
year.
Personal Religious Observances — Traditional Jewish customs are
observed in connection with Circumcision, Naming of Girls, Pidyon
Ha-ben, Bar Mitzvah, Bat*Mitzvah, Confirmation, Marriages, Conver¬
sion to Judaism, Bereavement.
Educatior — Daily Kindergarten and Nursery with outstanding pro¬
gram of secular and religious content. Hebrew School, carefully graded
with intensive Hebrew content; Sunday School, with a curriculum in¬
cluding Jewish history and customs; Solomon Schechter Day School,
a graded secular and Hebrew School, 1st through 6th grade; with Pre-
Jr., Junior, Teen and Confirmation Congregations and Minyan Clubs.
Extension Services — Library, outstanding collection of juvenile and
adult Jewish books; Adult Education, religious and cultural Jewish
courses; Music programs of religious and cultural Jewish music; Sis¬
terhood and Young Men’s Clubs; Social activities provided by Mr.
and Mrs. Club, Single Adults; Family Recreation Program (Olympic
Pool), summer weekdays and Sundays for all ages at our Campsite.
Youth Activities — ATID for college age youth; United Synagogue
Youth, various age levels are served by Center Youth League, Charles
Palot Club, Kadima Club; Scouting (Boy Scouts, Cub Scouts); Girl
Scouts; Brownies; Athletics, programs for youth and adults; Center
Day Camp & nine weeks summer program at our 36 acre Campsite on
State Road No. 13.
Family Recreation Program — Center Campsite . . . Swimming,
athletics, snack bar, available for members during the summer months.
