The Tofield Memorial Library has a vast array of
books which have appeal to all members of the congre¬
gation. The library is open every day of the week
through the school office. Members of the committee
man the library on Friday evening after services and
on Sunday mornings.
& . • V.
An Oneg Shabbat sponsored jointly with the Sister¬
hood was a moving event with emphasis on Israel’s
anniversary. A Shalosh Seudot was also sponsored in
May with this same theme.
The congregation is kept abreast of new editions by
publicity in the Bulletin. A very able and dedicated
committee is interested in the library activities.
