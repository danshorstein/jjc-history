Who is the Jacksonville Jewish Center? Too
often we talk in abstract terms when describing the
Center and its activities. The fact is that the very
essence of our Synagogue-Cener is the member. That
is why the Year Book is such a treasured publication,
for it records the faces and actions of hundreds of our
friends who throughout the year get involved in the
varied programs of the Center.
Getting involved and doing is “where it’s at” —
the pleasure, the pain, the frustration — but most of
all the pride in seeing accomplishment and progress.
