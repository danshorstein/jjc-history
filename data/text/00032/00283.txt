The Young Men’s Club of the Jacksonville Jewish
Center enjoyed a year of varied and successful acti¬
vities. The Club was led by an Executive Committee
composed of Martin Edwards, Larry Figur, Alan Gor¬
don, Meyer Leibo, Dr. H. Ronald Levin, Dr. Harvey
Pearl, Ronald Pollock and Barry Sinoff.
The objectives of the Young Men’s Club are:
a. To serve as a forum for the presentation of
contemporary issues as well as social, athletic
and cultural activities.
b. To provide service to the congregation, and
community, when needed.
c. To broaden the perspectives and deepen the
feelings of Jewishness of the members.
One of the highlights of the year occurred on
Sunday, April 29 when Charles Ashman, noted author
and broadcaster, spoke to a large gathering. Ashman,
author of Kissinger: The Adventurers of Super-Kraut
was stimulating, entertaining and provocative. Every¬
one was in agreement that it would be a long time
before the enjoyment of the evening would be dupli¬
cated.
Other events sponsored by the Club included:
A Dinner-Discussion with Tom Wasdin, Basketball
Coach of Jacksonville University, a celebrity cocktail
hour and reception for husbands and wives prior to
the Ashman program, co-sponsoring the Athletic
Awards Ceremony for participants in the Basketball
Clinic, and sponsoring a Basketball Team in the City
Church League.
