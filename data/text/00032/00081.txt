B’nai B’rith Women are dedicated to serving you,
your family, your country and humanity. This is car¬
ried through by supporting different national B’nai
B’rith philanthropies such as Bellefaire Children’s
Center; Leo N. Levi Memorial Hospital; National
Jewish Hospital and B’nai B’rith Center for Jewish
patients at Mayo Clinic. B’nai B’rith Women also
sponsors the Children’s Home in Israel and other Is¬
raeli projects. Locally, our activities include: The Anti-
Defamation League (“Dolls for Democracy” program,
distribution of memo calendar books to the public
schools and keeping members aware of the problems
in the Jewish Community); helping at River Garden
Home; sponsoring the B.B. Girls; Hillel at U. of Fla.;
and helping in all community drives. In the coming
year we hope to make the name B’NAI B’RITH syn¬
onymous with our motto: Service, Fidelity and Devo¬
tion.

When I was a child I didn’t mind
To study Hebrew prayer and no other kind.
I went around the world to hunt what is good
So I’ll be a man to appreciate my childhood.

But the TORCH OF GOD kept always burning in front of me
everywhere.
I went on the first step to the last step
What a miracle I saw with advance and lots of pep.
Thanks to my parents, teacher and Rabbi,
As I am saved on the day of HOCHAANA-RABBI.
