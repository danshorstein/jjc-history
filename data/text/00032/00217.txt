DAVID GOODMAN,
President
The Zionist Organization of America is unquestion¬
ably the outstanding American Jewish organization
whose purpose is exclusively for Israel. It is a political
group (not a fund raising), designed to oppose pres¬
sure on the U.S. government by oil interests, etc. These
business interests, aided by other pro-Arab groups, con¬
stantly seek to force our government into a pro-Arab
policy.
The Z.O.A. is the only organization which can ef¬
fectively counter anti-Israel political pressure in Wash¬
ington. It behooves every American Jew to join the
Z.O.A., just as we must support Israel in other ways.
In addition to its main purpose, the Z.O.A. provides

It is imperative that in these times, when the Nixon
