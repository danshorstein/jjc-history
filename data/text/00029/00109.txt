We are proud of our Cub Pack 14 for the out¬
standing role it plays in helping young boys eight and
nine years of age to become good citizens to become
physically fit and morally strong in this fast moving
age when they are exposed to many temptations.
Pack 14 has had continuous charter renewal for
the past 31 years and during the year shared in many
new and different experiences each month, including
a Carnival, Folklore Pinewood Derby, Circus, Nature,
participation in Scout Sabbath Services, Arts and
Crafts and the city-wide Scout-a-rama. All boys aided
in the Goodwill Drive by distributing bags and picking
up articles for the needy.
Always an important part of the Cub Scout year
is the annual Blue and Gold Banquet at which time
awards and acknowledgments are given. This coming
year will initiate the presentation of the Larry Hacker
award to the outstanding Cub.
