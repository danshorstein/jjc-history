The events of the year were as follows: Regular
meeting in September; Dedication in October, Vet¬
erans’ Day Parade in November, Hanukah Party
in December, Camping one weekend in January,
Daughter-Father Banquet in February, Girl Scout
Sabbath in March, Camping one weekend in April
and Silver Springs Trip in May.
The Troop did service projects for Sisterhood,
the Jewish Family Services and the River Garden
Home, and planted trees at th Campsite. The
Troop also sold 2781 boxes of cookies in the yearly
sale, placing second in the Gateway Council, and
enabling the girls to pay for the Silver Springs
Trip. Troop 162 also received the Pier Award.
Leaders for this coming year will be Mrs. Albert
Levy, Mrs. Bernard Nachman and Mrs. Leonard
Saraga. The mothers’ Committee will be: Mes¬
dames Harry Berman, Chairman, Sidney Backer,
Eli Becker, Irwin Gendzier, Earl Horovitz, Gerald
Pincus and Philip Shapiro.
