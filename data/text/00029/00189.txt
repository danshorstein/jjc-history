B’nai B’rith Women are dedicated to serving you, your
family, your country and humanity. This is carried through
by supporting different national B’nai B’rith philanthropies
such as Bellefaire Children’s Center; Leo N. Levi Memorial
Hospital; National Jewish Hospital and B’nai B’rith Center
for Jewish patients at Mayo Clinic. B’nai B’rith Women also
sponsors the Children’s Home in Israel and other Israeli pro¬
jects. Locally, our activities include: The Anti-Defamation
League (“Dolls for Democracy” program, distribution of memo
calendar books to the public schools and keeping members
aware of the problems in the Jewish Community); helping at
River Garden Home; sponsoring the B.B. Girls; Hillel at U.
of Fla.; and helping in all community drives. In the coming
year we hope to make the nam B’NAI B’RITH synonymous
with our motto: Service, Fidelity and Devotion.
“Dolls for Democracy” project of B’nai B’rith Women
will bring historical figures into local schools. Here Mrs.
Gus Goldstein, President, shows part of collection to Mrs.
Murray Starr, left, and Mrs. Harry Glenn, right. Dolls shown
represent Chaim Weizmann, St. Francis, Sister Kenny, George
Washington Carver and Eleanor Roosevelt.
