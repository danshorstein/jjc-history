The Tofield Memorial Library is a depository of
books, information and materials of Jewish interest
for knowledge, recreation and leisure, and appreciation
of the arts. To insure that all holdings are easily ac¬
cessible and available to every member of the congre¬
gation, the library has been kept open every day of ehe
week, including Friday evening and Saturday after¬
noon after services.
The Sisterhood - Library Oneg Shabbat occurred
during Jewish Book Month. It was an enjoyable pro¬
gram. Appropriate book lists were distributed and at¬
tractive book displays were made to encourage con¬
gregation interest and participation.
In cooperation with the Youth Activities Program
of the Center, a “Story Hour” was held in the library
on Saturday afternoons. This was well received.
