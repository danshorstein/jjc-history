The Mr. and Mrs. Club in its eighteenth success¬
ful year promotes friendship among married couples.
Each month exciting and novel affairs are held with
committees formed to arrange each event. The themes
vary from month to month, but include an extravagant
New Year’s Eve party, mid-year Installation Banquet
and a picnic or outing during the summer.
All Couples between thirty-five and fifty years
of age interested in sharing good and friendly compan¬
ionship are urged to contact any club officer or board
member.
