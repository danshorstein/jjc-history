ABRAHAM E. SELBER TRUST FUND
The Jacksonville Jewish Center formally accepted the Abraham E.
Selber Trust Fund at Confirmation Services this year. This is a $20,000
endowed scholarship to encourage students of the Center in careers such
as Rabbi, Cantor, Educator, Teacher, Youth Worker.
The Selber Trust Fund will enable the Jacksonvihe Jewish Center to
strengthen its program of assisting and encouraging high school students to
expose themselves to national programs of intensive Jewish living and learn¬
ing, such as Camp Ramah and all day religious schools.
