This is the year of opportunity and challenge for
the Z.O.A. The American President has confirmed that
the continued existence of a strong Israel is of vital
interest to the United States. However, there are
strong pressure groups in this country (including the
oil interests and local and foreign Arabs) who seek to
discredit Israel and deprive her of American support.
It is the responsibility of the Z.O.A. to oppose the
untruth propaganda of these pressure groups and to
educate the American population, both Jewish and
Gentile, to the fact that America needs a strong Israel
almost as much as Israel needs a strong America. This
can be accomplished through a Speakers Bureau and
through Committees we have organized, together with
Hadassah, to answer the lying letters written by local
Arabs which regularly appear in the Times-Union. We
are also planning other action on this subject.
DAVID GOODMAN,
President
