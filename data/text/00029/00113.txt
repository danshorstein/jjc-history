This year the Troop decorated a grape arbor as a
Sukkah at the Campsite; they decorated a room at the
Campsite; made a Thanksgiving box; took a trip to St.
Augustine, Florida; made felt dreidles and matchbox
covers for Hanukah; had a Latke Party; made wash¬
cloth candles with other Brownie troops in Southside
for an Old Age Home; took part in Girl Scout Sabbath
at the Center; had a Mothers’ Day Tea for the moth¬
ers and presented gifts they had made.
The Fly-up ceremony was held at the end of May.
