There is an old expression in Jewish life, that the synagogue —
Bet Knesseth — is to be used for three functions: as a House of
Prayer, of Sociability, and of study. Without a strong link within these
three functions, the total structure becomes weak. The School De¬
partment felt in respect to study and prayer there has been a tendency
to relegate these activities to the young, mainly. To cope with this
problem the school has striven to reach as many individual students
as possible. Where these contacts were made, the results were excellent.
The most gratifying of all is the fact that our standards have risen
in respect to Bar Mitzvah qualifications, and have opened the way to
the biggest expansion our school has seen in any one year for the Solo¬
mon Schechter School. With five grades for the coming year, and a
definite commitment for a sixth grade next year, we can look forward
to a greater achievement in both areas mentioned above. With the new
rabbinical leadership we will again be in a position to cement relaton-
ships in all areas.
Loyalty is difficult to measure and cannot be repaid. It is with
deep gratitude that we thank the past Chairman of the Education
Committee, Mr. Jack Shorstein, and all the people who served with
him, and welcome Mr. Sheldon Gendzier as the new Chairman, who
has already affected much of our planning in the expanding program.
Thanks also to all the members of the staff in the various departments,
and in the office.
MRS. ALFRED ROSENZWEIG,
