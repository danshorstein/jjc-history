EDWARD WITTEN
Ch. Athletics Comm.
The athletic program of the Center this past year
was one of fun and enjoyment. The men’s basketball
team competed in the Southside Church League and
placed second in the league with an overall record of
13 victories and 7 defeats.
The men’s softball team, a new addition to last
year’s activities proved to be quite a successful unit.
The team participated in the Southeast Church league
placing second overall and compiling a record of 26
victories and 6 defeats. The team was invited to the
Metro tournament but because of the shortness of time
as to when the team was to play the team was unable
to participate.
The future for the athletic program is unlimited
but unfortunately it will only be as good as you the
members make it. We want everyone to keep in mind
the availability to all members the facilities of the
Center and the Campsite. Keep fit!!! Join in the ac¬
tive participation of the Center teams.
