The N.E.T.W.O.R.K. is an organization providing
both cultural and social activities for the single Jewish
community within the age group of 18-35.
Membership consists of a yearly fee plus a nominal
charge for each social affair. Projected plans call for
one party each month, complete with entertainment,
a weekend at Daytona Beach, including young adults
from other cities and periodic mailings to keep mem¬
bers informed of all activities.
All young adults are welcome to join, meet new
people and renew old friendships. For further infor¬
mation, please contact any of the above members of
the Board of Directors.
