The annual Music Festival was presented on Sun¬
day, February 1st at 4:00 p.m. by Cantor Abraham
Marton, the Center Choir and the Center Glee Club.
Featured soloist was Aaron Krosnick, Professor at
Jacksonville University.
The Choir presented Synagogue music by Le-
wandoski and Naumbourg as well as contemporary
composers. The Glee Club was featured in a medley
of Holiday and Israeli songs.
Throughout the year the Center Choir under the
direction of Cantor Marton provides beautiful musical
settings for Sabbath Services and for the High Holy
Days.
