AVA HOFFENBERG
Shalom,
Another year for Gators BBG passes. As we look
back on this year we would like to tell people of our
discoveries.
This has been a rough year for all and especially
our Past President. We had a Spaghetti Supper to
raise money for our yearly Muscular Dystrophy Drive.
At this supper we raised close to $200.00. Also at the
end of the year we received bad news that one of our
advisors were leaving us as she was moving to Miami.
Mrs. Hoffman, we would like to thank you for every¬
thing you did for us and lots of luck with your new
member of the family. With this we would like to wel¬
come our new advisor, Mrs. Louise Axelberg, a past
member of BBG.
Later on in the year we went to conventions for
which we received an award and also a President of
Council.
There is a saying in BBG.
IDENTITY, IDENTITY
WHO AM I?
I’M A BBG
I’M THE ONE
THE ONLY ONE
THERE IS SO MUCH I CAN DO.
So come join us and our brother chapter and make
this year the best of all and see what you can do.
