Nearly 300 persons turned out for the Installation Banquet on Monday
evening, March 30th at the Center, at which time the newly elected Board
and Officers of the Center were installed by Rabbi Dov Peretz Elkins. Mr.
Jack Price was Chairman of Arrangements and was assisted by Mrs. Jack
Price, Mrs. Hilbert Margol, in charge of decorations, Mr. and Mrs. M. D.
Evans, Mrs. Fanny Lippman and Mrs. Nathan Schneider at the door.
The U.S.Y. presented an original Cantata, the Cantor presented a
beautiful music program featuring Israeli songs and was assisted by several
children from the Glee Club.
