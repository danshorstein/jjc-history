The Jacksonville Jewish Center Sisterhood is an
affiliate of the National Women’s League, which in
turn is associated with the Jewish Theological Semi¬
nary of America.
Our program is wide in scope: We encourage adult
education through classes and study groups. Our Gift
Shop helps to introduce objects of Jewish art and
ceremony into every home. We support the building
of a secure State of Israel by means of education and
support of U.J.A. and Israel Bond drives. We try to
give guidance towards inculcating the ethics and cere¬
monies of Judaism into the home and daily life of
every Sisterhood member.
