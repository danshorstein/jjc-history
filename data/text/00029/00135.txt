THE RECREATION GAZEBO
The Center Southside site is open for family recreation throughout the
summer months. For a nominal charge, members of the community may use
all facilities of the site which include an Olympic swimming pool, complete
with dressing rooms and sun bathing areas, a well equipped children’s play¬
ground, tennis, basketball and handball courts and shaded picnic areas. Also
available are two ball fields, modern snack bar and recreational pavillion.
Many families took advantage of this wonderful program of family re¬
laxation by swimming, playing and picnicking together. The highlight of the
summer was a men’s handball tournament which offered much fun and ex¬
citement to the many participants and spectators. First place trophy win¬
ners were Stanley Kantor and Dr. Ronald Levin. Runners-up were Bernard
Brust and Warren Sefton.
The 1971 season will offer many new and interesting family activities.
Plan now to participate!
