I am looking forward to meeting all of our youth
groups and plan with them our athletic programs.
Participation in athletics and teaching have been
great sources of joy to me. I know there is great in¬
terest in revitalizing our athletic program at the Jack¬
sonville Jewish Center. With the help of the youth
leaders and parents, it will be done.
On Sunday afternoons there will be a basketball
clinic from the Fifth grade through the Tenth grade.
From the clinic I will form teams and a league to play
in. We hope we will have a representative team of 11th
and 12th graders to play other organizations.
If we have enough girls participating in volleyball
and tennis, we will also have teams and a league. In
the spring we will start our softball teams.
Let’s look forward to an exciting year in athletics
at the Center and remember its importance.
Sidney Cohen,
Executive Director
