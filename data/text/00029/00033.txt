The title of the cover picture is “KIDDUSH,”
an original color lithograph by Bertoldo Taubert of
Italy.
Bertoldo Taubert was born into an artistic family,
his father being portraitist of Victor-Emanuel, III.
In recent years, the artist has exhibited continually
in the United States at the Hammer or LeMonde
Galleries in New York, the Findley Gallery in Chi¬
cago, or the Marble Arch Gallery in Miami.
His works are warmly humanistic, and his special
additions on religious and ceremonial subjects are
in great demand.
I invite the congregation to my office at their con¬
venience, to see some of his religious pieces —-
“Studying the Torah,” “Sabbath Prayer,” “Spin¬
ning the Dreidel,” “Seder,” “Chanukah,” and “The
Wedding.”
Ben Friedman
