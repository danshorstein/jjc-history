SCENE AT CENTER CEMETERY
Selection of Memorial Property
Family ownership of memorial property simply makes good sense. The Center has plenty of family lots available and it
is not likely that the cost will decrease. As a matter of fact, with the increasing costs of maintenance, prices will likely increase.
Those of us who have seen a cemetery lot selected at a time of immediate need, perhaps by the widow, or by a son, daugh¬
ter, know that this is the worst possible time. There are too many other items demanding attention and it is difficult to give
the matter proper consideration. A cemetery lot should not be selected hastily. Since fate makes no appointments, the best time
to purchase family memorial space is when you DON’T need it.
Visit our Memorial Park on 40th Street east of Main Street. Look around. We take pride in the fact that the property is
well tended. Then, at your convenience, call the Center and arrangements can be made to meet and proceed with the space se¬
lection under ideal conditions.
