55+ Club
The 55+ Club, now in its sev¬
enth year, meets the second
Sunday of each month from
2 to 4 p.m. at the Center.
Programs include speakers,
musical performers, dancing ex¬
hibitions and interesting pre-
Pollie Copans sentations. The entertainment
is always exciting, refreshments are served and there’s
always time for socializing. The annual luncheon in
June is the highlight of the year. Membership is
open to the entire Jewish Community with annual
dues only $10 per person.

Center Choral Society
For nearly 50 years, the Center Choir has been
enhancing services on the High Holidays and
Shavuot with moving liturgical music. Over the
years they have given numerous concerts featur¬
ing members of the choir as well as nationally
acclaimed soloists. Many of the current mem¬
bers have been singing with this group for twenty
years or more. New members are always wel¬
come.
