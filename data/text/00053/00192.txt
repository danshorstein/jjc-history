HADASSAH
Hadassah is a national network of 385,000 women who
accomplish things in their communities and in Israel. From
acquainting women with the richness of their Jewish heritage,
to educating Jewish Youth to building medical centers and
educational institutions in Israel, Hadassah is an organization
that takes its work seriously.
In addition, Hadassah members raise their collective voices in
Washington on issues ranging from women’s freedom of
choice, the environment, education of children and the
security of Israel. 94% of its fundraising dollars is used for
Hadassah projects.
The Jacksonville Chapter is comprised of four vital groups
and the Dollar Power Thrift Shop. We have openings in every
area of expertise and there is a group to fit your schedule.
Won’t you join us?
