Jacksonville Jewish Federation
On behalf of the Officers and Board members of our Jewish Federation, we
wish the members of the Jacksonville Jewish Center a Happy and Healthy
New Year.
The Federation works in partnership with over 2,000 contributors to our
annual campaign to provide invaluable services to this Jewish community.
The services are made possible by the excellent work of our major local
beneficiary agencies...Jewish Community Alliance, Jewish Family and Com¬
munity Services and the River Garden Hebrew Home. Another beneficiary
is the Solomon Schechter Day School.
Our largest allocation goes to the United Jewish Appeal to support Jew¬
ish people in Israel and in over 30 countries throughout the world.
Federation is also the community relations resource for Jacksonville’s Jew¬
ish community and is involved with leadership development, Jewish conti¬
nuity and planning.
Our relationship with the synagogues is extremely important! We view the
goals of the Federation and the synagogues and our work on behalf of our
fellow Jews to be in harmony.
We hope this will be a terrific year for our Jewish community.
Richard L. Sisisky, President
Alan Margolies, Executive Director

The Jewish Community Alliance is a non-profit family ori¬
ented Jewish social agency whose activities include a preschool,
year-round intersession and summer camps, after school day
care, and activities for youth, singles and adults of all ages. At
the JCA children and adults can enjoy recreational, educational,
social, and cultural activities. Programs are designed to pro¬
mote Jewish continuity, strengthen family life, and help indi¬
viduals achieve their full range of potential growth.
The JCA serves as a bridge for Jews developing their identity
in Judaism and broadening it beyond the JCA to synagogues,
their homes, and Israel.
