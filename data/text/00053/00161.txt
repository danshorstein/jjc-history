Men*s Club
Dear Friends,
I have a tough act to follow.
Three years ago Rabbi Gaffney asked Ira Grann to start a Center Men’s Club
Chapter. Ira put together a board of directors, and began building the chapter
from scratch. He established the Men’s Club as a can-do service organization
that’s always ready to pitch in and help.
Neil Sandler took the hand-off from Ira and built the Jacksonville Chapter
into an important Center organization. He involved the Men’s Club in youth-
oriented activities such as Havdalah Under the Stars, Sukkah decorating and
donations to youth activities. He also made sure there were plenty of activities
where we could kick back and relax with the guys.
These two men left a tough act to follow, but a great tradition on which
to build.
We will continue book presentations to Bar and Bat Mitzvahs and
sponsoring the Golden Kippa Award. Our successful programs of past years
will be carried on. These include the River Garden fishing trip, the blood drive,
the Yom Ha Shoah candle project and cooking latkes for the Hanukkah
Carnival.
In August we kicked off the new year with a tubing trip to the
Itchetucknee River. We are also planning a mayoral candidate debate, a wine
tasting, a coffee tasting and an oldies dance.
This year “Boys Night Out” will feature a different activity each month.
For instance, we plan to be making trips to the dog track, a sports bar
and a guy’s action movie.
Gentlemen, come and join us for a year of fun, fellowship and good works.
If you don’t, man are you missing out!
Ken Organes
President
