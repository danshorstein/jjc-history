Message from the Executive Director
At the end of July each year I prepare the new Center calendar (Sept.-June).
Luckily we have a computer program that prints out a calendar with every
Jewish holiday, corresponding Hebrew dates for each day of the year,
every Rosh Hodesh and all Torah readings. The rest is my responsibility...to
enter all our congregation’s lifecycle events and Center special events. By
the time I’m finished the calendar is full. Mind you, I’m not complaining, I’m
kvelling. While some synagogues are struggling to create programs, the
Jacksonville Jewish Center continues to maintain a full calendar of congre¬
gational activities. The creation, implementation and presentation of all
these activities denotes a great deal of work, time and commitment by our
many volunteers, professional staff and support staff of our synagogue.
What we all want most is for you to support the efforts of our dedicated
volunteers by your participation. Whether you are a committee member,
financial supporter or interested congregant, your physical presence is
needed at as many events as possible.
Decide now to choose from the many programs from our varied menu of
religious, educational, cultural and recreational activities.
Isabel J. Balotin
Remember, there’s always a place for you at the Center. I look forward to seeing many of you this year
at our activities. My family and I wish all of you a year of happiness and good health.
