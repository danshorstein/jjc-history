Sunday School
The Sunday School of the Jacksonville Jewish
Center is planned expressly for children in
kindergarten, first and second grades. A weekly,
two-hour program gives each child the opportu¬
nity to experience the richness of his/her Jewish
heritage through song, dance, role playing,
cooking, short stories, games, arts and crafts.
Emphasis is placed on the holidays and festivals.
Jewish values and mitzvot are encouraged.
