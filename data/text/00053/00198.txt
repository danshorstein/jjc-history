Consecration 1993/5754
Consecration
Celebrated December 11, 1993
The Aleph classes of the Solomon Schechter
Day School and Afternoon Hebrew School
demonstrated their knowledge and devotion to
Judaism in a lovely program during the Shabbat
services. Each consecrant received a siddur, a
certificate and a sweet treat that is traditionally
given to mark the beginning of formal Hebrew
education.
