Recreation
The Jewish Center’s Recreation Department’s goals are to provide the Jewish
community with programs and activities that promote the improvement of physical
skills, health, the development of special interest and/or hobbies,and to provide the
opportunity for relaxation and social camaraderie.
Many of the recreation activities take place at the Center’s Recreation Park which
consists of approximately 10 acres of beautifully wooded campgrounds, complete
with an olympic-size swimming pool, bath houses tennis courts, softball fields, an
outdoor basketball court, volleyball and badminton area, children’s playground,
gazebo, snack bar and shaded picnic area.
Jane Grann
Recreation Chair
Within the main building is a regulation basketball court, the Neal Portnoy Youth
League and an arts and crafts room, a large room for dances and a versatile stage
for musical and dramatic productions and a meat and dairy kitchen with the modem equipment. We take
pride in our recreation program and the marvelous facilities of our Center.
Gayle Bailys coordinates our Day Camp and Sunday Basketball program. Jane Grann is the chairperson
of the Recreation Committee.
