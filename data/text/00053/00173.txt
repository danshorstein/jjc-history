Family Council
After six years FAMILY COUNCIL is still providing innova¬
tive Family Education programs. This year we took a bold
next step - the development of large scale projects that
span a preparation and presentation time of a month or
more. All facets of the congregation are involved: the
classroom teachers in all Center schools (Center Preschool,
Solomon Schechter Day School, Afternoon Hebrew School
and Sunday School), synagogue professionals, volunteer
experts, parents and children.
Under the creative direction of Rabbi Dov Kentof with the inspirational guidance of Rabbi David Gaffney, and Robin
Morris and parents of the congregation, many families enjoyed two exciting programs:
Noah’s Ark -
A Valuable Voyage - Again!
Rabbi Dov Kentof, as the biblical Noah, took a
group of students and parents on an open touring
boat for an educational recreation of Noah’s
famous voyage. The group cruised up the St. John’s
River to the Jacksonville Zoo, to reinforce the sidra
of Noah that had been studied in the Center’s
schools that week. The highlight of the experience
was a tour of the zoo complete with biblical refer¬
ences and Hebrew names of all the animals.
A Fantasy Flight to Israel took off from the Jacksonville
Jewish Center’s “airport.” Every detail was addressed to make this
adventure as real as possible. Students of levels kindergarten
through second grade in all the Center’s schools had studied Israel
as a month long unit which explored all aspects of Israeli life and
culture.
