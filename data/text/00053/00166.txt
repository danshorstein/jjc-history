Mission Statement
The general nature of the objects and
purposes of the Jacksonville Jewish
Center shall be to exercise, promote
and protect the Jewish religion in the
City of Jacksonville, Florida, and to
maintain a synagogue for the religious,
social, physical, cultural, educational
and recreational needs of the Jewish
people of Jacksonville.
Stuart Hecht Jeffery Morris
