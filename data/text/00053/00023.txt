Performing Artist Series
Jacksonville Jewish Center Proudly Announces
Performing Artist Series
The 1993-94 season of this highly successful Performing Artist Series brought
exceptionally talented and popular Jewish performers to Jacksonville from all over
the world. Devoted to promoting and enhancing Jewish life and culture in our
community, the Series presented each performance in a relaxed and intimate
atmosphere with limited seating that allowed every member of the audience to feel
personally entertained.
The 1993-94 Series featured three varied and outstanding
programs:
•ACHINOAM NINI Thursday, October 21,1993
•MITCHELL ZEIDWIG Sunday, January 9,1994
•ZALMEN MLOTEK- JOANNE BORTS Sunday,
February 13,1994
Another year of exciting programs has been planned.

•ACHINOAM NINI
Thursday', October 21,1993
This charismatic Israeli born singer/songwriter
combined American Jazz and Pop with Israeli and
Yemenite folk songs. She was accompanied by
acclaimed guitarist, Gil Dor. The sound and lighting
were as sensational as the singers.
•MITCHELL
ZEIDWIG
Sunday, January 9,1994
In a very unusual way, Zeidwig
combined brilliant piano
technique and sensitivity with a
flair for the outrageous. He can
and does play the piano with
everything but the kitchen sink.
A hilarious program.
