Adult Education
Adult Education at the Jacksonville Jewish Center continues to expand to meet the needs
of its members. Most importantly, one of the most basic tenets of Judaism, the impor¬
tance of learning, is not only being acknowledged but it is being given a life of its own at
the Center.
The wide variety of programs being offered this year include: A Shabbaton, Dec. 2 & 3
featuring Deborah Lipstadt; Setzer-Levit Lecture; cultural Arts program featuring videos,
films & artists; Israeli dancing, book reviews, holiday workshops, Hebrew literacy classes,
conversational Hebrew, Firing Line discussion, Talmud study, Torah study, and Torah &
Haftorah reading classes.
In addition, Rabbi Gaffney will continue to teach the Sisterhood Bat Mitzvah class and a
discussion group on Saturday afternoons entitled: Jewish Personal & Social Ethics.
