Early Childhood Department
JACKSONVILLE JEWISH CENTER
EARLY CHILDHOOD DEPARTMENT
Marilyn Sandler, Director
The Early Childhood Department of the Jacksonville Jewish Center
includes Pre-school and Extended Day Care. The school is accredited by
the Florida Kindergarten Council and affiliated with the Early Childhood
Association of Florida.
Located in the Selevan Pre-school and Child Care Center, this depart¬
ment advocates policies which enhance the quality of life for all Jewish
children and families in the community, regardless of affiliation. We
strive to provide the very best early childhood educational programs
available.
This past year has been rewarding, thrilling and a growing experience
for all involved. We anticipate another large enrollment of enthusiastic
and loving children for the 1994-95 school year.
We look forward to another challenging school year as we keep working
for Jewish children!
