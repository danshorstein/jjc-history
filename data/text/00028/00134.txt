Our Campsite offers families of the Jacksonville Jewish community the
opportunity to play and relax together in a setting of restful beauty, min¬
utes from the city.
The Olympic size pool is perfect for an early morning dip, mid-day
sun worshipping or cool afternoon swims. There’s a Kiddie pool too!
The large shaded picnic area and the beautiful open-air gazebo is an
ideal spot for family picnics or delicious Kosher food can be obtained from
the modern snack bar.
The Fun Time Program includes exhibitions and entertainment each
Sunday that the Camp is open, plus the use of all facilities for active sports,
a swinging playground for the younger set and air-conditioned game rooms
for those who wish to play cards or just relax in the cool.
The membership fee is nominal for a fun-packed summer starting the
1st of May through Labor Day.
