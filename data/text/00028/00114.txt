Brownies of Troop 147 participated in a very ac¬
tive and rewarding Scout year. New girls were invested
in October and by November enjoyed a trip to
Marineland. In December the girls had a gay Hanu-
kah party and participated in a service project for the
Jewish Family and Children’s Services.
March is the month of “Girl Scout Week” and
Troop 147 joined the other Scouts of the Center to
take part in the late Friday evening Services and
sponsoring the Oneg Shabbat which followed.
In April the Troop received the Piper Award pre¬
sented by the Gateway Council for meeting required
standards and closed the season in May with a Moth¬
er-Daughter Tea at the Center Campsite.
