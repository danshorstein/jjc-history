The Center placed a basketball team in the
Southside Church League this past season, one of
the tougher basketball leagues in the city. The team
played well at times but due to injuries and incon¬
sistency they faltered in the standings.
Edward Witten, Chairman
The Sunday afternoon basketball program held
at the Center and directed by Dr. Arnold Silverberg
was successful and will be a good source of future
stars for Center teams. He was assisted by Meyer
Leibo Jeff Morris, Jack Schemer and Herbert Gordon.
On Sundays and Holidays, the Athletic facilities
at the Center Campsite were kept busy with players
taking advantage of the fine basketball, handball,
tennis and volleyball courts.
We look forward to a great athletic season for the
entire Center family. Available to members will be
the excellent facilities of the Center and the Camp¬
site. Keep fit! Join in the active participation of com¬
peting on the Center teams.
