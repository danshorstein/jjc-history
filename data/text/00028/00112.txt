GIRL SCOUTS - TROOP 162
Leaders: Mrs. Sylvester Kilishek and Mrs. Sidney
Backer.
Scouting is a very important phase of the youth
program at the Center and Troop 162 has done its
share to prove that Scouting develops character and
teaches girls to be useful, to appreciate the out-of-
doors and to create from simple things.
During the past year the girls did extensive work
to earn merit badges. Two exciting field trips included
the Naval Air Station and Cumner Art Gallery. The
girls also participated in the Center Friday evening
services at the Center in honor of Girl Scout Week
and celebrated the birthday of Juliette Low, the
founder of Girl Scouts, with an original skit prepared
by Sandra Franzblau.
As a service project the Troop prepared a food
basket for a needy family. The girls look forward to a
busy year as they continue to find their place in Scout¬
ing, the Synagogue and in service to their community.
