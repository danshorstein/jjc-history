Shown above is the newest addition to our fleet of buses. We have
three large vehicles like the one above and one small bus.
Our staff of drivers is headed by Morris Montford, a twenty year plus
veteran, who has an enviable record. He teams with the other two drivers
to transport our students from their homes and schools to classes at the
Center.
Herbert Lewitt is Chairman of the Transportation Committee which
supervises the system of routes, under the direction of Kenneth Bowden.

Our maintenance staff shoulders one of the heaviest burdens in our
Center program, that of maintaining our facilities so that they are always
in a presentable state for our use and for the various activities at the Center.
Pictured are Tim Riley and Annie Mae Fort, both of whom have been
loyal and devoted members of our maintenance crew for many years. Annie
Mae Fort also maintains the Kitchen and prepares many meals.
The local caterers, Mr. and Mrs. Meyerhoff and Mrs. Mary Safer pre¬
pare many Simchas for our members and their families.
Our appreciation to this loyal and devoted staff who have given many
years of unselfish service to the Jewish Center.
