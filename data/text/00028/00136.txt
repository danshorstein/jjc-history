EDWARD SIEGEL
Chairman
The Adult Education Committee, with Edward Siegel as Chairman and
in coordination with Rabbi Jerome Lipnick, launched on eight week series
of discussions on “Moral Issues of Our Time”, and featured outstanding
speakers and panelists.
The first session on January 22, 1968 featured Rabbi Philip Lipis. On
February 12 Rabbi David Arzt spoke, Rabbi Marc Tannenbaum spoke on
March 27.
The series included:
1. Jewish Values and Traditions: Still Valid Today?
2. The “Bad” Child: Whose Fault?
3. Crisis on the Campus: The College Revolt
4. Law and Order vs. The Right to Dissent
5. Black Militancy and Anti-Semitism: A Real Danger?
6. The Protest Movement: What’s It All About?
7. Integrity and Compromise: Is the Honest Man Obsolete?
8. Crisis in Religion: Same Old Problem or a New Threat?
Serving on the Committee besides the Chairman were Gerald Sohn and
Warren Leve.
