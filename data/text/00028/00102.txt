CENTER YOUTH ORGANIZATIONS
Center Youth League (C.Y.L.) — A coed United
Synagogue Youth Chapter for students in the
10th, 11th and 12th grades. Meetings are held
on the second and fourth Sunday of each month.
Charles Palot Club (C.P.C.) — U.S.Y. Chapter for
8th and 9th grade boys and girls. Meetings on
the second and fourth Sunday of each month.
Kadimah U.S.Y. — A coed club for 6th and 7th grade
students with varied social, religious and athletic
functions held monthly.
Boy Scouts — Troop 14 — Meetings on Tuesday
nights at 8:00 at the Center. Patrol meetings are
held weekly in neighborhoods.
Cub Scouts — Pack 14 —
Den Meetings held weekly in neighborhoods.
Pack Meetings — last Thursday of each month
at 7:30 P.M. at the Center.
Brownies — Troop 147 — Meetings every Friday.
Girl Scouts — Troop 162 — Meetings every Friday.
Girl Scouts — Troop 462 — Meetings every Friday.
