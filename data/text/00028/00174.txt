Serving the entire Jewish community of Jackson¬
ville, the Jewish Community Council is the central
Welfare Fund and Community Relations body of the
Jewish community. An association of individuals,
synagogues, organizations and social service agencies,
the program encompasses key activities which give
unity, cohesion and coordination to local Jewish com¬
munal life.
A community-wide annual Welfare Fund Cam¬
paign provides support to significant local, national
and overseas organizations. The National United Jew¬
ish Appeal has expressed American Jewry's respon¬
sibility for the welfare and rehabilitation needs of
Jewish communities the world over for over 20 years.
River Garden Hebrew Home for the Aged and the
Jewish Family and Children's Services are the local
beneficiaries.
The Council publishes a monthly newspaper known
as the Commentator. It is the official organ of the
Council and its member organizations.
The Council conducts a leisure time activities
program which includes Youth Council programs such
as college preparatory workshops, a youth news page
in the Commentator, and a youth division in the
annual Jewish Welfare Fund Campaign. The Council
also provides a community summer Day Camp for
children between the ages of 4 and 15.
