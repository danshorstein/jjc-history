Women’s American O.R.T., while only 4 years
old in Jacksonville, has been in the United States
since 1924. O. R. T. has been a National Organiza¬
tion for 89 years.
0. R. T. is a program for teaching people work
skills so they can make their own way in life. It is the
CHARITY TO END ALL CHARITY.
Briefly, the 0. R. T. Program consists of:
• 650 Trade & Technical Training Centers.
• Located in 23 countries, including North
America, South America, Iran, India, France,
and Italy.
• Approximately 50,000 persons, adults as well
as children, are helped annually.
• Teaching staff, mostly ORT graduates, num¬
bers about 1,700.
The President and Vice-Presidents of O.R.T.
• O.R.T. Schools teach 72 different job skills.
• Annual cost of this program is in excess of

• Largest program is in Israel, with over 35,000
trainees.
