The Center Day Camp, founded in 1946 by the
Jacksonville Jewish Center, has continued its growth
each year and broadened its scope of activities to in¬
clude the finest facilities and programming offered in
any Day Camp.
For nine exciting weeks under the supervision of
a well trained staff, children of Center members and
of the total Jewish community have the opportunity
to spend the summer in the out-of-doors, making new
friends, developing new interests and acquiring new
skills.
Numerous activities include swimming, skiing,
scuba diving, fishing, arts and crafts, nature lore and
Israeli arts, as well as all active sports. The camp is
operated on a weekly basis with bus transportation to
and from Camp.
Mrs. Barbara Nykerk, Secretary; Barry Setzer,

Our Campsite consists of 36 acres of beautiful
woodlands, including an Olympic swimming pool, bask¬
etball, tennis and handball courts, baseball and soft-
ball diamonds. We are proud of our membership in
the American Camping and National Water Ski As¬
sociations, which assures our campers of the highest
standards in Camping.
