The Mr. and Mrs. Club is now in its seventeenth
successful year, with a dual goal of promoting friend¬
ship, and enabling married couples to share
good times. Each month exciting and novel affairs are
held and guided through the leadership of committees
formed to arrange each gala event.
The themes vary from month to month, but tradi¬
tional events include an extravagant New Year’s Eve
party, a mid-year Installation banquet, and a picnic
or outing during the summer.
As in all other social clubs, there is a central core
of officers and board members, and the organization
is governed by husbands and wives serving and work-
together. Through each member’s sharing the responsi¬
bility of serving on a monthly committee, an atmos¬
phere of warm congeniality always prevails, and the
promotion of good fellowship is the end result.
