Cubmaster Sidney Engelhardt with the Cub Pack
Cub Scouts of Pack 14 have completed another
successful year and look forward to continued growth.
Den meetings are held weekly at the homes of the Den
Mothers and Pack Meetings are held on the last
Thursday of each month at the Center.
Many exciting and challenging projects were un¬
dertaken by the ambitious clubs including a Fall Car¬
nival with members of the dens designing and operat¬
ing game booths, a race car derby involving cars made
by the Cubs, and numerous skits presented at the
Den meetings.
Highlighting the year was the participation of the
Cubs in the annual Scout Sabbath during Boy Scout
Week and the Blue and Gold Banquet at which time
each year the Pack charter is presented by the North
Florida Council of the Boy Scouts of America.
A program such as this could not be possible
without the dedication of many interested men and
women who give of their time to serve our youth.
Thanks for a job well done go to Mr. Sidney Engel¬
hardt, Cubmaster; Mrs. Robert Berinhout, Den Moth¬
er coach; to Mrs. Fred Kupperman, Mrs. Donald
Kupfer, Mrs. Sid Ralph and Mrs. Mitchell Peltz, Den
Mothers; and Mr. Ted Freedman, Webelos leader.
Thanks also to William Rose, Institutional Rep¬
resentative and the following members of the Pack
Committee: Charles Mizrahi, Chairman; Murray
Starr, Milton Berman, Sidney Ralph, Bernard Brust,
Leonard Saraga and Donald Young.
