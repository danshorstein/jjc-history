(“The Future” — College Age Youth)
ATID is an organization for Jewish college age students which meets
once a week during the summer vacation. The meetings are composed of
discussions on relevant topics of today’s world, followed by a social hour.
Among the topics discussed are the campus demonstrations, the black re¬
volts, the importance of religion today, and the value of personal goals.
These weekly meetings are both inspirational and enjoyable and all
college age Jewish youth are invited to attend.
