Message of the President
I am very happy to report that the Center has
*■
progressed along the lines of finance, education,
religion, recreation and community service. The pages
of this book will describe this growth. Permit me,
however, to emphasize this one thought - it is very
easy to become satisfied with the progress that has
been made, it is easier to slide backward than to move
forward. To hold the gains that have been made, will
require your support and cooperation as a worker and
as a contributor. It is not sufficient to provide
religious needs - you and your children must partici¬
pate in them. It is not sufficient to supply educa¬
tional facitilies - you and your children must use them.
Nor is it sufficient to arrange recreational activi¬
ties - you and your children must find a need for them.
Only in this way will the Jewish Center grow and expand
its functions, only with this cooperation will we be
successful.
Let us pray that the New Year will bring peace to all
the world, and that the United Nations will emerge the
victors, and then we will move forward towards even
greater accomplishments. May God grant you all a very
Happy New Year.
ABE NEWMAN.
