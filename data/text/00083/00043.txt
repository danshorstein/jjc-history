An opportunity for adventure
and achievement is open to boys
of nine, ten and eleven years of
age through the fine program of
Cubbing. It is a colorful activity
that is quite natural to boys of this
age and leads to the gateway of
Scouting where they begin a new
and different kind of adventure.
Cubbing gives the parents the
finest opportunity for joint partici¬
pation with the child, as Mother,
Dad and the home are all tightly
bound into the Cubbing program.
During the past three years Cub
Pack 14 enjoyed considerable suc¬
cess. However, last vear because
of the preoccupation with war ac¬
tivities and shortage in help, there
was a decrease in the activities of
the Pack. Parents found it dif¬
ficult to attend meetings and to
cooperate in Pack projects. It is
hoped that during the coming year
the Pack will be able to launch a
full program of activities.
