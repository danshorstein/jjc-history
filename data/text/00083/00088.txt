September 1—Junior Hadassah Yom Kippur Dance.
October 7—Daughters of Israel Opening Luncheon enjoyed by many.
October 11—Sunday School Registration.
October 13—Senior Hadassah Opening Meeting and Thrift Luncheon has large
attendance.
October 20—Ladies’ Aid Society Opening Luncheon successful.
October 27—Daughters of Israel have successful card party.
November 11—Zionist District installs Harry Katz as president. I. M. Lieberman
and Rev. J. Robin honored for outstanding service to organization.
November 13—Debate on timely subject followed late services. Enjoyed by large
congregation.
November 20—Harold S. Cohen, editor of Jacksonville Journal, speaks at late
services.
November 18—B’Nai B’Rith Auxiliary initiated with impressive ceremonies at joint
meeting of B’Nai B’Rith and Auxiliarly. Rabbi Amram Prero and
Mrs. J. Goldstrom guests of the evening.
November 24—Ladies Aid Society enjoy bridge party.
November 20—Harold S. Cohn, editor of Jacksonville Journal, speaks at late
November 27—Special Thanksgiving Service held. Choir renders appropriate
selections.
December 6—Jacksonville Jewish Center has elaborate Chanukah Dinner. Rabbi
S. Benjamin guest speaker and Hyman Silverstein, master of cere¬
monies.
December 9—Joint meeting of Senior, Junior and B. & P. W. Hadassah enjoyed
by many.
December 31—Daughters of Israel sponsor brilliant New Year’s affair.
January 10—Center Mortgage is paid off.
January 21—Pregressive Credit Union holds annual banquet for membership.
January 24—Jacksonville Zionist District announces opening of Bond Drive.
January 27—Senior Hadassah Membership Tea is lovely event.
February 5—Troop 14, Boy Scouts of America, sponsor late service.
February 12—Annual Daughters of Israel Sabbath enjoys large congregation.
February 13—A. Z. A. Fraternity‘sponsors successful Valentine Dance.
February 28—Daughters of Israel have 28th annual affair. Show, “Hi-Di-Ho” en¬
joyed by many.
March 10—Senior Hadassah holds Linen Shower. Large amount of linens sent to
Palestine.
March 21—Purim Affairs sponsored by the Center and Jewish Welfare Board. Max
Auerbach and Rabbi M. D. Margolis speakers.
April 2—Center-Temple Joint Service. Dr. Abram Sachar, national head of
Hillel, guest speaker, enjoyed by large gathering.
April 9—Dr. Norman Salit, author and lecturer, guest speaker at Friday night
services. Large congregation attends.
April 13—Dr. Nacham Yud addresses audience at Center.
April 16—Girl Scout Troop 2 sponsors Friday night service.
April 19-20—Center conducts Passover Sedorim for service men and women.
Large number attend.
May 7—All youth groups participate in special Mother’s Day Service. Last late
service of the season.
May 12—Ludwig Lewisohn, noted author, appears before large audience at the
Center, sponsored by the lo cal Zionist District.
May 18—Center officers and Board members re-elected to serve another year.
June 6—Closing exercises of Sunday School. Prizes and awards presented to out¬
standing pupils.
June 13—Sixteen boys and girls Confirmed at impressive ceremony.
June 14—Abe Newman elected as Jacksonville District Representative to the
American Jewish Conference.
June 21-23—Zionist Youth Commission sponsors three day conference. Dr. Aharon
Kessler and Miss Helen Seff lead sessions.
August 15—A. Z. A. Fraternity sponsors annual minstrel and dance, which
proves very successful.
