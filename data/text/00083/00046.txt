During the preceding year the
Senior Daughters of Judea organized
a club for the girls who were too
young to join the seniors. They called
this club the Junior Daughters of
Judea, which they sponsored until the
Junior Hadassah appointed for them
a capable leader, Now it is one of the
more active clubs in Jacksonville.
The Senior club sponsored a hot dog
supper and a club party during the
year. They also participated in Young
Judean Sabbath, Mother’s Day ser¬
vices and similar affairs.
