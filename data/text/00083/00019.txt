MORRIS WOLFSON
We are happy to pay this tribute to Mr. Morris Wolfson. Both as
chairman of the Mortgage Clearance Drive and as a contributor, he was
greatly instrumental in the retirement of the Center Mortgage. His loyal
support of our president and his generosity gave the drive the impetus
it needed to assure its success.

he and his beloved ones be granted many years of life, good health and
happiness.
