Pledge recited by the Confirmands at the conclusion of the
Confirmation service:
I consecrate my life to my people and to my people’s faith. With all
my heart, with all my soul and with all my might will I endeavor to live
in the spirit of the lofty ideals of Judaism. I solemnly vow to help maintain
Jewish institutions, national, religious and charitable, to assist in the re¬
building of Eretz Yisroel as a national home for our oppressed brethren.
To live up to the highest ideals of American citizenship, to marry within
the Jewish faith and to live the life of a loyal Jew to the end of my days.
I shall ever be faithful to the belief in the oneness of God and the brother¬
hood of man. Sh’ma Yisroel, Adonoi Elohenu, Adonoi Echod. Hear, Oh
Israel the Lord Our God, the Lord is One.
