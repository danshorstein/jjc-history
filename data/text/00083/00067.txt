Our Jacksonville B. & P. Division has made a parallel growth with the national
figures, and numbers at present 50 members.
As a part of Senior Hadassah, the B. & P. Division helped fill every quota in the
various Senior Hadassah projects: Hadassah Medical Organization, Child Welfare,
Jewish National Fund, Youth Alivah, and. Palestine Supplies. The Jax B. & P. Division
has also taken an active part in USO work, sponsoring several dances, buffet suppers,
and home parties. Our members have helped with many Re’d Cross endeavors. We
have also assisted in obtaining rooms for rent in private homes to relieve the critical
housing shortage" that Jacksonville is faced with. The Jacksonville B. & P. Division
is justly proud of our constructive work in a world where so much is being torn down.
The year 1943-44 will demand even more? of our time and energy. Zionism is facing
a crucial test. Jewish immigration into Palestine will ce?ase in March, 1944, if the
final phase of the British White Paper is carried through. The end of Jewish immi¬
gration would be a blow at the very foundations of the Zionist program in Palestine
and would crush the last hope of Jews in Nazi Europe. Our weapon is an American
Zionist movement of powerful mass strength. Hadassah, which has ably proved its
ability to med crises before, is now called upon to exert extraordinary effort to achieve
a vastly greater membership. EVERY MEMBER must be stirred to a realization of
her responsibility to the Jewish people at this decisive hour. None must remain
outside the working ranks.
