Boy Scout Troop 14
Boy Scout Troop 14 moved forward and expanded this year through its inten-
sifted scouting program. This was highlighted by the attainment of the rank of
Eagle by Byron Kolitz and Bronze Palm by Herbert Fishier.
This year the Center Men’s Club sent George Glossman, Stephen Freedman
and Byron Kolitz to the Junior Woodbadge Course, a leadership training course, at
Camp Echokotee. George Glossman was chosen Quartermaster of Troop No. 1,
North Florida Council and Stephen Freedman was chosen Patrol Leader of Troop
No. 3, Sunnyland Council at the National Jamboree at Valley Forge, Penn.
Byron Kolitz was awarded the Ner Tamid, the highest religious Scouting award
at Scout Sabbath.
The troop is looking forward to bigger and better things in the coming year.
