Building Fund - School Project
The membership of the Jacksonville Jewish Cen-
ter is asked to examine carefully the pictures on this
page.
You will say these are pretty pictures.
The pictures are pretty because they show fine
looking youngsters.
Consider the space these classes are using for
Religious School instruction:
1. The Synagogue balcony serves for one
classroom.
2. Another class met in a room with unfin-
ished walls.
3. An old residence about to be demolished
accommodated one of the classes.
4. The Lobby of the Synagogue was convert-
ed to classroom use on Sunday morning.
5. Other classes meet in rooms not designed
for classrooms.
We all agree these conditions cannot continue
much longer.
Praised be the Lord, we have a large number of
children to accommodate. See the report in the
Religious Education Section.
HELP PROVIDE A PROPER CLASSROOM
FOR EVERY RELIGIOUS SCHOOL PUPIL.
CONTRIBUTE TO THE BUILDING FUND.
Harry Gendzier
Chairman
