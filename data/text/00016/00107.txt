The J. C. A. (Jewish Community Alliance) is located on a large acreage on the
banks of the St. Johns River at 8301 Ft. Caroline Road. It is a common meeting
ground for all Jewish people, and furnishes cultural, educational, recreational and
social activities.
The J.C.A. is affiliated with the National Jewish Welfare Board and cooperates
closely with all religious, community and social organizations in Jacksonville. Jewish
tradition is followed in all its aspects, such as the serving of Kosher food.
The J.C.A. Day Camp had its third banner year with a large attendance of children.
A number of children were able to participate this year due to the J.C.A. Scholarship
Fund.
