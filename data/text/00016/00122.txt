The season of activities of the Jewish Center Men’s Club was initiated last year
with a paid-up membership dinner at which the Hon. Claude Pepper was principal
speaker. Every one of our projects was supported generously and the membership
enjoyed a most successful year.
The Minyan Club, composed of Bar Mitzvah age boys, has continued to meet
regularly Sunday mornings under the guidance of Cantor Marton for a service, fol-
lowed by breakfast.
We sponsor the Bernard Glossman Award for Boy Scout Troop 14. This year
three boys attended the National Jamboree at Valley Forge, Penn.
The Center Men’s Club awards prizes and scholarships to the boys and girls of
the Hebrew School and our Sunday Religious School.
Membership in the Center Men’s Club includes dinners served in connection
with monthly meetings. On these occasions features of civic interest, educational
content and sports interest are introduced. An event of great enjoyment annually is
the Father-Son and Daughter dinner meeting.
The Club has assisted with the adult education features of the Center and has
sponsored a discussion series of its own. In the field of sports we organize activ-
ities as the demand arises.
The newly elected Board promises to give the administration ever greater sup-
port for all undertakings.
