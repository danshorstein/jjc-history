The Mr. and Mrs. Club is now in its fourth year of existence as a social club
affiliated with the Jacksonville Jewish Center. The Club has brought together many
of our old established Jacksonville residents and many newcomers to the city in a
bond of friendship and sociability. We are extending a welcome to those who are
desirous of becoming associated with the Mr. and Mrs. Club.
Harold Pausman
The sincere untiring efforts on the part of the Officers, Board of Directors and the various committees who served willing-
ly since the inception of our Club have established one of the city’s leading social clubs.
The outstanding gala events of our Club, including the Installation affair, the Invitational Affair whereby our membership
are permitted to bring local guests, and our New Year’s party have always been successful affairs at no additional cost to our
members. In addition to these affairs, every month we have a small get-together and on every third Saturday night a dance with
a live orchestra and the serving of food, which is handled by a designated committee.
In electing our Officers and Board of Directors, we have maintained a policy to have both husband and wife serve together,
creating a stronger working body to provide ideas and benefits for our Club.
