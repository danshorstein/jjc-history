SUSAN TUCKMAN
The B’nai B’rith Girls’ year began with an installation and dance on behalf of the new members and officers. Throughout the year the seven-
fold program of B.B.G. was carried out.
Among the service projects carried out by the B’nai B’rith Girls are visiting the River Garden Home for the Aged and helping at Hope
Haven Hospital.
Two of our most outstanding projects for the year were a Fashion Show, sponsored by Susan Gayle and Suro’s, and a rummage sale.
This year B.B.G. sent several representatives from our chapter to the Florida Regional Convention in Miami. A very large delegation of
A.Z.A.’s and B.B.G.’s also attended the North Florida Council College at Tampa in April. In June we sent two delegates and two alternates to
the B’nai B’rith Youth Organization District Convention.
