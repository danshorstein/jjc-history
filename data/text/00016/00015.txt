STUART LEVINE
For the Esquire Club, 1957 was a most successful year under the capable leadership of Stuart Levine. Much credit is due Miss
Elaine Leif and Joel Stein for devoting so much of their time and effort to the affairs of the Club. Credit also goes to all the club
members and their officers.
This past year the Esquire Club contributed to the Cerebral Palsy drive, Hope Haven Hospital and the Jacksonville Jewish
Center.
In athletics the Esquires won from all rivals in football and softball.
