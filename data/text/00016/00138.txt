Vice-President ........Sheldon Gendzier

The Couples Club enters its sixth year of existence as an important social outlet
for the young married couples of the Center family. This year marks the division of
the club into two organizations in order to more effectively integrate the group’s program.
As in the past, social functions of various types are the major activity of the Club.
A major social affair is held each month, with several small "informal” affairs being
interspersed during the calendar year.
The Couples Club performs a service to newcomers in the community and to
newlyweds by acquainting them with the membership of the organization. The Club
helps build friendships and encourages community and Center participation. We look
forward to the coming year with the hope that our program may be carried out to its
most successful conclusion.
Lewis Meisel
The Amity Club
President Irwin Gendzier
Vice-President Stanley Trussel
Treasurer Morris Spiwak
Recording Secretary Mrs. Leo Moskovitz
Corresponding Secretary Mrs. Marvin Ganson
The Amity Club is the newest social club of the Center Family.
We have been in existence only a few months, but are already making great strides
toward fulfillment of our program.
The regular monthly affairs are looked forward to and attended by 75 couples
who comprise our membership.
For the coming year, the Amity Club plans to continue with an active program
for its members. We feel certain that the future activities will be more varied.
Irwin Gendzier
