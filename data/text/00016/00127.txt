Cub Scouts Troop 14
This year was indeed a banner year for the
Cub Scouts of the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
A total membership of 99 were registered at
Scout Headquarters. This includes Den Moth-
ers and Committee, but it was the largest reg-
istration of any Pack in the history of this
District.
The annual Minstrel Show, directed by
Cuhmaster Dr. Harry Yergin, and featuring an
all Cub Scout chorus, was again a very success-
ful project.
Cub Pack 14 was awarded the White Ban-
ner “Onward for God and My Country—Na-
tional Recognition, 1957” and Plaque for out-
standing service of the unit, its leaders and boys
by the National Council of Boy Scouts of
America.
Among the outstanding activities of the past
year were the Father and Sons Banquet at the
Center and a visit to one of the carriers at
Mayport.
We are looking forward to another active
and enjoyable year.
