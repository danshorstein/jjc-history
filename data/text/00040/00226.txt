Israel Kaplan Lodge #800 B’NAI B’RITH is an active organization in the Jewish
Community of Jacksonville. The lodge meets monthly with general meetings and
has dinners several times a year. Lodge membership is at the 275 mark for this
year. Various projects are held during the year to raise funds for B’NAI B’RITH
youth services. Hillel Foundations at the various state universities are supported
by B’NAI B’RITH as well as local A.Z.A. Chapters and B.B.G. Chapters. The local
lodge under the direction of Milton Hecht raised funds during the year 1979 and
purchased a torah for the Hillel Foundation at Florida State University in
Tallahassee. Every December lodge members gather to ring the bell for the
Salvation Army in downtown Jacksonville. Any member of the Jewish Community
interested in joining B’NAI B’RITH should contact the lodge president, Stuart
Hecht at 731-3406 for further information.
