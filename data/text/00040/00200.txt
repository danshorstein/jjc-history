Boy Scout Troop #14 is for boys ages 11-17. The troop has
been in existence 60 years and is among the oldest in the state
of Florida. Scouting activities include numerous outdoor
camping activities- learning of many skills and participation in
programs which contribute to the spiritual, physical, and
emotional growth of the individual boys.
Experienced scoutmaster, Randall Silverberg and assistant
scoutmaster Billy Wilensky provide excellent leadership for
the troop.
Some of the many activities this season have been:
Appalachian Trail Hike
Suwannee Raft Race
Scout Sabbath
Blue and Gold Banquet
Scout Basketball game sponsored by Me Donald’s
