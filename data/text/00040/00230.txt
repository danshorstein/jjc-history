The Progressive Credit Union was begun in 1940 to provide a measure of financial service to persons of the Jewish faith in
Jacksonville.
Consumer loans are made to members for family, personal and business needs. New ideas are constantly being explored to
assist in the financial needs.
Belonging to the Credit Union is an excellent form of savings. The safety of savings is fully protected at all times. All
members receive free Life Insurance on their savings, up to $2,000.00. All loans are covered with free Credit Life Insurance for
the full outstanding balances. The interest rate charged is lower than the interest rate of most lending institutions in the
Jacksonville area. All savings are insured up to $100,000 by the Florida Credit Union Guarantee Corporation.
The Progressive Credit Union is supervised by the Board of Directors who are elected by the membership. All officers are
directors of this community project serve without compensation whatsoever.
We extend a cordial invitation for all to join in the Progressive Credit Union to build a stronger Jewish Community in
Jacksonville. For information please call the Progressive Credit Union Office. 354-4770.
