Through its programs the Jewish Center seeks to foster individual
growth and development, encourage participation in community life,
provide opportunity for friendship, fun, and the acquisition and
expression of Jewish knowledge and identification.
To achieve these objectives, the Jewish Center employs the services
of professionally-trained staff as well as experts in a variety of related
fields.
The Center’s 36 acre complex contains full and comprehensive
facilities for cultural arts, community forums, social, athletic and club
activites.
