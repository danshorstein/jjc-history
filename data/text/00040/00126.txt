Neal F. Portnoy Youth Lounge
The Neal F. Portnoy Youth Lounge was made possible
through the generosity of the Louis Portnoy family. This well-
equipped and well designed facility is used to serve the needs of
the youth of our congregation and the Jewish youth of the
entire community. It is the central location for formal
meetings, programs and activities. It also serves as a
synagogue for youth services.
Equipped with all sorts of games, such as pinball
machines, foozball, bumper pool, electronic and board games,
the youth lounge has something for everybody.
During March, which is National Physical Education and Sports Month, the schools of the Center promoted physical
fitness with Jump Rope for Heart. The event was sponsored by the Florida Association for Health, physical education
and recreation and our schools to benefit the American Heart Association. Sponsors paid each participant according to
the time each jumped rope. Many parents and students participated.
