Message from the Administrative Director
To me, the Center has become a way of life — not because I have my daily
duties as Administrative Director, but because the position evolved from the
many years of volunteer work that started with membership in the Sisterhood,
leadership in all phases of the Girl and Boy Scout programs, advisor to all ages of
Center youth, Chairman of the Youth Commission, Sisterhood President,
member of the Center Board of Directors and finally Day Camp Director.
I mention all of these not in a boastful way, but with the hope that those of
our membership who are not involved in the multi-dynamic programs of our
Synagogue-Center will know that they are missing many inspiring and long
remembered experiences. There is more to be gained in “Nachas” and rewards
than the time and effort given.
As Administrative Director, I am here to maintain our building, oversee the
food program and to serve our members in all areas, to encourage your
volunteer involvement, assist you with organizational, Bar/Bat Mitzvah and
wedding plans — or whatever the need may be.
To all our members. I extend an open invitation for you to call me anytime.
My husband, Louis, who is my silent partner, right hand and sounding
board when the going gets rough, joins me in wishing our entire congregation a
joyous and rewarding New Year.
BLANCHE SLOTT
Administrative Director
