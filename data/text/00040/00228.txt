Cassia Garden Circle for Jewish Ladies
Organized in 1960, Cassia Garden Circle, an affiliate of the Garden Club of Jacksonville, is a
group of Jewish women who are interested in gardening. Many of the charter members are still
active in the group.
Cassia is a group of tropical trees, shrubs and plants with compound leaves, yellow flowers and
long pods, found in Israel. The Garden Club meets monthly. Programs have included Japanese
Flower Arranging, bonsai, hanging baskets, underwater arrangements and potpourri. Many
exciting plans have been made for next year’s programming. Spend an interesting morning, and
enjoy a light lunch with some pleasant ladies. If you are interested call Jean Goodman at 733-1565.
