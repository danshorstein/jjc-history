Girl Scout Troop #162
Girl Scouting is more than a club, it is a nationwide, worldwide
movement for girls ages 7-17. Girl Scouts make a promise and
live by the Girl Scout laws. These simple guides prepare a young
girl to live in our bewildering modern world.
GIRL SCOUT PROMISE
On my honor, I will try;
To do my duty to God and my country,
To help other people at all times,
To obey the Girl Scout laws.
Troop 162 has fulfilled all of the obligations of scouting throughout
the year under the leadership of Clara Mann. Arts and crafts
camping, working on all badges has made some very worthwhile
experiences. Together the girls worked on many badges such as
troop camping, cooking, active citizen, which included a tour of
the Police building.
The troop had a great time bowling once a month and is making
up their own badge for it. Our troop also takes pride in being the
third top salesgirls for the annual cookie sale.
