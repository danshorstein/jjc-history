Sisterhood Salutes the Gift Shop Committee
We recognize the Gift Shop committee
for the many hours and years of dedica¬
tion they have given on behalf of Center
Sisterhood. These women have earned
for themselves and for our Sisterhood a
record of great distinction. The gift
shop has always been well-stocked with
a varied selection of Judaica, of fine
craftmanship, most of which have been
made in Israel. Ceremonial objects,
holiday gifts and decorations, educa¬
tional materials and religious articles
are readily available.
We thank the following ladies for
their tireless efforts on behalf of the
Sisterhood Gift Shop. Their talents and
knowledge have made the shop one of
the finest in Jacksonville.
