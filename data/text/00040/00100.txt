Sunday School
The Sunday School of the Jacksonville Jewish Center is
planned expressly for children in Kindergarten, First and
Second Grades. A two hour program provides each child the
opportunity to experience the richness of their Jewish
Heritage through song, dance, role playing, cooking, short
stories, games, arts and crafts. Emphasis is placed on the
holidays and festivals. Jewish values and mitzvot are
encouraged.
This year the Sunday School was under the direction of
Rabbi Dov Kentof and is also coordinated by Tricia Bloom.
The staff includes: Nancy Minkoff, Kindergarten teacher,
Scott Zimmerman, First Grade teacher, Karen Zimmerman,
Second Grade teacher. Music teacher is Judy Geller and
tutors are Jody Roey and Sabrina Katz.
