PLAYSCHOOL CAMP
For children 2 years old by June 1, 1982. This camp is designed for the young child’s first
separation from home. Movement, crafts, music and stories are a few of the activities
introduced under the guidance of experienced adults. Monday and Wednesday or Tuesday
and Thursday, 9:30-12:00.
JUNIOR CAMP
Operated as a separate camp within our Camp, this group of three and four year olds will
receive an enriched program which will include swimming instruction, arts and crafts,
songs, dancing and games. Field trips and sports activities are geared to their age level. 9
a.m.-12:30 (includes lunch). A full day program is also available.
