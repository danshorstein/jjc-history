Nathan Friedlin
The State of Israel honored Nathan Freidlin at
an Israel Bond Breakfast on November 16,1981.
Mr. Freidlin was chosen to receive the “Lion of
Judah Award” because his life best exemplifies
the highest tradition of service to his synagogue,
community and to the state of Israel.
A lifelong resident of Jacksonville, Mr.
Friedlin has been a member of the Board of the
Center for over 30 years and is a past president.
He has also served on the Religious committee
for over 20 years. He is a familiar face to all those
who attend Shabbat Services. A visitor of Israel
four times, Mr. Freidlin recalls one of the most
memorable times when he visited Israel in 1976
with the Chumash Study Group.
Nathan is married to his lovely wife, Ann.
They have two sons, Mel and A1 and two grand¬
children. We wish the family mazeltov.
