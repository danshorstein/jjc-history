Mindful of the special interests and needs of our youth
entering 5th and 7th grades, we offer a new and exciting
program adapted to the advanced level of this ‘tween’
age group. Activities of Chalutzim will be a combination
of trips, tours, outdoor recreational activities, regular
camp programming. Campers will plan many special
activities with their counselors and our professional staff.
A tentative schedule includes snorkling and fishing at
Salt Springs, canoeing at the Canoe Outpost, a beach
trip, and others to be planned by the group.
A transportation fee will be charged for all trips.
