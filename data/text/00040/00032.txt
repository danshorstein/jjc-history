CENTER SISTERHOOD
As our theme, “Therefore Choose Life”, guides us for a
second year, we continue to strive for new ways to make more
knowledgeable choices. The theme for the coming year
provides guidance for wise choices to make our personal lives
richer and more meaningful. When we, as individuals, choose
to learn, we are taking a significant step toward choosing life.
The word “choice” reflects the current mood among women
today. Never before have women had so many choices open to
them and never before have women had to be so serious and
resolute in their decision making. We are aware of the influence
a woman has upon her family. We know that many women
have chosen to work outside the home, sometimes not by choice but of necessity. At
the same time, it is incumbent upon each woman to choose the perpetuation of
Judaism as a prime responsibility.
I feel it is important that you understand the true meaning of Sisterhood: we are able
to create and foster Jewish sentiment in the home, further Jewish education in the
family, sponsor and encourage the youth programs that insure our heritage, and
participate in activities to strengthen our relationship with our community and Israel.
It is our desire to keep you informed through reports of Social Action, Israeli Affairs
and Jewish Family Living. You may want to participate in a special interest group such
as Creative Handicrafts or join our Sisterhood Bat Mitzvah class. In addition our
Educational Activities Committee lets you select the area of study that stimulates your
intellectual and cultural growth. At the same time that you are enjoying activities of
your choice and learning new skills, you will be meeting new people and forming
lifelong friendships.
Center Sisterhood welcomes you to participate in another productive year.
I want to extend to you and your families my wishes for a Happy and Healthy New
Year.
Fondly,
Rene'e Gordon
