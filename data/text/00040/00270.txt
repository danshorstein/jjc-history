Passover for All Project
Ricky Plotkin and Steven Shapiro are busy calling for sponsors.
“Let all who are hungry come to eat.” The youth of the Jacksonville Jewish
Center undertook a most meaningful project to make those words a reality.
Each student in each of the schools and youth groups was requested to bring a
minimum of .50 to be used to purchase food for needy Jewish families for
Passover.
Another part of this project was the sponsorship of those individuals who had
no seder to attend. Our young people were responsible for calling the entire
congregation and asking for their support and help in this effort. The response
was most gratifying. Over 20 boxes of Passover foods were distributed.
