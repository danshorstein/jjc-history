EVENTS OF THE YEAR
To record in detail all the activities of the Cen¬
ter would take more space than is provided in this
volume. The reader who is acquainted with Syna¬
gogue life realizes that the account of a year’s ac¬
tivity by the affiliated organizations and major depart¬
ments do not convey a complete picture. The pic¬
ture would be equally incomplete if only events of
which the Center was the sole sponsor were spread
over these pages. As a Center Synagogue we cooper¬
ate with and encourage communal organizations
whose programs are compatible with our own. The
chronological list of events of the Center are held
at the Center, whether at worship or on other oc¬
casions, it is hoped will aid in the appreciation of the
service rendered by the organization to the local com¬
munity and to the community of Israel. For a com¬
plete record and closer examination of the Center’s
functioning, one would have to go to the files of the
Center Bulletin published weekly through the active
season.
