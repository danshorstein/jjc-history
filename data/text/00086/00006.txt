Hamishah Asar BeShevat (Fifteenth of Shevat) is Palestinian
Arbor Day. Also called the New Year of the trees. In Palestine
school children use the day for planting thousands of trees each
year. In the Diaspora it is an occasion for stressing the signifi¬
cance of the upbuilding of Palestine.
Shabbat Shekalim the Sabbath when every adult Jew in the
days of the Temple was reminded to pay his poll tax of a half
a shekel on the first day of Adar. In our day, a shekel is paid
by every adult Jew, entitling him to vote for delegates to the
Zionist Congress.
