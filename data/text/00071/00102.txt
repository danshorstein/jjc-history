SHALOM BABY, A GREAT PLACE TO BEGIN!
The DuBow Preschool’s Shalom Baby program will begin its seventh year this fall. Our special program offers
bonding time for parents, babies and toddlers. Each class offers songs, stories, circle time, crafts, special visitors and
hot topic discussions.
Early childhood experts agree that there are numerous benefits of having friends and socializing at a young age.
Friendships will add spice to your child’s life. They can help children learn new skills, increase their vocabulary, and
provide them with lots of opportunities to learn to share and cooperate with others. It also offers a chance for parents
to network and get support. Early childhood is a unique, exciting and special time in a family’s life. Many friendships
that begin among parents in playgroups such as Shalom Baby continue and grow in years to come. If you are interested
in learning more, please call Shereen Canady at 268-4200 ext. 143.
