V~ A Message from the President
A MILLION REASONS TO BE THANKFUL
I appreciate the opportunity to serve the Jacksonville Jewish Center as
President for a 3rd year. We are very fortunate to have an excellent leadership
group who, along with an outstanding professional team/staff, are engaged and
committed to meeting the needs of our membership and families throughout
the year.
We continue to evolve and grow as a synagogue....and, the ongoing
financial generosity and commitment from our members is, and will continue
to be, critical to our future success. This past year, we raised more funds
than any year in the Center’s history! Between the High Holiday Appeal, the
L’Dor V’Dor Annual Fundraiser, the 50th Anniversary of the Day School,
Annual Donations and Matching Funds, the total comes to approximately One
Million Dollars! That is an amazing accomplishment we are extremely fortunate to have so many of
our congregants who understand how important and vital it is to continue “investing” in our synagogue.
I’d like to personally thank everyone who has been extremely supportive and given of their time,
energy and effort during my Presidency and, for your desire to continue to do so for one more year....
it is greatly appreciated!
Helen, Sophie, Charlie and I (along with Max and Oliver) wish our entire Center Community a healthy
and happy year 5773.
Shana Tovah,
Michael DuBow
