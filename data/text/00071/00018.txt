Jewish National Fund gives all generations of Jews a unique voice
in building a prosperous future forthe land of Israel and its
people.
JNF:
• Greens the desert with millions of trees and builds thousands
of parks to create new communities and cities for generations
of Israelis to call home.
• Pioneers innovative solutions to stretch Israel’s water supply
by 12%.
• Educates both young and old about the founding and importance
of Israel and Zionism.
