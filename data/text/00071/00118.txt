MAKOM: What an amazing year! MAKOM
Hebrew High saw its numbers rise in its second year
oi existence. Students enjoyed the opportunity to learn
about Israel, and Jewish ethics while having important
conversations about God, current events, and Jewish identity in America.
Electives ran the gamut from the arts to Jews in sports to Jewish dance & film.
This year’s highlights included a number of special programs and tiyulim
(trips). We were blessed to have workshops by The Bible Raps and Arie Hasit,
program coordinator for Ramah’s semester in Israel. Our MAKOM on “Hot”
Wheels theme brought us to Christinas, FL for airboat rides followed by a journey
to Daytona to go-cart and visit the International Speedway. A number of students
participated in the Panim el Panim Conference in Washington D.C. in conjunction
with our senior year program, Siyyum. As we strengthen our connection with
our USY chapter, we hope that we continue our social action work and Shabbat
presence in the community. MAKOM is the “place” to be for a great social
atmosphere, engaging learning opportunities, and lasting memories. A big yasher
koach and todah rabah to all of our students, faculty, and parents for making
MAKOM such a meaningful experience!!
