SUMMER PROGRAMS FOR JEWISH YOUTH
On the Ramah Israel Seminar, Ramah campers spend six weeks on an
exciting and fun-filled in-depth program, complete with hikes, archaeology,
desert treks, simulation games and an incredible variety of hands-on
experiences! The program focuses on contemporary Israel in the context
of Jewish history, the Jewish tradition and the Middle East. A pre-Seminar
option is to spend eight days in Poland, emphasizing the vitality of Jewish
life prior to the Holocaust.
