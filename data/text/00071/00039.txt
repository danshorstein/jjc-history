ADULT EDUCATION COMMITTEE
We are now reviewing the classes and our
annual Marilyn and Louis Safer Shabbaton with
great pride. Our clergy offered many informative
classes in Torah Studies, Talmud, Music and Liturgy,
as well as current events. We offered a monthly film
series, Hebrew reading and conversation classes,
trop class, and a new adult B’nai Mitzvah class.
Meditation and Yoga continue, and Hazzan Holzer
organized a wonderful “Shabbat at Sea” cruise. We
co-sponsored a Sisterhood Shabbaton, featuring
Lisa Kogen. The congregation thoroughly enjoyed
the Ramat Gan dancers, an amazing group of teens
from Israel, who entertained and taught us.
This year’s Louis and Marilyn Safer Shabbaton offered an education through “unlearning.” We learned
more about being Jewish through the unlearning of some misconceptions of Islam.
Our Adult Education Community has grown this year with new members joining our already motivated
group of committed individuals.
I would like to give a special todah to Lois Tompkins, who organizes, schedules and is ultimately responsible
for all of the fine details that make our Shabbaton and our Adult Education Community run so smoothly. Thanks
to our incredible clergy and the administrative staff of the Jacksonville Jewish Center, our programs offer the
best topics and subject matters to promote learning in our community. I would also like to welcome as my co¬
chairperson, Andrea Raitt, who I am looking forward to working with this coming year.
Wishing you a sweet, peaceful and healthy new year.
Shana Tovah,
Kimberly Miller
