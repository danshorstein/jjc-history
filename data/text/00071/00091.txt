Galinsky Academy
From generation to generation the importance and significance of a Jewish
education has been a cornerstone of the Jacksonville Jewish Center. L’Dor V’Dor
annual campaign for education at the Jacksonville Jewish Center was developed by
stakeholders in the community who recognize the value of the Martin J. Gottlieb
Day School, the Bernard and Alice Selevan Religious School, the DuBow Preschool,
and Makom Hebrew High. The goal of the campaign is to annually provide financial
support that will ensure the sustainability of these vital institutions.
All gifts made to the L’Dor V’Dor campaign are vital to the advancement of the
Galinsky Academy. Your contribution will help our schools develop and maintain a
challenging curriculum, reflective of our mission to provide the highest quality of
education to our children.
Interested parties can become donors to the L’Dor V’Dor annual campaign through
many different opportunities. Please contact Don Kriss at 904-292-1000 or email
