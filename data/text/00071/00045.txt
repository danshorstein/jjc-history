BOY SCOUT TROOP 14
Every year is a great year for
troop #14 of the North Florida
Council, the only Jewish Troop in
Northeast Florida. As it enters its
ninety-second year, it continues to
be a vibrant part of youth activities
of the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
All campouts begin with Friday
night services, with lighting of
the Shabbat candles, and continue
with services on Saturday morning.
Scouts learn not only outdoor
skills, like camping and hiking,
but also leadership skills, and
the importance of service to our
community.
We are open to all young males
between the age of 11 and 18 years
who are interested in adventure.
Please contact Jeff Sandler for
more information.
