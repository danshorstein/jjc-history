SOCIAL ACTION
As I step into the position as Social Action Community chair, I am aware of how much there is to do in order
to fulfill our mission. The dedicated committee has been interested in feeding the hungry through Operation Isaiah,
seeing to those who are in need of a smiling face by doing art projects at Brooks Rehab and helping the Jacksonville
Jewish Center at our second annual Community Service Day. There is so much more to do.
At Rosh Hashanah, we collect food for the JFCS Pantry with our Donate The Weight Campaign. We ask that
you recycle your magazines and books by donating them to Baptist South so those who find themselves there as
patients can have something to read to pass the hours or days. We are asking for items to be donated to the Teacher
Depot and have scheduled Sundays at Sulzbacher Center when we can prepare and serve dinner.
There is so much more to be done and we are looking forward to a year in which each congregant will be
involved in at least one mitzvah project. There will be drives and hands on projects and something for each one of
us to participate in. Several different groups at the Center are involved in mitzvah projects and need the support of
you, our congregants.
1 look forward to your suggestions and support and together we can help each other and help to make our world a
better place. Meetings are scheduled for the 2nd Wednesday evening of each month at 7:30 p.m. and each of you are
welcome to join us. We are currently scheduled to be
at Sulzbacher Center on October 14, December 16,
February 17, April 14 and June 16. We are happy to
be able to bring Danny Siegel to spend several days
with us next spring for our Shabbaton weekend. We
look forward to spending time with Danny Siegel and
going into our community to perform mitzvot.
