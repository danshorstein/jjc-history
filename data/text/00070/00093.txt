Kids Club
KIDS CLUB CATERS TO ALL
The Jacksonville Jewish Center is proud to offer an outstanding before and after-school
program for children ages one through twelve. Kids Club, our extended day program,
meets the needs of our families by providing nurturing, engaging, educational and fun
programming before and after school.
The theme for the 2011-2012 year is called, “Alphabet Afternoons”. Our preschool
children will enjoy activities that are built upon the alphabet. During “C” week they might
enjoy one of our favorite authors, Eric Carle, build a home for their caterpillars and begin to
learn about the lifecycle of a butterfly by watching their caterpillars mature, have crawling
caterpillar races, create colorful collages and sing their favorite songs.
Our MJGDS elementary students who join us after school will have a homework and
study period in our supervised homework room, a snack, go outside to play and then engage
in a fun activity. We are also thrilled to share our exciting and varied afternoon enrichment
programs. We offer a variety of classes for our preschoolers and elementary aged children.
We welcome back Mad Science, soccer, art, cooking, Bricks 4 Kidz, dance classes taught by Mark Spivak’s Dance
Studio, basketball, flag football, Mind Lab, and more.
