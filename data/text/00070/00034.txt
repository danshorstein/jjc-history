SOCIAL ACTION
The mission of the Social Action Community
(SAC) is to encourage congregant participation in
Tikkun Olam, repairing the world. The goal of the
community to create local projects to assist those in
need. The Social Action Community aims to make
community service a defining part of our Jacksonville
Jewish experience on a daily basis, in every
interaction, and in everything we do.
We held our 1st Annual Community Service
Day, A Mitzvah Day of Spruce-Up at the Center on
Sunday, June 12, 2011, with over 75 congregants who
rolled up their sleeves and participated in projects
around the building including painting, ritual repair
and landscaping. In the upcoming year we plan to
hold a Sulzbacher Coat Drive, continue the High
Holiday food drive, and participate in preparing and
serving meals at the Sulzbacher Center. SAC will
be collaborating wherever possible with all of the
Center’s schools and organizations. We have also
inaugurated a new S AC-initiated Center Youth Corps,
which invites all of our youth to participate.
It is our hope that volunteers will connect to one
another through rich community service experiences
that will be meaningful and long-lasting.
