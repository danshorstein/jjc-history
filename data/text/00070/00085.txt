LEADERSHIP IN EDUCATION
Over 300 people gathered at the Jacksonville Jewish Center on June 5 for the Second Annual L’Dor V’Dor
Celebration to enjoy a complimentary brunch and honor Robin Morris, one of the most-loved teachers at the
Jacksonville Jewish Center schools with the Rabbi David Gaffney Leadership in Education Award. Family,
friends, students and colleagues came together to celebrate the success and impact of Robin Morris and her
34-year career as an educator.
Current and past students shared stories and a musical presentation to honor her outstanding contributions to the
Martin J. Gottlieb Day School, the Jacksonville Jewish Center Preschool, the Bernard & Alice Selevan Religious
School and the Center’s Hebrew High program.
The event, co-chaired by Helen DuBow, Michelle Margol and Jennifer Plotkin, was also an opportunity to
celebrate the success of the Center schools and the L’Dor V’Dor Society, the annual fundraising campaign that
supports all Jacksonville Jewish Center schools. The dollars raised by L’Dor V’Dor ensure that the Center schools
are able to continue to offer quality secular and Judaic currículums.
