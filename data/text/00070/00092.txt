SHALOM BABY PROGRAM
The Jacksonville Jewish Center Preschool’s Shalom Baby program is in its sixth year. Classes meet weekly to offer
mommies and babies an environment to play, leam and meet new friends. Each class offers songs, stories, circle time,
crafts, special visitors and hot topic discussions.
This year we will celebrate many milestones.. .first smiles, first steps, first teeth, first words, and first friends. We
welcome in Shabbat, celebrate Rosh Hashanah, Passover, Hanukkah, and more. Mother’s Day and Father’s Day both
become extra special as we help moms and/or dads and babies create beautiful keepsake projects.
If you are interested in learning more, please call Shereen Canady at 268-4200 ext. 143. Hope you can join us!!
