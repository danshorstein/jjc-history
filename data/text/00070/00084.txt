Atthe Jacksonville Jewish Center
From generation to generation the importance
and significance of a Jewish education has been
a cornerstone of the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
The L’Dor V’Dor Society, an annual giving
campaign, was developed by stakeholders in
the community who recognize the value of the
Martin J. Gottlieb Day School, the Bernard and
Alice Selevan Religious School and the JJC
Preschool. The goal of the society is to annually
provide financial support that will ensure the
sustainability of these vital institutions.
All gifts made to the annual giving campaign
are vital to the advancement of the Jacksonville
Jewish Center schools. Your contribution will help
our schools develop and maintain a challenging
curriculum, reflective of our mission to provide
the highest quality of education to our children.
Interested parties can become members of the L’Dor V’Dor Society through many different levels
of giving. To become a member of the L’dor V’Dor Society please consider a minimum gift of $18
per year.
