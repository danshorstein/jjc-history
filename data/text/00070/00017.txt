After an impressive history of 39 years, Beth Shalom Congregation has integrated
with the Jacksonville Jewish Center. Beth Shalom was a leader in many areas of Judaism
in Jacksonville and an asset to our community - both Jewish and secular.
The Jacksonville Jewish Center welcomes members of the Beth Shalom family and is
pleased to have them join the Center family and enhance our activities and spiritual life
together. We will share ideas and ideals, pray together, play together, study and learn
together, observe Shabbat and holidays together, celebrate life cycle events together,
renew friendships, make new friends and socialize together.
