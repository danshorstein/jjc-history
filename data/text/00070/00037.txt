WHAT A YEAR IN SISTERHOOD!
Center Sisterhood had
such a great year in 5771!
We got things rolling
with a program teaching
us to take the stress out of
our lives and kept going
through the year with
a Torah Fund Brunch
featuring JTS graduate
Hazzan Jesse Holzer
and Kol Train. In the
spring we took a look
back at our history with
a lunch honoring our Past
Presidents and the visit
by the hosts of First Coast Living was quite a highlight of
our year.
Sisterhood women were mitzvah mavens. We
provided games for kids through the Malvai Washington
Foundation, Best Buddies, the Davis Performing group,
Girls Inc., Wolfson Children’s Hospital, and the Center
Day Camp. We also donated food for the JFCS F.A.N.N.
Food Pantry, books through Books a Go Go, and toiletries
for the Children’s Home Society.
Lara Werwa
Sisterhood President
We continue to offer financial support to the Youth
Department, Religious School and Camp Scholarships.
Sisterhood debuted a fresh take on education with Hot
Topic, an opportunity to look at current events through a
Jewish lens.
Center Sisterhood women have a history of leadership
at the highest levels of Women’s League for Conservative
Judaism and this year was no exception as Phyllis Goldberg
was installed at the Biennial Convention as an International
Vice President, Barbara Safer as an International Board
member, and Lara Werwa as International Z’havah Co-
Chair.
At that same convention, our Sisterhood was presented
with the Jewels in the Crown award recognizing the
programs and community engagement of our Sisterhood
during Gayle Bailys’ presidency.
Sisterhood women know that when you need
friendship, togetherness and support, or education
opportunities, Sisterhood is the place to go.
We hope you are ready to join us for a fabulous 5772!
