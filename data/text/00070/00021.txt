>- A Message from the President
REFLECTING ON A BUSY YEAR...WHILE LOOKING TO THE FUTURE
Michael DuBow
As I sit to write this article, it presents an opportunity to reflect on a busy, yet
very fruitful, year.. ..one that has provided key building blocks for the foundation
of our Center Community in the years to come. We are fortunate to have a
committed and forward-thinking Team in place.. .Clergy, School Administrators/
Teachers, Front Office Staff, Maintenance Department and Margo’s Catering all
working together with a dedicated Lay Leadership Team to ensure the continued
success and growth of the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
We welcome our fellow Conservative Jews from Beth Shalom.. .it has been
a pleasure having you join us at the Center and we look forward to your growing
involvement in our future together. Our “Community of Connection” initiative
continues to expand through relationships with Sulzbacher and Habijax, as well
as through several social action programs.. ..I invite you to become involved.
An exciting opportunity lies ahead as an Education Task Force has started work on the development of
a strategic plan for our schools to explore the viability and structure of an “Academy Model” where all of
our educational programming would share key resources and incorporate a common vision. Stay tuned for
additional details as this process moves along.
There are several other initiatives underway and many others in the planning stages....! welcome your
input and involvement as we continue to build on our strengths and seek ways to capitalize on the opportunities
before us. A big “thank you” to all who have been extremely supportive and given of their time during my first
year as President your efforts are greatly appreciated!
Helen, Sophie, Charlie and 1 wish our entire Center Community a healthy and happy New Year.
Shana Tova,
Michael DuBow
