DANIEL F. HUBSCH
The Young Peoples’ League, better known as Y.P.L.,
functions under the auspices of the Jacksonville Jewish
Center. As a group we wish to extend our thanks to the
Center for the use of its most adequate facilities and to
Rabbi Sanders A. Tofield for his help and guidance.
Y.P.L. during the past year, has provided Jacksonville’s
young Jewish adults with programs which have included
social and religious activities, sponsored dances, and organ¬
ized religious and social discussions.
Along with our regular activities, Y.P.L. has extended
the group s functions to include service men stationed at
Jacksonville military installations.
Lor the coming year, Y.P.L. plans to continue with an
active program for its members and promises to provide
wider fields in which its members can express themselves.
Y.P.L., like Jacksonville, has set its aims high and will
endeavor to grow with the Jacksonville Jewish community.
