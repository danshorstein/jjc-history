CHARLES HAIMOWITZ
The Esquire Club is happy to annouce that 1955 was one of the most successful
years in the history of the organization, under the capable leadership of its president,
Charles Haimowitz.
Without the cooperation of all the officers and members, the Club could not have
grown into the outstanding organization that it is today. Special credit is due to our mascot,
Miss Brenda Kramer, who graciously assisted in all of the Club’s functions.
This year the Esquire Club contributed to the Center Building Fund, the Cerebral
Palsy Foundation, and to the building fund of the Hebrew Home for the Aged.
The Esquire Club has also had the most successful sports program in many years,
which was topped off by the defeat of our arch-rival, A.Z.A., in both football and basketball.
Again this year the Club paid a visit to Hope Haven Hospital and entertained the
children with games and gifts.
