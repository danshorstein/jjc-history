ISIDORE WEISS
Now in its fourth year, the Couples Club has
firmly established itself as an asset in the social life of
the young maried couples of the Jacksonville Jewish
Community.
Through the medium of monthly social and cul¬
tural affairs, fellowship and companionship among
the 125 couples, who are members, are achieved.
Our regular monthly social affairs have included
dinners, dances, costume affairs, swimming parties
and various other activities for the entertainment of
our members and their guests. Our smaller affairs
are cultural in aim and have also included game
nights and informal get-togethers.
in furthering the benefits of the Club to its mem¬
bers, we have sponsored bridge lessons, bridge tour¬
naments, golf tournaments, dramatic groups and
dance lessons.
Looking into the coming year, we feel certain
that we will continue to grow socially and culturally,
and look forward to even more varied activities in
the future.
