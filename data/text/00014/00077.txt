IRENE KELLER
Junior Hadassah, the Young Women's Zionist Organi¬
zation of America, provides fully organized social, educa¬
tional and cultural activities. From the very beginning, it
has been Junior Hadassah’s purpose to participate actively
and concretely in the building of the Jewish homeland
through specialized projects. Weier Shfeyah, the under¬
privileged children’s village is a source of pride to our group.
We also assist in the support of the Henrietta Szold School
of Nursing, Youth Aliyah and the Jewish National Fund.
Not only are Junior Hadassah members inspired by the
tremendous task of building our homeland, but we also
remember our responsibility as American citizens, and as
members of our local community. Junior Hadassah plays
an integral part in development of the Jewish community.
The Jacksonville Chapter of Junior Hadassah has been
recognized by our national organization as one of the lead¬
ing chapters in the Southern region. With the help of a
strong membership, we can continue to achieve our goals.
