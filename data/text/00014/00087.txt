Cover
In view of the special interest that our congregation has this year in the Holy
Ark, the Year Book Cover depicts an Ark with decorated drawings designed by the
well known artist, A. Raymond Katz, for Congregation Beth Shalom of Lawrence,
Long Island. The photograph is distributed by the Jewish Museum of the Jewish
Theological Seminary of America. Last year's cover carried a sketch of the Ark
designed for our Synagogue by the Rambusch Company of New York. By next
year we hope to feature a photograph of our own Ark.
