Girl Scout and Brownie Troops 29, 197, 212 and
25, under their respecitve leaders, Mrs. Louis Slott,
Mrs. Sidney Stillman, Mrs. Irvine Kowitz and Mrs.
Joe Fraden, completed a very successful year.
Members of the Troops participated in all phases
of the Girl Scout program as established by National
Headquarters; International Thinking Day, Cookie
Sale, Court of Awards and Girl Scout Sabbath.
Individually, the Troops have enjoyed cook-outs,
hikes, over-night trips and socials.
The girls participated in many civic projects such
as making napkins and favors for the patients’ trays
at the Duval Medical Center, working with the T.B.
Association and taking part in Community Parades.
