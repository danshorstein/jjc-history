WILLIAM SILVERMAN
Organized in 1953 by a small group of men and women, the
Mr. and Mrs. Club has attained a membership of one hundred
couples, fulfilling a long needed social functioning group in the
Jacksonville Jewish community. Created for the purpose of social
get-togethers, this club has been the means of bringing its members
together in a harmonious spirit of friendship and good fellowship.
In electing officers and board of directors, it has been the cus¬
tom for husband and wife to serve together, thereby creating a
governing body that works cooperatively and wholeheartedly to¬
ward providing the maximum benefits the organization has to offer
for its membership.
Among the gala events this year were the installation cere¬
monies ,the first anniversary celebration, a New Year’s party, and
many costume and informal affairs, planned for the entertainment
of members, their families and guests.
The sincere efforts of the officers, board of directors and com¬
mittees who served during its first year of organization, have estab¬
lished a firm foundation for the Mr. and Mrs. Club, and we feel
that it will continue to progress and prove to be one of the most
outstanding social groups in the city.
Success for its affairs is due also in no small part to the co¬
operation of the Jacksonville Jewish Center and for the facilities
made available for our use.
