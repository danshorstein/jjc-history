Our Book and Gift Shop has greatly facilitated
acquiring ceremonial and religious objects for our
homes. With funds raised on this project, Sisterhood
has, this past year, supplied additional filing cabinets
and new desks in the main office. These have made
it possible for the office staff to render more efficient
service.
Through our “Donors for Beautification ”, Sister¬
hood annually makes a worthwhile contribution to¬
ward the improvement of the beauty or usefulness of
our buildings.
Sisterhood is affiliated with the National Wom¬
en’s League from which we get guidance, informa¬
tion and suggestions. The past two years, the National
Women’s League has sent us a speaker for our An¬
nual Membership Tea.
In December we held a Hanukah Institute that
proved very helpful and interesting to all who par¬
ticipated.
We have a very active program planned for the
year ahead of us. We need the help and support of
every Sisterhood member in order to render the ut¬
most in service and assistance to our Jewish Center.
