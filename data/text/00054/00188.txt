Lhe B’nai B’rith Youth Organization
(BBYO) is the largest youth organization
in the world. It consists of two unique chap¬
ters, Aleph Zadik Aleph (AZA ) for young
men and B’nai B’rith Girls (BBG) for young
women. AZA, which stands for fraternity
and harmony, focuses on building Jewish
fraternal bonds while taking part in projects
that benefit the community. BBG focuses
on sisterhood and social action and en¬
courages members to take active roles in
the community - as Jews and as concerned
citizens.
Each group is actively involved in the Jew¬
ish community, volunteering at local
synagogues and attending regularly sched¬
uled programs on Jewish ethics and modern
values. BBG and AZA also work together
to make positive changes in their communi¬
ties.

The club meets at the Jacksonville
Jewish Center every third Saturday
of the month for a social evening
of dancing, entertainment and

dinners throughout the year and a
gala New Year’s Eve Party with the
works!
