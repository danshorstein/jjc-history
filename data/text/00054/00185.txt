On behalf of the officers and
board members of our Jewish Fed¬
eration, we wish the members of
Jacksonville Jewish Center a Happy
and Healthy New Year.
The Federation works in partner¬
ship with over 2,000 contributors
to our annual campaign to provide
invaluable services to this Jewish
community. The services are made
possible by the excellent work of
our major local beneficiary
agencies...Jewish Community Al¬
liance, Jewish Family and
Community Services and the River
Garden Hebrew Home.
The Jacksonville Jewish Federation
is also proud to help support the
Solomon Schechter Day School and
Beit Midrash Hebrew High.
Our largest allocation goes to the
United Jewish Appeal to support
Jewish people in Israel and in over
sixty countries throughout the
world.

Our relationship with the synagogue
is extremely important! We view the
goals of the Federation and the syna¬

in the coming years.
We hope this will be a terrific year
for our Jewish community.

The Jewish Community Alliance is a non-profit family
oriented Jewish social agency whose activities include
a preschool, year-round intersession and summer
camps, after school day care, and activities for children;
teens; and adults of all ages. At the JCA, youth and
adults can enjoy recreational, educational, social, ath¬
letic, and cultural activities. Programs are designed to
promote Jewish continuity, strengthen family life, and
help individuals achieve their full range of potential
growth. The JCA serves as a bridge for Jews develop¬
ing their identity in Judaism and broadening it beyond
the JCA to synagogues, their homes, and Israel.
