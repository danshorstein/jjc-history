t the conclusion of the activity year,
the Youth Commission sponsors a banquet
to honor the officers, advisors and
volunteers from each Center Youth Group.
Newly elected officers for the coming
year are introduced. The lovely ceremony
and program are always enjoyed by the
young people and their families who attend
the banquet. This year a special
presentation was made to Horty Gendzier
for her many years of dedication on behalf
of the Youth of the Jacksonville Jewish
Center congregation. The evening was
topped off with entertainment by the Mark
Spivak Dancers and a duet by Cantor Joel
Fox and Mari Wilensky.
