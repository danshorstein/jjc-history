The 1994-95 season of our
Performing Artist Series was
extremely successful.
Exceptionally talented and
popular Jewish entertainers
were brought to Jacksonville.
This series is devoted to
promoting and enhancing
Jewish life and culture in
Jacksonville. Each performance
was presented in a relaxed and
comfortable atmosphere,
allowing each member of the
audience to establish a rapport
with each entertainer.
Singer/guitarist Itzik Becker flanked by
co-chairs of the Performing Artist Series,
Merle Selevan and Joan Levin.
Sunday, October 23, 1994
Israel’s most prominent and best¬
loved entertainer, Chava Alberstein
combined singing and guitar artistry
in a dramatic presentation and
theatrical performance. The unique
timbre of her voice and the warmth
and passion of her style entertained
a large audience at the start of the
1994-95 Series.
Angels Edith and Milton Berman and
Rabbi Gaffney enjoyed Itzkik Becher’s
performance.
Sunday, April 9, 1995
In a festive Kumzitz-Israeli style,
singer & guitarist Itzik Becher
brought laughter and tears with his
versions of Hebrew and Jewish
folklore in his very own special,
high-spirited way.
The dessert reception was enjoyed by all.
Ticket sales were brisk. Thanks to June
Shenkman, Jane Grann, Helene Pollan.

The Jeff Warschauer
Klezmer Ensemble
Sunday, February 5, 1995
Internationally known mandolinist,
solo guitarist, tenor banjoist &
singer, Jeff Warschauer, in
combination with the extraordinary
talent of some of the finest young
veterans of the Klezmer and Yiddish
revival, performed traditional and
original Klezmer instrumental
music, on the clarinet, piano,
accordion, string bass and drums.
