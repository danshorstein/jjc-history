Magen David Adorn (Red Star of David) which was
founded in 1930 as a First Aid Society for the city of
Tel Aviv, now provides life-saving and health related
services for the entire population of the State of Israel,
as well as performing the usual tasks carried out by
Red Cross Societies throughout the world. While the
symbol of the Magen David Adorn is recognized by the
United States as a member of the international life-sav¬
ing societies, work is still being done to achieve
international recognition.
