PTA is a group of parents, teachers,
administrators and students working
together for a single purpose - the
enrichment of our schools.
Our schools benefit from the PTA on a
weekly basis. Our volunteers are truly
dedicated to the children in providing
quality programs throughout the school
year.
The PTA provides much needed
equipment for the Day School and
Solomon
Schechter
PTA
Preschool. Science program kits,
playground equipment, stencil machine
equipment, computer software and
much more are purchased through our
fundraising. This year money was
provided to purchase new computers
for our computer lab.
Educational and fun programs enhance
our children’s school days.
• Book-A-Bration honors children,
teachers and friends by directly adding
to our library.
• The Cultural Arts Committee provides
art enrichment programs.
• Holiday observances such as Model
Seders, Consecration, Shabbat and
Havdalah Boxes require the helping
hands of the PTA.
