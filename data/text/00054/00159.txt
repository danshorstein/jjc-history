Where have all the Brownies gone?
They have gone to the Humane Society,
Grove House, Camp Chowenwaw, Old
MacDonald’s petting zoo and Pelotes Island
Nature Reserve. We are planning a trip to
St. Augustine and a Summer Splash Party
to finish our year. Our 15 first through third
graders have had a wonderfully productive
year, preparing for our camp-out, earning
eight Try-its, doing creative crafts and

and practice of Judaism. At the city-wide
ceremony for all Scouts receiving religious
