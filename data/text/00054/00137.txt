Solomon Schechter Day School Grade 1-A
Solomon Schechter Day School Grade 1-B
Afternoon Hebrew School Aleph Class
Consecration 5755
January 14,1995
The Aleph classes of the Solomon Schechter Day School
and Hebrew School participated in a consecration
ceremony preceding the holiday of Tu B’Shevat, which
was celebrated on January 16. A lovely service whose
theme revolved around creation and preservation marked
a public declaration by the students to diligently pursue
Judaic knowledge. Aleph students receive Hebrew
instruction under the direction of liana Manasse and
Renee Mibab in the Day School and Alina Kentof and
Carole Goldberg in the Afternoon study program.
