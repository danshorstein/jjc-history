For the socially conscious, the
Men’s Club sponsored a talk by
Carol Pollard, sister of Jonathan
Pollard, imprisoned for passing mili¬
tary secrets to Israeli intelligence.
Her talk, entitled, “Why is Jonathan
Pollard Still in Prison,” focused on
the reasons why Pollard has been
imprisoned longer than any other
person convicted of spying for a
friendly nation - and remains in
prison still.
