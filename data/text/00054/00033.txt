JL he Jacksonville Jewish Center is a
valuable source of Adult Education in
the community. Our members are
challenged to broaden their perspectives
on many issues that face them each day.
Expanding one’s knowledge in Judaism
is more important than almost any other
of the 613 commandments. Anyone who
has attended one of Rabbi Gaffney’s
courses on Jewish ethics, Talmud Study
or Family Education is aware of how
much satisfaction learning in a group
setting can provide.
Adult Education programs offer a broad
range of subjects with interesting
instructors including: A Shabbaton,
Setzer-Levit Lecture, Jewish Ethics
Confronts Modern Day Dilemmas;
discussions on Jewish character, Firing
Line, Talmud Study, Torah & Haftorah
reading classes, book reviews and
holiday workshops. Rabbi Gaffney’s
new program for Family Education,
“HomeStart”, provides monthly topics
which has included “Talking To Your
Child About God”, Jewish Values &
Principles” and “How Does Prayer
Work.”
