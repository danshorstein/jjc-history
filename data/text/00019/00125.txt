BOARD OF MR. AND MRS.
The Mr. and Mrs. Club of Jacksonville, now in its seventh year, is one of the community’s outstanding social organizations. With
a membership of approximately 100 couples, the Club attempts to bring about a closer fellowship among Jewish couples in the commun¬
ity. Husbands and wives, serving together, govern the affairs of the organization.
Each month there are two social functions, a small informal get-together and a more elaborate dinner-dance. These social functions
are arranged by committees selected from among the membership for each month’s affairs. During the year the members enjoyed these
social gatherings with the outstanding occasions being the New Year’s Eve Party and the Installation Banquet.
Interested couples are requested to contact any of the officers or members for details concerning membership.
