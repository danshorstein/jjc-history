The Jewish War Veterans of the United States is the oldest veterans organization in
America. Organized in 1896 under charter of the U. S. Congress, it has served with honor
and distinction throughout the United States and its possessions in the years since its inception.
The aims and objectives of J.W.V. is allegiance to the Constitution of the United States
of America, its flag and institutions; to combat the forces of hate and bigotry and to defend
the honor of our people; to always practice good citizenship and loyalty to our Country. To
perpetuate the memory and honor of our heroic men who have made the supreme sacrifice.
It also gives council and aid to the veteran and his family in time of need.
MAX M. BECKER
