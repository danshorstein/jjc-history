WHAT THE JEWISH THEOLOGICAL SEMINARY OF AMERICA
MEANS TO YOU AND YOUR FAMILY
The Jewish Theological Seminary of America in New York is an academy of
learning which keeps alive the values and traditions of Judaism symbolized by
the Eternal Light. It is a fountainhead—for through its teachers, scholars, and
leaders come the dynamic interpretation of Judaism that inspires, furthers creative
Jewish life and encourages inter-group understanding.

AT THE SEMINARY
Rabbis are trained in the Rabbinical School to transmit the heritage of Juda¬
ism to congregations in the United States and Canada.
The world’s largest collection of Jewish books and manuscripts helps scholar
and layman to understand Jewish ideals.
Students in the Teachers’ Institute are trained to make Jewish learning an
exciting experience for our children.
Textbooks, programs guides, regional educational conferences, teacher and
cantor placements are made through the United Synagogue of America.
Leaders of all faiths plan the course of study for the Institute of Religious
and Social Studies.
An unusual and interesting Jewish Museum offers unlimited studies.
Ramah Camps for youngsters of Jewish environment, combining the physical
with the spiritual.
At the Seminary comes the planning for the award-winning Eternal Light
radio program, the Frontiers of Faith on Television and other programs which
reach millions.
Our Congregation Seminary Chairmen, Mr. Jack Becker and Mr. Philip Bork,
are charged with the responsibility of collecting the funds for supporting all
these activities.
