OFFICERS OF AMITY CLUB
The Amity Club has completed three successful years of social activities at the Jacksonville Jewish Center. Its function of bringing
together its members once a month in a friendly and sociable atmosphere has proven to be very successful, both to the old-timers as well
as to the newcomers of our city.
The outstanding affairs of the Club are the New Year’s Eve dinner-dance and the June installation banquet. The theme of each
monthly affair is determined by a committee in charge of its particular month.
This organization is open to married couples between the ages of 31 to 46 with a maximum membership of 85 couples.
The Amity Club holds only one general meeting a year, at which time the officers and board of directors are elected. Regular month¬
ly board of directors meetings are open to the entire membership.
The officers and board members of the Amity Club hope to make this fourth year the biggest and best yet.
