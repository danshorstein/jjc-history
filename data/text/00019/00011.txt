ADMINISTRATOR S MESSAGE
This Rosh Hashanah, the advent of the Jewish year, marks the
advent of my fifth year as your Administrator.
My efforts have been directed to contribute in some measure to
the growth of the Center and to emphasize the importance of the
Synagogue in the lives of the Center Family.
The advice, counsel and friendship of the Officers and Mem¬
bers of the Board have guided me in executing our Synagogue
policies.
My thanks are especially extended to the "Administrative” com¬
mittee chairmen and committeemen whose areas of responsibility
are outlined below.
The valued opinions and advice of the other members of our
staff, Cantor Marton, Mr. Gross and Mr. Fischer have made it
possible to implement successfully the Center’s religions, educa¬
tional and youth programs.
To carry out the myriad of details of Center administration,
my appreciation is extended to the efficient and friendly office
personnel, Mrs. Ann Stegman, Mrs. Florence Robin, Mrs. Esther
Fishier and Mr. Kenneth Bowden.
For the proper maintenance of our physical plant my thanks
go to Timothy Riley, Morris Montford, Eugene Davis, Wallace
Mazon and Anna Mae McNeil.
DAVID WEINSTEIN
Administrator
Just as each and every member feels the loss of our beloved
Rabbi Tofield, of blessed memory, so do I deeply miss his wise
counsel, warmth and religious inspiration.
As your Administrator I look forward this coming year to the
many rewarding experiences which I derive from my association
with the leaders and members of the Center Family.
