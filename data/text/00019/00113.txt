MRS. IRVING KOWITZ
Girl Scouting was established to aid young girls to grow into womanhood with loving care and guidance. Dedicated mothers offer
their time and abilities to assure their daughters the lasting friendship, enriching experiences, and the capable leadership which make
for a full and rewarding childhood.
This encourages the growth of maturity which is so necessary for good living and happiness for the rest of their adult lives.
The Center Sisterhood sponsors one Girl Scout Troop and four Brownie troops.
