The I960 Center Day Camp was the most popular to date as witnessed by the record breaking attendance of 211 campers.
For the second consecutive year the Day Camp sponsored a little league baseball team composed of the Senior Boy campers.
Another successful record was enjoyed by these veterans of two years.
Highlights of the summer included private use of a pool, color war, our baseball teams and our annual closing Day Camp Show
and Presentation of Awards Night.
Activities that proved most popular this past summer were Jai Alai, paddle ball, soccer, arts and crafts, swimming, horseback
riding and our weekly visits to the lake.
