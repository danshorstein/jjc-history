CUB SCOUTS PACK 14
44 Cub Scouts of Pack 14 of the Jacksonville Jewish Center were registered at Scout
Headquarters this year. The Pack held a very successful year with varied activities.
Pack meetings are held monthly at the Center. The dens meet weekly under the super¬
vision of Den Mothers and prepare an exhibit for their monthly project on the theme of
the month.
One of the outstanding events of the year was the Father and Son Banquet held with
the Boy Scouts at the Center. Another event of interest was the Outing held in March. The
entire Cub Pack were guests of the Jacksonville Baseball Club again this year.
We are looking forward to another enjoyable year for the boys as well as their parents.
All boys over the age of 8 yrs. are urged to join and are asked to contact the Cubmaster.
