Message by
Rabbi Rosenberg
• |
In the Year Book of last year, Rabbi Sanders A.
Tofield wrote, “No one year is like another year.”
j
Indeed it isn’t! As we usher in the New Year of
5721, the beloved Rabbi of the Center is of “Blessed
Memory! ”
In a real sense, however, Rabbi Tofield lives and
his blessed memory shall always endure! His noble
spirit, his dedicated leadership, his devoted service
permeate throughout the entire Jacksonville Jewish
Center as well as throughout the community as a
whole.
The respect and reverence which all people had
for him must continue to manifest itself. It must be

reflected in dedicating yourselves with ever increasing
devotion to those ideals and institutions for which
he gave of himself so indefatigably.
As we greet 5721, let us pledge that the Center
will continue to grow as that spiritual “Powerhouse”
from which Torah and learning, ethics and morals,
love of Torah—the land the people, devotion to our
America and its ideals, and conscious worship of
God will become daily realities in the lives of all the
Center families, in a world of universal brotherhood
and everlasting peace. AMEN.
