RELIGIOUS SERVICES
As a Conservative Synagogue our religious services include daily morning and evening minyans as well as the traditional observance of
the Sabbath and Festivals.
High Holidays: Members and their families, service-men and out-of-town guests are accommodated in our air-conditioned Auditorium
which seats 1400. (We do not "sell seats,” as the expression goes in some communities.)
PERSONAL OBSERVANCES
Our formal public worship services are supplemented by the guidance of our religious leadership on personal occasions at the syna¬
gogue and home. They are the observances of the customs in connection with Circumcision, naming of girls, Pidyon Ha-ben, Bar
Mitzvah, Bas Mitzvah, Confirmations, Marriages, Conversion to Judaism, Bereavement.
