During Scout Week Troop 14 awarded the Ner Tamid religious award to 14 boys, which is the largest group of Scouts ever to
receive the Ner Tamid award at one time. Presentation was made by Dr. Flarry Yergin.
Our Troop was represented by six Scouts at the 50th National Jamboree held at Colorado Springs, Colo, in July: Bruce Yergin,
Jerry Safer, Charles Moskovitz, Charles Slott, Eddie Mizrahi and Michael Safer.
Courses in First Aid, swimming, life saving were given during the year. Troop 14 is indebted to Red Cross Instructor Harold
Collins and Frank Woloszynowski who contributed their time so that the Scouts would have a more thorough knowledge of these skills.
