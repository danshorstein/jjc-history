THE YEAR’S RECORD
Basketball — The Center sponsored a Men’s All Star Team and a
Boys’ All Star Team.
Softball — Men’s Softball Team played in the Southside Church
League, supervised by the City Recreation Department.
Baseball — Midget Baseball Team represented the Center in the
city-sponsored Willow Branch League and came in second
place.
The pictures on this page indicate the type of program we
offer. In addition to softball, basketball and baseball we have
bowling leagues for both sexes, volleyball, ping pong, weight lift¬
ing and all other forms of gym activity.
