JACK BECKER
The Zionist Organization of America is the only overall Jewish movement throughout the world which encompasses the totality of
Jewish life with Israel as the central point of gravity.
Before the establishment of the State, our main goal was a political one. Now the cultural phase of our program as well as public
relations, is the foremost task. We continue the struggle for the revival of the Hebrew language wherever a Jew may live. The Zionist
idea permeates the overwhelming majority of Jews everywhere . . . Regardless whether one has Orthodox, Conservative or Reform reli¬
gious affiliation, or perhaps none at all, he may nevertheless be an ardent associate and supporter of the Zionist Movement.
The American Zionists have accomplished a fine and remarkable record of good public relations between the American people and
the State of Israel.
The Z.O.A. welcomes all Jewish men to participate in its activities.
