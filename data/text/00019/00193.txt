MURRAY GLICKSTEIN
The Grey Oaks Lodge located at Switzerland in St. Johns County was organized in 1945 by members of the Jacksonville Jewish
community as a social and recreational club with a limited membership.
The club grounds are located on a picturesque wooded bluff overlooking the widest expanse of the St. Johns River. In addition to
the rustic clubhouse and auxiliary buildings, the club has a 1,000 foot dock, an Olympic-sized swimming pool, bath house and other
facilities.
Although the membership list is limited, there remains an opportunity for members of the community to join and share in the
delightful activities of this unusual recreational organization.
