B’nai B’rith Girls is the junior division of the B’nai B’rith Women. The past year’s
activities have included a rummage sale and bake sale. The girls also worked on the Cere¬
bral Palsy Telethon along with their brother organization, the A.Z.A.
In May the B.B.G.’s and A.Z.A.’s went to West Palm Beach to attend the North Florida
Council College. At this convention Judy Weiss was elected Treasurer of the North Florida
Council.
At the Installation of Officers Mrs. Herbert Lewitt was installed as new advisor.
With the inspiration of the new Board and new Advisor, B’nai B’rith Girls can expect a banner year
