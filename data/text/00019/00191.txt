The Progressive Credit Union, sponsored by the Jewish Progressive Society and char¬
tered by the State of Florida, has continued to grow in its 21 years of operation. It is one
of more than 10,000 Credit Unions in the U. S. The combined membership of these Credit
Unions totals more than 10 million members and has over four billion dollars in assets.
From a very modest beginning of 20 members and $500.00 in assets, the Progressive
Credit Union has grown to over 350 members and more than $200,000.00 in assets. During
its existence it has made over 2000 loans to members, amounting to nearly $3,000,000.00
without a single loss. It is organized to encourage thrift and provide a safe, convenient
medium whereby members can invest and accumulate savings. Being regularly examined
by the Comptroller of the State, the savings of members are safe-guarded in several ways.
Life insurance equal to a member’s savings up to $1,000.00 and Loan Protection Insurance is paid by the Progressive Credit Union
without any cost to the individual member. The climax of each year’s activities comes with the annual meeting held in the Jacksonville
Jewish Center Auditorium. Last year the Board declared a 2l/z% dividend on savings and 10% rebate of interest to borrowing members.
A cordial invitation is extended to anyone who wishes to join.
