The PTA Purim Carnival took place on
March 5,2017, drawing hundreds of people
from all over the Jacksonville community.
The carnival proved to be even bigger and
better than last year with over 17 fun booths
with prizes, oversized games, toddler
town, bounce houses, rock climbing
wall, petting zoo, horseback rides, and
so much more! There was something for
every carnival-goer from infants through
adults. Children and adults dressed up
in a variety of costumes and competed
in costume contests. Participants also
purchased raffle tickets to win incredible
prizes such as a wine tasting party from
PRP Wine International, a massage or
facial at Massage Green, money towards
Painting With a Twist classes, gift cards to
wonderful area restaurants, and more!
The Purim Carnival’s success is due
in large part to the volunteer efforts of
parents and teachers from the Gal insky
Academy as well as members of the
Galinsky Academy PTA Board. Please
look out for communications from the
PTA regarding next year’s carnival, taking
place on February 25, 2018 from 12-4pm,
and how you can get involved!
