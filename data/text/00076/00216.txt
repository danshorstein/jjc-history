Galinsky Academy
l’dor v’dor annual giving campaign
The Galinsky Academy is the umbrella organization that
includes the Jacksonville Jewish Center’s three schools as
well as youth programming: DuBow Preschool, Bernard
& Alice Selevan Religious School, Martin J. Gottlieb Day
School, and Setzer Youth Education. The L’Dor V’Dor
Annual Giving Campaign supports the more than 300 children
and teens enrolled in these programs, and we are grateful that
the Center community shows unwavering support for L’Dor
V’Dor every year.
The best way to ensure a thriving, vibrant Jewish
community for years to come is to provide our children with a solid foundation upon which
to build their own Jewish identities. The L’Dor V’Dor Annual Giving Campaign is critical
to bring this vision to fruition. Raising the next generation of active, engaged Jewish adults
requires the entire community to support educating today’s children.
