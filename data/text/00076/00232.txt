Bernard & Alice
SELEVAN
RELIGIOUS SCHOOL
The Bernard & Alice Selevan Religious School
continues to bring the joy of Jewish culture and Hebrew
prayer to our families with an exciting, experiential
curriculum. Our program includes Kindergarten
and 1st grade Sunday school classes, where loving,
dedicated teachers introduce our youngest students to
the Torah, Hebrew, and holidays. As they grow, we add
one weekday as we continue to expand the extremely
successful “Judaism Through the Arts” program for
grades 2 through 5, using a rotation of crafts, literature,
technology and music to nourish a love of Jewish culture. In our middle school,
we challenge our 6th and 7th graders, regardless of their level of formal Jewish
education and observance, as they discover the relevance of Jewish learning and
culture in their own young adult lives. Inclusive family programming, allowing
for parental involvement and instruction, round out this exciting new concept in
Jewish education.
We pride ourselves on our healthy blend of traditional and modern methods.
The school looks forward to the involvement of the new assistant rabbi, Rabbi Shira Rosenblum, as well as
all of our professional clergy and staff, as we blend our Religious School family with the Jacksonville Jewish
Center community.
Wishing you a sweet and healthy new year: L’shana Tovah Umetuka!
Lois B. Tompkins
