Kids Club
EXTENDED DAY IS FILLED WITH FUN!
The DuBow Preschool of the Galinsky Academy is pleased to provide “Kids Club,” an excellent before and after
school program for children ages one through twelve. We offer outstanding winter and spring break camps, as well
as fun, active, and enriching days when school is not in session.
We open at 7 am each morning to greet children enrolled in early morning drop off, allowing parents to get to
work on time. During this time, children are given an extra opportunity to socialize with friends, play, or study until
school begins.
Kids Club continues in the afternoons, as an extension to the regular academic day. We are open until 6 pm
Monday - Thursday and until 5:30 pm on Fridays. Every week a new theme is introduced, including art activities,
cooking, and science experiments that correlate with the themes. Jewish values and traditions continue to be embedded
throughout Kids Club. Children are able to further develop their cognitive, language, social, and motor skills through
a plethora of hands on experiences in a safe and nurturing environment.
Our Martin J. Gottlieb Day School elementary students who join us after school have a supervised daily homework
and study period, snack, and outdoor sports and activities.
