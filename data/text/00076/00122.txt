The Jacksonville Jewish Center has been the proud home of our preschool for 75 years! We are so excited to
celebrate this very special anniversary with you on the evening of March 18th, 2018 as the DuBow Preschool
will be commemorated at this year’s L’Dor V’Dor awards dinner and celebration. The celebration will be
honoring the DuBow family, along with Jewish Family and Community Services, who will receive the
Community Partner Award. We kicked off this terrific year in the preschool on Thursday, September 12th!
At that time, the committee unveiled the logo to be used throughout the event and gave a glimpse of what
the evening will have in store. There was children’s artwork displayed throughout the preschool, and many
families, both old and new to our school, had an opportunity to share their special preschool stories with one
another. Some of the preschool’s current students are not only 4th, but even 5th generation members of our
synagogue. Both this event and our institution bring L’Dor V’Dor (from generation to generation) to the next
level. Be sure to mark your calendars for March 18th and be on the lookout for additional information. We
cannot wait to celebrate with you! After all....
“It’s all about the children. Always has been, it is now, and always will be.” -Lawrence DuBow
