PURIM SHPIEL AND PARTY
Our purim shpiel took on a decidedly
adult twist this year thanks to our
“Saturday Night Live” adult Purim
party held on Erev Purim. Adding
to the fun, we created a series of
parody videos that gave homage to
the greatest Saturday Night Live
skits of all time. Highlights included
Hazzan Holzer’s rendition of
Melissa McCarthy’s impersonation
of White House Press Secretary Sean
“Shushan” Spicer, our incoming
President Dave Bielski taking on the
role of Garth alongside his father in
the hilarious “Abe’s World” short,
Brad Herbst and Andy Zunic honing
in on their Night at the Roxbury
brethren, Cindy Wohl’s take on
Debbie Downer, and Rabbi Tilman’s
Stuart Smally impersonation. The
party guests also got to see a VIP
(and for one night only) performance
of “Purim Balls” starring Rabbi
Lubliner, Lois Tompkins and Helen
Hill. Thanks to all of the performers,
writers, and Purim party participants
for making the holiday so much fun!
