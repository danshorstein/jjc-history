PROGRAMMING COMMITTEE
The Programming Committee at the Jacksonville Jewish Center is very excited for the upcoming year!
Encouraging and supporting our congregants to make personal connections, while having a great time, is
the primary goal when programs are being created. Get ready for a few new events, both big and small, and
prepare yourself for a few old events to be enhanced and revamped. The Programming Committee looks
forward to working alongside both the Membership Committee and Social Action Committee this year to
ensure some terrific programming and turnout for all the special programs and events that take place at
the Jacksonville Jewish Center. Planning is already underway for Sukkah celebrations, special Hanukkah
happenings, author visits, concerts, fun Purim events and pool parties!
The Programming Committee is being newly structured for 5778 and welcomes friends interested in
becoming part of the team. Please contact the committee chair, Faye Hedrick at fayemhedrick@gmail.com
with any ideas, questions, or to simply join in on the fun.
We look forward to seeing you and your family and wish you a Happy and Healthy New Year!
