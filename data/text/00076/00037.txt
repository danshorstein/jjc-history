In the DuBow Preschool Treehouse, we have something for every stage of infant and
toddlerhood. Classes offer you a wonderful first taste of preschool in the warm and
welcoming environment of the DuBow Preschool. Family Music and Movement is a
perfect first class for baby as we learn about rhythm, movement, and the world of music.
Music makers and their loved ones learn about animals, ABCs, our bodies through
singing, dancing, parachute play, story time, ASL, and so much more. In Color Splash
use your 5 senses to explore the world of colors while learning and having fun. Little ones
get hands on exposure to varying textures, smells, and tastes in an environment that
encourages learning through experience, reinforcing vocabulary and overall language
development. At Shalom Baby free playgroup, come play, chat, meet other moms,
socialize your baby, and celebrate Jewish holidays and Shabbat. We look forward to
meeting you and your little ones - see you in the Treehouse! Learn more
on our website: http://treehouse.dubowpreschool.org
