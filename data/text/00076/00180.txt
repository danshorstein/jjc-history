Synergies abounded this year between our annual music programming and the music of the greater Jacksonville
community. Our “Season of Song” expanded our local partnerships. From March through May, we hosted three
concerts in conjunction with the Jacksonville Symphony, culminating with a performance on May 4 featuring the
entire Jacksonville Symphony. Amidst this series is a partnership with Avant, a local non-profit that highlights
Radical Jewish Culture and alternative music in the Jacksonville area. We co-sponsored New York based band
Pharaoh’s Daughter on April 2. Collectively, the four concerts brought in almost a thousand people to the
synagogue. We opened doors to greater collaboration to bring diverse and high level music to our community.
We’ve already worked on this upcoming year’s musical programming to continue building on these partnerships.
