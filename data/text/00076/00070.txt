JACKSONVILLE JEWISH CENTER FUTURES COMMITTEE
Two years ago, under the leadership of Mike Mass, the Jacksonville Jewish Center launched an amazing
program called the JJC Futures. The Center has over 100 years of rich and beautiful history that is made possible
by two important components; incredible loyal congregants and the devotion of strong leadership. The JJC
Futures Committee was formed to ensure the continuation of education about our history and governance
process of current and future leaders within our community. It was decided that an infusion of knowledge for
our membership would be greatly beneficial to potential incoming leaders.
Since its formation, two groups of JJC members have gone through our “leadership” course. As we went
along, the committee itself went through a learning process demonstrated by the evolution of the course between
the first and second class offerings. Regular feedback from the members indicates that there has been great
satisfaction with the overall experience. The course will be offered at regular intervals to ensure that our present
and future leaders will always have a basic knowledge about the functions of the JJC. It is also intended to
teach and exercise various leadership skills. This course and its governing committee are to be supported by an
endowment for governance at the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
The JJC Futures committee will meet on a regular basis to continue the development and presentation of our
leadership course. We also hope to ensure the evolution of this course to explore new ideas as we all move into
our collective future in Conservative Judaism.
If you are interested in participating in the class or on the committee or have further questions, please
contact Mike Mass, (904) 737-6773, massjax@comcast.net.
