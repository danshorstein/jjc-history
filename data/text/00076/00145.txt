► Setzer Youth Education 4
GIRL SCOUTS
The Galinsky Academy’s Girl Scout Troops have been very active this past year. We attended a lot of exciting
programs and earned many badges and fun patches.
Our youngest scouts are the Daisy Troop #1367, under leadership of Robyn Bell and Naida Gould. This troop
attended many trips such as Trad’s Nursery, Mandarin Garden Club, Goodwill, Firehouse Tour, Girl Scout Office,
and Bank Tour. The girls also enjoyed attending The Little Mermaid play. We worked so hard that we earned a
special pin called the Summit Pin. We earned a special pin, called “My Promise, My Faith,” with the help of a
special guest. We earned all three Daisy Journeys too.
The Brownie Troop #1371 was under leadership of Florence Margalit. The Brownie troop was also very busy
earning badges such as: Dancer, Home Scientist, Snacks, Making Games, and Philanthropist. The girls hosted a
present wrapping fundraiser as well.
The Junior and Cadettes Troop #1372, under leadership of Rebecca Fixel, is a community service based troop.
Their goal is to do mitzvahs through community service. The girls performed many projects such as organizing the
clothes closets at Hubbard House, making Purim masks with the residents of River Garden, and cleaning up local
parks. Their year started with a fun trip to Kennedy Space Center and SeaWorld.
We all had the opportunity to have a special meet and greet with Israeli Scouts. We played games and learned
about their exciting program. And, of course, we sold many boxes of delicious Girl Scout cookies! If your daughter
is interested in joining our troops, please contact Rebecca at info@beccasbling.com.
