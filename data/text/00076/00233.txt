SISTERHOOD
A busy year in the
‘Hood7 I am honored
to be the president of the
Jacksonville Jewish Center
Sisterhood. I can honestly
say that our programming
and our fundraising this year
has been phenomenal and
I thank everyone who has
dedicated their precious time
and generous donations. I
always strive for women to
feel welcome and connected
at our events. My dynamic
board has worked hard
to change with the times as our Sisterhood has evolved to adapt
to women’s busy schedules. Nothing gives me, along with my
Sisterhood Sistas, more pleasure than making a difference in our
synagogue community. We are the backbone of the Center and I
am proud to announce that Sisterhood generously donated $23,500
so all children within our synagogue will be able to benefit from
the incredible educational and youth programming offered at the
Jacksonville Jewish Center and Galinsky Academy! Included in that
donation was a specific $10,000 donation to L’Dor V’Dor to help
fund day school, preschool, and professional development in the area
of special education. I am so proud that we were able to purchase a
defibrillator for the day school, which is critical to the well-being of
our youth. Thank you to all who have donated their time or funds
to make our generous 2017 donation possible. My main goal as
president is to help women make connections within our synagogue
community. By providing relevant and timely programming, I hope
to create an opportunity to learn, be stimulated, and to socialize. I am
proud that the Jacksonville Jewish Center Sisterhood is the recipient
of the 2017 Jewels in the Crown Award, which is the prestigious
Women’s League Award for excellence in programming. Some of
our highlights were a Conversation with Anita Zucker, Sisterhood
Shabbat highlighting Woman’s League International President
Carol Simon, Painting with a Twist and Comedy Zone evenings
in collaboration with the Galinsky Academy PTA, Shalom Yoga,
and Hot Topics with relevant topics. Whether you purchased gifts
from the Judaica Shop, sent our beautiful Rosh Hashanah flowers
and Leagrams, or simply just supported Sisterhood by paying
dues, I thank you on behalf of Sisterhood for making our year not
only productive, but also creating a warm community of women
within our synagogue community. Please go to our website www.
jycsisterhood.com or follow us on Facebook (JJC Sisterhood) for
more information.
Marjie Rogozinski
Sisterhood President
