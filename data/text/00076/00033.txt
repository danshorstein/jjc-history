The DuBow Preschool was first established in 1942,
making this year our 75th anniversary. We are proud
that through the years we have provided an outstanding
secular and Jewish education for our youngest and most
adorable students.
Over the past 75 years many changes have occurred in
programs, classrooms, and even our location and name,
but our dedication to provide our youngest children with
an educational foundation that is second to none, in a
nurturing and safe Jewish environment, remains the same.
Our philosophy of play-based learning, supported by a carefully chosen
curriculum, helps establish the foundations necessary for learning. Our
experienced and skilled staff are enthusiastic about working with children who
are rapidly developing language and cognitive skills. This all takes place in
a classroom environment in which children are free to explore and develop
creative thinking and problem solving skills. We strive to instill a love of learning in an environment which
also promotes a strong Jewish identity.
We are proud to be a part of the Galinsky Academy at the Jacksonville Jewish Center, which makes it
possible for us to provide an exceptional pre-school with warm and caring teachers, and enhanced in so many
ways by our devoted clergy.
If you would like to see why we are so excited about our upcoming year, visit us at www.dubowpreschool.
org, or schedule a personal tour.
On behalf of our faculty and families, we wish you L’Shanah Tovah!
