JONI SHMUNES: MENSCH OF THE YEAR AWARD
We are delighted to announce the establishment of the Joni
Shmunes “Mensch of the Year” Award, set up as recognition
for a graduating 7th grader from the Bernard and Alice
Religious School who displays a commitment to Jewish studies.
In memory of our beloved teacher, Joni Shmunes z”l, we will
continue each year to honor students with consistent attendance
and a sincere willingness to learn in Religious School - especially
one who puts his or her heart and soul into learning about their
Judaism despite any challenges or roadblocks along the way!
The first (2016) recipient was Sydney Leach, and this year’s
2017 honoree was Dustin Sandler. Mazel tov to both of these
very special students!
