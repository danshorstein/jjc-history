OUR SECOND HOME
David Bielski
Board President
The end of all of my email correspondence reads the tag line, “All we want is an
opportunity, the rest is up to us.” Whether it relates to business, social settings,
family or religious life, if given the chance, I am a believer that we are all capable
of tremendous success.
Looking back at the past 40 plus years as a member of the Jacksonville Jewish
Center, I realized that I owe the JJC a huge thank you for my own success...
Thank you for opening your arms, especially to my parents, Abe and Shirley,
as our family moved in 1975 from Melbourne, Australia to Jacksonville, Florida
and including us as part of this community. Thank you for having a wonderful
pre-school where my sister, Talie Zaifert and I, along with our own children, were
able to grow and develop in a warm and loving Jewish environment. Thank you
for having an outstanding day school and religious school where we matured into
educated, focused young adults with a passion to continue our Jewish heritage.
Thank you for having a fabulous youth program where many of the friends I made 25 years ago are still my
friends today. Thank you for being there for my family as we dealt with life cycle events from the deaths of
my grandparents to the baby namings and bat mitzvahs of my own children. And thank you to our entire 800+
family membership at the Jacksonville Jewish Center for sharing your memories with our family as well.
We are all so very fortunate to be a part of this wonderful community that many of us call our second home.
Our continuous emotional, social, and financial support will ensure that future generations have the opportunity
to thrive as successful Jewish people.
“All we want is an opportunity,
the rest is up to us.”
Together with my wife Carrie and
daughters Jamie and Allie, we wish
you a sweet and healthy new year!
David Bielski, President
Jacksonville Jewish Center
