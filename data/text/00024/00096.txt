The Daily Kindergarten and Nursery conducts sessions throughout the school year composed of the following
staff: Mrs. M. J. Reiser, Mrs. Carl Hackel, Mrs. Harry Jaffe and Mrs. Edward Sussman. Each of the above
teachers have been with our school system for many years and are well qualified for their respective classes.
The Kindergarten and Nursery meets daily Monday through Friday from 9 a.m. to 12 noon with transporta¬
tion provided. A mid-morning snack is served to each child and a playground program is also included each day.
Several plays are presented throughout the year depicting the various Jewish Holidays in conjunction with
Open House for parents and visitation of the classrooms.
The two oldest classes of Kindegarten pictured with veteran teachers Mrs. Carl Hackel and Mrs. M. J. Reiser.
The majority of these two groups comprise the Graduating Class of 1965. These youngsters receive instructions
in Jewish festivals and customs, with a Kiddush planned on Fridays to usher in the Sabbath.
