Shown at the left are the Chairman of the Board,
Mr. Burton I. Grossman, and President Philip Selber,
presenting their annual report to the special guest
Dr. Max Arzt, Vice-Chancellor of the Jewish Theolo¬
gical Seminary of America.
Dr. Arzt gave several interesting facts on national
Jewry and depicted briefly the Conservative Move¬
ment in the United States and its founding by Solo¬
mon Schechter. He also emphasized the need for Con¬
servative Jewry to support the Jewish Theological
Seminary and the many facets which it sponsors.
The Annual Meeting is conducted in January at
which time the individual committees render their re¬
ports and progress along with the financial statement
presented by the Auditing Committee. Elections are
conducted following remarks by the President, Chair¬
man of the Board and the Rabbi.
