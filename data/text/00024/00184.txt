The essential function of the Zionist Organization
of America is to keep alive in this country an interest
in the State of Israel. This interest is many-sided, as
the efforts of Zionism always were. The political ob¬
jective was the creation of a Jewish State. To this end
it was necessary to revive the Hebrew language, ac¬
quire land, train colonists for settlement and raise
funds to finance the project. All this the Zionist
movement did.
Myer Cohen, President
Today, the Z.O.A. continues to foster support for
the State of Israel. Moreover, the Z.O.A. seeks to
strengthen the bonds between American Jewry and
the Jewish State on the basis of the cultural tradi¬
tion that is our common heritage.
Religious differencs present no obstacle as Zionism
encompasses all shades of religious belief in every
global area.
The Z.O.A. invites all Jewish men to join its ranks.
