"I regard the centrality of the Synagogue In Jewish
life not so much In organizational terms as in the implication
that the Synagogue is the basic and primary unit of Jewish
organization. This means that every Jew regardless of his
other associations and activities should associate himself
with a Synagogue in his community. More important still,
the spirit of the Synagogue and the influence of its teach¬
ings should be incorporated into and be an active influence
in the I ives of the men and women, lay and professional, who
guide the destinies of these other Jewish communal organ¬
izations and furnish the Jewish communal leadership."
MAXWELL ABBELL
