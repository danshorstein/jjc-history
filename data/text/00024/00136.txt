MR. BEN CASLAR, President
With its dual goal of spreading friendship and
enabling married couples to share good times, the
Mr. and Mrs. Club is now in its 12th successful year.
Each month, exciting and novel affairs are held and
guided through the leadership of committees formed
to arrange each gala event.
The themes vary from month to month, but tradi¬
tional events include an extravagant New Year's Eve
party, a mid-year Installation Banquet, and a family
picnic and fun day during the summer months.
As in all other social clubs, there is a central
core of officers and board members, and the organiza¬
tion is governed by husbands and wives serving and
working together. Through each member's sharing
the responsibility of serving on a monthly committee,
an atmosphere of warm congeniality always prevails,
and the promotion of good fellowship is the end result.
All couples who are interested in joining and
sharing all the wonderful things offered by the Mr.
and Mrs. Club are urged to contact any of the club's
officers or board members.

Knights & Dames was organized in 1964 for the
purpose of furnishing a social outlet for single Jewish
adults. Socials are held every six to eight weeks. Invi¬
tations are sent out to single Jewish adults and adult
groups in neighboring cities of Florida and Georgia.
Anyone who is interested in joining this group
or who knows of anyone who might be interested in
this organization is invited to contact any of the
officers or members in order that their names may
be added to the mailing list.
Highlight of the past season was a weekend party
at the Thunderbird Motel. The attendance included
single Jewish adults from as far north as Atlanta, Ga.
and as far south as Miami, Fla. Reciprocal invitations
have been extended from the Savannah and Atlanta
single adult groups.
