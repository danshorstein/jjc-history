Cub Pack 14 with Scoutmaster, Sidney Engelhardt
The enthusiasm and devotion shown by our Den Mothers and Cub
Scouts has lifted our Pack among the leaders in the city. The 41 Cubs
between the ages of 8 through 10 participate in weekly Den meetings at
the respective Den Mothers’ homes with monthly Pack Meetings held on
the last Thursday of the month at the Center.
Special thanks are due to the devotion and dedication of Cubmaster
Sidney Engelhardt, Assistant Cubmaster Harry Kowitz and Mrs. Jerome
Fink, Chief Den Mother. These leaders, along with the support and strength
of the Den Mothers and Pack Committee, have paved the way of Pack 14
as the number one Pack in the North Florida Council.
