THE CENTER CHORAL SOCIETY PICTURED AT THE ANNUAL CONCERT
The 17th annual Concert of Jewish Music
was presented on January 11, 1965 and
featured the internationally famous Cantor,
Jacob Barkin, as guest artist. The “Hav-
dalah’' by Zilberts was the highlight com¬
position. This annual event is one of the
highlights of our congregation’s calendar.
A large audience enjoyed this program.
This year’s Concert proceeds were divided
between the Sisterhood and the Cantors’
Institute of the Jewish Theological Semi¬
nary of America.
Our junior choral group, the Glee Club,
is composed of members of our Religious
School and performs at various Holidays
in the year and at the Annual Concert. The
Choral Society and Glee Club are under
the direction of Cantor A. Marton.
