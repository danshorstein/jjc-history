For the second consecutive year the Picnic has been held at our
own new 36 acre Campsite, making full use of the large barbecue
pits, Olympic Swimming Pool and all the athletic facilities on
the premises.

This annual event which is anticipated by all the
pupils of the Center Religious School is the culmin¬
ation of the year’s religious instruction and a spring¬
board to our summer program.The Men’s Club and
Sisterhood undertake this venture jointly, with all
proceeds earmarked for Camp Ramah scholarships.
Special thanks are due to the President of the
Men’s Club, Stanley Kantor, and his committee for
making the 1965 Picnic successful. The Sisterhood also
deserves much credit for their devoted efforts in pre¬
paring the food and manning the game booths which
were under the supervision of Mrs. Beryl Weinstein
and her committee.
Highlight of the Picnic was the Beauty Contest as depicted on
this page with our beauty queens of the future. The winners of
the six and under category are: Ronnie Rosenberg, Laura Seebol
and Annette Bernard.
