HANUKAH (Dedication) — Anniversary of the day when the
Jews, led by Judah Maccabee and his brothers, freed the Temple
from Syrian invaders. At the Temple's rededication, only enough
undefiled oil was found to light the Holy Lamp for one day. Mira¬
culously, it burned eight days. Thus, on the first day of Hanukah,
Jews light one candle of the Menorah, adding another flame each
day until all eight are lit.
