Ladies Hebrew Sheltering Aid Society is entering
its 42nd year of giving “Service with a Heart". Our
revered founders’ dream of establishing a resident
home for the aged of our people became a realty in
1946, and since that time our role has been primarily
that of an auxiliary to River Garden Home for the
Aged.
We provide the extra budgetary needs as fur¬
nishing, redecorating, air conditioning, kitchen and
informary equipment and hobby shop materials. Funds
are raised through our Tombola, Donor and social
printing projects, as well as dues and life member¬
ships. Linens at the Home are replenished through
our annual Linen Shower affair.
In the field of service, Ladies Aid sponsors ac¬
tivity and volunteer programs. Residents participate
in projects such as ceramic and art classes, hobby
shop, resident’s magazine, and the newly formed
Spanish class.
Personal service to the residents is rendered by
our Grey Ladies and our Gardenettes, a teen-age
corps of volunteers who serve on week-ends and school
Officers of Ladies Hebrew Sheltering Aid
vacations. Our members also provide door-to-door
transportation for non-residents to attend the weekly
Golden Sunshine meetings held at River Garden and
continue to lend our support and guidance to the
group. On special holidays our women provide enter¬
tainment and refreshments for the residents.
We pledge our continued devotion and service to
River Garden and its residents in the hope that we
can help bring some measure of comfort and mean¬
ing to their lives.
