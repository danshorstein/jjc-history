Strengthening of the office staff has enabled us
to enforce regulations concerning attendance and
progressive training in synagogue skills. The High
Holiday and Festival Holiday skills program was
also instituted on an all-year basis. Discipline pro¬
cedures were developed so that parents could be in¬
formed regularly of the special methods being used
by the school.
Among the people who have greatly added to
this program are Mr. Jack Becker with his help in
conducting the Hebrew High School classes, and
Mrs. Sheldon Gendzier, who joined our office staff
and enabled us to organize a better public relations
program.
To Dr. Nathan Schneider, the previous Chair¬
man of the Education Committee, and Mr. Jack
Shorstein, the new Chairman, and all the officers
of the Center who have helped directly in the school
program, go our heartfelt thanks.
