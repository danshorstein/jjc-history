Shown on this page and the preceding and follow¬
ing pages are some of the varied functions which the
Men's Club perform at the Center. In addition to their
monthly dinner meeting and the fellowship which is
enjoyed, they look forward to the annual joint gather¬
ing of the three brotherhoods. These annual functions
are rotated between the local congregations.
With the financial success of the Picnic which
made possible several scholarships for worthy students
to attend Camp Ramah and the assistance given at
the weekly Minyan Clubs, the Men's Club continues
to serve in these and other projects to make the Cen¬
ter program more complete. Special thanks are due the
Food Chairman David Safer and his committee and
Morton Harris, Bernie Heyman and Dave Silver for
their assistance with the weekly Minyan Club pre¬
paration.
