The Amity Club enjoyed a very successful season
last year with various monthly affairs at the Center
climaxed with the June Banquet. The members en¬
joyed outstanding activities outside the Center such
as the Ice Show at the Coliseum, Jai Alai at Daytona
Beach, and New Years Eve at the Mayflower Hotel.
The Amity Club is a social club for married
couples of the Jewish faith and we welcome inquiries.
If interested, please call one of the above officers or
board members.

For the 5th year, the 32 Club serves as a social
pinnacle for the young Jewish couples of Jacksonville.
One member of the couple must be under 32 years of
age to join the club but once you are a member there
is no age limit. We try to vary our monthly affairs to
include all types of desirable entertainment. Each
Board Member is Chairman of one affair and the type
of affair is limited only to his imagination and the
budget allotted to his committee per year.
Any interested couples should contact Barry Zisser,
membership chairman, or any officer or board member.
