River Garden Auxiliary, formerly the Ladies
Hebrew Sheltering Aid Society, is entering its 55th
year of “Service With A Heart’. River Garden
Auxiliary was founded by a group of dedicated women
who realized their dream of a Home for our Jewish
eee in 1946, when River Garden Home opened its

oors.

The Auxiliary continues in their tradition to
assist and help the River Garden Board to provide a
Home for our Senior Citizens, who are no longer able
to live alone ...a Home, where they can find comfort,
security, and medical care . enjoyment and
intellectual stimulation . . . creativity and pride in
their own accomplishments... and, all this, combined
with the feeling and knowledge that “someone cares’’.

The Auxiliary provides the extra budgetary needs
of the Home, such as furnishings, redecorating, air
conditioning, office and infirmary equipment, and
Hobby Shop materials. Funds are raised through our
Annual Tombola, Donors, Tree of Life, Social Printing
and Candelabra projects, as well as through dues from
our 860 members, which included 190 Life Members.
Our Annual Linen Shower, to replenish the linens of
the Home, % held each January.

The Auxiliary not only contributes financially to
the Home, but, also, sponsors many activities and
volunteer programs. The non-resident Senior Citizens
of our community are transported to the Home each
Wednesday, to join with our residents for the Golden
Sunshine meeting and social.

Residents of the Home also participate in many
programs such as ceramics and art classes, Hobby
Shop activities, and the Resident’s Magazine. The
residents enjoy the Special parties planned and given
by the Auxiliary for Chanukah, Purim, Mother’s Day
and Father’s Day.

The Auxiliary also sponsors the Red Cross
Volunteers, at the Home, and these ladies extend both
their hands and their hearts in services to our
residents.

As the New Year begins, we pledge our continued
devotion and service to River Garden Home and our
residents with the everlasting hope and prayer that
we can provide a Home for our aged that will add life to
their years and not just years to their lives.
