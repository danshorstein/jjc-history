STUDENTS ENJOY A SUPERIOR, bi-cultural curriculum

taught in an innovative and success-oriented learning
environment. Extending from Nursery School through grade
six (with a junior high school projected), 160 students are
presently enrolled. A highly structured yet individualized
program in language arts, math, science, and social studies
has been developed by a skilled staff of instructors, resource
personnel and university consultants. An emphasis on music,
art, drama, dance and physical education enriches the basic
curriculum.

The Hebrew Language and a full program of Judaic-
Hebraic learning experiences are integrated into the daily
schedule. Youngsters gain deep insights into the teachings of
Judaism and develop an appreciation of the values and ideas
expressed in the modern and classical literature of the Jewish
people. A major emphasis is placed on spoken Hebrew.
