Whatis Hadassah? Hadassah has been in existance for
65 years but what has it done?

Hadassah is dedicated to the thesis that Israel will
remain a strong viable democracy in the Middle East. Israel
has shown the world that it can exist and be productive.
Israel has lifted the spirit of every Jew everywhere.

In Jacksonville Hadassah is 51 years old. Our varied
projects include the Hadassah Medical Organization, Youth
Aliyah, Hadassah Israel Educational Services, Jewish
National Fund, Zionist Youth Activities and American
Affairs.

Our theme this year is, “Hadassah, A Force For
Democracy.” From our opening meeting in September to our
Oneg Shabbat in December until our closing Donor in April
we have something for everyone. The Jacksonville Chapter
consists of three active groups: TIKVA, the afternoon group;
HANNAH SENESCH, the evening business and
professional women’s group; and SHIR LI, the young
women’s group also meet in the evening.

We heartily welcome one and all to join with us - the.
world’s largest women’s Zionist organization. We offer you
the opportunity to get to know us and work with us. Call our
Chapter President Sally Sternstein, 733-0246 for further

information.
