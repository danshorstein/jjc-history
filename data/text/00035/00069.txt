In order to achieve the many curricular objectives
established for the new year, a whole complement of new
books, exciting teaching materials, audio-visual aides,
colorful maps, charts, posters, and interesting records and
tapes are being selected by teachers. Also, dozens of
significant Jewish-content movies, feature films, filmstrips,
and slide shows are on order for incorporation into lesson
plans which will challenge the student’s mind and ethical
sense as well.

The Jacksonville Jewish Center conducts five schools
for the imparting of Jewish knowledge, synagogue skills, and
the Hebraic ethical-religious heritage. 1) THE SOLOMON
SCHECHTER DAY SCHOOL, encompasses grades 1 - 6
and a Pre-School. A full-day, superior bi-cultural curriculum
is offered. 2) PRE-SCHOOL: Nursery (ages 3-4 years) anda
Kindergarten (5 year olders) meet five mornings per week.
The Pre-School integrates Judaic values and observances
with the general Nursery and Kindergarten experiences. 3)
SUNDAY SCHOOL is an introductory, primary program
for children attending public or private schools.
Kindergarten, first and second grades are conducted. 4)
HEBREW SCHOOL is a 6 hour per week experience for
children in grades 3 - 7 and leading both to Bar/Bat Mitzvah
at age 13 and to continuation in the high school in grade 8.
Sessions meet on Monday and Wednesday, 4:00 - 6:00 p.m.
and on Shabbat mornings.
