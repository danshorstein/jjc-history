CENTER SISTERHOOD

Our theme for this year is found in Chapter one, Verse
seven of the Book of Joshua — “Hazak Ve’Ematz Me’od, Be
Strong and Very Courageous.” Hazak means strong in the
sense of solidity, of resistance to pressure, of concentration
into a solid mass. Amatz means to be strong in the sense of
determination and courage.

As we enter a new year — our fifty-second year of
Sisterhood — in our new home, we open a new period of
great achievements. Let us be strong and united in our
dedication to the tasks at hand. Through our efforts,
celebrations, and programs of education for our members
and our families, we will ensure the future of our people.

Join us this year in Sisterhood activities. There’s a place
waiting for you!
