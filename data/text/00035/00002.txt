The Jacksonville Evening Chapter of ORT is proud to
contribute to the work and the ideals of the World ORT

Union.

Our chapter is very active throughout the year. During
the fall we have our annual Chinese Auction. We thank the
many merchants and donors who contribute to this affair,
as well as the people who support it. During the spring we
have our Performing Arts Night when local performers,
donating their talents, provide an entertaining evening for
all. Throughout the year, we sell personalized T-shirts. We
participate in the Area Council’s raffle. In addition we
have several smaller functions for the chapter.

During our meetings we have programs designed to
increase the awareness of and to stimulate the thinking
