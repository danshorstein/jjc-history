A NEW, WELL-EQUIPPED FACILITY ON A 36-
ACRE CAMPUS, serves as the ideal setting for an

enriched educational experience. It includes a multi-
media resource center, a library, open classrooms,
student synagogue, woods, nature trails, ponds,
complete sports fields, camp grounds, and an Olympic
sized swimming pool. All of the facilities are utilized by
the 30 staff members in planning a super-rich program
for the nearly 350 children and youth enrolled.
