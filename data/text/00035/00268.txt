HA’RAYIM

Ha'Rayim, the young couples Club of the Jacksonville Jewish Center, will be celebrating its second
anniversary this year. In this short time the group has been steadily growing in size and accomplishments,
both on the social, cultural and religious level.

Numerous social events have met with great success. Our square dance, pizza party, Hav-a-nosh
(which will be repeated by popular request), family Spaghetti dinner, Sukkot celebration at our campsite,
our July Jewbilee Picnic and our Hassidic Guru all took place this past year.

Ha’Rayim has become a vibrant and vital part of our Center Family. We look forward to continued
growth and activity, and we invite you to join us. It has been a group which welcomes newcomers to the
community. Couples between the ages of, 25 and 45 are all invited to participate.

Be part of an exciting and enjoyable experience. Plans have already been made for this coming year.
Join in!

Shalom,

Isabel Balotin, President
