TWENTY ODD CLUB

The Twenty Odd Club was organized in January 1924 by Messrs. Samuel
Witten and Benjamin Schemer. It is a social club and its object is to create
good fellowship among its members. The club now has 20 members. Member-
ship is open to young men over 18 years of age.

Meetings are held every Sunday. The officers are:—Harry Falis,
president; Sam Cherry, secretary; Israel Schemer, treasurer; and Hyem
Kramer, sergeant-at-arms. The organization has succeeded in creating a
sense of unity among some of the Jewish young men in Jacksonville, and
has materially helped various charitable organizations.

It has officially endorsed the movement for the erection of a Jewish
Community Center in Jacksonville and has pledged to this movement its full
hearted support and cooperation.
