SENIOR YOUNG JUDAEA CLUB
The Senior Young Judaea Club was organised in 1920 by Mrs. Nathan
Dwoskin. It now has 30 members of both sexes ranging in ages between 17 and
20. The present officers are Morris Diamond, president; Esther Hammerman,
vice-president; Rose Shapiro, secretary; Pauline Puldey, corresponding secretary;
Minnie Selber, treasurer; and Naomi Becker, chaplain
The club meets every Monday night at 8:30 at the Y. M. H. A. It is
affiliated with the National Young Judaea Organisation with headquarters at
114 Fifth Avenue, New York City. The club conducts holiday celebrations on
the occasion of the Jewish festivals and participates in the collection of funds
for the Zionist cause.
Its object is to spread the ideals of Jewish nationalism among the youth and
a knowledge of Hebrew and Jewish history.
JUNIOR YOUNG JUDAEA CLUB
The Junior Young Judaea Club was organised October 1924 by Mrs. Issac
Cohen. It has 30 members ranging in ages between 15 and 17. Its present
officers are Eva Berman, president; Eva Greenstein, vice-president; Goldie Pul¬
dey, secretary; Sara Lieberman, treasurer. The club meets every Wednesday
night at the Y. M. H. A.
It has presented a play, engaged in debates and participated in collections
for the Jewish National Fund. It is affiliated with the National Young Judaea
Organisation and its object is identical with that of the Senior Club.
