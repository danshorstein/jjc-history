RABBI SAMUEL BENJAMIN
FOREWORD
In this first attempt to produce a Year Book for the Jacksonville Jewish
Community and a Souvenir Journal for the proposed Community Center the
object is two fold. In the first place it is intended to give a picture of Jewish
life and activity in this newly created but quickly growing Jewish community in
one of the largest and most important cities in Florida so that the world at
large-may become acquainted with it, and secondly to center the attention of
the Jacksonville Jewish community itself on the great problems facing it in the
hope that knowing the truth it will be better able to solve them.
A careful survey was made of the individual Jewish organizations in the
city and diligent efforts were made to secure the fullest information about them
individually and the community generally. The picture presented is not alto-
gether a pleasant one. The shadows heavily predominate. There is much dark¬
ness and little light.
