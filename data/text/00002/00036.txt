FLORIDA LODGE 365 I. O. B. S.
The Florida Lodge 365 is the largest and most active organisation in the
South of the Independent Order Brith Sholom. It was organised in 1915 by
Mr. J. Weintraub. It counts 158 members.
Former presidents of the lodge are M. Herman" and Mike Schemer now de'
ceased, and L. J. Weintraub, Fred Sofereko, T. Snyder, Harry Kats and
Nathan Paul. The present president is Mr. Joseph Goldstein. The lodge meets
every second and fourth Sunday of the month at the Y. M. H. A.
It is a fraternal organisation with sick benefit and life insurance features.
It lays great emphasis, however, on the social, spiritual and cultural development
of its members. It is sympathetic to Zionism and actively aids the Jewish Na'
tional Cause.
