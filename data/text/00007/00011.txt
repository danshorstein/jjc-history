Hadassah, the Women’s Zionist Organization of
America, was started in 1912 by Miss Henrietta Szold
to foster Zionist ideals in America and to maintain and
support various activities in Palestine which contribute
towards its upbuilding as the Jewish National Home. Or-
ganized on a chapter and a membership basis, Hadassah
functions in 500 communities.
The Jacksonville Senior Hadassah chapter sponsors
the following projects, which are part of the national
program: (1) Youth Aliyah—funds for the maintenance
of and training of refugee children in Palestine admin-
istered by the Jewish Agency; (2) Hadassah Medical
Organization—a system of curative and preventive medi-
cal services; (3) child welfare projects, including kinder-
gartens, school lunches, etc.: (4) Jewish National Fund
collections of a traditional character; (5) Palestine sup-
plies, including linens, food, drugs, etc. for institutions.
The Jacksonville group during the past year also
supported the war effort. Members of Hadassah partici-
