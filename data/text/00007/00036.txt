Jews in Farming
There are well over 100,000 Jewish persons who
earn a living from engaging in agriculture in the
United States. The Jewish Agricultural Society, es-
tablished almost half a century ago to encourage and
aid Jews to go into farming, estimates that ap-
proximately 1,500,000 acres are cultivated by Ameri-
can Jews.
