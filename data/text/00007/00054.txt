There’s Plenty of
ELECTRICITY
But there isn’t Any to Waste
Wasted electricity means waste of fuel, equipment and transpor-
tation . . . essentials of war that go into the production of elec-
tricity. Waste slows down our war effort, delays the day of
Victory. That is why it is common sense for all of us to join
with our fellow Americans in using that electric service we need
but being very sure we need what we use Waste is always use-
less extravagance, in time of war it is inexcusable.
CITY ELECTRIC DEPARTMENT
GEORGE A. PIERCE, Commissioner of Utilities
