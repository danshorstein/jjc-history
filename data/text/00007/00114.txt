Organizational Affiliations
The alphabetical list on the following pages contains the names of all who hold membership in a Jack-
sonville Jewish organization or who have contributed to the 1944 campaign of the Jacksonville Jewish Com-
munity Council. ^
Each organization has been given a code number. By consulting the explanation of this code which appears
at the beginning and at the end of the list, the reader may identify the affiliations of all people listed. Names of
husbands and wives have been listed together. However, wherever a son or a daughter in the same house-
hold belongs to an organization, that fact has been indicated by a separate listing and code number.
To illustrate, let us take these names under the letter "K”:
Krestul, Mr. and Mrs. Joe — 1, 2, 4, 6, 10, 13, 15, 16, 21
Krestul, Nathan — 18
Looking at the code, we learn that this means Mr. Krestul is a member of A. B. Weil Lodge No. 800
of Bnai Brith, YMHA, Jacksonville Zionist District, and the Jacksonville Jewish Center. (The latter member-
ship automatically extends to the entire family.) He also has contributed to the 1944 campaign of the Jack-
sonville Jewish Community Council . . . Mrs. Krestul holds membership in Senior Hadassah, Daughters of
Israel, Ladies’ Hebrew Sheltering Aid Society and Bnai Brith Women of Jacksonville . . . Nathan, the Krestuls‘
son, whose name appears separately, is a member of Boy Scouts, Troop 14.
