Because of the need for an extension headquarters to
supplement the JWB-USO facilities on Duval street, the
YMHA has concentrated upon war service activities. The
statistics reveal an interesting picture. During the past
year over 15,000 men and women of the armed forces
visited the Y. The open house held every Wednesday
night catered to 3,000 servicemen. Over 100 service-
men’s wives were aided with personal problems, includ-
ing employment and housing. Engagement parties and
weddings were arranged for 15 couples. 1500 break-
fasts were served to servicemen on Sunday mornings at
a nominal cost.
