To Cheer Their Homecoming
Millions of Russian families, returning to their homes in the wake
of Red Army victories, lack the most elementary necessities of life.
Above, 1. to r., are Mrs. I. Gartner, Mrs. Charles Fink and Mrs. Ben
Chepenik, as they pack part of the 100 kits being filled by the
Daughters of Israel to help cheer the homecoming of these Rus-
sian civilians. Other members of the committee are Mrs. M. D.
Margolis and Mrs. Joseph Krestul (who together with Mrs. Chepe-
nik are co-chairmen of the undertaking), Mrs. Jacob Bloom and
Mrs. Jack Gartner.
Each kit, which is being prepared in cooperation with Russian
War Relief, Inc., is of standard size and contains an assortment of
specified commodities, including sugar, evaporated milk, dehydrated
soup mix, cigarettes, etc., as well as a personal message of sympathy
and good will from the sender.
