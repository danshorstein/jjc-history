The Business and Professional Women’s Division of
Hadassah closely follows the program established by the
Jacksonville Chapter of Senior Hadassah. It includes
that portion of the Senior group which is unable to at-
tend the monthly meetings because of business or pro-
fessional demands. The B and P Division during the
past year has aided in filling every quota in Senior Ha-
dassah projects: Hadassah Medical Organization, Child
Welfare, Jewish National Fund, Youth Aliyah, and Pal-
estine supplies.
