For over twenty years the Ladies’ Hebrew Sheltering
Aid Society has served the needy, supplying food, fuel,
clothing, medical care and housing. Through its non-
interest loan fund it has enabled many families to over-
come financial difficulties and re-establish themselves.
Transients are given food and lodging. Each year, at
Passover, money is sent to various institutions. Food is
bought and prepared for inmates in prison.
