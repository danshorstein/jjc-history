Junior Hadassah, the Young Women’s Zionist Or-
ganization, has more than 250 chapters, which, in addi-
tion to a Zionist educational program among its mem-
bers, conduct fund-raising campaigns for Palestinian
projects. The most important of these are: the Mier
Shfeyah village, which in the last year housed a pro-
gram for 180 children, many of them brought to Pal-
estine through Youth Aliyah, and the Henrietta Szold
School of Nursing, supported jointly by the Junior and
the Senior Hadassah. The Junior Hadassah also collects
funds for Youth Aliyah and the Jewish National Fund.
The Jacksonville chapter participates in these projects.
