Temple Choir
*
The Temple choir during 1944 included Mrs. Francis M. Holt, contralto and director, Mrs. Robert Lee Hutchinson,
organist, Mrs. Sidney Grossman, soprano, Mr. Stewart Comer, bass and Mr. Ernest White, tenor.
For the past several months Mrs. Russel Carlson, soprano, and Mr. Marvin Maher, tenor, have assisted the choir.
Mr. Herbert Zacharias has substituted from time to time for Mrs. Hutchinson at the organ and Miss Marion Freed played
the organ on Sabbath mornings.
