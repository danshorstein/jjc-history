The Brandéis Branch of the Jewish National Workers’
Alliance is perhaps the newest organization formed in
Jacksonville. In February, 1944 a member of the na-
tional executive committee of the JNWA (commonly
known as Farband) visited the local community and in-
spired a group of people to apply for a charter from the
national headquarters.
