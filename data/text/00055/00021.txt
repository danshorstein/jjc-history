Center Sisterhood
Bonnie Sandler
President
It is with a great deal of excitement and
enthusiasm that I look forward to the next
two years as Center Sisterhood President.
Sisterhood is a very important arm of
the Jacksonville Jewish Center. Now, when
you think of Sisterhood you might think of
Leagrams, High Holiday flowers, an art
auction, Donor, or a bowling party. What I
want you all to realize is that we don’t just raise money without a
specific goal in mind. We raise money, hold monthly meetings,
book reviews, Sisterhood Shabbats, and Women’s Minyans for
one specific goal. You can sum up Sisterhood’s main goal in one
word - EDUCATION. The entire incoming board is dedicated to
teaching all of us. The more you participate, the more time and
effort you devote to our Sisterhood, the more benefits you’ll gain.
We will provide the vehicle, but you need to jump on and join us
for the ride.
Sisterhood’s new board is excited and very enthusiastic. We
hope that this excitement will be contagious. Join us! Attend our
functions! Help behind the scenes! Be a part of Sisterhood! We
promise you an exhilarating experience, and at the same time,
you will have enhanced your growth as a knowledgeable Jewish
woman.
Neil, Scott, Laurie, and Paul join me in wishing all of you a
very happy and healthy New Year.
Bonnie
