D aisy Troop 40 had a
wonderful inaugural year! These
five-year olds learned all about the
basics of Scouting - the Promise,
songs, opening and closing
ceremonies, crafts, and best of all,

Brownie Troop 882 has had
another fabulous year! Twenty-five
first through third graders enjoyed
camping, making crafts, doing science
experiments, making up skits, singing
Girl Scout songs and, of course, going
on field trips. We visited River
Garden, the Kennedy Space Center,
Camp Tomahawk and Savannah,
Georgia, the birthplace of Girl
Scouting. We are ended our year with
a splash - at Adventure Landing! The girls earned this trip with cookie money profits. Our fun continued into the
summer with our annual Summer Splash pool party. For more information, call Beth Shorstein, 262-7234.
