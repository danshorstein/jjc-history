Brandéis University National Women’s Committee
The purpose of the Brandéis University National
Women’s Committee is to provide financial support and
growth for the Brandéis University Libraries, which serve
a community of students and scholars, in education and
research, united by their commitment to the pursuit of
knowledge. The National Women’s Committee offers its
members opportunity for intellectual pursuit, continuing
education, community service, social interaction,
personal enrichment, and leadership development. The
members act as emissaries to support and enhance the
image of Brandéis, a Jewish-sponsored, non-sectarian
university where excellence is the tradition.

The Jacksonville Jewish
Federation serves as a central
resource for the Jewish community.
In summary, Federation is
dedicated to providing for the
welfare of every Jewish person living
in Jacksonville. Through a
partnership with the United Jewish
Appeal, we are equally committed
to the survival of and quality of life
for every Jew living in Israel and
throughout the Diaspora.
The three major local recipient
agencies of funds raised by Federation
are the Jewish Community Alliance,
Jewish Family and Community
Services and the River Garden Hebrew
Home. We are proud of the invaluable
services provided by these three
outstanding Jewish agencies. It is a
privilege to assist them in trying to
meet their goals.
Jewish education is extremely
important and Federation is proud to
support the Solomon Schechter Day
School and Beit Midrash Hebrew
High.
The newest recipient of
significant Federation funding is the
Jacksonville Jewish Foundation.
Jacksonville Jewish Federation
Established in partnership with
Federation, five area synagogues,
JCA, JFCS and River Garden, the
Foundation will work towards
insuring the future financial security
of our Jewish community.
The Federation is involved
with community relations, leadership
development, Missions to Israel, and
Jewish Continuity planning. We will
continue to work in cooperation with
our local agencies UJA, and the
synagogue to address the vital issues
and challenges in the entire arena of
Jewish Continuity. We are committed
to this important aspect of our Jewish
community’s agenda.
The Jacksonville Jewish News
is the Jewish newspaper representing
all of the agencies, synagogues,
organizations and members of the
Federation.
This is a vibrant and growing
Jewish community. The synagogues
always have and always will play a
vital role to insure the quality of life
for every member of the community.
Federation welcomes and encourages
the members of our community to
become involved with any aspect of
our work, to actively support this
synagogue and to participate with the
other outstanding Jewish agencies and
synagogues in Jacksonville.
