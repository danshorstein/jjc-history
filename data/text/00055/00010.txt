Center Catering Staff
Creative menus and handsome presentation are the
hallmark of the Center Catering Department. Under the direction
of Michael Fleet, a well trained staff prepares everything from
school lunches and congregational dinners to gala celebrations,
with panache! It is a genuine pleasure to plan a simcha with the
help of Michael and his capable staff.
Michael Fleet
Catering Director
Maintenance & Housekeeping Staff
The maintenance and housekeeping staff work diligently
to keep our building clean and presentable at all times. Richard
Ford, our maintenance supervisor, takes pride in his staff, and
devotes much of his time to taking care of all the physical needs
required by the many events which take place throughout the
year. Volunteers appreciate the special attention they receive from
the maintenance department.
Richard Ford
Maintenance Supervisor
