Rabbi Gaffney Visits UF Students at Hillel
Fourteen of our University of Florida students had the opportunity to have dinner with Rabbi Gaffney
on Tuesday, February 13 at the Hillel House in Gainesville. This annual event is sponsored by the Southeast
Region of the Rabbinical Assembly, and was
attended by approximately 110 students from
10 congregations in our region.
This event gives Rabbi Gaffney and
those students from our congregation who
attend UF, as well as Santa Fe Jr. College,
the opportunity to get together informally and
talk about campus life and current issues.
