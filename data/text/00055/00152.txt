Solomon Schechter PTA
The Solomon Schechter Day School PTA is a group of parents,
teachers, administrators, and students working together for a single purpose,
the enrichment of our schools.
Our schools benefit from the PTA on a weekly basis. Our volunteers
are truly dedicated to the children in providing quality programs throughout
the school year.
The PTA provides much needed equipment for the Day School and
Preschool. Science program kits, playground equipment, stencil machine
equipment, and much more are purchased through our fundraising. Profits
from the PTA cookbook have been earmarked for new computer software
for our computer lab.

Educational and fun programs enhance our children’s school days.
• Book-A-Bration honors children, teachers, and friends by directly
adding to our library.
• The Cultural Arts Committee provides art enrichment programs.
Volunteers serving as the “Picture Lady” visit classrooms on a regular basis.
• Holiday observances such as Model Seders, Consecration, Shabbat
and Havdalah Boxes require the helping hands of the PTA.
• Red Ribbon Week for drug awareness, as well as vision screening,
are needed health programs that the PTA provides.
Our programs are possible because of our fundraising projects: magazine
sale, book fair, candy sales, and the PTA Cookbook.
Your commitment to the PTA makes these programs and events
successful. PTA provides a fun, often social, opportunity for involvement in
the education of our children. Try it out.. .pick a project.. .you may benefit
as much as the schools!!
