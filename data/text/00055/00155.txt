Sunday School
Th e Sunday School of the Jacksonville Jewish Center is planned expressly
for children in Kindergarten, first and second grades. A weekly, two-hour program
gives each child the opportunity to experience the richness of his/her Jewish
heritage through song, dance, role
playing, cooking, short stories,
games, arts and crafts. Emphasis is
placed on the holidays and festivals.
Jewish values and mitzvot are
encouraged. Many thanks to our
wonderful Sunday School teachers

Family Council
For eight years, FAMILY COUNCIL has provided innovative
Family Education programs involving all facets of the congregation.
Several large scale projects like Rabbi Dov Kentof’s Fantasy Voyage
on Noah’s Ark to the Zoo and Sukkah Decorating inspire the entire
congregation including the Center
Preschool, Solomon Schechter Day
School, Afternoon Hebrew School
and Sunday School. Synagogue
professionals, volunteer experts,
parents and children provide
valuable input and assistance and
are rewarded by the experience.
Golden Kippah Award
Youth Congregations
This much sought-after award is given to third
and fourth grade Hebrew students of both the
Afternoon and Solomon Schechter programs. It is
given to those who demonstrate their knowledge of
Shabbat prayers, services and customs. The proud
recipients for this year are Ori Demri, Mathew
Shorstein, Ari Friedman, Ron Perets, Zachary
Morris & David Selber. Congratulations to second
grader Jonathan Mizrahi on being the youngest
recipient of the Golden Kippah award.
