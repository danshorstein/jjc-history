Center Day Camp
The Place to Be!
Another camp year
Has come to an end.
We’ve laughed and played
And made many a friend.
We canoed in the river
And went blueberry picking.
We visited Publix and Carvel
And did lots of finger-licking.
Bracelets were beaded
And t-shirts were dyed.
Candles, mosaics and
Paper mache were tried.
We swam and ate
And read and grew.
We sold cookies we baked
And candy bars too.
A carnival, some camp-outs
And a very special day.
We watched the Olympic Torch
As it passed our way.
Food was collected
For those in need.
Another great year at camp
Most successful indeed!
