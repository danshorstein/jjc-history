Hadassah women comprise the largest Zionist volunteer
organization in America today. But we’re only a strong force
because our various groups all come together to make a critical
difference in their communities, their country and the world.
And now, right now, is the time to get together. Never has
the need for togetherness or ties been stronger as international
tensions heighten, and the search for peace contineus....
Ties that bind us with our Jewish Homeland bind us together
as women in an ever-changing present and with women together
through the years. Ties, togetherness, commitment. . . . Hadassah
makes the most of our potential for ourselves and our world.
Once again the whole world is looking at Israel, and Israel
is looking to Hadassah for support and strength!
