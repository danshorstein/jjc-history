The Woman of the Year
Estelle is her name - she is swell.
She does everything so well.
She can cook, she can talk, and she can dance.
In these departments I don't have a chance.
She is a wonderful mother and grandmother.
But best of all we love each other.
She is my honey, my love and my dear.
She is “The Woman of the Year”.
She is my darling, my sweetheart.
She will be mine, 'til death due us part.
From Her Man of the Year
