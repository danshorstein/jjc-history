The Jewish Women’s Bowling League is composed
of women of all ages who are members of all
congregations. Bowling for some goes back as early
as the 40’s and 50’s.
Team play begins at 9:30 a.m. each Tuesday at
Bowl America. For those who like to bowl, but not
regularly, pacers are welcome. If interested, please
call Michelle Frisch, 773-5382.
