In its 38th year, the Mr. & Mrs. Club continues to en-
joy success, achieved through the enthusiasm, coopera-
tion and imagination of its members, who conduct the
club’s entertainment and general business.
Monthly affairs are planned by committees consisting
of couples and singles, who are dedicated to presenting
an original and outstanding evening of entertainment for
the club members and guests.
Close friendships, sustained for years by many
members’ participation in the club, speak well for the con-
gentiality of the group and new members are always
welcome. Anyone interested in joining is urged to con-
tact any board member.
