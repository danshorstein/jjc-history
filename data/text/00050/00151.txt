Sunday School
A “Hands on” Experience
The children of our Sunday School program learn
about holidays, Bible stories, life cycle events, and sym-
bols of Judaism. Through song, dance music, arts and
crafts, storytelling, games and a variety of other ac-
tivities, children learn about these topics by experien-
cing them in creative and innovative ways. Children also
become familiar with the synagogue as a comfortable,
secure, and caring place. In addition, the values of
tzedakah and helping others are emphasized.
