Center Receives Synagogue of Excellence Award
from the Southeast Region of United Synagogue
At the Southeast Region of the United Synagogue held in Atlanta
November 29 to December 2, the Jacksonville Jewish Center was recogniz-
ed as one of the finest synagogues in the Southeast Region. Other major
awards presented to us were: Afternoon Religious School, College
Activities, Social Action, Solomon Schechter Day School, Youth
Activities, Education on Wheels, Holocaust Commemoration
and Family Events.
We are grateful to the devoted corps of volunteers and our professional
staff for making these programs so successful. Our programs are designed
to inspire and educate our members. We encourage you to take advantage
of these wonderful opportunities for Jewish growth for yourself, your children
and your grandchildren.
