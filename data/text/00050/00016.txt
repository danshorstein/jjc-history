Brownie Troop
Brownie Troop 882 enjoyed a great year! We made Hanukkah cards and visited River Garden, learned to say
NO to drugs, sang funny songs and played games.
Through all of our activities we learned to be caring troop members, supported each other and built positive
self-esteem.
Our year ended with a trip to Kennedy Space Center that was fun for Brownies, siblings and parents!
Thank you to the JJC for a great place for our troop activities.
Bruce and Jan Lipsky
