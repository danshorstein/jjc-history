Message From The
Pre-School Director
Marilyn Sandler
CHILDREN. . .our concern, our future, our
foundation.
A well rounded preschool program should offer
appropriate learning activities that encourage children
to develop socially, emotionally, physically and intellec-
tually. A variety of activities will encourage a child to
make choices and promote a preschooler’s in-
dependence. Our program includes activities in
science, social studies, pre-math, language develop-
ment, art and music.
We try to develop a conscious attitude toward
religion and tradition. We do it through song and story,
through actual participation in ceremonies, obser-
vanees and through the general atmosphere created.
The children learn to sing many prayers in Hebrew.
With the rapidly increasing demand for quality
child care programs, the Jacksonville Jewish Center
has provided for parents a successful day care pro-
gram. We are constantly upgrading and providing op-
portunities for the children as well as satisfying all
parental concerns for the welfare of their children.
We have proven to be a successful preschool and
day care facility for all Jewish children in the com-
munity that desire these type programs. We know as
being Jewish we are heirs to a rich tradition.
Marilyn Sandler
Director
