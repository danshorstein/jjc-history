75 YEARS YOUNG!
Jewish Family and Community Services is proud to
announce the attainment of a milestone annivesary: 75
years of servicing the Jacksonville Jewish community!
From our founding in 1917 as the “United Jewish
Charities,” our activities have been an outgrowth of the
vigorous philanthropic spirit of our community.
As the direct service arm of the Jewish communi-
ty, we provide numerous services. These services reflect
our mission, which is rooted in Jewish values and in-
eludes a community’s obligation to support and assist
its members. We are committed to complete
confidentiality.
