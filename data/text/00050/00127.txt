ISRAEL NEEDS OUR HELP .... AIPAC SUGGESTS
COMMUNICATE WITH YOUR ELECTED REPRESENTATIVES IN GOVERNMENT
With the dramatic changes that have taken place in the Middle East since the Persian Gulf War, most
members of Congress have yet to crystallize their views of the problems and opportunities that lie ahead for
U.S. policy in the region. Your letters and visits to Senators and Congressmen should serve to help formulate
those views, particularly as they relate to the U.S./Israel relationship and to Israel's significance to the United
States. Ongoing foreign aid to Israel is essential.
DEAL WITH THE MEDIA
Frequently, we find ourselves responding to anti-Israel articles or broadcasts. The importance of responding
in an accurate and timely manner cannot be overstated.
However, a key aspect in effectively communicating with the media entails proactive work. That is,
identifying events or themes which can generate positive stories. You can approach the media with your
views before a crisis hits.
