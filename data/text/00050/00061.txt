Recreation
The Jewish Center’s Recreation Department’s goals are to provide the Jewish
community with program and activities that promote the improvement of physical
skills, health, the development of special interest and/or hobbies, and to provide
the opportunity for relaxation and social camaraderie.
Many of the recreation activities take place at the Center’s Recreational Park
which consists of approximately 10 acres of beautifully wooded campgrounds,
complete with an olympic-size swimming pool, bath houses, tennis courts, soft-
ball fields, and outdoor basketball court, badminton area, children’s playground,
gazebo, snack bar and shaded picnic area.
Within the main building is a regulation basketball court, the Neal Portnoy Youth Lounge and an arts and crafts room,
a large room for dances and a versatile stage for musical and dramatic productions and a meat and dairy kitchen with
the most modern equipment. We take pride in our recreation program and the marvelous facilities of our Center.
