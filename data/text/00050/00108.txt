Administrator’s Message
What major Jacksonville institution:
(1) has been in existence for 90 years?
(2) sponsors activities and uses its building every day of the year?
(3) has a budget in excess of $1 million and serves more than 900 family
members?
The answer: the Center (of course). But then again, you probably already knew
that — since most Center members make good use of our modern facilities and people-
oriented programs.
Each member of the Center’s staff works very hard to allow for all the many pro-
grams and services that are held at our beautiful building in Mandarin. It’s certainly
a big difference from 3rd and Silver!
Along with our many volunteers, we take a great deal of pride in our personal service. If I may be of any assistance
to you and your family at any time during the year, please feel free to contact me.
Together, let us continue to make the Jacksonville Jewish Center the “center” of our lives!
With best wishes for a Happy and Healthy New Year.
