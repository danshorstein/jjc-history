The Jacksonville Jewish
Shalom,
What a year it has been, and what
a year it is going to be!
This past year our Sisterhood has
seen many wonderful things happen.
We have excelled in educational pro-
grams such as our first All Women’s
Mincha, Shalosh Seudah, Maariv and
Havdalah Service, Sisterhood Shabbat
and wonderful Book Reviews. Our
fundraising has been entertaining and
profitable, our service to our synagogue
impeccable We have honored our past
Presidents and have laughed at Yenta’s
Hideaway. We have greatly increased
our commitment to the new Building
Fund Campaign.
We are a proud group of women, ac-
tively involved in activities which enrich
our congregation, support our schools and enhance our youth programs. We also
provide learning opportunities for you to grow as a concerned Jewish woman of
the 1990’s. Since our members span a wide range of interests, ages and
backgrounds, our programs are designed to heighten your individual personal
growth, Jewish involvement and feeling of community.
The only thing we need to continue our wonderful work is you — JOIN US
TODAY! Be a part of us — we are a part of you.
This New Year let the joy of life fulfill us with contentment, peace and happiness.
