About the Cover:
Once again, artist Raymond
Cohen has designed a dynamic and
significant logo in tribute to the
excellence in education that has been
a central part of the mission of our
congregation for 100 years. Here is
his concept:
‘The torch is a universal Jewish symbol for
education. We are commanded to be a “light unto
the nations,” bringing the truth of the Torah and the
beauty of Judaism to each generation. ‘ Torah orah -
Torah is light.’ The three-pronged flame and the icons
of children boldly represent the Jacksonville Jewish
Center’s Religious Schools, Preschool and Solomon
Schechter Day School. It illustrates the evolution of
Jewish education at the Center from a chedar, to a
modern 20th century Hebrew School, to today’s all-
encompassing educational experience that not only
imparts knowledge but immerses our children in the
richness of Jewish life. Moreover, the tri-flamed
torch exemplifies our reverence for the traditions of
the past, our efforts to keep Judaism vibrant in our
own time and our understanding that the future of
our congregation rests on bringing our children into
the fold through meaningful Jewish education.”
