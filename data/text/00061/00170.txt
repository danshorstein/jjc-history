Center Men’s Club
The Jacksonville Jewish Center
Men’s Club is dedicated to the
enrichment of Center life and the
Jacksonville Jewish community. Men’s
Club members work together supporting,
sponsoring and participating in programs
that bring meaning to our youth,
companionship to our elders, and
involvement in our community.
Center Men’s Club is also an active
member of the Federation of Jewish
Men’s Clubs, an international
organization dedicated to the ideals,
philosophy and traditions of
Conservative Judaism. Among the
successful national programs that we
participate in is the Yom
Hashoah Yellow Candle
program. Each year we
distribute hundreds of
Holocaust Memorial
candles to families of our
congregation. Another
program, “Hearing
Men’s Voices” is a
series of workshops for
discussing issues of
significance to Jewish
men in today’s world.
Topics for the coming year will cover
family, religion, society and health, in
thought provoking and fulfilling agendas
led by leading experts and professionals.
We encourage our post Bar/Bat
Mitzvah teens to continue reading Torah,
and reward those who read 18 or more
portions by age 18 with the Yad Shel Chai
(Hand of Life) award.
Center Men’s Club’s “Horty T.
Gendzier Summer Scholarship Fund”
provides the financial resources for
children to attend Camp Ramah or other
Jewish enrichment programs, and honors
the memory of Horty Gendzier, who was
so instrumental in the pioneering of these
Sunday morning speaker programs
were well attended.
programs. Funding is provided in large
part by proceeds from the annual “Man
of the Year” banquet.
We support the Jacksonville
Jewish Federation, Jacksonville Family
and Community Services and the
community at large through tzedakah
programs and volunteer efforts.
We are visible friends of our Center,
with programs like selling and building
sukkot for our Center families, and
helping to decorate the Center’s Goldman
Sukkah. You can find Men’s Club
members at the Lag B’omer Picnic every
year serving hot dogs and cokes. Our
members are the
friendly ushers
who greet you
at services on
Shabbat and
Holidays.
Men’s Club
members
sponsor, lead
and participate
in Men’s Club
Shabbat and
Armed Forces
Shabbat. We take an active role in
preparing B’nai Mitzvah students of all
ages by sponsoring events and
providing tutors and assistance.
These events have earned Center
Men’s Club First Place and Honorable
Mention Torch Awards from the
Federation of Jewish Men’s Clubs.
The Men’s Club is also a great way
for friends to have fun. Every summer
we unite for the infamous deep sea
fishing trip, and take our families to
sporting events, such as a Jacksonville
Suns baseball game. In the fall we “kick
off’ our new year with a membership drive
featuring games of chance and raffles,
while we watch a Jacksonville Jaguars
away game on a big screen TV.
All this and more is waiting you if
you are at least 18 years old, male, and a
member of the Jacksonville Jewish
community. Join now. We welcome your
fellowship and participation.
