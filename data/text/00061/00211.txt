Parent-Teacher Association
The Parent - Teacher Association of the Solomon Schechter Day
School and the Center Preschool is instrumental in providing quality
programming and services that enhance our already successful learning
environment. The PTA also conducts necessary fundraising that directly
benefits our students. These funds provide everything from books to
playground equipment.
We are proud of our PTA and all the volunteers who support it. We work directly
with the principals of our schools, Willard Kennedy and Dale Schemer, to ensure the
proper focus of all our endeavors.
The following are just some of the great programs, services and fundraisers that
PTA provides throughout the year for the students in our schools: Red Ribbon - Drug
Awareness Week, Purim Carnival, Kaleidoscope Day, Vision Screening, Shabbat Boxes,
SSDS Yearbook, Passover candy sales and magazine sales, as well as celebrations
for all the holidays.
Membership in PTA is vital to maintaining the quality of our programming. At
the beginning of each school year, parents and teachers are encouraged to join, and to
become valued volunteers.
I look forward to another successful year, as we continue to add value to the
educational programs enjoyed by our children.
