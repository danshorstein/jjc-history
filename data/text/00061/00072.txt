Men’s Club Man of the Year Banquet
Over 160 of Evan’s friends and family watched as he was roasted
by special guests including Circuit Court Judges Brian Davis and Jack
Schemer and U.S. Congresswoman Corinne Brown. The theme of the
evening was a political convention to nominate Evan Yegelwel as the
“Secretary of Schmooze.” Anyone who knows Evan agrees that this is
an honor he justly deserved and an office he was destined to hold. The
evening of good-natured fun and laughter included a wonderful buffet
dinner by Margo’s Catering. Evan was a good sport and a great time was
had by all.
All proceeds of the evening went to the Men’s Club’s “Horty T.
Gendzier Summer Scholarship Fund” that helps needy Jewish children
attend Camp Ramah and USY Pilgrimage programs.
Evan Yegelwel
“Secretary of Schmooze”
