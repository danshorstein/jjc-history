Youth Basketball League
The goal of the Youth Basketball League (YBL) is to provide area boys and girls the opportunity to have fun,
improve physical skills, propmote good sportsmanship and team spirit and experience a strong and positive
athletic environment. All the games and practices are held in the Fletcher-Selber Auditorium of the Jacksonville
Jewish Center. Thank you to Scott Zimmerman who coordinated a very successful program and to the volunteer
coaches for their valuable assistance and time. We look forward to an exciting 2002-2003 season.
