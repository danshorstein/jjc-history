Installation of Rabbi Stephen Grundfast
Rabbi Stephen Grundfast was
formally installed as our spiritual
leader on Sunday, March 3, 2002.
After his arrival last summer,
Rabbi Grundfast quickly became
active in every aspect of
congregational life, adding new
programs and innovative approaches to
synagogue observances. Rabbi and
Mrs. Grundfast are welcome additions
to our congregation and professional staff, as well as to
the community at large.
Event chairs, Arlene and Evan Yegelwel planned
a wonderful evening, which included greetings by
Harry Silverman, executive director of Southeast
Region United Synagogue of Conservative Judaism, as
well as remarks and presentations by
past Center presidents, Jeffery
Morris, Michael Shorstein and
Evan Yegelwel. Judy Grundfast led
students of the Center’s Preschool,
Religious School and Solomon
Schechter Day School in musical
performances. A dessert reception
followed the program.
Guest speaker Rabbi Steven M.
Glazer of Congregation Beth Emeth in Herndon, VA,
officiated at the installation ceremony. A longtime friend
of the Grundfast family, Rabbi Glazer is an adjunct
Professor at George Washington University in
Washington, DC. and George Mason University in
Fairfax, as well as a visiting lecturer at the Jewish
Theological Seminary and at Harvard Divinity School.
