JACKSONVILLE FLORIDA CHAPTER

HADASSAH, the Women’s Zionist Organization of America, with an enrollment
of 385,000 members, celebrated its Diamond Jubilee in 1987. Locally JACKSON-
VILLE HADASSAH observed the 75th Anniversary with a gala dinner dance on
January 31, 1987 with Judge Louis Safer as the keynote speaker and honoring all
Jacksonville past presidents and Male Associates. The Jacksonville Chapter,
which consists of four groups: Tikva, Hannah Senesch, Shir Liand Avodah, has a
combined membership of over 1,000 with almost 50% being Life Members.

We have just completed another successful year, having fulfilled our National
quotas in both financial and membership areas. We steadfastly continue our
commitment to Israel and support of Hadassah’s projects, namely Hadassah
Medical Organization which encompasses the Hadassah-Hebrew University
Medical Center and Mount Scopus Hospital, providing medical service to people
of all races and creeds. Here, too, doctors, dentists, nurses, etc. are trained and
taught, while research, done with imagination and the latest technology, regularly
results in new discoveries and techniques promptly shared with all the world.
