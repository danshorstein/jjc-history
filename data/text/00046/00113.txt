rownies and Girl Scouts

For many years, the Center Sisterhood has actively sponsored the Brownie and Girl Scout Troops
of the Jacksonville Jewish Center. Scouting is a very important phase of the youth program in the
Center. It helps develop character, usefulness at home, appreciation of nature and the out-of-doors,
creation of objects of beauty and usefulness from simple things, and it helps these young people to find
their places in the synagogue and community through service.

Our Brownie and Girl Scout program provides all of these opportunities for our young women of
tomorrow. For further information regarding Girl Scouts, call Clara Mann 733-6472; for Brownies, call
Jane Grann 262-2234
