Performing Artist Series
1986-87

WOW — WHAT A SEASON! In September 1986, the Jacksonville Jewish Center launched its most spectacular Series
ever with the high energy performance of Mike Burstyn, an extraordinary singer, dancer and showman. It was
non-stop laughter in November as commedian Sy Kleinman presented the sold-out-audience with an evening of
side-splitting stories and jokes. The excitment continued with a Purim concert by the musically versatile and

thoroughly enjoyable Fabrangen Fiddlers.
