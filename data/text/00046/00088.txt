SUNDAY SCHOOL

The Sunday School of the Jacksonville
Jewish Center is designed for children in
kindergarten, first and second grades. This
two hour program gives each child the
opportunity to experience the richness
of his/her Jewish heritage through song,
dance, role playing, cooking, short stories,

games, arts and crafts. Emphasis is placed
on the holidays, festivals, Jewish values
and mitzvot.
