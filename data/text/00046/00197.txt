Jewish Community Alliance

8505 San Jose Boulevard e Jacksonville, Florida 32217 e 904-730-2100

As we approach a new year in our Jewish calendar, a new member of the Jewish organizational
community is growing on the site of the former Beauclerc Country Club. The Jewish Community
Alliance is coming! |

The dream of a centralized facility for cultural, recreational, and enrichment activities for the
entire Jewish community is coming true. Plans call for the 78,000 square foot JCA to open its
doors June 1988.

The JCA will provide opportunities for the development of an educated, knowledgeable
leadership and intelligent, participating citizenry. Its rich and varied programs of Jewish and
general activities will include:

e Cultural Arts
eCommunity Services
e Social

e Youth
e Adult and Senior Adult
e Single Adult

Thirteen volunteer task forces are actively at work to provide the framework for the JCA’s
programs and policies. These volunteers represent every sector of the Jewish community. The
needs, wants and “if only’s” are being examined by over 200 dedicated task force members, and
recommendations for comprehensive activities will be the result. The JCA will be “state-of-the-
art,” staffed by the very best professionals to be found.

The Jacksonville Jewish Federation will move its offices to the JCA when the building is
completed. Meeting space and aspecial Organization Room will be provided for all local Jewish
organizations.

Our new Jewish Community Alliance aims to enhance the sense of cohesiveness within our
Jewish community, and to foster a renewed dedication to the values of American and Jewish
democratic traditions. Its mission is to serve all Jews, of any age or circumstance.

From all of us, may your year be blessed with good health, peace, prosperity and a feeling of
K’lal Yisrael.
