Solomon Schechter Day School

Message from the Principal

 

Solomon Schechter Day School serves students from kindergarten through seventh grade.
Founded twenty-six years ago, the school is committed to the ideals of academic excellence,
personal growth, and Jewish values.

The school offers a challenging educational program with a wide range of learning
opportunities in a caring atmosphere. Blending American democratic heritage and Jewish
ideals, we teach how the secular and religious realms enrich each other and enhance our
lives.

The curriculum consists of two major components, General Studies and Judaica Studies.
Both areas complement each other so that the educational benefits of each are enhanced by
a knowledge of the other. On standardized national achievement tests, our students score in |
the superior range, indicating high proficiency in such areas as language arts, reading Willard Kennedy
comprehension, mathematics, science and social studies. In Judaic Studies, our school has established a reputation of
outstanding achievements in the Hebrew language and in the study of Bible, prayer and culture. Our students are infused
with the joys and responsibilities of being Jewish.

The school is blessed with strong parental involvement coordinated by an active PTA. The PIA provides cultural
activities, holiday treats, educational equipment. In addition, this group provides funds for library books and our computer

learning center. :
Parents who are interested in admissions are invited to contact the school office for an appointment.
