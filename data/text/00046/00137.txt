AUTO

(Adults United to Organize)

AUTO is the parent-teacher association that services those pupils attending the Supple-
mental School programs, as well as their families. It is under the direction of Rabbi Kentof.
AUTO stands for Adults United to Organize. And organize we did! . . . with such programs
this past year as: a “Family Day” of study and family frolics, havadalah with an “Israeli
Experience,” a campout, “Tu B’Shevat buffet,” a “Teacher Appreciation Banquet” as well as
other projects. Next year, watch for “Havdalah Club,” family retreat, holiday how-to work-
shops. . . just to name a few.

As we in AUTO like to say, “Watch out! Here comes AUTO . . . always on the move!”

— Elen Rubens, President
