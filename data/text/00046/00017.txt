The general aim of the United Synagogue Youth program is to bring about a meaningful and fully reciprocal encounter of
Juadism, the Jewish people, and the synagogue on one side, and the Jewish teenager on the other. As a result of this encounter,
the Jewish teenager will, firstly, demonstrate and experience how the Jewish way of living, through study, action and fellowship
leads the way to personal fulfillment and growth while meeting his spiritual and social needs; and secondly, make a significant
contribution to his synagogue, community, society and the Jewish people.
