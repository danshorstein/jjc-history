Jacksonville Jewish Center Blood Drive

Many years ago the Center established a blood
bank for its members. The purpose of the annual
blood drive is to replenish the Center’s supply. This
year’s response was overwhelming. More than 50
pints were donated. Our thanks to Ron Pollock for
coordinating the drive and our appreciations to all
the donors.

If you wish to donate blood during the year,
contact the Jacksonville Blood Bank and please
have your donation credited to the Center’s account.
