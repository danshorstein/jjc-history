Center Embarks on
A New Frontier:

Jewish Education For The
Entire Family

 

In years past, Jewish identity was forged by a number of merging
forces. For many of our ancesters, it was a program or persecution.
For our grandparents, it was the immigrant experience and ethnic
neighborhoods. But for our children, these forces do not exist. So the
question for today’s parents is “If we don’t show our children a
meaningful Jewish life, who will?”

It is in answer to this question that the Family Council has begun a
yearlong series of programs that will touch all phases of Jewish life and.
experiences. The purpose of these programs is to broaden the
opportunities for education and celebration of Jewish life for the entire
family, and to promote the home as an equal partner with the school in
creating a vital Jewish environment for our children.
