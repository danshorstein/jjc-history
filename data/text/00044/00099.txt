SUNDAY SCHOOL
The Sunday School of the Jacksonville Jewish Center is planned expressly for children in Kindergarten,
First and Second Grades. A two hour program which is held 10:00-Noon, gives each child the opportunity
to experience the richness of his/her Jewish heritage through'song, dance, role playing, cooking, short
stories, games, arts and crafts. Emphasis is placed on the holidays and festivals; Jewish values and mitzvot
are encouraged.
Sunday School at the
Center is also a family
experience-building a
Sukkah together, baking
hamentaschen, and enjoying
relays and games on the
final day of school are just a
few of the many activities.
