Cantor emeritus Abraham Marton coordinated the annual Center Choral Society
Concert on March 3.
Featured artists were Cantor Louis Danto of Toronto and pianist Tali Kentof.
Many soloists added to the evening’s entertainment. The Center Choral Society
was joined by the Carter Nice, Jr. Chorus, accompanied by the Starlight
Symphonette.
We are grateful to Cantor Marton for his dedication to this worthwhile event and
his continued support of Jewish music in our community and the Cantor’s Assembly
of the Jewish Theological Seminary.
