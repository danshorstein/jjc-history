Hadassah
Hadassah, the Womens Zionist Organization of America, consists of over 370,000
members who work devotedly to maintain a level of excellence in services to Israel and
the U.S. The Jacksonville chapter has 900 members of which half are life members.
Our American-Scene projects include American Affairs, Jewish Education, Zionist
Affairs and Zionist Youth Activities through club and camp programs.
In Israel, the Hadassah University Medical Center at Kiryat Hadassah in Ein Karem,
Jerusalem, is a 720 bed teaching hospital serviced by a staff of 3,000. The Hadassah
University Hospital Mt. Scopus, is a regional hospital servicing the community of
eastern Jerusalem with health care and highly specialized facilities. The hospital has a
300 bed capacity, an Outpatient Department and a 13 bed Emergency Room.
Hadassah’s Youth Aliyah villages and schools have always reflected the demand of
Israels rapidly changing society. Today, Youth Aliyah reaches out to Israeli youths from
deprived socio-economic backgrounds.
Our Hadassah Israel Education Services encompass our Seligsberg-Brandeis
Comprehensive High School, the Hadassah Community College and our Vocational
Guidance Institute.
Hadassah and the Jewish National Fund (JNF) has been a Partnership since 1926.
The Jacksonville Chapter is a coordinating board which includes four groups..
TIKVA, which meets during the daytime on the second Tuesday, September through
May: HANNAH SENESCH, which meets on the second Wednesday evening; SHIR LI,
which also meets on the second Wednesday evening, and AVODAH which meets on
the third Wednesday evening.
Currently, the Jacksonville Chapter, in conjunction with all the state of Florida, is
designated as a pilot for a National Membership Drive named “2002” of which Roberta
Peters is National Chairman. Chairing the local “2002” Drive is Ronnie Adsit (262-1772)
and Evelyn Moskovitz (733-3568).
New residents and unaffiliated women are invited to join and learn more about
Hadassah. There is a group for everyone and we welcome your call. Call either of the
above phone numbers or one of the Presidents or Membership Chairpersons.
We wish everyone a Shana Tova, a year of good health and much happiness.
