Center Day Camp
Day Camping can be one of the most meaningful experiences a child will ever have. Our camp offers a situation in which
children can learn and develop by interacting with other children, a full scope of organized activities designed to make a child
aware of our Jewish heritage, develop new friendships, enjoy the wonders of nature that may be found on our large wooded
campsite, and the importance of active sports.
This year the Center summer camp programs offered a full-or half-day program and Day Care program for campers ages 2-8;
and our specialty camps included a fine arts and computer camp for older campers.
