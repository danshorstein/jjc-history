Solomon Schechter P.T.A.
The Solomon Schechter P.T.A. continues to serve as a great vehicle for bringing parents, faculty
and students closer together. P.T.A. provides the parents with an active role in the child’s
education, therefore, it is a vital part of the Solomon Schechter Day School. This year the Solomon
Schechter Day School will be celebrating its 25th anniversary and we would all love to help make it
a super successful year.
Special projects for the P.T.A. this year will include a sale of magazine subscriptions, book fairs,
candy sale and our Birthday Book-A-Bration Club. Through the various fund raising programs we
were able to purchase a laminating machine, two audio card readers, new playground equipment
for the Pre-School and many more needed supplies for the school. We look forward to an even
better year ahead. Without the help of our parents we could not have accomplished as much as we
did. Thanks to all of you.
This year our monthly meetings will be held on the third Thursday of each month. We have some
interesting speakers lined up and the topics for discussion should be interesting for everyone.
As president of the P.T.A., I look forward to working with all the parents, teachers and
administration of the Solomon Schechter Day School and Center. I hope that all the parents will
cooperate and assist in our efforts to improve the education of our children.
Pat Frisch
President
