RECREATiaN
The Jacksonville Jewish Center’s Recreation Program provides
good wholesome fun for both our members and for the entire
Jewish community. From the annual Alexander Brest Tennis Tournament in
October through Day Camp which begins in June, the Center provides
activities for all ages. Cub, Boy and Girl Scout Troops are sponsored by
the Center, Sunday Sports Association Basketball is the only sports program
in Jacksonville which caters to Jewish youth, and our facilities are unmatched
anywhere. An Olympic sized pool, surrounded by woods, tennis courts, ball fields,
playground, all provide unlimited recreation for your family.
Summer activities included our expanded Family Program, which features EXCLUSIVE
use of our beautiful nine acre campsite facility and pool seven days per week. Our eight
week summer Day Camp has programs for children age 2 thru grade 9. For more information
and rates, please call the Center.
