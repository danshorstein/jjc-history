Adult Education
EXPANSION IS TAKING PLACE ...
. . . Jewish mind expansion that is! Adult education has become an integral part of the ongoing activities at the Jacksonville Jewish
Center. During the year new classes and groups for study have been meeting regularly. Whether it was attendance at a
Minyainnaire program, viewing and discussing a Heritage tape or being part of a reading circle group, our members came
together to grow Jewishly. They thus lent testimony to the fact that men and women of all ages in our congregation are interested
in becoming better informed Jews.
As we look to the New Year in terms of Adult education, there is reason to feel excited. Stimulating speakers for Shabbaton
Weekends are going to be visiting and sharing their expertise with us. Interesting classes will be presented on Tuesday nights.
New Sunday morning programs are in the works. Most importantly, however, one of the most basic tenets of Judaism, the
importance of learning, is not only being acknowledged but is being given a life of its own at the Center.
Dot Verstandig
Chairperson
