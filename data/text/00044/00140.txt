Jewish Center
Experts agree that the survival of Judaism depends on the commitment of our youth today.
The Jacksonville Jewish Center sponsors three outstanding youth groups for youngsters in grades 5-12. Each group meets
regularly and enjoys a program of a religious, cultural and social nature.
Through active participation in these groups, a sense of pride and an identification with Judaism are instilled. Jewish values, attitude
and behavior patterns are discussed and carried out through various Mitzvah programs and study.
Conventions, regional activities and encampments give our youth the opportunity to meet other Jews from the Southeast
Region.
The youth programs are co-ordinated by Rabbi Dov Kentof and supervised by the Youth commission. All groups are affiliated
with the Southeast Region and United Synagogue of America.
