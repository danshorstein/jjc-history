Sunday Sports Association
The Sunday Sports Assocition is jointly sponsored by the four Synagogues in
Jacksonville and provides area Jewish youth with alternative athletic programs on
Sunday afternoons at the Center.
This very successful program is co-ordinated by Ira Goldfield. We are grateful to him
for his time and dedication to this important youth activity.
