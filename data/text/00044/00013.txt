About the Cover . . .
This year’s cover is the creation of George Strumlauf. It was
designed to represent a very meaningful and significant
celebration . . . “Simcha 25”
“Simcha 25 — A Season of Torah” will offer all members of
the Jacksonville Jewish Center — both young and old — a
joyous opportunity to reaffirm their loyalty to Torah and to
Judaic education.
Scheduled from January 22, through January 26, 1986,
“Simcha 25” will embrace three important and closely related
occasions:
* The Silver Anniversary of the Solomon Schechter Day
School.
* The dedication of a Sefer Torah.
* The establishment of the Jacksonville Jewish Center
Educational Trust Fund.
We look forward to your participation.
