An individual normally may expect to receive from
his effort or investment at least as much as he exerts
or spends.
One great exception exists. You cannot expect to
receive from the government as much as the government
takes from you in taxes, regardless of the glowing prom¬
ises about "security" of many types. The cost of ad¬
ministration prevents getting back more than a portion
of what is taken from you.
Let's not be deluded by politians' promises. Let's
maintain the great American traditions of independence,
thrift, productiveness, and pride. Only by those prin¬
ciples can we avoid the ruin which has come upon
European nations.
