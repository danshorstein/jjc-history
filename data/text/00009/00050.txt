To record in detail all the activities of the Center
would take more space than is provided in this volume.
The reader who is acquainted with Synagogue life realizes
that the account of a year’s activity by the affiliated or¬
ganizations and major departments do not convey a com¬
plete picture. The picture would be equally incomplete
if only events of which the Center was the sole sponsor
were spread over these pages. As a Center Synagogue we
cooperate with and encourage communal organizations
whose programs are compatible with our own. 1 he chrono¬
logical list of events of the Center or held at the Center,
whether at worship or on other occasions, it is hoped will
aid in the appreciation of the service rendered by the or¬
ganization to the local community and to the community
of Israel. For a complete record and closer examination of
the Center’s functioning, one would have to go to the files
of the Center Bulletin published weekly through the active
season.
