The year lq49 will always be remembered in the an¬
nals of Jacksonville Jewry. This is the year of the
establishment of the Y. M. H. A. Health Club, Gym, and
Youth Building. For the first time since the coming of
the post-war era, the dream was being fulfilled and satis¬
fied.

The Young Men s Heb rew Association has advanced
in the right direction. We have now an active organ¬
ization with a constructive, real, and concrete program to
offer everyone in the Community—young or old, man or
woman, boy and girl. Our activities are located at 2525
Riverside Ave., directly across the street from the River
Garden Home for the Aged. We have a modern, up-
to-date gymnasium and health club, equipped with the
latest innovations known in the athletic world. : Mr.
