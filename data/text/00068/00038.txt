Kids Club
Jacksonville Jewish Center Schools’ Extended Day Program
Kids Club is the Jacksonville Jewish
Center’s Extended Day Program for
ages 1-12. The program has steadily
grown over the past couple of years under
the leadership of Shereen Canady and the
wonderful Kids Club & Preschool staff
We are thrilled to welcome our new
Kids Club Coordinator, Rochelle Golomb.
Rochelle comes to us with 25 years of
experience working as an educator in Baltimore Jewish
schools. Her varied experiences include teaching preschool,
teaching cooking for school aged children, computer teacher,
and holiday youth service leader. She has participated
extensively in continuing teacher education and leadership
initiatives, as well as going on a faculty trip to Israel. She and
her husband, Jay Golomb, recently relocated to Jacksonville.
They look forward to joining our Center family and becoming
active Jacksonville Jewish Center members. They have two
beautiful, grown daughters. Please help us in welcoming
Rochelle and Jay to our community!
Kids Club highlights for the year ahead
include:
• Literature and famous author themes.
• Emphasis on fitness and fun.
• Opportunities for creative expression.
• Integrating Jewish values and mitzvot.
• Engaging and stimulating age appropriate
activities.
• Fantastic cooking and science experiences.
We are also excited to share our well-rounded afternoon
enrichment programs. We will be offering a special program
each day to all children attending our JJC preschool, day
school and religious school. Here are just a few classes being
offered this coming year:
• Dance classes taught by instructors from Mark Spivak’s
Dance Studio.
• Karate & Tae Kwon Do taught by instructors from Sun
Belt Karate Studio.
• Soccer taught by Jay Golomb & the Greater Experience
Soccer Academy.
• Art classes taught by Kim Miller of Art Expressions.
• Spanish taught by Renaissance Kids.
