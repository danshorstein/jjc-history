Summer at Camp Ki Tov 2009 can be summarized in
one word: AWESOME!!! Campers enjoyed daily activities
such as swimming, nature, drama, arts and crafts, sports
and gaga; not to mention the exciting projects initiated by
counselors that had campers singing songs, participating
in crafts, and playing games utilizing the entire campus.
Needless to say, by the end of summer, our goal of creating
memories to last a lifetime was achieved!
Each week’s activities and visitors revolved around
a theme such as Blast to the Past, Summer Safari, Home
Town Heroes to Super Heroes and Mitzvahs Around the World. Tuesdays were campers’ favorite
day as a different visitor came to camp and entertained us with everything from alligators and snakes
to scientific experiments. We were also fortunate to have Rabbi Dov Kentof lead the series, “When
a Jew Celebrates,” where campers played games, cooked latkes and listened to stories all while
learning about religious and cultural traditions of Judaism.
Our summer at Camp Ki Tov would not be complete without exciting field trips and overnights.
Our 4-year-old campers through our 6th graders ventured far and wide.
Simply stated Camp Ki Tov 2009 was a summer to remember!! The strong bonds formed
between the children and counselors were apparent as summer drew to an end and all were sad to
see the other go.
L’Shanah Tovah
—Erica Jolles
