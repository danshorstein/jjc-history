Special Events
Annual Fundraiser of Excellence in Education
The Fundraiser ended with the Patron Appreciation
Golf Classic on March 16 played at the Ponte Vedra Ocean
Course.
The beautiful, Waterford crystal, Horty T. Gendzier
Cup was presented to the tournament winners.
Horty T. Gendzier was a dedicated beacon of Jewish
education and a champion of youth at the Jacksonville Jewish
Center and the community. Once again she was remembered
and appreciated when this year’s Waterford crystal award,
the “HORTY,” was presented to the tournament champions.
