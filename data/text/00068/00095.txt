US Y-ON-WHEELS
USY on Wheels, sponsored by United Synagogue of
Conservative Judaism, gives teenagers an opportunity
to travel around the United States seeing landmarks,
historical sites and national parks. In addition, they meet
Jewish youth in the cities in which they stay.
