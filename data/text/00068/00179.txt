Shalom Baby
Mommy & Me with a Jewish Twist
The Jacksonville Jewish Center Preschool’s Shalom Baby program is in its fourth year now. Classes meet weekly to offer
mommies and babies an environment to play, learn and meet new friends. Each class offers songs, stories, circle time, crafts
and hot topic discussions.
This year we celebrated many milestones....first smiles, first steps, first teeth, first words, and first friends. We brought in
Rosh Hashanah, Yom Kippur, Passover, Hanukkah, Shabbat, as well as,Thanksgiving, Mother’s Day and Father’s Day together
with crafts and family traditions.
Shalom Baby offers its classes following the Preschool’s calendar and is easily available around the drop-off times of
both the Preschool and Dayschool.
If you are interested, please stop by the Preschool office for more information, or call Jennifer Katz (Class Instuctor) at
268-4200x143. Hope you can join us!!
