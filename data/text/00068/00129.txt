Shavu’ot - Confirmation
6 & 7 Sivan 5769, May 29-30, 2009
Shavu’ot celebrates the receiving of the Torah and
Ten Commandments. It is fitting that this festival should
acknowledge the accompanying sense of commitment to
Jewish life that Confirmation represents.
Throughout the festival, our Confirmands demonstrated
the knowledge they had gained through completion of the
Beit Midrash Hebrew High program by fully participating
in Shavu’ot services. In addition, students read essays they
had prepared on the theme “What Israel means to me”. As
is customary, the Center Sisterhood gave each participant a
Tanakh and booklet of mazel tov greetings from members
of the congregation. The Center Men’s Club presented a
Kiddush cup to each Confirmand.
