The Center
fundraiser for
Excellence
in Education began
Sunday, March 15,
with a silent auction
and brunch honoring
Judy Paul as the
2009 recipient of
the Rabbi David
Gaffney Leadership
in Education Award. A beautiful presentation was led by David
Bielski, Robin Morris and Gabrielle Magid. While the adults
enjoyed the raffle and brunch, children attended a complimentary
“Mini Raffle” with fun activities including a bounce house, a
puppet show and games with My Gym. The main prize of the
raffle was won by the Jolles family.
