Our maintenance staff shoulders one of the heav¬
iest burdens in our Center program, that of maintain¬
ing our facilities so that they are always in a present¬
able state for our use. Pictured at the right are Tim
Riley and Annie Mae Fort who have been loyal and
devoted members of our maintenance crew for a com-
bind total of 38 years.
Tim Riley coordinates all maintenance at the Cen¬
ter under the direction of our Executive Director.
Annie Mae Fort maintains our kitchen and prepares
our meals, not only for our adults but also for our vast
food program for our school children.
Also preparing tasty food at many of the Center’s
affairs and members’ simchas are three of our local
caterers: Mr. and Mrs. Albert Meyerhoff, Mrs. Mary
Safer and Mrs. Lena Berkowitz.
Our appreciation to this loyal and devoted staff
who have given many years of unselfish service to our
Synagogue-Center.
