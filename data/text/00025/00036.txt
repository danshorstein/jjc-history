THE CENTER CHORAL SOCIETY PICTURED AT THE ANNUAL CONCERT
Cantor Arthur Koret was the guest artist at the 18th Annual Concert
of Jewish Music, an annual event that was enjoyed by all. Proceeds realized
by his Concert were donated to the Cantors’ Institute of the Jewish Theo¬
logical Seminary of America.
Pictured below is our junior choral group, the Center Glee Club, and
is comprised of members of our Religious School performing for different
occasions during the course of the year. Both the Choral Society and Glee
Club are under the direction of Cantor Abraham Marton.
