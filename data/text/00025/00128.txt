With its dual goal of spreading friendship and en¬
abling married couples to share good times, the Mr.
and Mrs. Club is now in its 13th successful year.
Each month, exciting and novel affairs are held and
guided through the leadership of committees formed
to arrange each gala event.
The themes vary from month to month, but tradi¬
tional events include an extravagant New Year’s Eve
party, a mid-year Installation Banquet, and a family
picnic or outing during the summer months.
As in all other social clubs, there is a central core
of officers and board members, and the organization
is governed by husbands and wives serving and work¬
ing together. Through each member’s sharing the re¬
sponsibility of serving on a monthly committee, an
atmosphere of warm congeniality always prevails, and
the promotion of good fellowship is the end result.
All couples who are interested in joining and
sharing all the wonderful things offered by the Mr.
and Mrs. Club are urged to contact any of the club's
officers or board members.
