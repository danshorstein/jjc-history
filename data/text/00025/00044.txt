The Center Men's Club offers its membership the
opportunity to gather in Jewish fellowship, once each
month at a dinner meeting, and participate in a pro¬
gram that coincides with the current events of the day.
Commencing with the Emmy Award winning doc¬
umentary on the “Ku Klux Klan,'' we were able to
include a program with Senators Jack Matthews and
Tom Greene and Commissioner-elect Bill Basford, as
well as that outstanding joint affair with the Sister¬
hood when they presented the thought provoking skit
entitled “Stop the Judaism, I Want to Get Off," and
a joint brotherhood meeting. The culmination of this
year's programming occurred at the final meeting
where Mayor Louis Ritter, Reverend Robert Libby and
our own Rabbi Turetsky shared their experiences of
their recent Israel Pilgrimage with the Club.
Included in the price of Men's Club membership
is the annual paid-up affair Dinner-Dance, which in¬
cludes a free cocktail party as part of the membership
fee.
The Center Men's Club assists weekly in the pre¬
paring and serving of breakfast to the Minyan Club
and the years' activities are climaxed with the annual

fund raising Sunday School Picnic held in conjunction
with the opening of the Center Day Campsite. The
net proceeds from the Picnic are used to support youth

Special thanks are due this year to the Food
Chairman David Safer and his committee, to S. Robert

membership for their active participation.
