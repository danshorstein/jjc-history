The Tofield Memorial Library Committee is always
striving to involve many members of our congregation.
One of the new programs initiated this year was pub¬
lishing book reviews of recently acquired current books
in the Center Bulletin when space permitted. These
short sketches by Howard Haber, Publicity Chairman
for the library, have made the membership more aware
of the library’s many facilities and the outstanding
books which are offersd.
Appropriate books were made available again for the
summer day camp as was done in 1965. The campers
Members of the Library Committee
and the counsellors made use of these books during the
camp session and especially during inclement weather.
The additional library hours on Friday evenings fol¬
lowing services, Wednesday evenings in conjunction
with Adult Education classes and during day time
school hours have made our books more readily avail¬
able to a greater number of our congregants.
The library is open daily except Saturday. Many
thanks are due the committee members who work
throughout the year to make our library among the
best equipped Jewish libraries.
