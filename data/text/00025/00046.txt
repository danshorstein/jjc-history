Mr. Burton I. Grossman, Chairman of the Board, presenting a gift to
Bob and Linda Fischer on behalf of the congregation for seven wonderful
years of dedication and devotion to his job as Youth Director and Execu¬
tive Director of the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
Seven years of loyal devotion — For seven years the
Jacksonville Jewish Center was fortunate to have in
its midst a man with drive and dedication to his job,
his Center and his people. One cannot think of the
Jacksonville Jewish Center and not remember Bob
and Linda and all that they accomplished in their ef¬
forts for a more meaningful program for their congre¬
gants. To name just a few of the highlights of his
accomplishments one need only drive out State Road
13 and as you approach a sign indicating Center Day
Camp marvel at its success and beauty; one need
only visit with the youth of the congregation and see
the inspiration they received from Bob Fischer por¬
trayed in their actions and deeds; one need only visit
our Gym in the evenings or on weekends and realize
that the athletic program is a success because of his
endeavors. We hope that Bob will remember us as we
remember him.
Mr. Jack Becker, newly installed President, welcomes Mr. Irwin Wiener,
the new Executive Director and bids farewell to Bob Fischer and wishes
him success in his new venture.
