Rabbi Simcha Kling of Louisville, Kentucky delivered
the address at the Installation of Jack Becker as President
of the Jacksonville Jewish Center. Rabbi Kling is a personal
friend of our President and studied with him at the Seminary
during the year Mr. Becker took a Sabbatical. Rabbi Kling’s
message inspired all who attended with his “This is Your
Life” portrayal of our President.
