SHAVUOT — On this day, the Ten Commandments are read in the synagogue to
commemorate the giving of the Law (Torah) to Moses on Mount Sinai. Shavuot
also marks the early wheat harvest in the Holy Land. On this holiday Jews decorate
their homes and synagogues with greens and flowers.
