The Center Cub Pack with Sidney Engelhardt, Ted Freedman and
Harry Kowitz.
With enthusiasm and devotion by all concerned,
the Pack has remained among the leaders in the city.
The 44 Cubs between the ages of 8 and 10 participate
in weekly Den meetings at the respective Den Mothers'
homes with monthly Pack meetings held on the last
Thursday of the month at the Center.
At the annual Blue and Gold Banquet several
awards and recognitions were presented to our past
Director Robert Fischer, our past Cubmaster Sidney
Engelhardt and to Mrs. Hannah Fink, our dedicated
and overworked Chief Den Mother. Best Cub awards
were received by Neil Berinhout, Robert Fink and
Michael Nykerk.
