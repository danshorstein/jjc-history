JACKSONVILLE ZIONISTS
The local chapter of the Zionists Organization of America was estab¬
lished to further the aims and ideals of the Jewish homeland in Palestine.
The two Hadassah units are sister organizations to this great institution.
The Zionists meet the first Wednesday of every month for a social
and cultural gathering at which time important speakers and interesting
programs are presented to the group.
The Zionist officers are as follows: George B. Mehlman, president;
Nathan Newman, vice-president; Isadore Moscovitz, secretary; and H.
I ley m an, treasurer.
