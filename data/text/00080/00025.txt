THE MARCH OF PROGRESS
Has brought all Jews thruout the world face to face with
various problems. Even within our midst strange things happen.
There has been an awakening to the fact that Jewry must think
and act for itself.
In order to insure and retain our places in Society, we must
place ourselves in readiness to serve our people, our community
and our country. We shall press forward and energetically sup¬
port every sphere of activity for the relief of human suffering.
We Jews are endowed with a noble past — what are we go¬
ing to give to the next generation? Let’s forget petty differences
and unite all Jewry under the banner of service and righteous¬
ness. We, the members of the Jewish Progressive Society are
doing our part. Will you join and help?
Membership Dues $5.00 Per Year
We meet every second and fourth Thursday in the Jackson¬
ville Jewish Center, 8:30 P. M.
