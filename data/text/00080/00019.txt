The Daughters of Iseral
The Daughters of Israel serve in the capacity of a Sisterhood to the
Jacksonville Jewish Center, aiding the Congregation financially, and as¬
sisting in all Center activities. In addition the Daughters of Israel main¬
tains theHebrew School and Sunday School of the Center.
Meetings—First Wednesday of the month at the Center.
The outstanding affairs sponsored by the Daughters of Israel this year
were the Thangsgiving Dance and the annual Cabaret held January 31st,
which enjoyed the largest attendance in many years.
The organization sponsored the Chamisho Osor, Lag B’Omer,
Chanukah and Purim entertainments as well as the childrens’ Seder, of
the Sunday 'school and Hebrew School. The Daughters of Israel were of
great assistance to the Purim ball committee, and have especially helped
to produce this Reference Book and Directory.
