The Commission Club is an organization provid¬
ing both cultural and social activities for the single
Jewish community within the age group of 18-35.
Membership consists of a yearly fee. Projected
plans call for one party each month, complete with
entertainment, a weekend at Daytona Beach, including
young adults from other cities and periodic mailings
to keep members informed of all activities.
All young adults are welcome to join, meet new
people and renew old friendships. For further infor¬
mation, please contact any of the above members of
the Board of Directors.
