The Mr. and Mrs. Club is now in its 19th success¬
ful year, with a dual goal of promoting friendship, and
enabling married couples to share good times. Each
month exciting and novel affairs are held and guided
through the leadership of committees formed to ar¬
range each gala event.
The themes vary from month to month, but tra¬
ditional events include an extravagant New Year’s Eve
party. As in all other social clubs there is a central
core of officers and board members, and the organiza¬
tion is governed by husbands and wives serving and
working together. Through each member’s sharing the
responsibility of serving on a monthly committee, an
atmosphere of warm congeniality always prevails, and
the promotion of good fellowship is the end result.
