The newest organization to become affiliated with the Jacksonville Jewish Center is the PACESETTERS.
This is a social organization that is open to all Jewish couples of our community between the ages of 28
and 36. Membership is limited to 65 couples, with affairs being held every other month. The parties are
great fun and good times are had by all the PACESETTERS.
