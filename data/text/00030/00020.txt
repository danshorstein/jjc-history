SOUTHSIDE SITE MEMBERSHIP
The Center Southside site is open for family recreation throughout the
summer months. For a nominal charge, members of the community may use
all facilities of the site which include an Olympic swimming pool, complete
with dressing rooms and sun bathing areas, a well equipped children’s play¬
ground, tennis, basketball and handball courts and shaded picnic areas. Also
available are two ball fields, modem snack bar and recreational pavillion.
Many families take advantage of this wonderful program of family re¬
laxation by swimming, playing and picnicking together. Many new and in¬
teresting activities are being planned.
Plan now to participate!
