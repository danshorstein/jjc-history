A GROUP OF JUDAEANS AT A LEADER’S MEETING
The aim of our program is to make the member
desirous of actively participating in the development
of Eretz Yisrael; to enable him, through his experi¬
ence within Hashachar (Young Judaea), to make a
mature decision as to what form his participation will
follow.
Jewish traditions are something quite dear and
meaningful to us, and through our program, projects
and personal contact, we introduce them to our mem¬
bers.
We meet and sing and discuss and dance together
in a spirit of chavershaft.
