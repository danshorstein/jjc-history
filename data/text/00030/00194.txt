We are happy to announce the appointment of
Mr. A. Robert Gast as the new Executive Director of
the Jewish Center as of August, 1971.
Mr. Gast received his B.A. degree at Moravian
College and his M.A. in education at Lehigh Univer¬
sity. He is 31 years old, married and has two daugh¬
ters, Jody, age 6, and Jamie, age 2.
He was Chairman of the Department of Social
Studies at Liberty High School in Bethlehem, Pa. He
was also Hebrew teacher and youth advisor at the
Brith Sholom Community Center in Bethlehem and
Youth Activities Director at the Easton Jewish Com¬
munity Center and for the past five summers he has
been Director of the Easton Community Center Day
Camp.
The entire Center Family extends a hearty wel¬
come to the Gasts and hope that we will have many
years of pleasant association.
A. ROBERT CAST
Executive Director
SIDNEY COHEN
During the past year Mr. Sidney Cohen has been
our Executive Director and he will be missed by many
people who benefitted from his presence in many ways.
Perhaps even more than anyone, our children will miss
his tender guidance and loving direction on the basket¬
ball court, on the baseball field, and wherever sports
are involved. He showed our children not only how to
develop more confidence in themselves, but how the
sports field can be a place not only to have fun but
to develop character and learn a great deal about how
to get along with other people, how to cooperate with
the team, and how to face defeat.
May God bless Sid Cohen with good health and
happiness in the years to come.
