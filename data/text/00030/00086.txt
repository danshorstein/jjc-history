Another year has passed and “Gator Chapter” of
Aleph Zadik Aleph has fulfilled its pledge to Jewish
youth. We have done this by fulfilling B.B.Y.O.’s
“five fold” program consisting of Charity, Religious,
Athletic, Cultural and Social projects. Through these
functions, our chapter has become an integral part of
this community.
This past year, we have had fund raising drives
consisting of car washes, collecting for Muscular Dys¬
trophy, lending a helping hand to the Salvation Army
collection. We have also attempted to help our brothers
behind the Iron Curtain by participating in Soviet
Jewiy marches and demonstrations, bringing speakers,
sending telegrams, and creating a community interest
in this matter.
ABE ROGOZINSKI
President
This year, as every year, has been complemented
by new and imaginative concepts in youth conventions.
This past Florida Region Convention, the biggest and
most successful, was held at a Dude Ranch.
Our fraternal order extends an invitation to all who
want to meet the challenges facing today’s Jewish
youth.
Shalom,
