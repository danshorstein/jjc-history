The newest edifice to enhance the Jacksonville
skyline and to enrich the lives of the Jacksonville
Jewish Community is the 17-story high rise on Uni¬
versity Boulevard — Mount Carmel Gardens.
Mount Carmel Gardens was conceived by a dedi¬
cated group of men and women to provide maximum
living environment at a minimal rental. It is a non¬
profit corporation and is sponsored by the Jewish
Community Council of Jacksonville.
Mount Carmel Gardens apartments are designed,
•even in the smallest detail, to provide comfortable and
pleasant surroundings. From the initial concept, Mount
Carmel Gardens has strived to crease a living commu¬
nity which provides much more than housing. Mount
Carmel Gardens offers a 17-story building on a beau¬
tifully landscaped site, 24-hour staffing, community
