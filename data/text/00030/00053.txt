ATID is the College Age Organization of Judaism
sponsored by the United Synagogue of America. ATID
invites those who wish to involve themselves in Jewish
life and thought. ATID meets on a weekly basis dur¬
ing the summer vacation to discuss current topics of
interest, followed by a social hour.
Local ATIDniks had a most successful summer
with get-togethers each Wednesday at different homes
with varied and informative topics discussed as:
“Abortion”, “Jewish Defense League,” “Power of the
Press” with Brad Davis from Channel 4 as guest
speaker, and many other current topics of interest.
The outstanding summer would not have been
possible without the excellent work cjone by the two
Chairmen, Earl Berman and Margaret Rose, for the
Planning of programs and mailing out postcards.
ATID looks forward to seeing more interested col¬
lege Jewish youth for the coming summer.
