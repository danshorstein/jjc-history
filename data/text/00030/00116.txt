The Tofield Memorial Library is a depository of
books, information and materials of Jewish interest
for knowledge, recreation and leisure, and appreciation
of the arts. To insure that all holdings are easily ac¬
cessible and available to every member of the congre¬
gation, the Library has been kept open every day of
the week, including Friday evening and Saturday
afternoon after services.
The Sisterhood - Library Oneg Shabbat was an
enjoyable program. During the year through the Bul¬
letin and the Sisterhood representative, the congre¬
gation was informed about the many books available.
The Library continues to grow because of the in¬
terest of the congregation in making donations to
the Tofield Memorial Library.
