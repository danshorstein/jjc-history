Message from the President
WILBUR MARGOL
President
As this New Year 5732 approaches we look back
at the Center’s past accomplishments (and failures)
only to find new challenges to test our imagination
and dedication for the future.
Just as Abraham was tested in the biblical story
we read on Rosh Hashana, so will we be testing the
devotion, dedication, and the inner strength of our
congregation to build the new Synagogue Center on
State Road 13. Only once in a lifetime does the aver¬
age Jew have the privilege of taking part in such a
magnificent and beautiful “Mitzvah.”
With the tremendous growth of our Day School
from some 90 students to approximately 260, the need
for adequate facilities becomes even greater.
Will you be one of those who takes ONE STEP
forward to help?
May this New Year bring you good health, and
one in which God enables you to meet the challenge
of a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity.
