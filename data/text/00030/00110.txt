The Cub Scouts, as an integral part of the National
Organization of Boy Scouts of America continues to be
one of the outstanding opportunities offered to boys
between the ages of 8 and 10 years to become part of
a growing organization that strives to teach them
how to become better citizens, to explore the out-of-
doors and participate in new experiences.
Pack 14 has had continuous charter renewal for the
past 32 years with a varied program that offers all the
challenges of Scouting. To keep such a program at top
level for so many years requires the cooperation of
many people. We offer our thanks for a job well done
to Cubmaster Sidney Engelhardt; Den Mother Coach,
Mrs. Fred Kupperman; Den Mothers, Mrs. Paul Hen¬
nes, Mrs. Stephen Puldy, Mrs. Stanley Ruby and Mrs.
Ira Loeb; and to the Committee Chairman Stanley
Ruby.
During the year Cubs participated in the Goodwill
collection program, Pinewood Derby, Rocket Derby, a
Carnival and the annual Blue and Gold Banquet and
Boy Scout Sabbath. Recruitment of new members
starts in September and we look forward to a reward¬
ing year.
