ABRAHAM E. SELBER TRUST FUND
Mr. Philip Selber presenting a check to Edith Gendzier and Gary Gendzier
on behalf of the Abraham E. Selber Trust Fund. Ted Gross, Principal, is
shown at left. Not pictured: Michelle Ansbacher.
The Abraham E. Selber Trust Fund is a $20,000 endowed scholarship to
encourage students of the Center in careers such as Rabbi, Cantor, Edu¬
cator, Teacher, Youth Worker. This Fund enables the Jewish Center to
strengthen its program of assisting and encouraging high school students
to expose themselves to national programs of intensive Jewish living and
learning, such as Camp Ramah and all day religious schools.
