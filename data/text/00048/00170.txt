Brownie Troop #882
The girls of Brownie Troop #882 continue the commitment of living by the Girl Scout Law which
Juliette Gordon Low, Girl Scout Founder, began 78 years ago.
Our girls often fill the halls of the Center with the sounds of singing, laughing and talking. The
meetings pass all too quickly “edible” first and knots, trail blazing, cooking, sewing and having a great
time. This year we brushed and fed horses and learned to make frozen yogurt.
Under the supervision of the Jacksonville
Jewish Center, our group of girls, grades 1st -
3rd, has become an overflowing, active troop.
Congratulations to our “Fly-Ups” Elizabeth
Baker, Sonya Dunkle, and Hadassah Phillips.
