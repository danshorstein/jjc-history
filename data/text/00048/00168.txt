Solomon Schcchter PTA
The Solomon Schechter PTA continues to serve as a great vehicle for bringing
faculty, school administration, students and parents closer together.
The PTA plays a significant role in supplying much needed equipment and
materials for the Day School and Pre-School through our successful fund-raising
projects.
PTA also provides the parents with an opportunity to take
an active part in their child’s education.
With your help and enthusiasm we shall have a very
successful year. I look forward to working with all of you.
Best wishes for a very happy and healthy new year.
Pat Frisch
President
Special projects for the PTA this year will include a sale of magazine subscrip-
tions, two book fairs, a candy sale and our Book-A-Bration Club. The PTA issues
a school newsletter and sponsors the newsletter “Your Child,”
the Shabbat Box program and holiday observances.
