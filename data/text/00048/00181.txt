From the Executive Director
5749 has truly been a busy year. It was during the past year that the major expansion and
renovation of our Center was completed. Our new facility with a total of about 100,000 sq. ft. is
now one of the most functional of any synagogue Centers in the entire country. We should all be
proud to call the Jacksonville Jewish Center “our Center”.
It was a very active year on a programming level also and in spite of our construction program,
not one scheduled activity was cancelled due to construction.
Beginning at last High Holy Days, our year started with over 2000 in attendance for services in
our expanded sanctuary. Jr. Congregation for the Holy Days also as at record levels.
Our Professional Staff is dynamic and makes our Jewish Center family truly outstanding. I
would personally like to thank all for making my life enriching and rewarding, and I look forward
with great anticipation to many years to come. £ ric q Reiter
