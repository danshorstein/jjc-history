The Sunday School
A “Hands on”Experience
The children of our Sunday School pro-
gram learn about holidays, Bible stories,
life cycle events, and symbols of Judaism.
Through song, dance music, arts and
crafts, storytelling, games and a variety
of other activities, children learn about
these topics by experiencing them in crea-
tive and innovative ways. Children also
become familiar with the synagogue as a
comfortable, secure, and caring place. In
addition, the values of tzedakah and help-
ing others are emphasized.
Junior Congregation
Junior Congregation is modeled after the Main Congregation. It is an organization of all students
attending our Supplemental School. Its goal is to conduct worship, led by their own advisor and
the children themselves. We have found that children who attend Shabbat morning services not
only become familiar with the service and feel comfortable attending, also have an opportunity to
read Hebrew extensively and to put into practice that which is taught during the week.
