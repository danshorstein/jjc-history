Marilyn Sandler
“Within our school we are all different and we are all alike.
What matters most, however, is that we are all significant.”
Whatever the child’s needs, the curriculum is geared to
those needs. The idea is to give the children the best education
possible.
Our child-care program is unique. We feel the differences
and strong points are, we offer a specialized environment for
each child.
Our environment fosters a strong sense of individual identity.
We enable each child to feel safe and secure within various
groups. Trusting others, forming friendships are possible only if
the child posesses a positive self-concept.
The contribution our school and day care make to the
development of young children is the most wonderful part of
being involved in their care and education. We welcome all
Jewish children in the community regardless of affiliation.
Marilyn Sandler
