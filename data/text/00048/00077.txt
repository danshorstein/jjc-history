The Jewish Women’s Bowling League is composed of women who are members of all congregations.
They meet together weekly to enjoy a morning of good fellowship and exercise. Bowling for some goes
back as early as the 40s and 50s.
Consisting of six teams with four players each, the league begins play each Tuesday at 9:30 a.m. at Bowl
America in Mandarin.
For those who like to bowl, but not regularly - pacers are welcome.
