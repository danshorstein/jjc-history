HANUKKAH 4
CELEBRATION
This year’s Hanukkah celebration was a combined effort off all the Center’s youth, scout and education groups and Sisterhood.
The festivities began with the lighting of the Menorah followed by a festival musicale presented by our school children. The excitement
continued with a fun-filled carnival which was enjoyed by over 200 people.
Our thanks to Peggy Organes and Blanche Slott for coordinating this family celebration.
