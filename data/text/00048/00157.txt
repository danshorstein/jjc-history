Jacksonville, Florida Chapter Hadassah
Hadassah, the Women’s Zionist Organization of America, Inc. was
born out of Henrietta Szold’s vision of a new kind of Jewish
woman and a new kind of organization to channel her commitment
and compassion for the benefit of our people.
385,000 American Jewish women are awakened to who and what
they are, and how they can live richer, fuller lives in service to
others.
75 years here in Jacksonville, our membership of over 1,000 has
shared a dream of major growth and achievement. Advances in
research, treatment and technology have kept the Hadassah
Medical Organization at the forefront of health care worldwide.
The Hadassah Medical Relief Association has extended our reach
into 22 nations. We have made important gains in nuturing both
the young minds and the fragile soil of Israel. New career programs
at the Hadassah Community College, our work with the Ethiopians
through youth Aliyah, and Hadassah Vocational Guidance Institute
are all helping thousands of young Jewish men and women to
build better lives for themselves and a stronger Jewish nation.
New residents and unaffiliated women are invited to join us. Please
call one of our presidents so that we may welcome you!
We wish all of you the Blessings of Health, Peace, and Joy in the
coming year.
Joan Waltz
