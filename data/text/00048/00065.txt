THE CENTER CEMETERY
The Cemetery Committee maintains two cemeteries for its members. Arrangements for cemetery plots can be made by calling the Center
office.
Serving on the committee are: Michael Schneider, chairman; Joe Becker, Sid Berkowitz, Joel Bressler, Charlotte Fialkow,
Sonny Sandler, Blanche Slott.
It is with deep regret that we record the passing of Kenneth Bowden, long-time employee of the Center. During his tenure, Mr. Bowden
served as comptroller and supervisor of the Center’s cemeteries.
In 1985, Bowden retired from his position as comptroller but continued to supervise the Center’s cemeteries from his home. At his
retirement party he remarked that it was his pleasure to work with such a fine group of volunteers and staff members.
Although he had a “business only” attitude at times, he was a very generous and caring person. He was consistently admired for having the
keen ability to get the job done efficiently.
