Sunday Funday Basketball
Exciting and Unbeatable
Gayle Bailys, Recreation Director
There were 120 children, 28 volunteer coaches and 5 volunteer referees who enjoyed an 8-game season. From Hanukkah
to Purim the gym was bustling with activity almost every Sunday. The competition was fierce but fun.
Our children learned and improved their basketball skills. Dribbling, passing, team work and of course shooting. The
8'-10' baskets were hit, time after time. Basket after basket the points climbed. Each week with the continued support and
knowledge of the coaches, the children improved, from four baskets a game to 20. Plays were practiced and implemented,
rules were learned and followed. The excitement mounted until the very end.
It was another successful season for the Sunday Sports Association Basketball League. A big thank you to those who
volunteered their time and knowledge, you helped make our season a triumph.
Some were undefeatable!
