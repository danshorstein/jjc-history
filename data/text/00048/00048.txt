Hebrew High School
Director: Rabbi Dov Kentof
The Judaica High School is the creation of the synagogue which offers Jewish youth an opportunity to explore the broad
expanse of resources available through our heritage. This program seeks to provide exciting, innovative exploration of
Judaism upon which each student might build. It involves children in 8th through 11th grades and is required for
Confirmation.
Courses: Current Jewish Issues, Introduction to Jewish Philosophy, Famous Jewish People, Jewish History, Holocaust.
