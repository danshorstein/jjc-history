The Jewish Community Alliance
(JCA) is the centralized facility for
social, recreational and cultural acti-
vities for the entire Jacksonville
Jewish community. The JCA is a
home away from home, a meeting
place for children, teens, singles and
single parents, adults, and seniors. It
is a day care center for children while
their parents work; a place to swim,
to work out, to play basketball, rac-
quetball, and tennis; to enjoy plays,
musical concerts, lectures, and
classes. The JCA is the place where
you will meet new friends and enjoy
old friends.
The JCA’s goal is to enhance a
sense of cohesiveness within our
Jewish community and to foster a
renewed dedication to Jewish and
American traditions. The JCA serves
all Jews, regardless of age or
circumstance.

Jewish War Veterans
Lt. Meyer Leibovitz Post 199, Jacksonville, Florida
After a busy year, Post 199 is still one of the major Posts in the North-Central Florida District
Council of J.W.V., as well as being very active in the North-East Florida Veterans Council.
Once again, we have enjoyable speakers and programs at our monthly meetings. At our July
meeting we had Ms. Ann Hyman, Features Columnist of the Florida-Times Union, recounting her
experiences and interesting visits to various Russian historic cities.
We are very active and supportive of hospitalized Veterans in Lake City and Gainesville Medical
Centers, and are also interested in Jacksonville Community affairs.
We had the largest turn-out of veteran organizations at the Memorial Day services at Evergreen
Cemetery.
Post 199 also led the parade marching with our colors and the Israeli flag to the J.C.A. grounds with
a large contingent in honor of Israel’s 40th Anniversary. We also had an information booth and even
wrote new applicants.
Come join us in promoting Judaism and the community, fight anti-semitism and help the Veterans!
