Shalom,
“A Tradition of Faith” this is our theme
for the year and this is what Sisterhood is
truly all about. TRADITION. A TRADITION
that is going into its 67th year of service and
commitment to our Synagogue.
We are a proud group of women, actively
involved in activities which enrich our con-
gregation, support our schools and enhance
our youth programs. We also provide leam-
ing opportunities for you to grow as a con-
cerned Jewish woman of the 1990’s. Since
our members span a wide range of interests,
ages and backgrounds, our programs are
designed to enhance your individual per-
sonal growth, Jewish involvement and feel-
ing of community.
We are looking forward to an exciting and productive New Year. Our new board is full of
energy and a strong sense of commitment to our goals of 100% involvement in Center
life! We need you, your involvement to make this happen. Somewhere among our
activities is a place just right for you! Help us to continue our “Tradition of Faith.” We
have faith in you...please have faith in us. Join YOUR Sisterhood today!
May the New Year be bright with hope and blessed with peace, health and happiness.
L’Shana Tovah,
KATHY OSTERER
