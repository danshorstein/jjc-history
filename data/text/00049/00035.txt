The Sunday School
A “Hands on”Experience
The children of our Sunday School pro-
gram learn about holidays, Bible stories,
life cycle events, and symbols of Judaism.
Through song, dance music, arts and
crafts, storytelling, games and a variety
of other activities, children learn about
these topics by experiencing them in crea-
tive and innovative ways. Children also
become familiar with the synagogue as a
comfortable, secure, and caring place. In
addition, the values of tzedakah and help-
ing others are emphasized.
