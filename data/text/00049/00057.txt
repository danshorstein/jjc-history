AUTO (Adults United to Organize)
AUTO is the Supplemental School organization that involves the Afternoon
Hebrew School and Sunday School families in the religious education of its
children.
This organization provides the opportunity for parents, teachers and students to
share holiday programming and the experiences related to the learning environ-
ment provided at the Jacksonville Jewish Center.
Discussion groups, family campouts, Havdalah services and many special shared
moments between parents and children make the learning of Judaica and Hebraica
so much more meaningful. Supplemental School Hebrew education becomes a
warm and wonderful “family affair.”
We look forward to this coming year with enthusiasm and anticipation. Becoming involved in your child’s
total Jewishness will be rewarding and always fun.
Please plan on attending our Open House this fall and our special events throughout the year.
Best wishes for a joyous, healthy and happy New Year.
