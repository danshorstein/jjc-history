CENTER CEMETERY
The Jacksonville Jewish Center maintains two cemeteries, the Center Memorial Park
and a major section of Evergreen Cemetery. The Cemetery Committee assures the
proper maintenance, care and upkeep, guaranteeing a fitting tribute to our departed
loved ones.
