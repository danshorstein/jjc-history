The Jewish Community Alliance is a centralized facility for cultural, recreational, and
enrichment activities for the entire Jacksonville Jewish community. The dream of
many is now a reality!
The JCA provides opportunities for the development of an educated, knowledgeable
leadership and intelligent, participating citizenry. The rich and varied programs of
Jewish and general activities include:
Cultural Arts Early Childhood
Community Services Youth
Social Adult and Senior Adult
Education Single Adult
Health and Physical Education

The JCA is a “state-of-the-art” facility, staffed by the very best professionals, whose
main goal is to serve members in a first class manner. Meeting rooms and a special
Organization Room are open for use by all local Jewish organizations.
The Jewish Community Alliance’s goal is to enhance the sense of cohesiveness
within our Jewish community, and to foster a renewed dedication to the values of
American and Jewish democratic traditions. Its mission is to serve all Jews, of any age
or circumstance.
