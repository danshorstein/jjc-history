The Solomon PTA Book-A-Bration program encourages the parents of each child
enrolled in the Solomon Schechter Day School and the pre-school department to purchase
a book honoring their child. Mrs. Edith Horovitz, chairman of this program, has selected and
purchased 600 new books for the Day School Library. A special book plate with the child’s
name is placed in the book. Books may be dedicated in honor of teachers, friends and loved
ones. This program has a two fold purpose of providing new books for the students and for
satisfying the library’s requirements needed for accreditation of the Solomon Schechter Day
School.
For the past year since construction has been completed, the Tofield Library commit-
tee has been endeavoring to reassemble the library. Book shelves for the second and third
levels of the library were donated by the Gendzier family through the Rita Gendzier Judaica
Fund. The second level is designated as a “Reference Area.” The third level will house the
Tofield Hebrew and Yiddish collection and will be accessible for study and research.
