55+ CLUB
The 55+ Club has a joint membership of all Jacksonville syna-
gogues. It meets the second Sunday of the month from 2:00 to
4:00 P.M. at the Center. The annual dues is $10 per person.
Informative speakers discuss health and health care, psychology,
insurance and other interesting topics. Dance and singing groups
have presented exciting entertainment to the group. Refresh-
ments are always served and there is always time for socializing.
Also, the group offers its members an opportunity for discount
theater tickets and trips, plus our annual luncheon in June.
Members are the backbone for Minyans and for visitation of the
sick. Join us , we'd love for you to be a member!
