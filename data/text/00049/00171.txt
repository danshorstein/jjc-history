Early Childhood Department
Our Children, Our Treasures
The Jacksonville Jewish
Center, for over 40 years, has provided
an innovative and exceptional
educational program for pre-schoolers.
Open to all Jewish children of the
community, the pre-school blends a
general education program with
instruction in the fundamentals of
Jewish life and Torah. The goal is to
give our young children the best
possible start in life, and to provide
them with a solid educational
foundation.
Our physical facility is new, spacious, and geared to the pre-
school child. The full indoor and outdoor play areas and specially-
designed classes allow for an enjoyable learning environment. Our
highly-experienced teachers are sensitive to the developmental needs of
each child and attempt to involve the child's family as much as possible
in the learning process.
In addition to our pre-school, we operate a fully-licensed day
care facility. We provide our day care students with a caring and secure
environment in a stimulating Jewish atmosphere.
Our children are our treasures. Ken Yirbu, "May they grow in
numbers."
Marilyn Sandler
