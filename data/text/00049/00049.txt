Three programs complement the Center’s Adult Educa-
tion efforts. They are featured at Shabbat Services on a
once-a-month basis. These programs have been a popular
addition and are designed to inform and enlighten our
congregation’s knowledge of Jewish life, as well as to
encourage more of our members to join in our very well-
attended services.
