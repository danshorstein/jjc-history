This year the Family Council expanded its programming to include projects of
service to the community. An inspiring Selichot program was presented to the
entire congregation. It was so well received that the Family Council shared it again
with a smaller group of its members. As in the past, Family Council decorated the
Goldman Sukkah and sponsored a family second night of Sukkot dinner. This
year’s Hanukkah Carnival was even more successful than years before. Again,
Blanche Slott joined the group to put on the Carnival booths and dinner. This year
a family Menorah Lighting added the proper spirit to this special holiday event. As
an outreach project, Family Council and Rabbi Kentof sponsored a Model Seder
for our New Jewish American friends from the Soviet Union as well as social
gatherings at the Center and Goldhead State Park.
