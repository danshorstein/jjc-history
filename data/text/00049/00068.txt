Sunday Funday Basketball
Exciting and Unbeatable
There were 114 children, 17 volunteer coaches and volunteer referees who enjoyed a 10-game season. From
Hanukkah to Purim the gym was bustling with activity almost every Sunday. The competition was fierce but fun.
Basketball skills were improved, team work experienced and friends were made. It was another successful season for
the Sunday Sports Association Basketball League. A big thank you to those who volunteered their time and
knowledge, you helped make our season a triumph.
Thanks to our Volunteer Coaches: Fred Abbott, Art Dworkin, Bobby Fleet, Ilya Giter,
Bruce Glassman, Alan Goldman, Bruce Horovitz, Wayne Law, Bill Sari, Gary Schoenberg,
Barry Schultz, Larry Smolley, Joni Shmunes, Tom Spriggs, Brent Trager, Keith True, Jay Tobin,
John Windom.
-Gayle Bailys, Recreation Director
