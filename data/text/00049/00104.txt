The Jewish Women’s Bowling League is composed of
women of all ages who are members of all congregations.
Bowling for some goes back as early as the 40’ s and 50’s.
At the present time the league consists of six teams with four players each. Team play begins at 9:30 A.M each Tuesday
at Bowl America. For those who like to bowl, but not regularly, pacers are welcome. If interested please call, Blanche

Veterans are Special People
On March 15, 1990, the Jewish War Veterans of the United States celebrated its 94th birthday. We pride
ourselves with being in the forefront among our nation’s civic groups in supporting the well-earned rights of all
veterans, in promoting American democratic principles, in defending Jewish causes, and in vigourously opposing
bigotry, anti-Semitism, and terrorism both here and abroad.
We are the patriotic voice of American Jewry. We must be alert and cannot afford to lessen our vigilance.
We need more J.W.V. members to let our legislators knowthat we have earned, the hard way, the right to be heard.
Irvin Glassberg
